<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Method: 通过研究有限张量范畴的投射生成元的自同态代数的Hochschild上同调性质，建立范畴上同调与代数上同调之间的联系。

Result: 证明了有限张量范畴具有有限生成上同调当且仅当其投射生成元的自同态代数的Hochschild上同调是有限生成的。

Conclusion: 该结果将有限张量范畴的有限生成上同调猜想转化为更具体的代数条件，为验证猜想提供了新的途径。

Abstract: It has been conjectured that finite tensor categories have finitely generated cohomology. We show that this is equivalent to finitely generated Hochschild cohomology for the endomorphism algebras of the projective generators.

### [3] [Universal $T$-matrices for quantum Poincaré groups: contractions and quantum reference frames](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.01058)
*Angel Ballesteros,Diego Fernandez-Silvestre,Ivan Gutierrez-Sagredo*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 该论文研究了量子群的通用T-矩阵（Hopf代数对偶形式）及其收缩理论，提出了新的(1+1)维中心扩展Poincaré李代数量子形变，并建立了相对论量子参考系变换的对称性结构。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 最近的研究表明，(1+1)维中心扩展Galilei群的Hopf代数对偶形式是非相对论量子参考系变换的代数基础。这启发了研究者探索相对论情形下的对应结构，即寻找描述相对论量子参考系变换的对称性代数。

Method: 1. 重新审视量子群的通用T-矩阵（Hopf代数对偶形式）并发展其收缩理论
2. 以(1+1)维类时κ-Poincaré T-矩阵作为第一个示例进行详细计算
3. 构造新的(1+1)维中心扩展Poincaré李代数量子形变
4. 提出该量子形变的通用T-矩阵
5. 应用Hopf代数对偶形式收缩理论，将Poincaré T-矩阵收缩到其非相对论对应物

Result: 1. 成功构造了新的(1+1)维中心扩展Poincaré李代数量子形变
2. 给出了该量子形变的通用T-矩阵的显式形式
3. 通过收缩理论证明，该Poincaré T-矩阵的非相对论极限恰好是量子参考系相关的Galilei T-矩阵
4. 在适当的基下，相关的量子Poincaré群被识别为(1+1)维类空κ-Poincaré对偶Hopf代数的非平凡中心扩展

Conclusion: 本文引入的Poincaré Hopf代数对偶形式是描述相对论量子参考系变换对称性结构的自然候选者。该工作建立了相对论与非相对论量子参考系变换代数之间的系统联系，为量子引力、量子参考系理论等领域提供了重要的数学工具。

Abstract: Universal $T$-matrices, or Hopf algebra dual forms, for quantum groups are revisited, and their contraction theory is developed. As a first illustrative example, the (1+1) timelike $κ$-Poincaré $T$-matrix is explicitly worked out. Afterwards, motivated by recent results on the role of the Hopf algebra dual form of a quantum (1+1) centrally extended Galilei group as the algebraic object underlying non-relativistic quantum reference frame transformations, a new quantum deformation of the (1+1) centrally extended Poincaré Lie algebra is obtained, and its universal $T$-matrix is presented. Finally, the Hopf algebra dual form contraction is applied to this Poincaré $T$-matrix, showing that its corresponding non-relativistic counterpart is precisely the Galilei $T$-matrix associated with quantum reference frames. In this way, the Poincaré Hopf algebra dual form introduced here stands as a natural candidate for describing the symmetry structure of relativistic quantum reference frame transformations. In the appropriate basis, the associated quantum Poincaré group is recognized, remarkably, as a non-trivial central extension of the (1+1) spacelike $κ$-Poincaré dual Hopf algebra.

### [5] [Dual Murnaghan-Nakayama rule for Hecke algebras in Type $A$](https://arxiv.org/abs/2503.12299)
*Naihuan Jing,Ning Liu,Yu Wu*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 本文为A型Hecke代数建立了对偶Murnaghan-Nakayama规则，通过顶点算子应用上划分λ的约化，并使用"砖块表格"组合模型给出了显式递归公式。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统的Murnaghan-Nakayama规则基于下划分μ的约化提供迭代算法。本文旨在建立对偶版本，通过约化上划分λ来研究Hecke代数不可约特征值χ^λ_μ的计算。

Method: 使用顶点算子技术，将约化应用于上划分λ而非下划分μ，并采用"砖块表格"组合模型来明确表述对偶Murnaghan-Nakayama规则的递归公式。

Result: 成功建立了A型Hecke代数的对偶Murnaghan-Nakayama规则，该规则通过顶点算子应用上划分λ的约化，并给出了基于砖块表格组合模型的显式递归公式，改进了作者先前的工作。

Conclusion: 本文为A型Hecke代数特征值计算提供了对偶Murnaghan-Nakayama规则，通过顶点算子和砖块表格组合模型实现了上划分λ的约化，完善了该领域的理论框架。

Abstract: Let $χ^λ_μ$ be the value of the irreducible character $χ^λ$ of the Hecke algebra of the symmetric group on the conjugacy class of type $μ$. The usual Murnaghan-Nakayama rule provides an iterative algorithm based on reduction of the lower partition $μ$. In this paper, we establish a dual Murnaghan-Nakayama rule for Hecke algebras of type $A$ using vertex operators by applying reduction to the upper partition $λ$. We formulate an explicit recursion of the dual Murnaghan-Nakayama rule by employing the combinatorial model of ``brick tabloids", which refines a previous result by two of us (J. Algebra 598 (2022), 24--47).

</details>
