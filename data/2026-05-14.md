Method: 通过$\imath$箭图代数、$\imath$Hall代数以及Nakajima-Keller-Scherotzke箭图簇的量子Grothendieck环，给出了拟分裂$\imath$量子群的两种实现；并在$\imath$Hall代数框架下构造了对偶典范基，证明了其辫群作用不变性以及转移矩阵系数的正性。

Abstract: We survey some recent developments on the theory of dual canonical bases for quantum groups and $\imath$quantum groups. The $\imath$quiver algebras were introduced by Wang and the first author, which are used to give two realizations of quasi-split $\imath$quantum groups of type ADE: one via the $\imath$Hall algebras and the other via the quantum Grothendieck rings of Nakajima-Keller-Scherotzke quiver varieties. The geometric construction of the $\imath$quantum groups produces their dual canonical bases with positivity, generalizing Qin's geometric realization of quantum groups of type ADE. Recently, the authors provided a new construction of the dual canonical basis in the setting of $\imath$Hall algebras, and proved that it is invariant under braid group actions, and obtained the positivity of the transition matrix coefficients from the Hall basis to the dual canonical basis. *Luan Bezerra,Iryna Kashuba,Hongda Lin*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 本文证明了量子环面代数与无扭仿射Yangian之间的退化同构，建立了PBW基并识别了古典极限。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 建立仿射Yangian与量子环面代数之间的严格代数对应关系，将Drinfeld猜想从有限维情形推广到仿射情形。

Method: 通过构造典范滤过，证明作为C[ħ]-代数，仿射Yangian与量子环面代数的分次代数同构；利用新的无挠性论证和拓扑Nakayama引理构造量子环面代数的PBW基。

Result: 对所有无扭仿射Kac-Moody李代数，建立了退化同构；给出了仿射Yangian的PBW基；识别了其古典极限为U(g[u])。

Abstract: We establish a degeneration isomorphism between quantum toroidal algebras and untwisted affine Yangians, valid for all untwisted affine Kac-Moody Lie algebras. Specifically, we prove that the affine Yangian $Y_\hbar(\mathfrak{g})$ is isomorphic, as a $\mathbb{C}[\hbar]$-algebra, to the associated graded algebra of the quantum toroidal algebra $U_\hbar(\mathfrak{g}^{\mathrm{tor}})$ with respect to a canonical filtration. A key ingredient of independent interest is our construction of a PBW basis for $U_\hbar(\mathfrak{g}^{\mathrm{tor}})$ itself, which relies on a new torsion-freeness argument for the quantum toroidal algebra and the topological Nakayama lemma.

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Method: 使用n个中心荷1/2的有理Virasoro TQFT副本构建3d量子引力模型，对所有3d拓扑求和计算配分函数，并在小n时与边界系综匹配，然后研究大中心荷极限下的行为。

We discuss these aspects in detail and conclude with lessons for semiclassical quantum gravity.
