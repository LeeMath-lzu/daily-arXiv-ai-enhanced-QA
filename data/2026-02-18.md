### [1] [Algebraic Phase Theory III: Structural Quantum Codes over Frobenius Rings](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.16346)
*Joe Gildea*

Main category: math.RA

TL;DR: 论文通过Frobenius对偶的代数框架，从有限交换Frobenius环中直接推导出量子相位配对、Weyl算子代数和稳定子码，无需传统量子力学中的希尔伯特空间或解析内积假设。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 建立量子稳定子理论的代数基础，将量子相位、非对易性和稳定子码统一为Frobenius对偶的必然代数结果，避免依赖传统量子力学的解析结构假设。

Method: 在有限交换Frobenius环上工作，从可容许相位数据中提取非退化相位配对、Weyl算子代数和量子稳定子码，将量子态空间视为忠实相位作用的最小载体。

Result: 稳定子码被规范地识别为Frobenius相位配对下的自正交子模，CSS型构造仅作为特殊分裂情况出现，一般Frobenius环允许固有的非CSS稳定子，环的幂零和挠结构产生代数保护的量子层。

Conclusion: 量子化表现为代数相位诱导而非解析完备化，量子结构在代数相位关系层面具有信息完备性，有限Frobenius环是使可容许相位数据成为强可容许的精确基环，在此框架下完整量子形式体系由Frobenius对偶强制产生。

Abstract: We develop the quantum component of Algebraic Phase Theory by showing that quantum phase, Weyl noncommutativity, and stabiliser codes arise as unavoidable algebraic consequences of Frobenius duality. Working over finite commutative Frobenius rings, we extract nondegenerate phase pairings, Weyl operator algebras, and quantum stabiliser codes directly from admissible phase data, without assuming Hilbert spaces, analytic inner products, or an externally imposed symplectic structure.
  Within this framework, quantum state spaces appear as minimal carriers of faithful phase action, and stabiliser codes are identified canonically with self-orthogonal submodules under the Frobenius phase pairing. CSS-type constructions arise only as a special splitting case, while general Frobenius rings admit intrinsically non-CSS stabilisers. Nilpotent and torsion structure in the base ring give rise to algebraically protected quantum layers that are invisible to admissible Weyl-type errors.
  These results place quantum stabiliser theory within Algebraic Phase Theory: quantisation emerges as algebraic phase induction rather than analytic completion, and quantum structure is information-complete at the level of algebraic phase relations alone. Throughout, we work over finite Frobenius rings, which are precisely the base rings for which admissible phase data become strongly admissible, and in this regime the full quantum formalism is forced by Frobenius duality.

### [2] [Automorphism group schemes of lattice vertex algebras](https://arxiv.org/abs/2502.06121)
*Scott Carnahan,Hayate Kobayashi*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 该论文研究了正定偶格点上的顶点代数的自同构群，将其描述为仿射群概形，并揭示了其作为ADE型可约群与格点外自同构群的扩张结构。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究正定偶格点上的顶点代数的自同构群结构，特别是这些自同构群如何作为仿射群概形来描述，以及它们与格点本身结构的关系。

Method: 通过标准构造方法，从正定偶格点和交换环构建格点顶点代数，并利用非负整数自然分级。然后分析这些分级顶点代数的自同构群，将其描述为仿射群概形。

Result: 证明了每个这样的自同构群都是ADE型分裂可约群与格点外自同构群的扩张，并给出了这些群的具体描述。

Conclusion: 正定偶格点上的顶点代数的自同构群具有清晰的代数结构，可以表示为ADE型可约群与格点外自同构群的扩张，这为理解这类顶点代数的对称性提供了完整的描述。

Abstract: Given a positive definite even lattice and a commutative ring, there is a standard construction of a lattice vertex algebra over the commutative ring, and it admits a natural grading by non-negative integers. We describe the groups of automorphisms of these graded vertex algebras as affine group schemes, showing in particular that each is an extension of an explicitly described split reductive group of ADE type by the outer automorphism group of the lattice.

### [3] [Refined half-integer condition on RG flows](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.12085)
*Ken Kikuchi*

Main category: hep-th

TL;DR: 该论文探讨了重整化群流动中对称性范畴的额外结构（如辫子结构）如何提供比传统't Hooft反常更强的约束条件，特别是提出了半整数条件及其细化条件。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统上，重整化群流动主要受对称性的't Hooft反常约束。然而，对称性范畴有时具有辫子等额外结构，这些结构可能提供更强的约束条件，从而更精确地限制可能的流动。

Method: 通过研究辫子对称性范畴中对称对象的共形维度，分析其在紫外和红外端的演化。在重整化群缺陷存在的情况下，推导出紫外和红外共形维度之和为半整数的条件，并进一步寻找该和成为半整数的必要条件。

Result: 发现了半整数条件的细化条件，即对称对象在重整化群流动中保持存在时，其紫外和红外共形维度之和为半整数的必要条件。应用这一细化条件解决了一些具体的重整化群流动问题。

Conclusion: 对称性范畴的辫子结构提供了比传统反常更强的重整化群流动约束。半整数条件及其细化条件为理解量子场论的重整化群流动提供了新的理论工具，并成功应用于具体流动的分析。

Abstract: Renormalization group flows are constrained by symmetries. Traditionally, we have made the most of 't Hooft anomalies associated to the symmetries. The anomaly is mathematically part of the data for the monoidal structure on symmetry categories. The symmetry categories sometimes admit additional structures such as braiding. It was found that the additional structures give further constraints on renormalization group flows. One of these constraints is the half-integer condition. The condition claims the following. Braidings are characterized by conformal dimensions. A symmetry object $c$ in a braided symmetry category surviving all along the flow thus has two conformal dimensions, one in ultraviolet $h_c^\text{UV}$ and the other in infrared $h_c^\text{IR}$. In a renormalization group flow with a renormalization group defect, they add up to a half-integer $h_c^\text{UV}+h_c^\text{IR}\in\frac12\mathbb Z$. We find a necessary condition for the sum to be half-integer. We solve some flows with the refined half-integer condition.

### [4] [Relative and lax volutive categories](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.15667)
*Tim Lüders*

Main category: math.CT

TL;DR: 本文引入相对volutive范畴概念，特化为lax volutive范畴，研究其在闭对称幺半范畴中的应用，并提供具体实例分析。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究相对volutive范畴的主要动机是：任何刚性对称幺半范畴都允许volutive结构，而任何闭对称幺半范畴都允许lax volutive结构。这为在更一般的范畴框架中研究volutive结构提供了理论基础。

Method: 引入相对volutive（高阶）范畴概念，发展相对volutive范畴的基本理论，提供lax volutive范畴的多种等价表述，并研究具体实例。

Result: 建立了相对volutive范畴的理论框架，给出了lax volutive范畴的等价表述，分析了包括完全有界向量空间范畴、星环上的模、希尔伯特空间之间的无界算子以及Morita 2-范畴在内的多个重要实例。

Conclusion: 相对volutive范畴为研究闭对称幺半范畴中的volutive结构提供了合适的框架，lax volutive结构在多种数学结构中自然出现，具有广泛的应用前景。

Abstract: In this paper we introduce the notion of a relative volutive (higher) category, specializing to the notion of a lax volutive (higher) category. Our primary motivation to study these objects is the following: while any rigid symmetric monoidal category admits a volutive structure, any closed symmetric monoidal category admits a lax volutive structure. We develop some of the basic theory of relative volutive categories and provide several equivalent formulations of lax volutive categories. We then study examples of interest, including categories of complete bornological vector spaces and modules over star-rings. We will also separately discuss unbounded operators between Hilbert spaces and Morita 2-categories, the latter of which in the context of fully closed symmetric monoidal 2-categories.

### [5] [Symmetry shifting for monoidal bicategories](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.15358)
*Raffael Stenzel*

Main category: math.CT

TL;DR: 该论文证明了在幺半双范畴上的辫子结构会诱导其幺半子范畴上的幺半结构，并将Joyal和Street的经典定理从幺半范畴推广到幺半双范畴。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将Joyal和Street关于幺半范畴的经典定理推广到幺半双范畴，研究辫子结构在不同范畴层次上的传递性。

Method: 应用∞-运算Additivity定理，避免了复杂的计算，通过范畴论和同伦论的方法进行证明。

Result: 证明了幺半双范畴上的辫子结构会诱导其幺半子范畴上的幺半结构，且如果原结构是sylleptic或对称的，则诱导的结构分别是辫子或对称的。

Conclusion: 成功将Joyal和Street的经典定理从幺半范畴推广到幺半双范畴，为高阶范畴理论提供了新的结果，证明方法简洁高效。

Abstract: We show that every braiding on a monoidal bicategory induces a monoidal structure on its bicategory of monoids, such that if the former is sylleptic or symmetric then the latter is braided or symmetric, respectively. This extends a classic theorem of Joyal and Street for monoidal categories. The proof presented in this paper is an application of the $\infty$-operadic Additivity Theorem and thereby averts any considerable calculations.

### [6] [Beyond Wigner: Non-Invertible Symmetries Preserve Probabilities](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.07110)
*Thomas Bartsch,Yuhan Gai,Sakura Schafer-Nameki*

Main category: quant-ph

TL;DR: 该论文解决了广义对称性与维格纳定理的矛盾，提出非可逆对称性通过量子通道而非酉算子实现，并依赖于幺正对称范畴。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统量子理论中的对称性通常是可逆的群对称性，但近年来发展的广义对称性（范畴对称性）包含非可逆对称性，这与维格纳定理要求量子对称性必须由（反）酉算子实现以保持概率相矛盾。论文旨在解决这一理论冲突。

Method: 提出对称性缺陷不是通过固定希尔伯特空间上的酉算子作用，而是作为不同希尔伯特空间（由扭曲扇区构造）之间的等距映射。这使得非可逆对称性自然地表现为保迹量子通道。该方法依赖于对称性范畴是幺正的。

Result: 成功解决了广义对称性与维格纳定理的矛盾，为非可逆对称性提供了合理的量子实现方式。通过Tambara-Yamagami、Fibonacci、Yang-Lee以及高阶范畴对称性等多个例子验证了该方案。

Conclusion: 非可逆对称性可以通过量子通道而非酉算子实现，这扩展了量子理论中对称性的概念，同时保持了概率守恒。幺正对称范畴是实现这一方案的关键条件。

Abstract: In recent years, the traditional notion of symmetry in quantum theory was expanded to so-called generalised or categorical symmetries, which, unlike ordinary group symmetries, may be non-invertible. This appears to be at odds with Wigner's theorem, which requires quantum symmetries to be implemented by (anti)unitary -- and hence invertible -- operators in order to preserve probabilities. We resolve this puzzle for (higher) fusion category symmetries $\mathcal{C}$ by proposing that, instead of acting by unitary operators on a fixed Hilbert space, symmetry defects in $\mathcal{C}$ act as isometries between distinct Hilbert spaces constructed from twisted sectors. As a result, we find that non-invertible symmetries naturally act as trace-preserving quantum channels. Crucially, our construction relies on the symmetry category $\mathcal{C}$ being unitary. We illustrate our proposal through several examples that include Tambara-Yamagami, Fibonacci, and Yang-Lee as well as higher categorical symmetries.

</details>
