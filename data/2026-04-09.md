### [1] [Fermionic extensions of $W$-algebras via 3d $\mathcal{N}=4$ gauge theories with a boundary](https://arxiv.org/abs/2304.03270)
*Yutaka Yoshida*

Main category: hep-th

TL;DR: 该论文研究3d H-扭曲N=4超对称规范理论边界关联的顶点算子代数性质，发现阿贝尔规范理论的VOAs是环面超凯勒簇VOAs的费米子扩展，并具体计算了N=3时的OPE，发现新的费米子扩展Bershadsky-Polyakov代数。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究3d N=4超对称规范理论边界关联的顶点算子代数性质，探索阿贝尔规范理论VOAs与环面超凯勒簇VOAs的关系，并通过3d镜像对称预测真空特征标表达式。

Method: 使用BRST上同调方法定义顶点算子代数，结合流、辛玻色子、复费米子和bc-鬼场构建VOAs。通过具体计算N=3情况下的算子乘积展开(OPE)，并与已知的W-代数结构进行比较。

Result: 发现3d N=4阿贝尔规范理论的VOAs是环面超凯勒簇VOAs的费米子扩展；具体得出3d镜像对称下N-味U(1) SQED对应的VOA是W^{-N+1}(sl_N, f_sub)的费米子扩展；N=3时计算得到新的费米子扩展Bershadsky-Polyakov代数W^{-2}(sl_3, f_sub)。

Conclusion: 3d N=4阿贝尔规范理论边界关联的顶点算子代数具有丰富的代数结构，与环面超凯勒簇VOAs存在密切关系，通过3d镜像对称可以预测这些费米子扩展W-代数的真空特征标表达式，为理解规范理论与代数结构的关系提供了新视角。

Abstract: We study properties of vertex (operator) algebras associated with 3d H-twisted $\mathcal{N}=4$ supersymmetric gauge theories with a boundary. The vertex operator algebras (VOAs) are defined by BRST cohomologies of currents with symplectic bosons, complex fermions, and bc-ghosts. We point out that VOAs for 3d $\mathcal{N}=4$ abelian gauge theories are fermionic extensions of VOAs associated with toric hyper-Kähler varieties. From this relation, it follows that the VOA associated with the 3d mirror of $N$-flavor $U(1)$ SQED is a fermionic extension of a $W$-algebra $W^{-N+1}(\mathfrak{sl}_N, f_{\text{sub}})$. For $N=3$, we explicitly compute the OPE of elements in the BRST cohomology and find a new algebra that is a fermionic extension of a Bershadsky-Polyakov algebra $W^{-2}(\mathfrak{sl}_3, f_{\text{sub}})$. We also suggest an expression for the vacuum character of the fermionic extension of $W^{-N+1}(\mathfrak{sl}_N, f_{\text{sub}})$ predicted by 3d $\mathcal{N}=4$ mirror symmetry.

Motivation: 需要一个新的域不变量来细化实谱，并与域的绝对伽罗瓦群建立联系，以更好地理解域的结构性质。

Conclusion: Artin-Schreier quandle是一个有效的域不变量，能够细化实谱并与伽罗瓦群相关，但在一般情况下存在关系约束。

For formally real number fields, it is freely generated in its variety by a Cantor space of indeterminates. For Laurent series fields, we compute it in terms of the Artin-Schreier quandle of the coefficient field. This result and other examples show that, in general, there are relations.

### [4] [Differential graded categories in holomorphic symplectic geometry](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.06630)
*Borislav Mladenov*

Main category: math.AG

TL;DR: 该论文研究了全纯辛流形上的规范拉格朗日D膜微分分次范畴及其形变量子化，证明了在可数定向紧致Kähler拉格朗日子流形具有成对干净交集的局部化条件下这些dg范畴的形式性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究全纯辛流形上规范拉格朗日D膜范畴及其形变量子化的代数结构，探索这些微分分次范畴的形式性条件，为辛几何与代数几何的交叉领域提供理论工具。

Method: 定义了Kaledin类作为最小A∞-范畴的形式性障碍，建立了平坦弱适当Calabi-Yau dg范畴的形式性判据，通过局部化技术在可数定向紧致Kähler拉格朗日子流形具有成对干净交集的条件下证明形式性。

Result: 证明了规范拉格朗日D膜范畴、形变量子化范畴和虚拟de Rham范畴在特定局部化条件下的形式性，建立了Kaledin类作为A∞-范畴形式性障碍的数学理论。

Conclusion: 全纯辛流形上的规范拉格朗日D膜范畴及其相关范畴在适当条件下具有形式性，Kaledin类提供了判断A∞-范畴形式性的有效工具，为辛几何中的量子化问题提供了新的代数框架。

Abstract: Let $(\mathrm{X},σ)$ be a holomorphic symplectic manifold. We study the differential graded category of canonical Lagrangian $\mathrm{D}$-branes $\mathcal{D}_\mathrm{Lag}(\mathrm{X},σ)$ along with its deformation quantisation, spanned by quantised orientations, $\mathcal{DQ}(\mathrm{X},σ)$, and the virtual de Rham category $\mathcal{DR}^{\mathrm{vir}}(\mathrm{X},σ)$. We prove the formality of these dg categories when localised at a countable collection of orientable compact Kähler Lagrangian submanifolds with pairwise clean intersections. Along the way, we define Kaledin classes of minimal $\mathrm{A}_\infty$-categories and show that they are the obstructions to formality. In addition, we obtain a formality criterion for flat weakly proper Calabi-Yau dg categories.

</details>
