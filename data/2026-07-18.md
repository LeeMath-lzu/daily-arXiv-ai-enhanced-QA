### [1] [On the triviality and non-triviality of the automorphism group of a skew brace](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.17276)
*Cindy Tsang*

Main category: math.GR

TL;DR: 本文研究了斜支架的自同构群，证明了对于某些斜支架族，平凡自同构群仅出现在阶为1或2的情况，并构造了任意奇素数p下阶为2p³且具有平凡自同构群的斜支架。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 受群论中平凡自同构群仅出现在阶为1或2这一简单事实启发，研究者希望探究类似性质是否在斜支架（skew braces）的代数结构中成立，从而深化对斜支架自同构群的理解。

Method: 采用群论与斜支架理论相结合的方法，首先证明某些斜支架族中平凡自同构群的条件与群论情形一致；然后通过具体构造，对任意奇素数p给出阶为2p³的斜支架实例。

Result: 证明了特定斜支架族中，平凡自同构群仅当阶为1或2时出现；同时成功构造了任意奇素数p下阶为2p³且具有平凡自同构群的斜支架。

Conclusion: 群论中关于平凡自同构群的经典结论在部分斜支架族中依然成立，但存在更大阶数的斜支架（如2p³阶）亦可拥有平凡自同构群，表明斜支架的自同构结构比群更为丰富。

Abstract: It is a simple fact that a group has a trivial automorphism group if and only if it is of order $1$ or $2$. We prove that the same holds for certain families of skew braces, and given any odd prime $p$, we construct a skew brace of order $2p^3$ that has a trivial automorphism group.

*Fabio Calderón,César Galindo*

Main category: math.RA

TL;DR: 本文对强G-分次拟Hopf代数的拟Hopf理想进行了分类，建立了理想与正规子群和不变理想之间的对应关系。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将分次Hopf代数的理想分类理论推广到拟Hopf代数情形，处理更广泛的群分次结构和非平凡结合子。

Method: 通过证明每个拟Hopf理想I对应于一个对(N,K)，其中N是G的正规子群，K是H_N的G/N-不变拟Hopf理想，且其像包含k[N]的增广理想。

Result: 建立了拟Hopf理想与正规子群-不变理想对的一一对应，并针对具有拟Hopf收缩或叉积提升的特殊类改进了该对应。

Conclusion: 该分类可用于处理无限分次、有限中性分量和非平凡再结合子的余中心阿贝尔扩张等应用。

Abstract: Let $H$ be a strongly $G$-graded quasi-Hopf algebra, with $G$ an arbitrary group. We classify its quasi-Hopf ideals by showing that each quasi-Hopf ideal $I$ of $H$ corresponds to a pair $(N,K)$, where $N$ is a normal subgroup of $G$ and $K$ is a $G/N$-invariant quasi-Hopf ideal of $H_N=\bigoplus_{n\in N}H_n$ whose image under the grading map contains the augmentation ideal of $\Bbbk[N]$. We further improve this correspondence for particular classes of quasi-Hopf ideals, such as those that have an associated quasi-Hopf retraction, or those whose lifted quotient is a crossed product. Applications include cocentral abelian extensions with infinite grading, finite neutral component and nontrivial reassociator.

### [3] [Bockstein braiding statistics](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.02280)
*Po-Shen Hsin,Yu-An Chen*

Main category: quant-ph

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种称为Bockstein编织统计的通用构造，用于描述相邻维度d=p+q+1中p维和q维激发之间的相互统计，并通过4N步微观幺正过程测量Berry相位，建立了该不变量与Bockstein同态之间的联系，可用于诊断混合反常并约束对称性破缺行为。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统的相互编织现象（如Aharonov-Bohm效应、分数量子霍尔系统中的任意子统计）通常发生在空间维度d=p+q+2中。作者希望将这一概念推广到相邻维度d=p+q+1，建立适用于任意N和任意激发维度的通用相互统计构造。

Method: 作者引入了一种基于晶格上局域激发算符的4N步微观幺正过程，通过测量积累的Berry相位来定义编织不变量。该过程测量一个激发与另一个激发的N重融合结的链接关系。同时，将激发算符解释为开放对称性算符，使该不变量成为混合反常的直接微观诊断工具。

Result: 建立了不变量的量子化和双线性性质，证明了其场论响应由Bockstein同态控制。在(1+1)D自旋链中展示了非平凡的Bockstein编织相位证明了自旋翻转对称性与最近邻受控-Z对称性之间的混合反常。构建了(2+1)D和(3+1)D晶格类比，得到了新的反常对称对，并将框架应用于强耦合(3+1)D连续规范理论。

Conclusion: 非平凡的Bockstein编织统计排除了完全对称的有能隙相，阻碍了两个激发的同时凝聚，并意味着高形式对称性的分数量子化。该工作为理解高维编织统计、混合反常诊断以及对称性约束下的物质相提供了统一的框架。

Abstract: Braiding phenomena, from the charge-flux Aharonov-Bohm effect to anyonic statistics in fractional quantum Hall systems, are paradigmatic manifestations of topology in quantum physics. Ordinary mutual braiding between $p$- and $q$-dimensional excitations occurs in $d=p+q+2$ spatial dimensions. In this work, we introduce a universal construction of mutual statistics in the adjacent dimension $d=p+q+1$, applicable to excitations obeying $\mathbb Z_N$ fusion for arbitrary $N$ and all excitation dimensions $p$ and $q$. The corresponding invariant is the Berry phase accumulated in a simple $4N$-step microscopic unitary process built from local excitation operators on lattices. This process measures the linking of one excitation with the $N$-fold fusion junction of the other, encompassing particle-particle statistics in one dimension, particle-loop statistics in two dimensions, and loop-loop or particle-membrane statistics in three dimensions. We establish the quantization and bilinearity of the invariant and show that its field-theory response is governed by the Bockstein homomorphism, motivating the name Bockstein braiding statistics. Interpreting the excitation operators as open symmetry operators turns the same invariant into a direct microscopic diagnostic of mixed anomalies between symmetries. We demonstrate this diagnostic in a (1+1)D spin chain, where the nontrivial Bockstein braiding phase proves the mixed anomaly between the spin-flip symmetry $\prod X$ and the nearest-neighbor controlled-$Z$ symmetry $\prod CZ$. We construct explicit (2+1)D and (3+1)D lattice analogs, yielding new anomalous symmetry pairs, and apply the framework to strongly coupled (3+1)D continuum gauge theories. Nontrivial Bockstein braiding rules out a fully symmetric gapped phase, obstructs simultaneous condensation of the two excitations, and implies fractionalization of higher-form symmetries.

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Result: 建立了12个PBW基（在交替生成元中）；证明了基在任意标量中心特化下保持；确定了两个中心化子；证明了四个单族交替多项式子代数是极大交换的。

Abstract: We prove that, over an arbitrary field and whenever $q$ is not a root of unity, the Baseilhac--Kolb root vectors form a PBW basis of the $q$-Onsager algebra for every total order on the positive roots of $\widehat{\mathfrak{sl}}_2$. This removes the previous transcendence hypothesis. We determine the centraliser of the negative alternating subalgebra and that of the first imaginary alternating generator, and deduce that the four single-family alternating polynomial subalgebras are maximal commutative. Together, these results settle four conjectures of Terwilliger and, in characteristic different from $2$, a conjecture of Baseilhac and Belliard.
