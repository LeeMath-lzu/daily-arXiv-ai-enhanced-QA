<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Method: 将q-Pochhammer符号$(z;q)_\infty$表示为伽马函数的无穷乘积，类似于Narukawa处理椭圆伽马函数的方法。然后利用这种表示推导出当$q$趋于1时的渐近展开公式。

Conclusion: 该工作建立了q-Pochhammer符号的新表示形式，扩展了Narukawa的方法，为研究q-级数和特殊函数的渐近行为提供了有效途径，特别是在q趋于1的极限情况下。

Abstract: We express the $q$-Pochhammer symbol $(z;q)_\infty$ as an infinite product of gamma functions, analogously to how Narukawa expressed the elliptic gamma function as an infinite product of hyperbolic gamma functions. ### [4] [The Dehn Twist Action for Quantum Representations of Mapping Class Groups](https://arxiv.org/abs/2311.16020)

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究非半单模范畴中Dehn扭曲在共形块空间上的作用，推广半单情形下的已知结果，特别是将De Renzi等人关于小量子群的结果推广到更一般的模范畴。

Method: 计算Dehn扭曲在共形块空间上的作用，分析非分离简单闭曲线和分离简单闭曲线两种情况，将Dehn扭曲的阶与ribbon扭曲的阶联系起来，并考虑正则end的monoidal幂上的ribbon扭曲。

Result: 对于非分离简单闭曲线，Dehn扭曲的阶等于ribbon扭曲的阶；对于分离曲线，用正则end的monoidal幂上ribbon扭曲的阶表示Dehn扭曲的阶。证明了Johnson核平凡作用的充要条件是正则end的ribbon扭曲与自身双辫子平凡。

Conclusion: 建立了非半单模范畴中Dehn扭曲阶与ribbon扭曲阶的精确关系，为理解映射类群在共形块空间上的表示提供了新视角，并将结果应用于Johnson核和Torelli群的可见性问题。

Abstract: We calculate the Dehn twist action on the spaces of conformal blocks of a not necessarily semisimple modular category. In the separating case, we express the order using the order of the ribbon twist on monoidal powers of the canonical end. As an application, we prove that the Johnson kernels of the mapping class groups act trivially if and only if for the canonical end the ribbon twist and double braiding with itself are trivial. We give a similar result for the visibility of the Torelli groups.

### [5] [Refined half-integer condition on RG flows](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.12085)
*Ken Kikuchi*

Main category: hep-th

TL;DR: 该论文研究了重整化群流动中的对称性约束，特别是辫子对称范畴中的半整数条件及其对RG流动的限制。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统上，重整化群流动主要受对称性的't Hooft反常约束。然而，对称范畴可能具有辫子等额外结构，这些结构可能对RG流动施加进一步限制。论文旨在探索这些额外结构带来的约束条件。

Method: 通过分析辫子对称范畴的数学结构，研究对称对象在紫外和红外下的共形维度关系。特别关注在存在重整化群缺陷的情况下，紫外和红外共形维度之和的半整数条件。

Result: 发现了一个必要条件，使得对称对象的紫外和红外共形维度之和为半整数：h_c^UV + h_c^IR ∈ ½ℤ。利用这个精化的半整数条件，成功解析了一些重整化群流动。

Conclusion: 辫子对称范畴的额外结构确实对重整化群流动施加了比传统反常更强的约束。半整数条件为RG流动提供了新的限制条件，有助于更好地理解和分类量子场论的重整化群流动。

Abstract: Renormalization group flows are constrained by symmetries. Traditionally, we have made the most of 't Hooft anomalies associated to the symmetries. The anomaly is mathematically part of the data for the monoidal structure on symmetry categories. The symmetry categories sometimes admit additional structures such as braiding. It was found that the additional structures give further constraints on renormalization group flows. One of these constraints is the half-integer condition. The condition claims the following. Braidings are characterized by conformal dimensions. A symmetry object $c$ in a braided symmetry category surviving all along the flow thus has two conformal dimensions, one in ultraviolet $h_c^\text{UV}$ and the other in infrared $h_c^\text{IR}$. In a renormalization group flow with a renormalization group defect, they add up to a half-integer $h_c^\text{UV}+h_c^\text{IR}\in\frac12\mathbb Z$. We find a necessary condition for the sum to be half-integer. We solve some flows with the refined half-integer condition.

</details>

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究Temperley-Lieb代数在特殊参数值β=0时的表示理论，特别是当n为偶数时的情况。这有助于理解该代数的模范畴结构，并与Jones多项式的数值性质建立联系。

Method: 使用箭图代数方法显式描述TL_n(0)的模范畴，构造标准模的精确序列，并将结果与Jones多项式在t=-1处的数值巧合进行范畴化。
