### [1] [On Polyhedral Formulas for Kirillov-Reshetikhin Modules](https://arxiv.org/abs/1901.00104)
*Chul-hee Lee*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种验证Kirillov-Reshetikhin（KR）模多面体分支公式的方法，通过将公式验证转化为单变量有理函数的留数计算，并在F4和G2型上给出了计算机辅助的新证明。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 对于例外型李代数，KR模的多面体分支公式在许多情况下仍停留在猜想阶段，缺乏严格的证明方法。

Method: 利用KR模字符满足的线性递推关系，将多面体公式的验证转化为两个单变量有理函数之间的恒等式证明，这些有理函数仅在已知位置有简单极点，因此只需比较这些极点处的留数即可。

Result: 在F4和G2型上，通过计算机辅助计算，在统一框架下获得了已知多面体公式的新证明，这些证明易于验证。

Conclusion: 该方法为验证KR模多面体分支公式提供了一种系统且可计算的新途径，有望推广到其他例外型情形。

Abstract: We propose a method to prove a polyhedral branching formula for Kirillov-Reshetikhin (KR) modules over an untwisted quantum affine algebra. When the underlying simple Lie algebra is of exceptional type, such a formula remains conjectural in many cases. Using a linear recurrence relation satisfied by the characters of KR modules, we convert the verification of a polyhedral formula into an identity between two rational functions of a single variable with only simple poles at known locations. It is then sufficient to compare the residues at those poles, which are explicitly computable quantities. By applying this strategy, we obtain new, computer-assisted and easily verifiable proofs of known polyhedral formulas in types $F_4$ and $G_2$ within a uniform framework.

We construct their irreducible modules, which are isomorphic to twisted modules of the corresponding chiral algebras, and use them to organize local and defect Hilbert spaces. ### [3] [Bockstein braiding statistics](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.02280)
*Po-Shen Hsin,Yu-An Chen*

Main category: quant-ph

TL;DR: 本文发现了一种新的互统计类型——Bockstein编织统计，它存在于比普通编织低一个空间维度的系统中，涵盖1维的粒子-粒子统计、2维的粒子-环统计以及3维的环-环或粒子-膜统计，其场论响应由Bockstein同态控制。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 编织统计是量子拓扑的核心现象，已知存在于任意子系统中。作者发现普通编织统计需要d=p+q+2的空间维度，但可能存在一种在更低维度(d=p+q+1)下的互统计类型，这激发了对这种新统计的探索。

Method: 通过场论响应（Bockstein同态）定义该统计不变量；在格点模型中构建局域激发算符的微观幺正过程，测量Berry相位；利用混合反常理论分析其对称性含义。

Result: 识别出Bockstein编织统计作为新的互统计类型，证明了它是相应对称性混合反常的统计体现，阻碍了完全对称有能隙相的存在、两种激发的同时凝聚以及高阶形式对称性的破缺。

Conclusion: Bockstein编织统计为量子物质中的拓扑相提供了新的分类工具，在(1+1)维自旋链和(3+1)维规范理论中均具有物理可实现性。

Abstract: Braiding statistics, familiar from anyons in fractional quantum Hall systems, are a central manifestation of topology in quantum physics. Ordinary braiding extends naturally to higher-dimensional excitations: a $p$-dimensional excitation and a $q$-dimensional excitation can braid in $d=p+q+2$ spatial dimensions. In this work, we identify a new type of mutual statistics that exists in one lower spatial dimension, $d=p+q+1$. This includes particle-particle statistics in one dimension, particle-loop statistics in two dimensions, and loop-loop or particle-membrane statistics in three dimensions. The corresponding field-theory response is governed by the Bockstein homomorphism, so we call the invariant Bockstein braiding statistics. On lattices, the Bockstein statistics is measured by the Berry phase accumulated in a universal microscopic unitary process built from local excitation operators. We further show that nontrivial Bockstein braiding is the statistical manifestation of a mixed anomaly of the corresponding symmetries. This anomaly rules out a fully symmetric gapped phase, obstructs simultaneous condensation of the two excitations, and implies fractionalization of higher-form symmetries. We illustrate these consequences in a $(1{+}1)$-dimensional spin-$\frac12$ chain, where Bockstein braiding statistics detects the mixed anomaly between $\prod_i X_i$ and $\prod_i \mathrm{CZ}_{i,i+1}$, and in strongly coupled $(3{+}1)$-dimensional continuum gauge theories.
