### [1] [Set-theoretic solutions of the Yang-Baxter equation from inverse braces](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.16045)
*Francesco Catino,Marzia Mazzotta,Susanna Tieni*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种称为"逆辫"（inverse brace）的代数结构，它同时具有两个逆半群结构并满足特定相容性条件，研究了其基本性质、与Yang-Baxter方程解的联系，以及通过匹配积和强半格构造新例子的方法。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 为推广弱辫（weak brace）的概念，建立更一般的代数框架，并探索其与Yang-Baxter方程集合论解之间的联系。

Method: 首先定义逆辫的代数结构（两个逆半群满足相容恒等式），然后研究其基本性质，特别分析加性幂等元与乘性幂等元的关系，刻画逆辫成为弱辫的条件。接着构建一类能产生Yang-Baxter方程解的逆辫，最后引入匹配积和强半格两种构造方法。

Result: 证明每个弱辫都是逆辫；刻画了逆辫成为弱辫的条件；提供了一类能产生Yang-Baxter方程解的逆辫并给出多个例子；证明了匹配积和强半格构造能保持产生解的条件。

Conclusion: 逆辫是弱辫的自然推广，为构造Yang-Baxter方程的新解提供了系统性方法，匹配积和强半格是有效的构造工具。

Abstract: We introduce the algebraic structure of an inverse brace, namely, a triple $(S,+,\circ)$ such that both $(S,+)$ and $(S,\circ)$ are inverse semigroups and the following identity holds $a\circ(b+c)=a\circ b-a+a\circ c$, for all $ a, b, c \in S$, where $-a$ denotes the inverse of $a \in S$, with respect to $+$. In particular, every weak brace is an inverse brace. We investigate the fundamental properties of inverse braces and analyze the relationship between additive and multiplicative idempotents, characterizing the condition under which an inverse brace is a weak brace. Our main results concern the connection with set-theoretic solutions to the Yang-Baxter equation. Specifically, we provide a class of inverse braces that yield solutions and give several examples. Finally, we introduce constructions of inverse braces via the matched product and the strong semilattice of inverse braces. We show that these constructions preserve the conditions required to produce solutions, thereby providing a systematic method for generating new examples.

### [2] [Continuous Hochschild Cohomology and Formality](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.21090)
*Patrick Antweiler*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 本文基于Positselski的contraderived范畴，建立了完备局部凸（弯曲）dg-代数的形变理论的同调框架，定义了相应的Hochschild复形，并证明了光滑函数Fréchet代数、de Rham代数以及复流形Dolbeault代数的形式性定理。此外，计算了各类矩阵分解的连续Hochschild上同调。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 为完备局部凸（弯曲）dg-代数建立合适的同调形变理论框架，填补现有理论在非交换几何与复几何应用中的空白。

Method: 基于Positselski的contraderived范畴理论，定义相应的Hochschild复形来控制形变，并采用形式性定理的证明方法。

Result: 证明了光滑函数Fréchet代数、de Rham代数及复流形Dolbeault代数的形式性定理；揭示了Dolbeault代数的Hochschild上同调与Kontsevich扩展形变复形、光滑射影簇导出范畴的Hochschild上同调以及Gualtieri广义复流形形变复形的等价关系；计算了多种矩阵分解的连续Hochschild上同调。

Conclusion: 该同调框架有效统一并推广了多种几何场景下的形变理论，为后续研究提供了新的工具。

Abstract: We define the appropriate homological setting to study deformation theory of complete locally convex (curved) dg-algebras based on Positselski's contraderived categories. We define the corresponding Hochschild complex controlling deformations and prove formality theorems for the Fréchet algebras of smooth functions on a manifold, the de Rham algebra and for the Dolbeault algebra of a complex manifold. In the latter case, the Hochschild cohomology is equivalent to Kontsevich's extended deformation complex, the Hochschild cohomology of the derived category in case $X$ is a smooth projective variety and to Gualtieri's deformation complex of $X$ viewed as generalized complex manifold. We also compute the continuous Hochschild cohomology for various categories of matrix factorisations.

### [3] [From affine algebraic racks to Leibniz algebras and Yang-Baxter operators](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.15909)
*Luc Ta*

Main category: math.AG

TL;DR: 本文引入代数簇上的"代数架"概念（即带基点的架对象），并构造了从仿射代数架到左/右Leibniz代数的函子，解决了Loday提出的问题。这些函子与闭子架和理想兼容，能恢复线性代数群的李代数（通过共轭quandle）和代数李架的Leibniz代数。最后，利用架概形在各种范畴中函子性地构造了（非）退化Yang-Baxter算子。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 解决Loday提出的问题，建立代数架与Leibniz代数之间的函子对应关系，并推广李代数与Yang-Baxter算子的构造。

Method: 在概形范畴中定义带基点的架对象（代数架），通过仿射代数架构造左/右Leibniz代数函子，并研究其与闭子架、理想的兼容性。利用架概形函子性地构造Yang-Baxter算子。

Result: 成功构造了从仿射代数架到Leibniz代数的函子，该函子能恢复线性代数群的李代数（通过共轭quandle）和代数李架的Leibniz代数；并利用架概形构造了（非）退化Yang-Baxter算子。

Conclusion: 代数架为Leibniz代数提供了统一的几何源头，架概形为Yang-Baxter算子提供了函子性构造框架。

Abstract: We introduce analogues of algebraic groups called algebraic racks, which are pointed rack objects in the category of schemes over a ground field. Addressing a problem of Loday, we construct functors assigning left and right Leibniz algebras to affine algebraic racks. These functors are compatible with closed subracks and ideals, and they recover the Lie algebras of linear algebraic groups (via conjugation quandles) and the Leibniz algebras of algebraic Lie racks. We also study properties of coordinate algebras and Leibniz algebras of affine algebraic racks. Finally, we use rack schemes to functorially construct (co-)nondegenerate Yang-Baxter operators in various categories.

</details>
