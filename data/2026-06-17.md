### [1] [Derivations of rational vertex operator algebras are inner](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.05854)
*Jianzhi Han*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 本文证明：对于CFT类型的单有理顶点算子代数，所有导子都是内导子。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究顶点算子代数的导子结构，特别是探究在何种条件下导子均为内导子。

Method: 利用CFT类型单有理顶点算子代数的代数性质，通过理论推导证明所有导子可由代数中某元素的作用实现。

Result: 证明了CFT类型的单有理顶点算子代数的每个导子都是内导子。

Conclusion: 该结果深化了对顶点算子代数结构理论的理解，为相关代数分类问题提供了重要工具。

Abstract: We show that every derivation of a simple and rational vertex operator algebra of CFT type is an inner derivation.

### [2] [Quivers with quantum Yang-Baxter equation and Hecke condition: Deformation of face algebras](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.16825)
*Cody Gilbert,Ashish K. Srivastava*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 本文提出带有量子Yang-Baxter与Hecke结构的箭图理论，并证明量子矩阵代数$\mathcal{O}_q(M_n)$作为双代数同构于由$GL_q(n)$ Hecke $R$-矩阵的RTT关系形变的花束箭图上的面代数。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 建立箭图与量子Yang-Baxter/Hecke结构的联系，并利用这一框架研究路径代数的代数结构。

Method: 引入携带量子Yang-Baxter和Hecke结构的箭图，研究其环空间上的RTT关系，并通过形变构造面代数。

Result: 证明了量子矩阵代数$\mathcal{O}_q(M_n)$与由Hecke $R$-矩阵RTT关系形变的花束箭图面代数同构（作为双代数）。

Conclusion: 该同构揭示了量子矩阵代数与箭图代数之间的深层联系，为量子群的表示论提供了新的组合视角。

Abstract: In this paper we initiate the study of quivers carrying quantum Yang--Baxter and Hecke structure, and we apply this framework to study path algebras over quivers whose loop spaces carry RTT relations determined by Hecke $R$-matrices. We show that the quantum matrix algebra $\mathcal{O}_q(M_n)$ is isomorphic as a bialgebra to the face algebra over a rose quiver deformed by RTT relations of the $GL_q(n)$ Hecke $R$-matrix.

### [3] [Universal Plücker coordinates for the Wronski map and positivity in real Schubert calculus](https://arxiv.org/abs/2309.04645)
*Steven N. Karp,Kevin Purbhoo*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 本文解决了逆Wronski问题：给定一个Wronskian多项式，找出所有对应的d维多项式子空间V。通过对称群群代数中的交换算子显式给出了Grassmann-Plücker坐标公式，并证明了在实数非负零点情况下这些算子半正定，从而验证了实Schubert演算中的多个著名猜想。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 逆Wronski问题（给定Wronskian求所有对应的子空间V）是一个经典问题，此前仅在某些特殊情形下有解。本文旨在给出该问题的完整显式解，并解决实Schubert演算中一系列长期悬而未决的猜想。

Method: 利用对称群𝔖_n的表示论、对称函数理论和KP可积层次结构的τ函数，在群代数ℂ[𝔖_n]中构造交换算子，这些算子的特征值恰好给出一般解V的Grassmann-Plücker坐标。通过限制到特征空间并将算子替换为特征值，得到ℂ上的具体解。

Result: 给出了逆Wronski问题的一般解的显式Grassmann-Plücker坐标公式。当z_i为实数非负时，证明了这些算子半正定，从而Plücker坐标全为实非负。这验证了Mukhin-Tarasov (2017)、Karp (2021)的 positivity猜想、Eremenko (2015)的disconjugacy猜想以及Sottile (2003)的secant猜想。

Conclusion: 本文完整解决了逆Wronski问题，建立了Wronskian与对称群表示论之间的深刻联系，并通过代数方法一举证明了多个实Schubert演算中的重要猜想，展现了可积系统与代数组合学在几何问题中的强大应用。

Abstract: Given a $d$-dimensional vector space $V \subset \mathbb{C}[u]$ of polynomials, its Wronskian is the polynomial $(u + z_1) \cdots (u + z_n)$ whose zeros $-z_i$ are the points of $\mathbb{C}$ such that $V$ contains a nonzero polynomial with a zero of order at least $d$ at $-z_i$. Equivalently, $V$ is a solution to the Schubert problem defined by osculating planes to the moment curve at $z_1, \dots, z_n$. The inverse Wronski problem involves finding all $V$ with a given Wronskian $(u + z_1) \cdots (u + z_n)$. We solve this problem by providing explicit formulas for the Grassmann-Plücker coordinates of the general solution $V$, as commuting operators in the group algebra $\mathbb{C}[\mathfrak{S}_n]$ of the symmetric group. The Plücker coordinates of individual solutions over $\mathbb{C}$ are obtained by restricting to an eigenspace and replacing each operator by its eigenvalue. This generalizes work of Mukhin, Tarasov, and Varchenko (2013) and of Purbhoo (2022), which give formulas in $\mathbb{C}[\mathfrak{S}_n]$ for the differential equation satisfied by $V$. Moreover, if $z_1, \dots, z_n$ are real and nonnegative, then our operators are positive semidefinite, implying that the Plücker coordinates of $V$ are all real and nonnegative. This verifies several outstanding conjectures in real Schubert calculus, including the positivity conjectures of Mukhin and Tarasov (2017) and of Karp (2021), the disconjugacy conjecture of Eremenko (2015), and the divisor form of the secant conjecture of Sottile (2003). The proofs involve the representation theory of $\mathfrak{S}_n$, symmetric functions, and $τ$-functions of the KP hierarchy.

</details>
