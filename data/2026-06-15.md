<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

### [3] [Nodal degeneration of chiral algebras II: Local structure and chiral Zhu algebras](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.13986)

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Zhu结合代数$A(V)$是研究顶点代数模范畴的重要工具，但缺乏几何理解。本文旨在通过因子化代数的几何框架，给出Zhu代数以及节点上模态转移代数的几何构造与形变。

Method: 将泛函因子化代数$\mathcal{A}$在双点半稳定属零曲线上积分，得到导出结合代数$\mathfrak{Z}_{\mathcal{A}}^0$；通过变体构造得到手性$\mathcal{A}$-双模$\mathfrak{Z}_{\mathcal{A}}$；利用节点的形式平滑化得到形变$\tilde{\mathfrak{Z}}_{\mathcal{A}}$。

Result: $\mathfrak{Z}_{\mathcal{A}}^0$的零阶同调在$\mathcal{A}$来自顶点代数时恢复Zhu代数；$\mathfrak{Z}_{\mathcal{A}}$的零阶同调恢复Damiolini-Gibney-Krashen定义的模态转移代数；$\tilde{\mathfrak{Z}}_{\mathcal{A}}$的零阶同调给出该代数的形变。

Conclusion: 本文建立了顶点代数Zhu代数与模态转移代数的几何统一框架，利用因子化代数与曲线模空间给出了这些代数的自然几何解释与形变理论。

Abstract: Given a vertex algebra $V$, Zhu constructed an associative algebra $A(V)$, whose representation theory provides an approximation to the category of $V$-modules. The construction is given by integrating $\mathcal{A}$ over stable configurations parametrized by two-pointed semistable genus zero curves. By a variant of this construction, we obtained a chiral $\mathcal{A}$-bimodule $\mathfrak{Z}_{\mathcal{A}}$ describing the value of a factorization algebra at a nodal point. We show that its zeroth homology recovers the bimodule underlying the mode-transition algebra $\mathfrak{A}(V)$ defined by Damiolini, Gibney, and Krashen, which they use to describes a vertex algebra at a node. Finally, the value over a formal smoothing of a node provides a deformation $\tilde{\mathfrak{Z}}_{\mathcal{A}}$ of $\mathfrak{Z}_{\mathcal{A}}$, whose zeroth homology describes a deformation of the mode-transition algebra in the case of a vertex algebra.

*Tianqing Zhu*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 本文构造了任意洗牌型量子环群的量子动力学Weyl群元，由此定义了量子格点算子和代数量子差分方程，证明这些算子是交换的并与qKZ算子对易。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 为了将trigonometric Casimir connection的差分类比推广到量子环群情形，并统一和推广已有构造。

Method: 通过构造量子动力学Weyl群元，定义量子格点算子与代数量子差分方程，并证明其交换性及与qKZ算子的对易关系。

Result: 构造了交换的代数量子差分算子族，并证明它们与qKZ算子对易，恢复了OS22的构造。

Conclusion: 该构造可作为有限型对称Cartan矩阵对应的trigonometric Casimir connection的差分类比，建立了量子环群与量子差分方程之间的新联系。

Abstract: We construct quantum dynamical Weyl group elements associated with quantum loop groups of arbitrary shuffle type. Main category: math.RA

TL;DR: 本文证明了泊松Ore扩张和泊松Laurent Ore扩张的Hilbert基定理类比，并推广到迭代情形，最后给出了泊松Noetherian而非Noetherian的例子。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Method: 通过类比经典Hilbert基定理的证明思路，结合泊松代数的结构性质，对泊松Ore扩张和泊松Laurent Ore扩张进行理论推导。

Result: 证明了泊松Ore扩张和泊松Laurent Ore扩张的Hilbert基定理类比，并得到了迭代情形的相应结果，构造了泊松Noetherian而非Noetherian的例子。
