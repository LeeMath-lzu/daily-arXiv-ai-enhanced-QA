### [1] [Free Skew Braces and Free Solutions of the Yang--Baxter Equation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.27056)
*Eric Jespers,Thomas Letourmy,Silvia Properzi,Marco Trombetti,Arne Van Antwerpen*

Main category: math.GR

TL;DR: 本文构建了任意类的自由右幂零斜臂环，证明其自由加法/乘法群、剩余有限性和霍普夫性，并引入右幂零解的概念，证明自由解的字问题可解，且有限解中的律法对所有同类型解成立。还给出了自由双边斜臂环（阿贝尔型）和中心幂零斜臂环（类2）的具体实现。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 为右幂零斜臂环提供可工作的构造，以便研究其代数性质（如自由群结构、剩余有限性、霍普夫性），并将右幂零解引入Yang-Baxter方程解理论中。

Method: 通过显式构造自由右幂零斜臂环，利用组合方法分析其加法群和乘法群结构，并推广至自由双边斜臂环和中心幂零斜臂环。

Result: 证明了自由右幂零斜臂环具有自由加法群和乘法群，是剩余有限且霍普夫的；自由右幂零解的字问题可解；有限解中的律法对所有同类型解成立；给出了自由双边斜臂环（阿贝尔型）和类2中心幂零斜臂环的显式实现。

Conclusion: 该构造为右幂零斜臂环和相应Yang-Baxter解的理论提供了基础工具，揭示了有限解与无限解之间的律法传递性，并为进一步研究其他自由对象铺平了道路。

Abstract: We offer a workable construction of the free right nilpotent skew braces of arbitrary class which allows us to prove (among many other things) that this free object has free additive/multiplicative groups, and that it must also be residually finite and Hopfian. We introduce the class of right nilpotent solutions, which correspond to right nilpotent skew braces. As a consequence of our construction, the free solutions in this class have a solvable Word Problem, and every law holding for finite solutions of the previous type also holds for every solution of the same type.
  In the remainder of the paper, we present further explicit realizations of free objects and explore their consequences. Among these are free two-sided skew braces of abelian type (with an abelian multiplicative group) and free centrally nilpotent skew braces of class 2.

### [2] [Feigin-Semikhatov duality at the critical level](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.02155)
*Thomas Creutzig,Xuanzhong Dai,Bailin Song*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 本文研究了 Feigin-Semikhatov 对偶在临界/大能级极限下的表现，通过将中心less的子正则W-代数描述为将主W-超代数的极限与大能级下的晶格顶点算子代数进行轨道折叠而得到。该构造建立了两个顶点代数之间某些范畴的函子，并在块层面上实现了范畴等价；通过对主W-超代数在大能级极限下的主块进行结构分析，揭示了子正则W-代数在权模范畴中的主块结构（该范畴远大于通常的有下界模范畴）。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Feigin-Semikhatov对偶联系了不同W-代数与W-超代数，但此前对其在临界/大能级极限下的具体行为和范畴结构尚未充分理解，本文旨在揭示该极限下两个顶点代数之间的精确关系及范畴等价。

Method: 通过将中心less的子正则W-代数在临界能级上描述为主W-超代数在大能级极限与晶格顶点算子代数的轨道折叠，构造了范畴间的函子，并分析了主块结构。

Result: 得到了块层面上的范畴等价，揭示了子正则W-代数在权模范畴中主块的结构。

Conclusion: 在临界/大能级极限下，Feigin-Semikhatov对偶可实现为具体的代数构造和范畴等价，且权模范畴（大于通常的有下界模范畴）中的主块结构可由此得到清晰描述。

Abstract: The Feigin-Semikhatov duality asserts that the Heisenberg cosets of the subregular $W$-algebra of $\mathfrak{sl}_n$ at level $k$ and the one of the principal $W$-superalgebra of $\mathfrak{sl}_{n|1}$ at level $\ell$ coincide when the levels satisfy the Feigin-Frenkel relation $(k+n)(\ell+n-1)=1$. A similar duality holds between the subregular $W$-algebra of $\mathfrak{so}_{2n+1}$ and the principal $W$-superalgebra of $\mathfrak{osp}_{2|2n}$.
  We study these dualities in the critical/large level limit.
  We describe the centerless subregular $W$-algebra at the critical level as an orbifold of the large level limit of the principal $W$-superalgebra times a lattice VOA. Our construction yields a functor between certain categories of the two involved vertex algebras. We show that in this set-up one in fact gets block-wise equivalences of categories. Studying the principal block of the large level limit of the principal $W$-superalgebra then gives us the structure of the principal blocks of the subregular $W$-algebras in the category of weight modules (which is much larger than the more common category of lower bounded modules).

</details>

### [3] [Permutation Jones Polynomials](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.01384)
*Sam Nelson*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种基于有限集置换着色的Jones多项式推广，适用于经典纽结和虚拟纽结，并展示了其与经典Jones多项式的区别及新信息。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 推广Jones多项式以获取经典和虚拟纽结的更多区分信息，尤其当有限集基数大于1时，能捕捉到经典Jones多项式无法区分的差异。

Method: 通过有限集X上的置换σ对纽结进行着色，构造新的多项式不变量。

Result: 新多项式在X={1}时退化为经典Jones多项式；当|X|>1时，能区分经典Jones多项式无法区分的虚拟纽结与经典纽结。

Conclusion: 该推广为纽结和链接提供了更强的不变量，并具有进一步研究的潜力。

Abstract: We introduce a generalization of the Jones polynomial for classical and virtual knots and links using colorings by a permutation $σ:X\to X$ of a finite set $X$. For $X=\{1\}$ and for classical knots, the invariant is equivalent to the usual Jones polynomial; for $X$ with cardinality greater than 1 the invariant expresses distinct information from the Jones polynomial or virtual knots and for classical and virtual links. We establish some properties of the new invariants and compute the polynomials for classical and virtual knots and links of small crossing number for a few small permutations.

</details>

### [4] [Bockstein braiding statistics](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.02280)
*Po-Shen Hsin,Yu-An Chen*

Main category: quant-ph

TL;DR: 本文识别了一种新型的互统计——Bockstein编织统计，适用于满足p+q=d-1的Z_N激发对，并通过场论中的Bockstein操作进行描述，该统计阻碍了两种激发的同时凝聚，并具有重要的物理意义。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统的编织统计要求p+q=d-2，本文旨在探索相邻情况p+q=d-1下的互统计，以扩展对高维激发间统计行为的理解。

Method: 通过构造支持具有交错一维重叠的局域产生算子X和Y，定义封闭幺正过程W_N(X,Y)=(Y^{-1}X^{-1})^N(YX)^N来测量互统计；在场论中引入Bockstein操作β_N，将响应描述为(2πi/N)∫A_{d-p}∪β_N B_{d-q}。

Result: 识别出Bockstein编织统计这一新不变量，适用于一维的粒子-粒子统计、二维的粒子-环统计、三维的环-环或粒子-膜统计；非平凡的Bockstein编织统计阻碍了两种Z_N激发的同时凝聚。

Conclusion: Bockstein编织统计排除了具有相应混合反常的系统的完全对称有能隙相，并在其中一个Z_N对称性破缺时意味着对称性分数化。

Abstract: Braiding statistics, from the Aharonov-Bohm phase to anyons in fractional quantum Hall systems, play a central role in quantum physics. For $p$- and $q$-dimensional excitations in $d$ spatial dimensions, ordinary braiding requires $p+q=d-2$. In a field-theoretic description of $\mathbb Z_N$ excitations, ordinary braiding is described by the linking response $(2πi/N)\int A_{d-p}\cup B_{d-q}$, where $A_{d-p}$ and $B_{d-q}$ are background fields coupled to the two excitation types. In this work, we identify new mutual statistics in the adjacent case $p+q=d-1$. For two invertible excitations obeying $\mathbb Z_N$ fusion, one can choose local creation operators $X$ and $Y$ whose supports have a staggered one-dimensional overlap. The closed unitary process $W_N(X,Y)=(Y^{-1}X^{-1})^N(YX)^N$ measures the resulting mutual statistic. Its field-theory description is $(2πi/N)\int A_{d-p}\cupβ_N B_{d-q}$, where $β_N$ is the Bockstein operation; we therefore call the invariant Bockstein braiding statistics. The construction yields particle-particle statistics in one dimension, particle-loop statistics in two dimensions, and loop-loop or particle-membrane statistics in three dimensions. Nontrivial Bockstein braiding statistics obstructs simultaneous condensation of the two $\mathbb Z_N$ excitations. It also rules out a fully symmetric gapped phase for systems with the corresponding mixed anomaly and implies symmetry fractionalization when one of the $\mathbb Z_N$ symmetries is broken.

</details>

### [5] [Unitriangular R-matrices of quantum affine algebras and Yangians via Theta series](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.02287)
*Huafeng Zhang*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 本文给出了量子仿射代数单位三角R矩阵的共轭公式，并推广到了Yangian情形。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 量子仿射代数的普遍R矩阵存在Gauss分解，但其中的单位三角部分在任意有限维表示下的具体形式尚不清楚，需要给出简洁的共轭公式。

Method: 利用Frenkel-Hernandez的T-series和之前工作中引入的Theta series，给出了单位三角R矩阵的共轭公式；在Yangian情形则借助shifted Yangian三重张量积表示的associator进行推广。

Result: 得到了量子仿射代数单位三角R矩阵的简洁共轭公式，并成功将其推广到Yangian情形。

Conclusion: 该共轭公式统一并简化了量子仿射代数和Yangian中单位三角R矩阵的表达形式。

Abstract: The universal R-matrix of the quantum affine algebra associated to a finite-dimensional simple complex Lie algebra admits a Gauss decomposition into an uper unitriangular part, an abelian part, and a lower unitriangular part. In this paper, we provide a simple conjugation formula for the unitriangular R-matrices with one tensor factor evaluated at an arbitrary finite-dimensional representation of the quantum affine algebra. Our formula involves the T-series of Frenkel--Hernandez and the Theta series introduced in a previous work.
  We also extend our conjugation formula to the Yangian case, making use of associators for triple tensor product representations of shifted Yangians.

</details>
