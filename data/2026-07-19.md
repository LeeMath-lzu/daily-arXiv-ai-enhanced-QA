### [1] [Quotients of strongly graded quasi-Hopf algebras](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.14381)
*Fabio Calderón,César Galindo*

Main category: math.RA

TL;DR: 本文对强G-分次拟Hopf代数中的拟Hopf理想进行了分类，建立了其与正规子群和不变理想之间的对应关系，并将结果应用于无限分次、有限中性分量和非平凡重结合子的共中心阿贝尔扩张。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 对强G-分次拟Hopf代数中的拟Hopf理想进行系统分类，以更好地理解其结构。

Method: 通过建立拟Hopf理想与正规子群N及G/N-不变拟Hopf理想K之间的对应关系，并改进该对应关系以适用于特定类别的拟Hopf理想。

Result: 每个拟Hopf理想I对应于一对(N,K)，其中N是G的正规子群，K是H_N中的G/N-不变拟Hopf理想，且其像包含k[N]的增广理想。

Conclusion: 该分类可用于处理无限分次、有限中性分量和非平凡重结合子的共中心阿贝尔扩张等应用问题。

Abstract: Let $H$ be a strongly $G$-graded quasi-Hopf algebra, with $G$ an arbitrary group. We classify its quasi-Hopf ideals by showing that each quasi-Hopf ideal $I$ of $H$ corresponds to a pair $(N,K)$, where $N$ is a normal subgroup of $G$ and $K$ is a $G/N$-invariant quasi-Hopf ideal of $H_N=\bigoplus_{n\in N}H_n$ whose image under the grading map contains the augmentation ideal of $\Bbbk[N]$. We further improve this correspondence for particular classes of quasi-Hopf ideals, such as those that have an associated quasi-Hopf retraction, or those whose lifted quotient is a crossed product. Applications include cocentral abelian extensions with infinite grading, finite neutral component and nontrivial reassociator.

### [2] [On the triviality and non-triviality of the automorphism group of a skew brace](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.17276)
*Cindy Tsang*

Main category: math.GR

TL;DR: 本文研究偏斜臂环的自同构群，证明某些族群的偏斜臂环自同构群平凡当且仅当其阶为1或2，并构造了一个阶为2p³且自同构群平凡的偏斜臂环。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 受群论中自同构群平凡性结果的启发，探究偏斜臂环这一代数结构中类似的性质是否成立。

Method: 通过理论分析证明某些偏斜臂环族满足自同构群平凡当且仅当阶为1或2，并针对任意奇素数p构造具体实例。

Result: 证明了特定偏斜臂环族中自同构群平凡性的等价条件，并成功构造了一个阶为2p³且自同构群平凡的偏斜臂环。

Conclusion: 偏斜臂环在自同构群平凡性方面与群有类似结论，但存在更高阶的平凡自同构群实例。

Abstract: It is a simple fact that a group has a trivial automorphism group if and only if it is of order $1$ or $2$. We prove that the same holds for certain families of skew braces, and given any odd prime $p$, we construct a skew brace of order $2p^3$ that has a trivial automorphism group.

*Vidas Regelskis*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 本文为分裂对称对（BI、CI、DI型）的扭曲Yangian引入了新的转置表示，基于此推导了Drinfeld型电流表示，建立了与R矩阵表示的等价性，并给出了张量积分解和PBW基等结果。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 建立扭曲Yangian的R矩阵表示与Drinfeld表示之间的严格同构关系，解决Lu、Wang和Zhang提出的猜想。

Method: 引入受扭曲反射方程支配的转置表示，结合高斯分解，在R矩阵框架内推导Drinfeld型电流表示，并通过提取系数恢复Drinfeld表示。

Result: 给出了特殊扭曲Yangian和扩展扭曲Yangian的Drinfeld型电流表示，证明了与R矩阵表示的同构，得到了扩展扭曲Yangian的张量积分解（Drinfeld表示扭曲Yangian与多项式环的直积），以及Drinfeld生成元的PBW基。

Conclusion: 本文完整解决了扭曲Yangian两类表示之间的等价性问题，并提供了结构分解和基的构造，推进了量子对称对理论的发展。

Abstract: We study twisted Yangians associated with the split symmetric pairs of types BI, CI and DI. We introduce a new presentation of these algebras, which we call the transposed presentation, governed by a twisted reflection equation that interacts naturally with the Gaussian decomposition of the generating matrix. Working entirely within the $R$-matrix presentation, we derive Drinfeld-type current presentations of the special twisted Yangian $SY^{\mathrm{tw}}(\mathfrak{g}_N)$ and of the extended twisted Yangian $X^{\mathrm{tw}}(\mathfrak{g}_N)$, in which the Serre relations are stated in a closed current form. Extracting coefficients recovers the Drinfeld presentation due to Lu. As a consequence, we establish the isomorphism between the $R$-matrix and Drinfeld presentations of these twisted Yangians conjectured by Lu, Wang and Zhang. As a byproduct, we obtain a tensor product decomposition of $X^{\mathrm{tw}}(\mathfrak{g}_N)$ into the twisted Yangian in the Drinfeld presentation and a polynomial ring in countably many central variables. We also obtain Poincaré-Birkhoff-Witt bases in the Drinfeld generators and describe the coideal coproduct on the low Drinfeld modes.

### [5] [PBW bases and centralisers for the $q$-Onsager algebra](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.10097)
*Haoran Zhu*

Main category: math-ph

TL;DR: 本文证明了在任意域上且q不是单位根时，Baseilhac-Kolb根向量构成q-Onsager代数的PBW基，解决了Terwilliger的四个猜想和一个关于交替中心扩张的猜想。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 移除先前PBW基构造中的超越性假设，证明q-Onsager代数在任意域上的PBW基存在性；解决Terwilliger提出的四个相关猜想以及Baseilhac和Belliard提出的猜想。

Method: 利用Baseilhac-Kolb根向量，在任意域上且q不是单位根的条件下，对正根的所有全序建立PBW基；通过交替中心扩张的标量中心特化研究PBW基的保持性；分析负交替子代数的中心化子和第一虚交替生成元的中心化子。

Result: 成功去除了超越性假设，建立了12种PBW基（交替生成元形式）；证明了这些基在任意标量中心特化下保持不变；确定了两个重要子代数的中心化子；推导出四个单族交替多项式子代数是极大交换的。

Abstract: We prove that, over an arbitrary field and whenever $q$ is not a root of unity, the Baseilhac--Kolb root vectors form a PBW basis of the $q$-Onsager algebra for every total order on the positive roots of $\widehat{\mathfrak{sl}}_2$. This removes the previous transcendence hypothesis. We establish twelve PBW bases in the alternating generators and show that they persist under arbitrary scalar central specialisation of the alternating central extension. We determine the centraliser of the negative alternating subalgebra and that of the first imaginary alternating generator, and deduce that the four single-family alternating polynomial subalgebras are maximal commutative. Together, these results settle four conjectures of Terwilliger and, in characteristic different from $2$, a conjecture of Baseilhac and Belliard.

### [6] [Bockstein braiding statistics](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.02280)
*Po-Shen Hsin,Yu-An Chen*

Main category: quant-ph

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种在d=p+q+1维空间中测量任意维度(p和q)的Z_N融合激发子之间互编织现象的新方法，称为Bockstein编织统计。该方法通过4N步局域幺正过程测量Berry相位，适用于粒子-粒子、粒子-环、环-环等编织统计，并与场论中的Bockstein同态对应。该框架还能作为混合反常的微观诊断工具，在(1+1)D、(2+1)D和(3+1)D体系中验证了新的反常对称对，并指出非平凡Bockstein编织会阻碍对称凝聚并导致高形式对称性的分数化。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统互编织统计只存在于d=p+q+2维空间，本文希望将这一概念推广到相邻维度d=p+q+1，以覆盖更多物理情形，并建立统一的微观构造和诊断方法。

Method: 提出一个4N步的微观幺正过程，利用格点上的局域激发算子构建Berry相位不变量；通过Bockstein同态联系场论响应；将激发算子解释为开放对称算子，用于诊断混合反常。

Result: 在(1+1)D自旋链中成功检测到自旋翻转对称性与近邻CZ对称性之间的混合反常；在(2+1)D和(3+1)D中构造了新的反常对称对；在连续规范理论中验证了框架的有效性。

Conclusion: 非平凡Bockstein编织统计排除了完全对称的能隙相，阻碍了两类激发的同步凝聚，并意味着高形式对称性的分数化。

Abstract: Braiding phenomena, from the charge-flux Aharonov-Bohm effect to anyonic statistics in fractional quantum Hall systems, are paradigmatic manifestations of topology in quantum physics. Ordinary mutual braiding between $p$- and $q$-dimensional excitations occurs in $d=p+q+2$ spatial dimensions. In this work, we introduce a universal construction of mutual statistics in the adjacent dimension $d=p+q+1$, applicable to excitations obeying $\mathbb Z_N$ fusion for arbitrary $N$ and all excitation dimensions $p$ and $q$. The corresponding invariant is the Berry phase accumulated in a simple $4N$-step microscopic unitary process built from local excitation operators on lattices. This process measures the linking of one excitation with the $N$-fold fusion junction of the other, encompassing particle-particle statistics in one dimension, particle-loop statistics in two dimensions, and loop-loop or particle-membrane statistics in three dimensions. We establish the quantization and bilinearity of the invariant and show that its field-theory response is governed by the Bockstein homomorphism, motivating the name Bockstein braiding statistics. Interpreting the excitation operators as open symmetry operators turns the same invariant into a direct microscopic diagnostic of mixed anomalies between symmetries. We demonstrate this diagnostic in a (1+1)D spin chain, where the nontrivial Bockstein braiding phase proves the mixed anomaly between the spin-flip symmetry $\prod X$ and the nearest-neighbor controlled-$Z$ symmetry $\prod CZ$. We construct explicit (2+1)D and (3+1)D lattice analogs, yielding new anomalous symmetry pairs, and apply the framework to strongly coupled (3+1)D continuum gauge theories. Nontrivial Bockstein braiding rules out a fully symmetric gapped phase, obstructs simultaneous condensation of the two excitations, and implies fractionalization of higher-form symmetries.

</details>
