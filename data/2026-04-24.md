### [1] [On quantum $K$-groups of partial flag manifolds](https://arxiv.org/abs/1906.09343)
*Syu Kato*

Main category: math.AG

TL;DR: 本文证明了部分旗流形的等变小量子K-群是全旗流形的等变小量子K-群的商群，且商映射保持Schubert类结构。这是Peterson定理的抛物版本在K-理论中的类比，但与量子上同调情形表现不同。部分Novikov变量被映射到1，该特殊化的几何意义在量子K-理论中尚不明确。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将Peterson定理（关于量子上同调）的抛物版本推广到K-理论领域，探究部分旗流形与全旗流形在等变小量子K-群之间的关系。

Method: 通过构造商映射，将全旗流形的等变小量子K-群商掉得到部分旗流形的对应结构，并验证该映射保持Schubert类。将部分Novikov变量特殊化为1。

Result: 部分旗流形的等变小量子K-群是全旗流形对应结构的商群，商映射保持Schubert类。但该结果与量子上同调情形不同，且Novikov变量特殊化为1的几何意义尚不清晰。

Conclusion: 建立了部分旗流形与全旗流形在等变小量子K-理论中的联系，揭示了与量子上同调不同的代数结构，同时指出Novikov变量特殊化的几何解释仍需进一步研究。

Abstract: We show that the equivariant small quantum $K$-group of a partial flag manifold is a quotient of that of the full flag manifold in a way that respects the Schubert classes. This is a $K$-theoretic analogue of the parabolic version of Peterson's theorem [Lam-Shimozono, Acta Math. {\bf 204} (2010)] that exhibits a different behavior from the case of quantum cohomology. Our quotient maps send some of the Novikov variables to $1$, and the geometric meaning of this specialization is unclear in quantum $K$-theory.

### [2] [Subsystem-Resolved Spectral Theory for Quantum Many-Body Hamiltonians](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.21929)
*MD Nahidul Hasan Sabit*

Main category: quant-ph

TL;DR: 本文通过子系统哈密顿量框架研究量子多体系统的谱性质，证明子系统谱具有局域近似性和近似可加性，揭示了谱结构直接反映相互作用的局域几何。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统方法难以直接建立多体哈密顿量的谱性质与相互作用局域结构之间的定量关系，作者希望提出一种更系统的框架，将谱数据按相互作用结构组织起来，从局域角度理解整体谱行为。

Method: 对每个子系统S定义子系统哈密顿量H_S及其谱S(S)，利用指数衰减的局域近似证明谱的截断稳定性，并通过距离依赖的误差界建立不相交子集谱的近似可加性。

Result: 证明了子系统哈密顿量可由有限邻域算子指数逼近，误差为|S|e^{-μr}||Φ||_μ；子系统谱在截断下稳定；不相交子集的谱近似可加，误差随距离指数衰减；有限距情形下精确可加。

Conclusion: 谱性质不仅反映算符层面的局域性，也在谱层面上体现局域性，提供了一个通过相互作用几何直接刻画多体系统谱行为的新框架。

Abstract: We study spectral properties of quantum many-body Hamiltonians through a subsystem-based framework. Given a Hamiltonian of the form $H = \sum_{X \subseteq Λ} Φ(X)$ acting on a tensor product Hilbert space, we associate to each subset $S \subseteq Λ$ a subsystem Hamiltonian $H_S$ and its spectrum $\mathcal{S}(S) = σ(H_S)$. This produces a family of spectra indexed by subsystems, allowing spectral data to be organized according to interaction structure. We show that subsystem Hamiltonians admit local approximations: $H_S$ can be approximated by operators supported on finite neighborhoods with an error bounded by $\|H_S - H_{S,r}\| \le |S| e^{-μr} \|Φ\|_μ$. As a consequence, subsystem spectra are stable under truncation in the sense that $d_H(\mathcal{S}(S), σ(H_{S,r})) \le |S| e^{-μr} \|Φ\|_μ.$ We then prove that for disjoint subsets $S_1, S_2 \subseteq Λ$, the subsystem spectrum is approximately additive: $d_H\big(\mathcal{S}(S_1 \cup S_2), \mathcal{S}(S_1) + \mathcal{S}(S_2)\big) \le (|S_1| + |S_2|) e^{-μD} \|Φ\|_μ,$ where $D = d(S_1, S_2)$. In the finite-range case, this relation becomes exact. The results show that spectral properties reflect the locality of interactions not only at the level of operators, but also at the level of spectra. The framework provides a way to study many-body systems in which interaction geometry directly shapes spectral behavior.

</details>

### [3] [Orthosymplectic quantum groups revisited](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.21785)
*Kyungtak Hong,Alexander Tsymbaliuk*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 本文针对任意奇偶序列的扩展正交-辛量子超群，给出了其RLL实现，并证明了该实现与广义双代数内部结构的兼容性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 为扩展正交-辛量子超群建立明确的RLL实现框架，统一不同符号约定，并揭示其内部代数结构。

Method: 基于先前工作(arxiv:2408.16720)中的R矩阵，构建RLL实现；通过2-上循环扭曲处理不同符号约定；分析简化R矩阵的分解性质。

Result: 成功构建了与广义双代数兼容的RLL实现，阐明了不同符号约定间的联系，并建立了简化R矩阵的分解公式。

Conclusion: 该工作为扩展正交-辛量子超群提供了系统的RLL代数框架，并为相关量子群的进一步研究奠定了基础。

Abstract: We present the RLL-realization of extended orthosymplectic quantum supergroups for any parity sequence, with R-matrices evaluated in the earlier work arxiv:2408.16720. Our isomorphism is compatible with the internal structure of generalized doubles. We also relate different sign conventions through 2-cocycle twists. Furthermore, we establish a factorization of the reduced R-matrix within the RLL-realization.

### [4] [Orthosymplectic $R$-matrices](https://arxiv.org/abs/2408.16720)
*Kyungtak Hong,Alexander Tsymbaliuk*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 本文提出了与任意奇偶序列相关的三角正交辛R-矩阵公式，并建立了它们到由正根参数化的q-指数有序积的分解，同时验证了仿射正交辛R-矩阵与Yang-Baxter化技术所得结果的一致性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 推导任意奇偶序列下三角正交辛R-矩阵的显式公式，并建立其与经典公式（Jimbo公式、Mehta公式等）的统一联系。

Method: 基于Clark-Hill-Wang发展的量子shuffle和量子超代数方法，通过q-括号化和支配Lyndon词的组合构造正子代数的正交基，实现R-矩阵的因式分解；利用Ge-Wu-Xue的Yang-Baxter化技术验证仿射正交辛R-矩阵。

Result: 给出了任意奇偶序列的三角正交辛R-矩阵公式，将其分解为q-指数的有序积；证明了仿射情形下的交织性质，并匹配了Yang-Baxter化方法的结果，复现了Jimbo（BCD型）和Mehta等人（标准奇偶序列）的经典公式。

Conclusion: 建立了任意奇偶序列下正交辛R-矩阵的统一框架，为量子超代数可积系统提供了新的构造工具。

Abstract: We present a formula for trigonometric orthosymplectic $R$-matrices associated with any parity sequence, and establish their factorization into the ordered product of $q$-exponents parametrized by positive roots in the corresponding reduced root systems. The latter is crucially based on the construction of orthogonal bases of the positive subalgebra through $q$-bracketings and combinatorics of dominant Lyndon words, as developed in [Clark, Hill, Wang, "Quantum shuffles and quantum supergroups of basic type", Quantum Topol. 7 (2016), no.3, 553-638]. We further evaluate the affine orthosymplectic $R$-matrices, establishing their intertwining property as well as matching them with those obtained through the Yang-Baxterization technique of [Ge, Wu, Xue, "Explicit trigonometric Yang-Baxterization", Internat. J. Modern Phys. A 6 (1991), no.21, 3735-3779]. This reproduces the celebrated formulas of [Jimbo, "Quantum $R$ matrix for the generalized Toda system", Comm. Math. Phys. 102 (1986), no.4, 537-547] for classical BCD types and the formula of [Mehta, Dancer, Gould, Links, "Generalized Perk-Schultz models: solutions of the Yang-Baxter equation associated with quantized orthosymplectic superalgebras", J. Phys. A 39 (2006), no.1, 17-26] for the standard parity sequence.

</details>
