### [1] [Vertex-transitive quantum graphs](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30730)
*Mac Hayes,Trevor Jess,Andre Kornell,Remi Salinas Schmeis*

Main category: math.OA

TL;DR: 本文定义了量子图的顶点传递性，并证明$M_2(\mathbb C)$中所有简单量子图都是顶点传递的，而$M_3(\mathbb C)$中许多不是。通过引入全景多项式这一不变量，完成了$M_3(\mathbb C)$中顶点传递量子图的完全分类。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将经典图论中的顶点传递性概念推广到量子图领域，并探索不同维度下量子图的对称性性质。

Method: 通过自同构群的连接与量子顶点集上的最大量子关系来定义顶点传递性；引入全景多项式作为量子图的多项式不变量来进行分类。

Result: $M_2(\mathbb C)$中所有简单量子图都是顶点传递的；$M_3(\mathbb C)$中顶点传递量子图已被完全分类。

Conclusion: 全景多项式是有效的量子图不变量，可用于区分和分类不同维度的顶点传递量子图。

Abstract: We define a quantum graph to be vertex-transitive if the join of its automorphism group is the maximum quantum relation on its quantum vertex set, in direct analogy with the classical case. All simple quantum graphs in $M_2(\mathbb C)$ are vertex-transitive, but many simple quantum graphs in $M_3(\mathbb C)$ are not vertex-transitive. We provide a complete classification of vertex-transitive quantum graphs in $M_3(\mathbb C)$ up to isomorphism. To do this, we introduce a polynomial invariant for quantum graphs in $M_n(\mathbb C)$, which we call the panoramic polynomial.

</details>

### [2] [Twisted representations of conformal nets and crossed balanced tensor categories](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.03623)
*Adrià Marín-Salvador*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 本文证明，对于带有离散群G作用的共形网A，其G-扭表示范畴Rep^G(A)自然地构成一个G-交叉平衡W*-张量范畴，将Müger关于局部自同态的结果推广到更一般的框架下。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将Müger关于有理共形网中G-交叉辫张量范畴的结果推广到非有理情形，并在W*-张量范畴的语言下建立更一般的理论框架。

Method: 利用W*-张量范畴理论，结合G-扭表示的结构分析，在非有理共形网上构造G-交叉平衡结构。

Result: 证明了Rep^G(A)是G-交叉平衡W*-张量范畴，推广了Müger在局部自同态语言下的结果。

Conclusion: 该工作为理解非有理共形场论中的对称性及其表示理论提供了新的数学框架，扩展了G-交叉张量范畴理论的应用范围。

Abstract: Let $\mathcal{A}$ be a (not necessarily rational) conformal net with an action of a discrete group $G$. We show that the category $\text{Rep}^G(\mathcal{A})$ of $G$-twisted representations of $\mathcal{A}$ is canonically a $G$-crossed balanced $\mathrm{W}^*$-tensor category. This extends the results of Müger arXiv:math/0403322, in the language of localized endomorphisms, that $\text{Rep}^G(\mathcal{A})$ is a $G$-crossed braided tensor category.

</details>


### [3] [Higher-dimensional Chiral Algebras in the Jouanolou Model and free-field realization](https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.26608)
*Zhengping Gui,Minghao Wang,Brian R. Williams*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 本文利用Jouanolou torsors理论建立d维仿射空间中点配置空间的相干上同调模型，并由此发展出高维手性操作概念，推广了Beilinson和Drinfeld的手性代数理论。通过受费曼图积分启发的留数概念，实现了高维Kac-Moody代数和Virasoro代数。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将Beilinson和Drinfeld的手性代数理论从一维推广到高维空间，建立更高维度的代数结构。

Method: 运用Jouanolou torsors理论建模配置空间的相干上同调，发展手性操作的操作化概念，并利用受费曼图积分启发的高维留数概念构造实例。

Result: 实现了高维Kac-Moody代数和Virasoro代数。

Conclusion: 成功将手性代数推广到高维，并建立了高维无穷维李代数的新实现。

Abstract: We appeal to the theory of Jouanolou torsors to model the coherent cohomology of configuration spaces of points in d-dimensional affine space. Using this model, we develop the operadic notion of chiral operations, thus generalizing the notion of chiral algebras of Beilinson and Drinfeld to higher dimensions. To produce examples, we use a higher-dimensional conceptualization of the residue which is inspired by Feynman graph integrals. One of our main results is the realization, using higher chiral operations, of the higher-dimensional Kac--Moody and Virasoro algebras.

</details>
