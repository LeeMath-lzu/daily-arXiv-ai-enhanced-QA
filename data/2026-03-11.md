<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Method: 通过量子元胞自动机（QCA）参数化对偶算子，建立QCA与可逆C-C双模范畴的对应关系，分析对偶算子形成的单纯形结构，研究外部对称性在UV到IR的流

Abstract: We systematically study categorical duality operators on spin (and anyon) chains with respect to an internal fusion category symmetry C. In particular, a QCA $α$ on $B$ defines an invertible C-C bimodule category $M_α$, and the duality operators extending $α$ form a simplex, with extreme points in bijective correspondence with the simple object of $M_α$. Then we consider the structure of external symmetries generated by a family of duality operators, and show that if the UV models are all defined on tensor product Hilbert spaces, these categories necessarily flow to weakly integral fusion categories in the IR.

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Method: 方法是在单子三角范畴的紧致部分定义的支撑簇理论基础上，定义到非紧致部分的扩展。通过假设原始支撑簇理论基于诺特拓扑空间、检测零对象、满足广义张量积性质并配备比较映射，来证明扩展支撑簇也检测零对象。

Result: 主要结果是当原始支撑簇理论满足特定条件时，扩展支撑簇也能检测零对象。在有限张量范畴的稳定范畴情形下，这为中心上同调支撑提供了检测零对象的扩展条件，证实了作者与Nakano和Yakimov最近提出的猜想的一部分。

### [4] [Modular families of elliptic long-range spin chains from freezing](https://arxiv.org/abs/2507.13104)
*Rob Klabbers,Jules Lamers*

Main category: math-ph

TL;DR: 该论文提出通过"冻结"可积量子多体系统来构造具有q变形长程相互作用的量子可积自旋链，特别关注椭圆情况，建立了从最近邻到长程相互作用的自旋链的可积插值。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 构建具有q变形长程相互作用的量子可积自旋链，提供从最近邻到长程相互作用的自旋链的统一框架，并理解冻结过程在混合可积系统背景下的作用。

Method: 1. 通过"冻结"自旋-Ruijsenaars系统及其底层无自旋经典Ruijsenaars-Schneider系统的平衡构型来构造长程自旋链；2. 在椭圆情况下定义模群作用，展示存在模族经典平衡构型；3. 使用形变量子化框架为在任意经典平衡处冻结椭圆自旋-Ruijsenaars系统提供统一框架。

The input is a (quantum) spin-Ruijsenaars system along with an equilibrium configuration of the underlying spinless classical Ruijsenaars-Schneider system. For a distinguished choice of equilibrium, the resulting long-range spin chain has a real spectrum and admits a short-range limit, providing an integrable interpolation from nearest-neighbour to long-range interacting spins. These typically have constant but nonzero momenta. Then we use the setting of deformation quantisation to provide a uniform framework for freezing elliptic spin-Ruijsenaars systems at any classical equilibrium whilst preserving quantum integrability. As we showed in previous work, the results include the Heisenberg, Inozemtsev and Haldane-Shastry chains along with their xxz-like q-deformations (face-type), or the antiperiodic Haldane-Shastry chain of Fukui-Kawakami, its elliptic generalisation of Sechin-Zotov, and their completely anisotropic q-deformations due to Matushko-Zotov (vertex type). Finally, we show how freezing fits in the setting of 'hybrid' integrable systems.

### [5] [Graph quandles: Generalized Cayley graphs of racks and right quasigroups](https://arxiv.org/abs/2506.04437)
*Luc Ta*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 该论文为研究右拟群（包括架和昆德尔）在图上的作用的几何群论类比奠定了基础，通过图的标记实现右拟群，并引入基于此类标记的图不变量。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 建立研究右拟群在图上的作用的几何群论类比框架，解决Valeriy Bardakov提出的两个问题，为右拟群、架、昆德尔等代数结构提供图论表征。

Method: 研究实现架的图标记，引入基于此类标记的图不变量，使用Schreier图表征右拟群的Cayley图，特别关注无向图和完全图的实现能力。

Result: 所有右拟群都可由无边图和完全图实现；所有架都可由其完全Cayley图实现；给出了右可消幺半群、右可除幺半群、右拟群、架、昆德尔、对合架和kei的标记Cayley有向图的图论特征。

Conclusion: 成功建立了右拟群在图上的作用的几何群论类比框架，解决了Bardakov的两个问题，为相关代数结构提供了系统的图论表征方法。

Abstract: This article lays the foundations for an analogue of geometric group theory that studies actions on graphs by right quasigroups, including racks and quandles. We study markings of graphs that realize racks, and we introduce (di)graph invariants based on such markings. We show that all right quasigroups are realizable by edgeless graphs and complete (di)graphs. Using Schreier (di)graphs, we also characterize Cayley (di)graphs of right quasigroups Q that realize Q. In particular, all racks are realizable by their full Cayley (di)graphs. This solves two problems of Valeriy Bardakov. Finally, we give graph-theoretic characterizations of labeled Cayley digraphs of right-cancellative magmas, right-divisible magmas, right quasigroups, racks, quandles, involutory racks, and kei.

</details>
