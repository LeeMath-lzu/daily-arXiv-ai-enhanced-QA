### [1] [Quantum Graph Theory by Example](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.23651)
*Gian Luca Spitzer,Ion Nechita*

Main category: math.OA

TL;DR: 该论文提出了一个参数化量子图家族，通过矩阵三元组(A,B,C)描述，将量子图结构分解为经典部分（奇怪图）和纯量子部分，并计算了多种量子图参数。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 量子图理论面临的主要困难是量子图不再是离散对象，难以构建有洞察力的非平凡示例。本文旨在提供可以用离散术语思考的量子图集合，并建立参数化模型来研究量子图参数。

Method: 使用Musto、Reutter和Verdon提出的图形式化方法，构建由经典矩阵群作用的量子图，通过矩阵三元组(A,B,C)参数化。将量子图结构分解为经典部分（由A和C描述的"奇怪图"）和纯量子部分（由B提供）。

Result: 建立了第一个大型参数化量子图家族，可以解析计算标准图参数。给出了量子图参数的精确公式或界限，包括连通分量数、色数、独立数和团数。

Conclusion: 该参数化模型揭示了量子图结构的清晰分解，为量子图理论提供了丰富的示例库，使得量子图参数的计算成为可能，推动了量子图理论的发展。

Abstract: Quantum graphs have been introduced by Duan, Severini, and Winter to describe the zero-error behaviour of quantum channels. Since then, quantum graph theory has become a field of study in its own right. A substantial source of difficulty in working with quantum graphs compared to classical graphs stems from the fact that they are no longer discrete objects. This makes it generally difficult to construct insightful, non-trivial examples. We present a collection of non-trivial quantum graphs that can be thought of in discrete terms, and that can be expressed in the diagrammatic formalism introduced by Musto, Reutter, and Verdon. The examples arise as the quantum graphs acted on by increasingly smaller classical matrix groups, and are parametrised by triples of matrices $(A, B, C)$. The parametrisation reveals a clean decomposition of quantum graph structure into classical and genuinely quantum components: $A$ and $C$ are described by a classical weighted graph called the strange graph, while $B$ provides a purely quantum contribution with no classical analogue. Based on this model, we give exact formulas or establish bounds for quantum graph parameters, such as the number of connected components, the chromatic number, the independence number, and the clique number. Our results provide the first large, parametric families of quantum graphs for which standard graph parameters can be computed analytically.

### [2] [Cyclic adjoint modules and their embeddings in quantized enveloping algebras](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.24490)
*Arnab Bhattacharjee*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 研究量子Levi子代数的伴随作用在量子包络代数上产生的循环伴随模，分析不可约模在量子包络代数中的实现，并建立嵌入映射和偏序关系。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究量子Levi子代数的伴随作用在量子包络代数上产生的相对局部有限部分，探索循环伴随模的结构性质，特别是不可约模在量子包络代数中的实现方式。

Method: 通过循环生成元分析不可约模在量子包络代数中的实现，描述固定类型的嵌入映射，建立从循环生成元到同构类的自然映射，引入循环伴随模的偏序关系。

Result: 揭示了循环生成元实现不可约模的非唯一性（通过映射纤维反映），证明了每个循环伴随模都由有限个不可约子模生成，并将偏序关系的最小元与不可约子模联系起来。

Conclusion: 系统地研究了量子Levi子代数伴随作用产生的循环伴随模结构，建立了不可约模实现的分类框架，为理解量子群表示理论中的伴随模提供了新的工具和视角。

Abstract: We study cyclic adjoint modules arising from the relative locally finite part of the adjoint action of a quantum Levi subalgebra on a quantized enveloping algebra. We analyze the realization of irreducible modules inside the quantized enveloping algebra via cyclic generators and describe embeddings of a fixed type. This leads to a natural map to isomorphism classes, whose fibers reflect the non-uniqueness of such realizations. We further introduce a partial order on cyclic adjoint modules and relate its minimal elements to irreducible submodules. In addition, we show that every cyclic adjoint module is generated by finitely many irreducible submodules.

### [3] [Shuffle algebra realizations for restricted Yangians](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.23243)
*Hao Chang,Hongmei Hu,Yue Hu*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 本文研究了特征p>2代数闭域上sl_n相关Yangian正子代数的洗牌代数实现，发现与特征零情况不同，自然同态不是同构，确定了其核和像，得到了限制Yangian的洗牌代数实现。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究特征p>2代数闭域上Yangian正子代数的洗牌代数实现，探索与特征零情况的差异，理解模特征对代数结构的影响。

Method: 使用特殊化映射方法和构造小Yangian，通过从复数域上Yangian的积分形式进行模p约化得到小Yangian，分析自然同态的核和像。

Result: 自然同态的核恰好是由Y_n^{>}(k)的p-中心生成的理想，像由满足与特征p相关的附加轮条件的元素组成，从而为限制Yangian Y_n^{>,[p]}提供了洗牌代数实现。

Conclusion: 在特征p>2情况下，Yangian正子代数与模洗牌代数之间的自然同态不是同构，其核和像的精确描述为限制Yangian提供了洗牌代数实现，揭示了模特征对代数结构的深刻影响。

Abstract: We study the shuffle algebra realization of the positive subalgebra $Y_n^{>}(\mathbb{k})$ of the Yangian associated to $\mathfrak{sl}_n$ over an algebraically closed field $\mathbb{k}$ of characteristic $p>2$. In contrast to the characteristic zero case, the natural homomorphism from $Y_n^{>}(\mathbb{k})$ to the modular shuffle algebra $W^{(n)}(\mathbb{k})$ is not an isomorphism. We determine its kernel and image, showing that the kernel is precisely the ideal generated by the $p$-center of $Y_n^{>}(\mathbb{k})$, while the image consists of elements satisfying an additional wheel condition related to the characteristic $p$, thus providing a shuffle algebra realization for the restricted Yangian $Y_n^{>,[p]}$. The proof relies on the specialization maps approach and the construction of the small Yangian $\bar{y}^{>}_n(\mathbb{k})$, obtained by the reduction modulo $p$ method from an integral form $\mathbf{Y}_n^>$ of the Yangian $Y_n^{>}$ associated to $\mathfrak{sl}_n$ over $\mathbb{C}$.

*Alexandr Garbali,Andrei Neguţ*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 该论文研究了矩阵洗牌代数中交换元素族，这些代数与量子环面代数同构，通过量子仿射代数的R矩阵部分迹给出了计算公式。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究矩阵洗牌代数中的交换元素族，这些代数与量子环面代数同构，旨在建立更深入的结构理解和计算方法。

Method: 使用量子环面代数工具和新的矩阵洗牌代数反同态，通过量子仿射代数R矩阵的部分迹来计算交换元素，并给出格路径解释。

Result: 得到了矩阵洗牌代数中各种交换元素族的计算公式，这些公式用量子仿射代数R矩阵的部分迹表示，并具有格路径解释。

Conclusion: 成功描述和计算了矩阵洗牌代数中的交换元素族，建立了通过量子仿射代数R矩阵部分迹的计算框架，为相关代数结构研究提供了新工具。

Abstract: We describe and compute various families of commuting elements of the matrix shuffle algebra of type $\mathfrak{gl}_{n|m}$, which is expected to be isomorphic to quantum toroidal $\mathfrak{gl}_{n|m}$. Our formulas are given in terms of partial traces of products of $R$-matrices of the quantum affine algebra $U_t(\dot{\mathfrak{gl}}_{n|m})$, and have a lattice path interpretation. Our calculations are based on the machinery of the quantum toroidal algebras and a new anti-homomorphism between matrix shuffle algebras.
