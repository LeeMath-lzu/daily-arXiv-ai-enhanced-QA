### [1] [Hyperbolic structures on link complements, octahedral decompositions, and quantum $\mathfrak{sl}_2$](https://arxiv.org/abs/2203.06042)
*Calvin McPhail-Snyder*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 该论文展示了如何从Kashaev-Reshetikhin在根单位处的量子群$\mathcal{U}_ξ(\mathfrak{sl}_2)$上的辫子结构，推导出链环补集的双曲结构方程。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究链环补集的双曲结构（即基本群到$\operatorname{SL}_2(\mathbb{C})$的表示）的代数描述方法，探索量子群理论与双曲几何之间的联系。

Method: 使用链环图确定的八面体分解（理想三角剖分），通过Kashaev-Reshetikhin在根单位处量子群$\mathcal{U}_ξ(\mathfrak{sl}_2)$上的辫子结构，获得形状参数的粘合方程。

Result: 证明了这些双曲结构方程可以从量子群的辫子结构推导出来，为链环的$\operatorname{SL}_2(\mathbb{C})$表示簇提供了坐标，并给出了几何解释。

Conclusion: 量子群理论中的辫子结构与链环补集的双曲几何之间存在深刻联系，为研究双曲结构提供了新的代数框架和几何解释。

Abstract: Hyperbolic structures on link complements (equivalently, representations of the fundamental group into $\operatorname{SL}_2(\mathbb{C})$) can be described algebraically by using the octahedral decomposition determined by a link diagram. The decomposition (like any ideal triangulation) gives a set of gluing equations in shape parameters whose solutions are hyperbolic structures. We show that these equations can be obtained from Kashaev-Reshetikhin's braiding on the Kac-de Concini quantum group $\mathcal{U}_ξ(\mathfrak{sl}_2)$ at a root of unity $ξ$. This braiding gives coordinates on the $\operatorname{SL}_2(\mathbb{C})$ representation variety of a link and our work shows how to interpret these geometrically.

### [2] [Deformation quantisation of exact shifted symplectic structures, with an application to vanishing cycles](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.07602)
*J. P. Pridham*

Main category: math.SG

TL;DR: 该论文建立了精确移位辛结构与带形式导数的非退化移位泊松结构之间的对应关系，并证明了这些结构具有唯一的自对偶形变量子化，应用于复(-1)-移位辛结构时得到BBDJS的消失循环层及其单值算子。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 扩展作者和CPTVV关于移位辛结构与泊松结构之间的对应关系，建立精确移位辛结构与带形式导数的非退化移位泊松结构之间的对应，为各种数学结构（包括导出代数、解析和光滑叠）提供统一的量子化框架。

Method: 通过形式导数概念（推广De Wilde和Lecomte的构造），建立精确移位辛结构与带形式导数的非退化移位泊松结构之间的对应关系，证明这些结构具有唯一的自对偶形变量子化。

Result: 1. 建立了精确移位辛结构与带形式导数的非退化移位泊松结构之间的对应关系；2. 证明了这些结构在任何可表述的设定中都具有唯一的自对偶形变量子化；3. 对于解析和光滑设定中的0-移位辛结构，量子化参数化不依赖于结合子的选择；4. 对于复(-1)-移位辛结构，其量子化产生BBDJS的消失循环层及其单值算子。

Conclusion: 该研究成功建立了精确移位辛结构与带形式导数的非退化移位泊松结构之间的对应关系，并证明了这些结构具有唯一的自对偶量子化，这一框架具有广泛的应用性，特别在复(-1)-移位辛结构的量子化中得到了BBDJS的消失循环层等重要结果。

Abstract: We extend the author's and CPTVV's correspondence between shifted symplectic and Poisson structures to establish a correspondence between exact shifted symplectic structures and non-degenerate shifted Poisson structures with formal derivation, a concept generalising constructions by De Wilde and Lecomte. Our formulation is sufficiently general to encompass derived algebraic, analytic and $\mathcal{C}^{\infty}$ stacks, as well as Lagrangians and non-commutative generalisations. We also show that non-degenerate shifted Poisson structures with formal derivation carry unique self-dual deformation quantisations in any setting where the latter can be formulated.
  One application is that for (not necessarily exact) $0$-shifted symplectic structures in analytic and $\mathcal{C}^{\infty}$ settings, it follows that the author's earlier parametrisations of quantisations are in fact independent of any choice of associator, and generalise Fedosov's parametrisation of quantisations for classical manifolds.
  Our main application is to complex $(-1)$-shifted symplectic structures, showing that our unique quantisation of the canonical exact structure, a sheaf of twisted $BD_0$-algebras with derivation, gives rise to BBDJS's perverse sheaf of vanishing cycles, equipped with its monodromy operator.

### [3] [Classification of 1-super-transitive quantum subgroups in type A](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.11431)
*Cain Edie-Michell,Jacques Katumba*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 该论文定义了étale代数对象的超传递性概念，并分类了所有1-超传递的étale代数对象，涵盖了已知的无限族和大部分已知非点状étale代数。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究étale代数对象的超传递性，这是子因子中超传递性概念的类比，用于衡量在A-模范畴中首次出现"新内容"的深度。旨在系统分类这些代数对象。

Method: 定义étale代数对象的超传递性概念，作为子因子理论中相应概念的直接类比。通过数学分析对$\mathcal{C}(\mathfrak{sl}_N, k)$中所有1-超传递的étale代数对象进行分类。

Result: 给出了所有$N,k \in \mathbb{N}$情况下1-超传递étale代数对象的完整分类。该分类涵盖了$\mathcal{C}(\mathfrak{sl}_N, k)$中所有已知的非点状étale代数的无限族，以及除16个已知例子外的所有非点状étale代数对象。

Conclusion: 成功分类了1-超传递étale代数对象，剩余16个已知例子的超传递性在2到4之间，表明更高超传递性的分类是未来研究方向。

Abstract: We define a notion of super-transitivity for ètale algebra objects $A \in \mathcal{C}(\mathfrak{sl}_N, k)$. This definition is a direct analogue of the notion of super-transitivity for subfactors, and measures at what depth the first ``new stuff'' appears in the category of $A$-modules internal to $\mathcal{C}(\mathfrak{sl}_N, k)$. Our main theorem gives a classification of all 1-super-transitive ètale algebra objects in $\mathcal{C}(\mathfrak{sl}_N, k)$ running over all $N,k \in \mathbb{N}$. Our classification captures all known infinite families of non-pointed ètale algebras in $\mathcal{C}(\mathfrak{sl}_N, k)$, and includes all but 16 of the known non-pointed ètale algebra objects in these categories. These remaining 16 known examples have super-transitivities between 2 and 4.
