Method: 采用Ellwood在非交换几何中给出的自由性定义，使用与经典情况不同的证明方法

### [2] [Quantum isometry groups of log-Laplacians on Cuntz--Krieger algebras](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.02835)
*Amaury Freslon,Dimitris Michail Gerontogiannis,Adam Skalski*

Main category: math.OA

TL;DR: 计算Cuntz-Krieger代数的量子等距群，这些代数配备了来自底层拓扑马尔可夫链的Ahlfors正则结构的谱三元组，发现了一类新的紧致量子群，混合了图的自同构量子群和简单量子群的特征。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究Cuntz-Krieger代数在特定谱三元组下的量子等距群，探索量子群理论在算子代数中的应用，特别是寻找具有特殊性质的量子群。

Method: 使用来自拓扑马尔可夫链的Ahlfors正则结构构建谱三元组，然后计算Cuntz-Krieger代数在这些谱三元组下的量子等距群。

Result: 发现了一类新的紧致量子群，这些量子群混合了图的自同构量子群和简单量子群的特征。与经典等距群不同，量子等距群在Cuntz代数情况下具有遍历性作用，并构造了紧致矩阵量子群在康托空间上的真正量子遍历作用。

Conclusion: 通过计算Cuntz-Krieger代数的量子等距群，成功构造了具有混合特征的新量子群，并展示了量子等距群在遍历性方面与经典等距群的根本差异，为量子群理论提供了新的实例。

Abstract: We compute the quantum isometry groups of Cuntz--Krieger algebras endowed with the spectral triples coming from the Ahlfors regular structure of the underlying topological Markov chain. This allows us to exhibit a new family of compact quantum groups, mixing features from quantum automorphism groups of graphs and easy quantum groups. Contrary to the classical isometry groups, whose actions on the Cuntz--Krieger algebras are never ergodic, the quantum isometry group acts ergodically in the case of the Cuntz algebra. This also leads to the construction of a (genuinely quantum) ergodic action of a compact matrix quantum group on the Cantor space.

### [3] [How Quantum Circuits Actually Learn: A Causal Identification of Genuine Quantum Contributions](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.16321)
*Cyrille Yetuyetu Kesiku,Begonya Garcia-Zapirain*

Main category: quant-ph

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种反事实因果中介框架，用于分解量子机器学习中不同架构间的性能差异，区分直接效应（电路参数化和表达能力）和间接效应（量子信息论可观测量的中介作用）。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 当前量子机器学习中难以区分性能提升是源于真正的量子资源还是经典架构扩展，需要一种方法论来解决这一挑战。

Method: 引入反事实因果中介框架，将架构间性能差异分解为直接效应（电路参数化和表达能力）和间接效应（通过纠缠熵、纯度、线性熵和量子互信息等量子信息论可观测量的中介作用）。在5种电路拓扑和3个基准数据集（共43个验证配置）上应用该框架。

Result: 直接架构贡献系统地超过量子中介效应，平均比率为13.1:1，平均间接贡献仅为0.82%。这表明当前变分量子电路远未发挥其量子潜力。

Conclusion: 当前变分量子电路在远低于其量子潜力的水平上运行，基于原则的资源感知电路设计是实现可测量的量子中介性能增益的可行路径。

Abstract: Attributing performance gains in quantum machine learning to genuine quantum resources rather than to classical architectural scaling remains an open methodological challenge. We address this by introducing a counterfactual causal mediation framework that decomposes inter-architectural performance differences into direct effects, attributable to circuit parameterization and expressivity, and indirect effects mediated by quantum information-theoretic observables: entanglement entropy, purity, linear entropy, and quantum mutual information. Applying this framework to five circuit topologies and three benchmark datasets (across 43 validated configurations) reveals that direct architectural contributions systematically exceed quantum-mediated effects, with a mean ratio of 13.1:1 and a mean indirect contribution of 0.82%. These results suggest that current variational quantum circuits operate substantially below their quantum potential, and that principled resource-aware circuit design represents a tractable path toward measurable quantum-mediated performance gains.

</details>

### [5] [CFTs with Large Gap from Barnes-Wall Lattice Orbifolds](https://arxiv.org/abs/2411.13646)
*Christoph A. Keller,Ashley Winter Roberts,Jeremy Roberts*

Main category: hep-th

TL;DR: 研究晶格共形场论的轨道折叠，利用Barnes-Wall晶格和额外特殊2群的自同构，构建具有大能隙的理论，最终得到中心荷128、能隙4的全纯CFT。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究晶格共形场论的轨道折叠构造方法，目标是构建具有大能隙的理论，这对于理解共形场论的结构和性质具有重要意义。

Method: 使用Barnes-Wall晶格（无短向量家族），通过额外特殊2群的晶格自同构进行轨道折叠。研究轨道折叠顶点算子代数及其扭曲模，需要特定异常3-上循环ω消失才能得到全纯CFT。

Result: 基于提供的证据，推测异常3-上循环ω确实消失。在此推测基础上，成功构造了中心荷128、能隙4的全纯共形场论。

Conclusion: 通过Barnes-Wall晶格的轨道折叠构造，可以构建具有大能隙的全纯共形场论，为相关理论研究提供了新的构造方法和实例。

Abstract: We investigate orbifolds of lattice conformal field theories with the goal of constructing theories with large gap. We consider Barnes-Wall lattices, which are a family of lattices with no short vectors, and orbifold by an extraspecial 2-group of lattice automorphisms. To construct the orbifold CFT, we investigate the orbifold vertex operator algebra and its twisted modules. To obtain a holomorphic CFT, a certain anomaly 3-cocycle $ω$ needs to vanish; based on evidence we provide, we conjecture that it indeed does. Granting this conjecture, we construct a holomorphic CFT of central charge 128 with gap 4.

</details>
