### [1] [Signatures in TQFT : Asymptotics and Modularity](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.13450)
*Julien Marché,Gregor Masbaum*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 该论文研究了SU₂-TQFT向量空间在亏格g曲面上的符号σ_g(q/p)，证明了当q/p趋近于无理数θ时，p²分之一乘以σ₂(q/p)收敛于Λ(θ)，并发现Λ(θ)是权为4的2级模形式的Eichler积分的边界值，提出了亏格2符号函数的变换规律猜想。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究SU₂-TQFT向量空间在曲面上的符号函数σ_g(q/p)的渐近行为，特别是当参数q/p趋近于无理数时的极限性质，探索其与模形式和Eichler积分的关系。

Method: 通过分析SU₂-TQFT向量空间的符号σ_g(q/p)，证明当q/p趋近于无理数θ时，p²分之一乘以σ₂(q/p)收敛于Λ(θ)。利用模形式理论，发现Λ(θ)是权为4的2级模形式的Eichler积分的边界值。

Result: 证明了在特定条件下，当q/p趋近于无理数θ时，p²分之一乘以σ₂(q/p)收敛于Λ(θ)=16/π³∑_{n≥1,奇数}1/[n³sin(nπθ)]。观察到Λ(θ)是权为4的2级模形式的Eichler积分的边界值。

Conclusion: SU₂-TQFT向量空间的符号函数在亏格2情况下表现出与模形式和Eichler积分的深刻联系，提出了类似于经典Dedekind和互反公式的符号函数变换规律猜想。

Abstract: We study the signature $σ_g(\frac q p)$ of $\mathrm{SU}_2$-TQFT vector spaces associated to surfaces of genus $g$, as a function of the defining root of unity $ζ=e^{iπq/p}$. We prove that $\frac{1}{p^2}σ_2(\frac{q}{p})$ converges to $Λ(θ)=\frac{16}{π^3}\sum\limits_{n\ge 1, \textrm{ odd}}\frac{1}{n^3\sin(nπθ)}$ when $\frac{q}{p}$ goes to an irrational number $θ\in [0,1]$ under certain conditions. We also observe that the function $Λ(θ)$ is the boundary value of an Eichler integral of a level $2$ modular form of weight $4$, and use this to propose a conjectural transformation law for the signature function in genus 2 similar to the reciprocity formula for classical Dedekind sums.

### [2] [A new product formula for $(z;q)_\infty$, with applications to asymptotics](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.11329)

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究q-Pochhammer符号的表示和渐近行为，建立与伽马函数的关系，类似于椭圆伽马函数的已有结果

Method: 将(z;q)∞表示为伽马函数的无穷乘积，类比Narukawa对椭圆伽马函数的处理，然后利用该恒等式推导q→1时的渐近展开

Result: 获得了q-Pochhammer符号的伽马函数无穷乘积表示，并推导出q趋于1时的渐近展开公式

Abstract: We express the $q$-Pochhammer symbol $(z;q)_\infty$ as an infinite product of gamma functions, analogously to how Narukawa expressed the elliptic gamma function as an infinite product of hyperbolic gamma functions. This identity is used to obtain asymptotic expansions when $q$ tends to $1$.

### [3] [Modular $\mathbb{Z}_2$-Crossed Tambara-Yamagami-like Categories for Even Groups](https://arxiv.org/abs/2411.12251)
*César Galindo,Simon Lentner,Sven Möller*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 本文构造了非退化辫子Z2交叉张量范畴Vect_Γ⊕Vect_{Γ/2Γ}，推广了Tambara-Yamagami范畴，允许Γ为偶数阶群，且非平凡部分有多个简单对象。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 动机来源于物理研究，希望从无限维Tambara-Yamagami范畴Vect_{ℝ^d}⊕Vect构造任意Γ的Z2交叉扩展，并通过凝聚过程实现从无限维到有限维的转换。

Method: 方法包括：1) 显式构造非退化辫子Z2交叉张量范畴；2) 证明凝聚过程与交叉扩展可交换；3) 通过Z2等变化得到新的模张量范畴。

Result: 成功构造了Vect_Γ⊕Vect_{Γ/2Γ}形式的非退化辫子Z2交叉张量范畴，推广了Tambara-Yamagami范畴，允许Γ为任意有限阿贝尔群（包括偶数阶），且非平凡部分有多个简单对象。

Conclusion: 该构造为任意格点顶点算子代数的轨道折叠提供了对应的模张量范畴，建立了物理构造与严格数学理论之间的联系。

Abstract: We explicitly construct nondegenerate braided $\mathbb{Z}_2$-crossed tensor categories of the form $\operatorname{Vect}_Γ\oplus\operatorname{Vect}_{Γ/2Γ}$. They are $\mathbb{Z}_2$-crossed extensions, in the sense of arXiv:0909.3140, of the braided tensor category $\operatorname{Vect}_Γ$ with $\mathbb{Z}_2$-action given by $-\mathrm{id}$ on the finite, abelian group $Γ$. Thus, we obtain generalisations of the Tambara-Yamagami categories, where now the abelian group $Γ$ may have even order and the nontrivial sector $\operatorname{Vect}_{Γ/2Γ}$ more than one simple object.
  The idea for this construction comes from a physically motivated approach in arXiv:2409.16357 to construct $\mathbb{Z}_2$-crossed extensions of $\operatorname{Vect}_Γ$ for any $Γ$ from an infinite Tambara-Yamagami category $\operatorname{Vect}_{\mathbb{R}^d}\oplus\operatorname{Vect}$, which itself is not fully rigorously defined, and then using condensation from $\operatorname{Vect}_{\mathbb{R}^d}$ to $\operatorname{Vect}_Γ$, which we prove commutes with crossed extensions.
  The $\mathbb{Z}_2$-equivariantisation of $\operatorname{Vect}_Γ\oplus\operatorname{Vect}_{Γ/2Γ}$ yields new modular tensor categories, which correspond to the orbifold of an arbitrary lattice vertex operator algebra under a lift of $-\mathrm{id}$, as discussed in arXiv:2409.16357.

### [4] [Deformation Theory of $\mathbb{E}_n$-Monoidal Categories](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.21431)
*Yining Chen*

Main category: math.AG

TL;DR: 该论文研究了E_n-幺半稳定k-线性∞-范畴的形变问题，证明其naive形变问题是2-近形式E_{n+2}-模问题，由特定非单位E_{n+2}-代数控制。在刚性幺半且由无阻碍对象紧生成条件下，该形变问题等价于形式模问题。论文还证明了形式模问题的唯一性定理，并应用于代数群表示范畴的形变，最后展示了因子化同调与形变的兼容性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究E_n-幺半稳定k-线性∞-范畴的形变理论，特别是理解其形变问题的代数结构控制机制，建立形式模问题与naive形变问题之间的等价关系，并将理论应用于代数群表示范畴的具体形变问题。

Method: 采用高阶范畴论和形式模问题理论，通过构造E_n-中心的概念，将形变问题与特定非单位E_{n+2}-代数联系起来。使用刚性幺半范畴和紧生成对象的性质建立等价关系，并应用唯一性定理处理代数群表示范畴的特殊情况。

Result: 证明了E_n-幺半稳定k-线性∞-范畴的naive形变问题是2-近形式E_{n+2}-模问题，由fib(End_{Z_{E_n}(C)}(1)→End_C(1))控制。在刚性幺半且由无阻碍对象紧生成条件下，naive形变问题等价于形式模问题。建立了形式模问题的唯一性定理，可应用于约化代数群表示范畴的E_1和E_2-幺半形变问题。

Conclusion: 该工作建立了E_n-幺半∞-范畴形变问题的系统理论框架，揭示了形变问题与高阶代数结构之间的深刻联系，为代数几何和表示论中的形变问题提供了新的工具和视角，并展示了因子化同调与形变理论的兼容性。

Abstract: In this paper, we prove that the naive deformation problem of an $\mathbb{E}_n$-monoidal stable $k$-linear $\infty$-category $\mathcal{C}$ is a $2$-proximate formal $\mathbb{E}_{n+2}$-moduli problem, whose corresponding formal moduli problem is controlled by the non-unital $\mathbb{E}_{n+2}$-algebra $\mathrm{fib}\big(\mathrm{End}_{\mathcal{Z}_{\mathbb{E}_n}(\mathcal{C})}(1)\rightarrow \mathrm{End}_{\mathcal{C}}(1)\big)$, where $\mathcal{Z}_{\mathbb{E}_n}(\mathcal{C})$ is the $\mathbb{E}_n$-center of $\mathcal{C}$. If $\mathcal{C}$ is rigid monoidal and tamely compactly generated by unobstructible objects, then this naive deformation problem is equivalent to the formal moduli problem. We also prove a uniqueness theorem for formal deformations of certain formal moduli problems, which can be applied to the $\mathbb{E}_1$ and $\mathbb{E}_2$-monoidal deformation problems of $\mathbf{Rep}(G)$ for a reductive algebraic group $G$ with a simple Lie algebra $\mathfrak{g}=T_e G$. Finally, we show factorization homology is compatible with deformations.

</details>
