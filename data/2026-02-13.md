### [2] [Modules of the Temperley-Lieb algebra at zero](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.11479)
*Eddy Li,Kenta Suzuki*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 在β=0时，对偶数n的Temperley-Lieb代数TL_n(0)的模范畴进行了显式描述，通过箭图代数表示，类似于Berest-Etingof-Ginzburg的结果。构造了标准模的精确序列，范畴化了Jones多项式在t=-1处的数值巧合，并推导出特征2对称群表示论的推论。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究Temperley-Lieb代数在特殊参数β=0时的表示理论，特别是偶数n的情况。动机包括：1）显式描述该代数在特殊参数下的模范畴结构；2）范畴化Jones多项式在特定点的数值巧合；3）探索与对称群表示论的联系。

Method: 使用箭图代数方法显式描述TL_n(0)的模范畴，类似于Berest-Etingof-Ginzburg的方法。构造了标准模的精确序列，将Jones多项式在t=-1的数值巧合范畴化。通过代数表示论技术推导出对称群在特征2下的表示论结果。

Result: 1）成功描述了偶数n时TL_n(0)的模范畴结构；2）构造了标准模的精确序列；3）实现了Jones多项式在t=-1数值巧合的范畴化；4）获得了特征2对称群表示论的新推论。

Conclusion: 该研究为Temperley-Lieb代数在特殊参数下的表示理论提供了显式描述，建立了与Jones多项式数值性质的范畴联系，并揭示了与对称群表示论在特征2下的深刻联系，拓展了代数表示论与拓扑不变量之间的桥梁。

Abstract: We explicitly describe the category of modules of the Temperley-Lieb algebra $\mathrm{TL}_n(β)$ under specialization $β=0$ for even $n$ in terms of a quiver algebra, analogous to a result of Berest-Etingof-Ginzburg. In particular, we explicitly construct an exact sequence of the standard modules of $\mathrm{TL}_n(0)$, which categorifies a numerical coincidence regarding the evaluation of the Jones polynomial at $t=-1$. We furthermore deduce a consequence in the representation theory of symmetric groups over characteristic two.

### [7] [Refined half-integer condition on RG flows](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.12085)
*Ken Kikuchi*

Main category: hep-th

TL;DR: 该论文探讨了重正化群流动中的对称性约束，特别是通过对称范畴的辫子结构推导出的半整数条件，为RG流动提供了新的限制条件。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统上，重正化群流动主要受对称性的't Hooft反常约束。然而，对称范畴可能具有辫子等额外结构，这些结构可能对RG流动施加进一步的限制。本文旨在探索这些额外结构如何约束重正化群流动。

Method: 利用对称范畴的辫子结构，研究重正化群缺陷存在时对称对象的共形维度变化。辫子结构由共形维度表征，通过分析紫外和红外共形维度的关系，推导出半整数条件。

Result: 发现了一个必要条件：在存在重正化群缺陷的RG流动中，对称对象在紫外和红外的共形维度之和必须为半整数（h_c^UV + h_c^IR ∈ 1/2 Z）。作者还利用这一精化的半整数条件解决了一些具体的RG流动问题。

Conclusion: 对称范畴的辫子结构为重正化群流动提供了超越传统反常约束的新限制条件。半整数条件及其精化形式为理解RG流动的对称性约束提供了新的理论工具。

Abstract: Renormalization group flows are constrained by symmetries. Traditionally, we have made the most of 't Hooft anomalies associated to the symmetries. The anomaly is mathematically part of the data for the monoidal structure on symmetry categories. The symmetry categories sometimes admit additional structures such as braiding. It was found that the additional structures give further constraints on renormalization group flows. One of these constraints is the half-integer condition. The condition claims the following. Braidings are characterized by conformal dimensions. A symmetry object $c$ in a braided symmetry category surviving all along the flow thus has two conformal dimensions, one in ultraviolet $h_c^\text{UV}$ and the other in infrared $h_c^\text{IR}$. In a renormalization group flow with a renormalization group defect, they add up to a half-integer $h_c^\text{UV}+h_c^\text{IR}\in\frac12\mathbb Z$. We find a necessary condition for the sum to be half-integer. We solve some flows with the refined half-integer condition.

</details>
