Motivation: 量子块编码（QBE）是大多数量子算法中的关键步骤，但现有高效技术主要集中在稀疏矩阵上，对数据稀疏（如秩结构）矩阵的研究较少。本文旨在填补这一空白，针对特定秩结构（一配对半可分离矩阵）开发高效的块编码方法。

Method: 提出一种新的块编码方法，将给定矩阵分解为三角因子和对角因子的乘积，利用该分解实现块编码。算法需要2log(N)+7个辅助量子比特。

Result: 假设数据输入预言机可以以多对数深度实现，或具有QRAM输入模型，所提出方法的时间复杂度为O(polylog(N))，误差为O(N²)。

Conclusion: 本文为秩结构矩阵（特别是半可分离矩阵）的量子块编码提供了一种高效的新方法，扩展了量子块编码的应用范围。

Abstract: Quantum block encoding (QBE) is a crucial step in the development of most quantum algorithms, as it provides an embedding of a given matrix into a suitable larger unitary matrix. Historically, the development of efficient techniques for QBE has mostly focused on sparse matrices; less effort has been devoted to data-sparse (e.g., rank-structured) matrices.
  In this work we examine a particular case of rank structure, namely, one-pair semiseparable matrices. We present a new block encoding approach that relies on a suitable factorization of the given matrix as the product of triangular and diagonal factors. To encode the matrix, the algorithm needs $2\log(N)+7$ ancillary qubits.
  Assuming that the data input oracles can be implemented with polylogarithmic depth, or that a QRAM input model is available, our proposed method requires $\mathcal{O}({\rm polylog} (N))$ time and has an error of $\mathcal{O}(N^2)$, where $N$ is the matrix size.
