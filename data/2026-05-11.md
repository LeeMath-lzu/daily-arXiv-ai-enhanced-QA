### [1] [Rank-two parabolic-type VOAs and nilpotency of nil ideals](https://arxiv.org/abs/2508.21662)
*Jianqi Liu*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 本文系统研究了秩二格顶点算子代数(VOA)的抛物型子VOA，分类了其类型与不可约模，并证明其简单商是满足强单位性质的C_1-余有限非理性VOA。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 系统地研究由第一作者引入的秩二格VOA的抛物型子VOA的结构与表示理论，填补相关理论空白。

Method: 通过分析子幺半群P ⊆ L来分类子VOA V_P的类型；利用Zhu代数A(V_P)研究不可约模分类及环结构性质；通过强单位性质分析简单商的结构。

Result: 完成了所有可能类型的分类；对每种类型分类了不可约模；发现Zhu代数提供了非幂零的幂零理想的新例子；证明了简单商V_H是C_1-余有限且满足强单位性质的非理性VOA。

Conclusion: 建立了抛物型子VOA的完整结构与表示理论，揭示了其与环论、非理性VOA理论的深刻联系。

Abstract: In this paper, we undertake a systematic study of the parabolic-type sub-vertex operator algebras (subVOAs) \(V_P\) of rank-two lattice VOAs \(V_L\), originally introduced by the first-named author. We first classify all possible types of such subVOAs by analyzing the corresponding submonoids \(P \subseteq L\). For each type of \(V_P\), we then classify its irreducible modules. Certain Zhu algebras \(A(V_P)\) provide new examples of rings with nil ideals that are not nilpotent. Finally, we show that the simple quotient \(V_H\) of any parabolic-type subVOA \(V_P\) is a \(C_1\)-cofinite irrational VOA satisfying the strongly unital property recently introduced by Damiolini--Gibney--Krashen.

### [2] [A new quasi-lisse affine vertex algebra of type $D_4$](https://arxiv.org/abs/2504.13783)
*Dražen Adamović,Ivana Vukorepa*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 本文研究了一族势拟lisse仿射顶点代数$L_{k_m}(D_4)$，证明了$m=1$时该代数是拟lisse的，且具有唯一不可约普通模，并分类了其所有不可约模（共405个），还证明了其相伴簇是$D_4$中子正则幂零轨道的Zariski闭包。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 作者旨在证明对于$m=1$，$L_{k_1}(D_4)$是拟lisse顶点代数，并验证其具有唯一不可约普通模的猜想，从而推进对势拟lisse仿射顶点代数族的系统理解。

Method: 主要采用计算方法：利用软件显式计算出泛仿射顶点代数$V^{k_1}(D_4)$中极大理想由三个共形权为6的奇异向量生成；应用Zhu理论分类所有不可约模；通过证明相伴簇包含于$D_4$的幂零锥中来说明拟lisse性质。

Result: $L_{k_1}(D_4)$在$\mathcal O$范畴中有405个不可约模，但仅有唯一一个不可约普通模；其相伴簇$X_{L_{k_1}(D_4)}$等于$\overline{\mathbb O}_{sreg}$，即$D_4$中子正则幂零轨道的Zariski闭包。

Conclusion: 成功证明了$m=1$情况下的猜想，确认$L_{k_1}(D_4)$是拟lisse顶点代数，且具有唯一不可约普通模，为更高$m$值的研究奠定了基础。

Abstract: We consider a family of potential quasi-lisse affine vertex algebras $L_{k_m}(D_4)$ at levels $k_m =-6 + \frac{4}{2m+1}$. In the case $m=0$, the irreducible $L_{k_0}(D_4)$--modules were classified in arXiv:1205.3003, and it was proved in arXiv:1610.05865 that $L_{k_0}(D_4)$ is a quasi-lisse vertex algebra. We conjecture that $L_{k_m}(D_4)$ is quasi-lisse for every $m \in {\mathbb{Z}}_{>0}$, and that it contains a unique irreducible ordinary module. In this article we prove this conjecture for $m=1$, by using mostly computational methods. We show that the maximal ideal in the universal affine vertex algebra $V^{k_1}(D_4)$ is generated by three singular vectors of conformal weight six. The explicit formulas were obtained using software. Then we apply Zhu's theory and classify all irreducible $L_{k_1}(D_4)$--modules. It turns out that $L_{k_1}(D_4)$ has $405$ irreducible modules in the category $\mathcal O$, but a unique irreducible ordinary module. Finally, we prove that $L_{k_1}(D_4)$ is quasi-lisse by showing that its associated variety is contained in the nilpotent cone of $D_4$. We also prove that the associated variety $X_{L_{k_1}(D_4)}$ is $\overline{\mathbb O}_{sreg}$, the Zariski closure of the subregular nilpotent orbit in $D_4$.

### [3] [Involutive (simple) latin solutions of the Yang-Baxter equation and related (left) quasigroups](https://arxiv.org/abs/2501.03660)
*Marco Bonatto,Marco Castelli*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 本文研究了具有正则位移群的Y ang-Baxter方程的非退化对合集论解，完全描述了不可收缩解的块和同余关系，并分类了具有极小大小p^p的简单解。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 深入理解Yang-Baxter方程集论解的结构，特别是具有正则位移群的解，以及不可收缩解与拉丁解类之间的关系。

Method: 利用块（blocks of imprimitivity）和同余关系（congruences）对不可收缩解进行描述；结合第一Weyl代数对位移群为交换正规子群的情形进行分析；通过枚举和分类方法处理极小大小的简单解。

Result: 完全刻画了不可收缩解的块和同余关系，证明了它们属于拉丁解类；刻画了具有幂零置换群的简单解；在位移群为交换正规子群时给出了更精确的描述；枚举并分类了大小为p^p的简单解。

Conclusion: 本文为Yang-Baxter方程集论解的分类提供了新的结构性结果，特别是对不可收缩解和简单解有了更完整的认识。

Abstract: In this paper, we study involutive non-degenerate set-theoretic solutions of the Yang-Baxter equation with regular displacement group. In particular, we completely describe the blocks of imprimitivity and the congruences of the irretractable ones, that we show belonging to the class of the latin solutions. Among these solutions, we characterise the simple ones having nilpotent permutation group. A more precise description involving the First Weyl Algebra will be provided when the displacement group is abelian and normal in the total permutation group, and we enumerate and classify the simple ones having minimal size $p^p$, for an arbitrary prime number $p$. Finally, we illustrate our results by some examples.

</details>
