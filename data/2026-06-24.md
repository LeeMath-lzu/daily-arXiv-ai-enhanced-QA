### [1] [Chiralization of Quiver Varieties](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.23807)
*Ioana Coman,Myungbo Shim,Masahito Yamazaki,Yehao Zhou*

Main category: math.AG

TL;DR: 本文研究了与加长型Nakajima箭图簇相关的两个顶点超代数：一个是作为几何量子化的$D^{ch}(\widetilde{\mathcal M})$，另一个是通过BRST约化构造的$\mathcal V(v,w)$，并证明了在适当条件下后者到前者的嵌入映射。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将加长型Nakajima箭图簇$\widetilde{\mathcal M}(v,w)$的几何结构手征化（chiralize），构造与之相关的顶点代数，并建立其物理对应——H-twisted 3D $\mathcal N=4$箭图规范理论的边界VOA。

Method: 1) 构造$\widetilde{\mathcal M}(v,w)$上的$\hbar$-adic顶点超代数层$\mathscr D^{ch}_{\widetilde{\mathcal M},\hbar}$，量子化其喷射丛；2) 取全局截面及$\hbar=1$特化得到$D^{ch}(\widetilde{\mathcal M})$；3) 通过$\beta\gamma bc$-系统与Heisenberg VOA的张量积的BRST约化构造$\mathcal V(v,w)$；4) 构造从$\mathcal V(v,w)$到$D^{ch}(\widetilde{\mathcal M})$的自然映射并证明其单射性。

Result: 证明了在技术假设下，与箭图Q相关的$\beta\gamma bc$-系统和Heisenberg VOA张量积的负次数BRST上同调为零；在更强假设下，$\mathcal V(v,w)$到$D^{ch}(\widetilde{\mathcal M})$的顶点超代数映射是单射。

Conclusion: 建立了从BRST约化构造的顶点超代数$\mathcal V(v,w)$到几何量子化得到的$D^{ch}(\widetilde{\mathcal M})$的嵌入关系，揭示了该顶点超代数与H-twisted 3D $\mathcal N=4$箭图规范理论边界VOA的物理联系。

Abstract: Given a quiver Q with gauge dimension $\bf v$ and framing dimension $\bf w$, one can define the extended quiver variety $\widetilde{\mathcal M}(\mathbf v,\mathbf w)$, which is a smooth family of deformations of the Nakajima quiver variety $\mathcal M(\mathbf v,\mathbf w)$. In this paper we discuss two vertex algebras which chiralize the geometry $\widetilde{\mathcal M}(\mathbf v,\mathbf w)$. We construct a sheaf of $\hbar$-adic vertex superalgebras $\mathscr D^{\mathrm{ch}}_{\widetilde{\mathcal M}(\mathbf v,\mathbf w),\hbar}$ on $\widetilde{\mathcal M}(\mathbf v,\mathbf w)$ which quantizes the jet bundle of $\widetilde{\mathcal M}(\mathbf v,\mathbf w)$, and define a vertex algebra $\mathsf D^{\mathrm{ch}}(\widetilde{\mathcal M}(\mathbf v,\mathbf w))$ to be the $\hbar=1$ specialization of the $\mathbb C^{\times}$-finite part of the vector space of global sections $Γ(\widetilde{\mathcal M}(\mathbf v,\mathbf w), \mathscr D^{\mathrm{ch}}_{\widetilde{\mathcal M}(\mathbf v,\mathbf w),\hbar})$. We define another vertex superalgebra $\mathcal V(\mathbf v,\mathbf w)$ by BRST reduction of the tensor product of the $βγbc$-system and Heisenberg VOA associated to the quiver Q, and show that there exists a natural vertex superalgebra map from $\mathcal V(\mathbf v,\mathbf w)$ to $\mathsf D^{\mathrm{ch}}(\widetilde{\mathcal M}(\mathbf v,\mathbf w))$. Under certain technical assumptions, we prove that the negative degree BRST cohomologies of the tensor product of $βγbc$-systems and Heisenberg VOA associated to the quiver Q are zero, and under stronger assumptions, that the aforementioned vertex superalgebra map is injective.
  Physically, the vertex superalgebra $\mathcal V(\mathbf v,\mathbf w)$ is closely related to the boundary VOA of the H-twisted 3D $\mathcal N=4$ quiver gauge theory associated to the quiver Q with gauge and framing dimension vectors $\bf v$ and $\bf w$.

### [2] [Vertex Superalgebras for Hypertoric Varieties and 3d Abelian Gauge Theories](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.24708)
*Tomoyuki Arakawa,Andrea E. V. Ferrari,Sven Möller*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 本文在光滑超环（hypertoric）流形上构造了$\hbar$-进顶点算子超代数层，证明其整体截面给出了3d $\mathcal{N}=4$规范理论的$A$-扭边界，并验证了相关的仿射簇与原奇异超环簇一致，从而在一类边界顶点算子超代数上证明了3d Higgs分支猜想。这些超代数是拟lisse的，与Kuwabara之前构造的（纯偶）版本形成对比，后者通常不是拟lisse的。本文还证明了新超代数是旧超代数的（费米子）简单-流扩展，并探讨了其对于辛对偶和特征标的影响。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 构造光滑超环流形上的$\hbar$-进顶点算子超代数层，以建立与3d $\mathcal{N}=4$超对称规范理论$A$-扭边界的联系，并证明3d Higgs分支猜想在该类边界顶点算子超代数上的成立。

Method: 通过Hamiltonian约化从环面作用的辛向量空间出发，在光滑超环流形上构造$\hbar$-进（微局域化意义下）顶点算子超代数层；利用其整体截面与3d规范理论边界对应，并分析拟lisse性质、简单-流扩展关系及特征标升级。

Result: 证明了该顶点算子超代数的仿射簇恢复原奇异超环簇，验证了3d Higgs分支猜想；新超代数是拟lisse的，且是Kuwabara旧超代数的（费米子）简单-流扩展；特征标从部分/伪theta函数升级为准模形式。

Conclusion: 建立了超环顶点算子超代数与3d规范理论边界的严格对应，推进了对辛对偶和拟模形式特征标的理解。

Abstract: Hypertoric (or toric hyperkähler) varieties are a class of symplectic singularities and their resolutions, obtained as Hamiltonian reductions of a symplectic vector space acted on by a torus. In physics, they appear as Higgs (and Coulomb) branches of 3d $\mathcal{N}=4$ supersymmetric quantum field theories with abelian gauge group.
  In this work, we construct an $\hbar$-adic (in the sense of microlocalisation) sheaf of vertex operator superalgebras over a given smooth hypertoric variety. Its global sections give the $A$-twisted boundary of the corresponding 3d gauge theory. We use this to prove that the associated affine variety of this hypertoric vertex operator superalgebra recovers the singular hypertoric variety. This proves the 3d Higgs branch conjecture for a large class of boundary vertex operator superalgebras. In particular, these vertex operator superalgebras are quasi-lisse.
  This is in contrast to the (purely even) hypertoric vertex operator superalgebras (and their $\hbar$-adic localisations) constructed previously by Kuwabara as global sections of sheaves on families of universal Poisson deformations of the hypertoric varieties. These are generally not quasi-lisse. We show that the vertex operator superalgebras defined in this paper are (fermionic) simple-current extensions of those defined by Kuwabara, and investigate the consequences for symplectic duality and characters. We observe that the latter are upgraded from partial (or false) theta functions to quasimodular forms.

### [4] [Drinfeld Realization for Quantum Affine Orthosymplectic Superalgebras](https://arxiv.org/abs/2405.05533)

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 辫子群作用对于构造量子仿射超代数的新Drinfeld实现至关重要，但此前B、C、D型超代数的辫子算符仅已知到归一化因子，尚未完全定义。

Method: 给出了任意奇偶性下辫子算符的显式公式，定义了直和上的辫子群作用；利用该作用形式化引入新Drinfeld实现，并证明Drinfeld-Jimbo量子群是其满同态像。

Result: 成功定义了B、C、D型量子仿射正交辛超代数的辫子群作用，建立了新Drinfeld实现，并证明了与标准Drinfeld-Jimbo量子群之间的满同态关系。

Conclusion: 本文解决了量子仿射正交辛超代数辫子作用的正规定义问题，为后续证明该同态为同构（猜想）奠定了基础。

Abstract: A well-defined braid groupoid action is an essential tool for constructing the new Drinfeld realization of a quantum affine superalgebra. In this paper, we solve this problem by providing the explicit formulas for these operators for any choice of parity. This yields a well-defined braid group action on the direct sum of these superalgebras. As a consequence, we use this action to formally introduce the new Drinfeld realization $U_q^D(\widehat{\mathfrak{g}}_s)$ for these types and prove that the corresponding Drinfeld-Jimbo quantum group $U_q(\widehat{\mathfrak{g}}_s)$ is its surjective homomorphic image. We conjecture that this map is an isomorphism.

### [7] [Classical Symmetry TFTs for Continuous Symmetries via Higher Symplectic Geometry](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.03368)

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Finally, in dimension three we compare the infinitesimal BF model $\mathrm B(\mathfrak g\ltimes\mathfrak g^\vee)$ with the factorizable double $\mathrm B(\mathfrak g\oplus \mathfrak g)$. The resulting topological boundaries are described by Lagrangian Lie subalgebras, and the factorizable case relates the SymTFT dictionary to $r$-matrices and Belavin--Drinfeld data.
