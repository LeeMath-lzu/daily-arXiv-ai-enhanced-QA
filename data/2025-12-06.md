### [1] [Graphical configuration spaces, Contractads and Formality](https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.21255)
*Anton Khoroshkin,Denis Lyskov*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 本文研究了小圆盘contractad $\mathcal{D}_d$的同伦和代数性质，构建了图形配置空间的Fulton-MacPherson紧化模型，证明了$\mathcal{D}_d$在特定条件下的形式性，并给出了$\mathcal{D}_2$的胞腔结构组合描述。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究图形配置空间$\mathrm{Conf}_Γ(X)$的代数结构，特别是当$X=\mathbb{R}^d$且点被小圆盘替代时，这些空间构成的小圆盘contractad $\mathcal{D}_d$。需要理解其同伦和代数性质，为图形配置空间的研究提供代数框架。

Method: 1. 构建并研究图形配置空间的Fulton-MacPherson紧化，为$\mathcal{D}_d$提供紧流形带边界的方便模型；2. 使用wonderful紧化技术；3. 证明$\mathcal{D}_d$在(Hopf)contractad范畴中的形式性；4. 识别循环图情况下共形式性的首项障碍；5. 给出$\mathcal{D}_2$的胞腔结构组合描述。

Result: 1. 证明了$\mathcal{D}_d$在$d=1$、$d=2$以及任意$d$下弦图情况的形式性；2. 识别了循环图情况下共形式性的首项障碍；3. 给出了$\mathcal{D}_2$的胞腔结构组合描述；4. 应用扭曲代数语言研究图形配置空间$\mathrm{Conf}_Γ(X)$。

Conclusion: 小圆盘contractad $\mathcal{D}_d$具有丰富的同伦和代数结构，其形式性结果在特定维度下成立，为图形配置空间的研究提供了有效的代数工具。Fulton-MacPherson紧化模型和胞腔结构描述为进一步研究奠定了基础。

Abstract: Given a finite simple connected graph $Γ$, the graphical configuration space $\mathrm{Conf}_Γ(X)$ is the space of collections of points in $X$ indexed by the vertices of $Γ$, where points corresponding to adjacent vertices must be distinct. When $X=\mathbb{R}^d$ and the points are replaced by small disks, the resulting spaces for all possible graphs fit together into an algebraic structure that extends the little disks operad, called the little disks contractad $\mathcal{D}_d$. In this paper, we investigate the homotopical and algebraic properties of the little disks contractad $\mathcal{D}_d$. We construct and study Fulton-MacPherson compactifications of graphical configuration spaces, which provide a convenient model for $\mathcal{D}_d$ within the class of compact manifolds with boundary. Using these and wonderful compactifications, we prove that $\mathcal{D}_d$ is formal in the category of (Hopf) contractads for $d=1$, $d=2$, and for chordal graphs for any $d$. We also identify the first obstructions to coformality in the case of cyclic graphs. In addition, we give a combinatorial description of the cell structure of $\mathcal{D}_2$ and present applications to the study of graphical configuration spaces $\mathrm{Conf}_Γ(X)$ using the language of twisted algebras.

### [2] [Reflection of Nichols Algebras over Coquasi-Hopf Algebras](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.04560)
*Bowen Li,Gongxiang Liu*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 该论文将Nichols代数的反射理论扩展到某些余拟Hopf代数，建立了有限群上带3-上循环的Yetter-Drinfeld模范畴中的反射理论，证明了半Cartan图的存在性，并给出了Nichols代数有限维性的判据。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 主要动机源于有限维点状余拟Hopf代数的分类问题。作者希望将Nichols代数的反射理论推广到更一般的余拟Hopf代数框架中。

Method: 在有限群G和3-上循环Φ的Yetter-Drinfeld模范畴中，为简单Yetter-Drinfeld模的元组发展反射理论。证明这样的元组在允许所有反射时产生半Cartan图，从而定义其Weyl群胚。

Result: 建立了半Cartan图的存在性，并给出了Nichols代数有限维性的几个判据。作为应用，为文献中研究的一类Nichols代数的无限维性提供了新的证明，避免了复杂的计算论证。

Conclusion: 成功将Nichols代数的反射理论扩展到余拟Hopf代数框架，为有限维点状余拟Hopf代数的分类提供了新的理论工具，简化了相关无限维性的证明过程。

Abstract: This paper extends the foundational reflection theory of Nichols algebras to the setting of some certain coquasi-Hopf algebras. Our primary motivation arises from the classification of pointed finite-dimensional coquasi-Hopf algebras. We develop a reflection theory for tuples of simple Yetter-Drinfeld modules in the category $\GG$, where $G$ is a finite group and $Φ$ is a 3-cocycle on $G$. We prove that such a tuple gives rise to a semi-Cartan graph if admitting all reflections. Consequently, its Weyl groupoid is well-defined. We further establish several criteria for the finite-dimensionality of Nichols algebras in terms of the associated semi-Cartan graph. As an application, we provide a new proof for the infinite-dimensionality of a specific class of Nichols algebras previously studied in \cite{huang2024classification}, bypassing extensive computational arguments.

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Result: 建立了拟双射和拟非退化解的系统理论框架；提出了拟rack概念并研究了其性质；描述了拟左非退化解与拟rack的对应关系；完全表征了可用Plonka和表示的拟rack类。

Abstract: This work initiates a systematic study of the class of quasi bijective and quasi non-degenerate solutions to the set-theoretic Yang-Baxter equation. The motivation stems from the observation that solutions that arise from dual weak braces belong to this class. The notions of quasi rack and derived solution are introduced and examined, extending the classical definitions. ### [4] [A functorial approach to Kashiwara-Vergne](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.04091)
*Rodrigo Navarro-Betancourt*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 该论文通过函子构造从操作数模块中推导出括号和余括号运算，建立了从操作数到李双代数的自然方法，并验证了Alekseev-Torossian注入的存在性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 受Kashiwara-Vergne猜想证明的启发，作者观察到GRT群可以视为带括号弦图操作数的自同构群，而KRV可以从三穿孔球面的Goldman-Turaev李双代数的自同构群中恢复，这暗示了从操作数推导李双代数的自然方法。

Method: 通过函子构造从操作数模块中推导出括号和余括号运算，利用Massuyeau和Turaev关于Fox配对和拟导子的工作作为理论基础，建立操作数与李双代数之间的关系框架。

Result: 成功重现了Alekseev-Torossian注入，建立了从操作数到李双代数的函子构造方法，并能够建立Gonzalez的高亏格GRT_g群与Alekseev等人高亏格KRV_g群之间的关系。

Conclusion: 该研究验证了从操作数自然推导李双代数的可能性，为理解GRT和KRV群之间的关系提供了新的函子框架，并扩展了高亏格情况下的对应关系。

Abstract: As a consequence of the proof of the Kashiwara-Vergne conjecture of Alekseev and Torossian, the authors obtained an injection $\mathrm{GRT} \hookrightarrow \mathrm{KRV}$. The group $\mathrm{GRT}$ can be regarded as the group of automorphisms of the operad of parenthesized chord diagrams, while $\mathrm{KRV}$ can be recovered from the automorphism group of the Goldman-Turaev Lie bialgebra of a thrice-punctured sphere. This suggests the existence of a natural way to derive Lie bialgebras from operads, and we verify this is the case. That is, we reproduce the Alekseev-Torossian injection by functorially constructing bracket and cobracket operations out of operad modules. This framework is enough to establish a relationship between Gonzalez' higher genus $\mathrm{GRT}_g$ groups, and the higher genus $\mathrm{KRV}_g$ groups of Alekseev, Kawazumi, Kuno, and Naef. Our construction is informed by Massuyeau and Turaev's work on Fox pairings and quasi-derivations.

*Sophie Raynor*

Main category: math.CT

TL;DR: 该论文将模块化操作数的现有结果扩展到一般电路代数，构建图形演算和单子，证明抽象神经定理，并将这些结果特化为轮式道具


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 电路代数是Jones平面代数的对称版本，起源于量子拓扑学，用于编码虚拟交叉。定向电路代数等价于轮式道具。论文旨在扩展模块化操作数的现有结果，为一般电路代数构建系统框架

Method: 构建图形演算和单子用于一般电路代数，证明抽象神经定理。方法依赖于分配律和抽象神经理论之间的微妙相互作用

Result: 成功构建了电路代数的图形演算和单子，证明了抽象神经定理，并将结果特化为轮式道具作为直接推论

Conclusion: 该研究为电路代数提供了系统的数学框架，揭示了分配律与抽象神经理论之间的深层结构关系，为轮式道具等特例提供了理论基础

Abstract: Circuit algebras are a symmetric version of Jones's planar algebras. They originated in quantum topology as a framework for encoding virtual crossings. Oriented circuit algebras are equivalent to wheeled props. This paper
  extends existing results for modular operads to construct a graphical calculus and monad for general circuit algebras and prove an abstract nerve theorem. Specialisations of these results to wheeled props follow as straightforward corollaries.
