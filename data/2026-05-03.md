### [1] [Resolving spurious topological entanglement entropy in stabilizer codes](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.27053)
*Peilun Han,Zijian Liang,Yifei Wang,Bowen Yang,Yingfei Gu,Yu-An Chen*

Main category: quant-ph

TL;DR: 本文识别了拓扑纠缠熵（TEE）中虚假贡献的微观起源，提出了一种无虚假贡献的Levin-Wen TEE计算方法，并研究了双变量自行车码在圆柱面上的纠缠熵行为。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 拓扑纠缠熵（TEE）是表征二维有能隙物态中长程纠缠的关键诊断量，但存在虚假贡献导致高估拓扑序的总量子维度的问题，因此需要识别其起源并提出修正方法。

Method: 通过识别虚假贡献的微观起源，引入凹形分割（concave partition）来计算平移不变稳定子码的Levin-Wen TEE，并在素数维度量子比特体系下严格证明该方法的无虚假性。同时，在双变量自行车码的双柱面几何上研究纠缠熵对柱面周长的依赖关系。

Result: 严格证明了所提出的凹形分割方法给出的Levin-Wen TEE无虚假贡献；发现双变量自行车码的纠缠熵对柱面周长敏感，揭示了任意子的拓扑阻挫效应。

Conclusion: 本研究为准确计算拓扑纠缠熵提供了理论保证，并揭示了有限尺寸几何中拓扑序的丰富行为。

Abstract: Topological entanglement entropy (TEE) is a key diagnostic of long-range entanglement in two-dimensional gapped phases of matter, but it can suffer from spurious contributions that overestimate the total quantum dimension of the underlying topological order. In this work, we identify the microscopic origin of spurious TEE and introduce a concave partition for computing the Levin-Wen TEE of translation-invariant stabilizer codes of prime-dimensional qudits. We rigorously prove that this prescription is free of spurious contributions. As a complementary probe, we study bivariate bicycle codes on a bipartite cylinder and show that the entanglement entropy depends sensitively on the cylinder circumference, revealing topological frustration of the underlying anyons.

### [2] [Holomorphic Jet Modules and Holomorphic Connections for Noncommutative Complex Curves](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.27481)
*Indranil Biswas,Satyajit Guin,Pradip Kumar*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 本文将Atiyah的全纯jet丛形式推广到非交换复曲线（即带有截断双次数微分算子的非交换代数上的全纯向量丛），证明了jet序列在全纯范畴中的正合性，建立了非交换阿蒂亚类与全纯联络的存在性对应，并应用于量子射影直线$\mathbb{CP}_q^1$。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将Atiyah关于Riemann曲面上全纯向量丛的经典jet丛理论与阿蒂亚类对应关系推广到非交换几何框架，特别是在非交换复曲线（截断双次数微分结构的非交换代数）上建立类似的理论。

Method: 在带有截断至双次(1,1)的大次微分微分的非交换代数上，定义非交换复曲线；构造全纯向量丛(E,∇̄_E)的第一jet模J_E^1及其全纯结构∇̄_J，证明jet序列的全纯正合性；定义全纯联络，证明其存在等价于jet序列分裂或阿蒂亚类消失；最后特化到量子射影直线$\mathbb{CP}_q^1$，分析双模联络条件。

Result: 成功构造了jet模上的全纯结构，使得jet序列在全纯范畴中正合；建立了全纯联络存在性与jet序列分裂及阿蒂亚类消失的等价关系；在量子射影直线上给出了∇̄_J成为双模联络的条件。

Conclusion: 实现了非交换复曲线上Atiyah经典对应定理的非交换类比，为非交换几何中的全纯向量丛理论提供了新工具。

Abstract: We extend Atiyah's holomorphic jet bundle formalism to holomorphic vector bundles over noncommutative algebras endowed with a bigraded differential calculus truncated at bidegree $(1,1)$; we refer to such structures as noncommutative complex curves. For a holomorphic vector bundle $(E,\overline{\nabla}_E)$ over such an algebra $\mathcal{A}$, we construct a canonical holomorphic structure $\overline{\nabla}_J$ on the first jet module $J_E^1\,$, making the jet sequence \[ 0\longrightarrow Ω^{1,0}(\mathcal{A})\otimes_{\mathcal A}E\longrightarrow J_E^1\longrightarrow E\longrightarrow 0 \] exact in the holomorphic category. The association $(E,\overline\nabla_E)\rightsquigarrow(J_E^1\,,\overline\nabla_J)$ defines an endofunctor on the category of holomorphic vector bundles over $\mathcal{A}$. We define the notion of holomorphic connection in this setting and prove that a holomorphic vector bundle admits a holomorphic connection if and only if the jet sequence splits in the holomorphic category, or equivalently, if and only if its Atiyah class vanishes. This yields a noncommutative analogue of Atiyah's classical correspondence for Riemann surfaces. Finally, we specialize to the quantum projective line $\mathbb{CP}_q^1\,$ and determine when $\overline{\nabla}_J$ defines a bimodule connection, assuming that $\overline{\nabla}_E$ does.

*Johannes Flake,Robert Laugwitz,Sebastian Posur*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 本文证明了沿着Hopf代数的Frobenius扩张的诱导函子在很大 generality 上是Frobenius幺半函子，特别地，对所有有限维和所有尖点Hopf代数都成立。作为应用，证明了从单模Hopf子代数到单位根处小量子群的诱导函子是Frobenius幺半函子，并分类了这类单模Hopf子代数。此外，给出了更强条件使诱导函子能扩展到Yetter-Drinfeld模范畴上的辫化Frobenius幺半函子，并证明这些条件对有限维半单且余半单（或更一般地，单模且对偶单模）Hopf代数的扩张成立。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究Hopf代数的Frobenius扩张下诱导函子的Frobenius幺半性质，并探索其在量子群分类中的应用。

Method: 通过代数结构分析和范畴论方法，证明诱导函子满足Frobenius幺半函子的条件，并分类满足条件的单模Hopf子代数。

Result: 证明了在很大 generality 下诱导函子是Frobenius幺半函子；分类了到小量子群的单模Hopf子代数；给出了扩展到辫化Frobenius幺半函子的更强条件。

Conclusion: 本文建立了Hopf代数Frobenius扩张与Frobenius幺半函子的深刻联系，为量子群和表示论提供了新的分类结果和理论工具。

Abstract: We show that induction along a Frobenius extension of Hopf algebras is a Frobenius monoidal functor in great generality, in particular, for all finite-dimensional and all pointed Hopf algebras. As an application, we show that induction functors from unimodular Hopf subalgebras to small quantum groups at roots of unity are Frobenius monoidal functors and classify such unimodular Hopf subalgebras. Moreover, we present stronger conditions on Frobenius extensions under which the induction functor extends to a braided Frobenius monoidal functor on categories of Yetter--Drinfeld modules. We show that these stronger conditions hold for any extension of finite-dimensional semisimple and co-semisimple (or, more generally, unimodular and dual unimodular) Hopf algebras.

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Result: 证明了稳定子群是连通的；将轨道空间按稳定子共轭类分层；构造了非一般轨道的*-积，推广了抛物Verma模和奇异模；构造了带平坦联络的亏格零真空/共真空向量丛。

We also construct Shapovalov forms for the corresponding representations of truncated-current Lie algebras, which enter into the category $\mathcal O$ of Chaffe--Topley; and we state a sharp irreducibility criterion. Finally, we use these representations to construct vector bundles of genus-zero vacua/covacua, equipped with flat connections à la Knizhnik--Zamolodchikov/Reshetikhin.

### [5] [The quantum group structure of long-range integrable deformations](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.27189)
*Koen Schouten,Marius de Leeuw*

Main category: math-ph

TL;DR: 本文从量子群理论角度，揭示了齐次Yang-Baxter可积自旋链的长程形变可被统一理解为量子群上的扭变（twist），并证明由此产生的Drinfeld结合子编码了长程相互作用信息，同时保证了微扰可积性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 长程形变在可积自旋链中已被广泛研究，但其背后的量子群结构尚未被认识。本文旨在填补这一理论空白，建立长程形变与量子群扭变之间的对应关系。

Method: 引入“代数荷密度”概念，通过量子群上的扭变构造形变，研究Drinfeld结合子与非结合代数结构，并在微扰一阶下验证RLL关系和Yang-Baxter方程。

Result: 长程形变对应量子群的扭变，扭变导致非结合代数结构，Drinfeld结合子编码长程相互作用项；形变后的量子群包含一个大的微扰可结合子结构，保证了微扰可积性；给出了形变模型的Lax算子和R矩阵的显式表达式。

Conclusion: 该工作为长程可积自旋链的形变提供了量子群理论基础，揭示了扭变、非结合代数与可积性之间的深刻联系，并为后续高阶形变研究提供了框架。

Abstract: Quantum integrable spin chains are known to possess a large family of long-range deformations generated by the local, boost and bilocal operators. Although these deformations are well-understood on the level of the pairwise commuting charges, the underlying quantum group structures had not yet been recognised. In this paper, we provide a quantum group-theoretical description for the family of long-range deformations of arbitrary homogeneous Yang-Baxter integrable spin chains up to first order in the deformation parameter. In particular, we show that the deformations are obtained via a twist of the algebraic structure of the underlying quantum group. This twisting results in a generally non-associative algebra that has a non-trivial Drinfeld associator. The Drinfeld associator is then shown to encode the information about the long-range interaction terms for the integrable spin chain. Moreover, the deformed quantum group is shown to contain a large perturbatively associative substructure, thus ensuring the perturbative integrability of the long-range model. The deformed quantum group provides explicit expressions for the Lax operators and R-matrices of the long-range deformed models, which manifestly satisfy the RLL relation and the Yang-Baxter equation up to first order in the deformation parameter. In order to derive the results, we introduce algebra elements that we call the algebraic charge densities. As a side result, we provide a conjecture for the explicit expressions of the undeformed charge densities in terms of these algebra elements.

</details>

### [6] [Global Homotopies for Differential Hochschild Cohomologies](https://arxiv.org/abs/2410.15903)
*Marvin Dippell,Chiara Esposito,Jonas Schnitzer,Stefan Waldmann*

Main category: math.DG

TL;DR: 本文通过结合微分几何的符号演算和van Est定理的余代数版本，为微分Hochschild上链构造了显式全局同伦，将经典的Hochschild-Kostant-Rosenberg映射升级为形变收缩核，并在主丛等场景中得到应用。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将经典的Hochschild-Kostant-Rosenberg映射升级为形变收缩核，以便在微分几何中处理更复杂的同调计算问题。

Method: 结合微分几何的符号演算与van Est定理的余代数版本两种关键技术，构造显式全局同伦。

Result: 成功构造了微分Hochschild上链的形变收缩核，并在主丛及不变性背景下发展了相关收缩核，恢复了经典HKR定理，计算了此前不可达的Hochschild上同调。

Conclusion: 提出的方法有效升级了HKR映射，拓展了其在相关几何结构中的应用范围。

Abstract: We construct explicit global homotopies for differential Hochschild cochains in differential geometry, thereby upgrading the classical Hochschild-Kostant-Rosenberg map to a deformation retract. Our approach combines two key techniques: a symbol calculus from differential geometry and a coalgebraic version of the van Est theorem. To demonstrate its effectiveness, we develop deformation retracts in several related settings, including principal bundles and invariant contexts. As a byproduct, we recover the classical Hochschild-Kostant-Rosenberg theorem and compute previously inaccessible Hochschild cohomologies.
