### [1] [Equivalence of toral Chern-Simons and Reshetikhin-Turaev theories](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.01982)
*Daniel Galviz*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 该论文证明了环面陈-西蒙斯理论与由有限二次模确定的Reshetikhin-Turaev理论之间的自然同构关系。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究环面陈-西蒙斯理论与Reshetikhin-Turaev理论之间的关系，建立两种拓扑量子场论之间的精确对应，深化对(2+1)维TQFT结构的理解。

Method: 使用几何量子化方法表述环面陈-西蒙斯理论，通过判别群G_K=Λ^*/KΛ及其诱导的二次形式q_K构造对应的尖点模范畴C(G_K,q_K)，然后建立两种TQFT之间的自然同构。

Result: 证明了环面陈-西蒙斯TQFT与由C(G_K,q_K)确定的Reshetikhin-Turaev TQFT之间存在自然同构，这一同构既适用于闭3-流形不变量，也适用于带边界的配边，从而建立了扩展(2+1)维TQFT的同构。

Conclusion: 环面陈-西蒙斯理论与Reshetikhin-Turaev理论本质上是等价的，这一结果为理解不同TQFT构造之间的关系提供了重要桥梁，深化了对(2+1)维拓扑量子场论结构的认识。

Abstract: We prove a natural isomorphism between toral Chern-Simons theory with gauge group $\mathbb T=\mathcal t/Λ\cong U(1)^n$ and the Reshetikhin-Turaev theory associated with the finite quadratic module determined by an even, integral, nondegenerate symmetric bilinear form $K:Λ\timesΛ\to\mathbb Z.$ More precisely, let $G_K=Λ^*/KΛ$ be the discriminant group of $K$, equipped with its induced quadratic form $q_K$, and let $C(G_K,q_K)$ be the corresponding pointed modular category. Using the geometric quantization formulation of toral Chern-Simons theory, we show that the resulting toral TQFT is naturally isomorphic to the Reshetikhin-Turaev TQFT determined by $C(G_K,q_K)$. The comparison is established both for closed $3$-manifold invariants and for bordisms with boundary, yielding an isomorphism of extended $(2+1)$-dimensional TQFTs.

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Method: 论文采用构建纤维函子的方法，为等价定理中的范畴建立函子结构，从而分析对应的弱Hopf代数的代数与解析特性。作者提供了核心论证的非技术性和历史性概述。

Result: 成功构建了与等价定理中范畴相关的纤维函子，从新角度解释了弱Hopf代数的结构特性，并将这些结果应用于共形场论中辫融合范畴的刚性和酉化问题。

Conclusion: 该方法为理解Finkelberg-Kazhdan-Lusztig等价定理提供了新的结构视角，在共形场论的辫融合范畴研究中具有应用价值，并提出了未来研究的自然方向。

Abstract: Our recent approach to the Finkelberg-Kazhdan-Lusztig equivalence theorem centers on the construction of a fiber functor associated with the categories in the equivalence theorem, which in turn explains the underlying algebraic and analytic structure of the corresponding weak Hopf algebra in a new sense. We provide a non-technical and historical overview of the core arguments behind our proof, discuss these structural properties, and its applications to rigidity and unitarizability of braided fusion categories arising from conformal field theory. We conclude proposing some natural directions for future research.

### [3] [Quiver Heisenberg algebras: a cubic analogue of preprojective algebras](https://arxiv.org/abs/2402.08162)
*Martin Herschend,Hiroyuki Minamoto*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 本文研究了称为箭图海森堡代数(QHA)的一类预投射代数的中心扩张，探讨了其在正特征域上的性质，建立了与路径代数表示理论的密切联系，并给出了在Dynkin情形下的范畴化结果。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究箭图海森堡代数(QHA)这一特殊类别的预投射代数中心扩张，探索其在正特征域上的性质，建立与路径代数表示理论的联系，并推广已有的特征零结果到更一般的特征情况。

Method: 通过研究QHA作为路径代数上的双模结构，构造了所谓的"万有Auslander-Reiten序列"，并利用根函子的幂来建立最小左右逼近，从而分析QHA的模结构。

Result: 证明了QHA提供了路径代数上的精确双模序列（万有Auslander-Reiten序列），建立了基于根函子幂的最小左右逼近，并在Dynkin情形下得到了QHA作为路径代数模的描述，这推广了Etingof-Rains的维数公式到正特征情况。

Conclusion: 箭图海森堡代数与相应路径代数的表示理论密切相关，类似于预投射代数的作用。在Dynkin情形下，QHA的模结构提供了Etingof-Rains维数公式的范畴化，并将结果推广到正特征域。

Abstract: In this paper we study a certain class of central extensions of preprojective algebras of quivers under the name quiver Heisenberg algebras (QHA). There are several classes of algebras introduced before by different researchers from different view points, which have the QHA as a special case. While these have mainly been studied in characteristic zero, we also study the case of positive characteristic. Our results show that the QHA is closely related to the representation theory of the corresponding path algebra in a similar way to the preprojective algebra.
  Among other things, one of our main results is that the QHA provides an exact sequence of bimodules over the path algebra of a quiver, which can be called the universal Auslander-Reiten sequence. Moreover, we show that the QHA provides minimal left and right approximations with respect to the powers of the radical functor. Consequently, we obtain a description of the QHA as a module over the path algebra, which in the Dynkin case, gives a categorification (as well as a generalization to the positive characteristic case) of the dimension formula by Etingof-Rains.

</details>

### [4] [A Rigorous Functional-Integral Construction of Toral Chern-Simons Theory](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02013)
*Daniel Galviz*

Main category: math-ph

TL;DR: 构建了阿贝尔陈-西蒙斯理论的泛函积分，使用zeta正则化高斯求值，得到拓扑不变量和边界态，满足(2+1)维TQFT公理


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 为具有环面规范群（U(1)^n）的阿贝尔陈-西蒙斯理论构建严格的泛函积分形式，通过精确的数学方法处理规范对称性，建立完整的拓扑量子场论框架

Method: 使用zeta正则化的高斯求值方法，对形式上的连接模规范商积分进行精确计算，其中规范群为环面群T = t/Λ ≅ U(1)^n，水平K是偶、整数、非退化对称双线性形式

Result: 对于闭3-流形，得到拓扑不变量；对于带边界的流形，相对泛函积分产生规范边界态；所构建的理论满足(2+1)维拓扑量子场论的所有公理要求

Conclusion: 成功构建了阿贝尔陈-西蒙斯理论的严格泛函积分形式，建立了完整的(2+1)维拓扑量子场论框架，为相关数学物理研究提供了精确的计算工具和理论基础

Abstract: We construct the functional integral of Abelian Chern-Simons theory with toral gauge group $\mathbb T=\mathfrak t/Λ\cong U(1)^n$ at level $K$, where $K:Λ\timesΛ\to\mathbb Z$ is an even, integral, nondegenerate symmetric bilinear form, by exact zeta-regularized Gaussian evaluation of the formal quotient integral over connections modulo gauge. For closed $3$-manifolds, this yields a topological invariant; for manifolds with boundary, the relative functional integral produces the canonical boundary state. The resulting theory satisfies the required axioms of a $(2+1)$-dimensional TQFT.

</details>
