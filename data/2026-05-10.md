### [1] [Equivariant 2-Poisson Algebra Bundles over Configuration Spaces](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.05398)
*Hai Châu Nguyên*

Main category: math-ph

TL;DR: 本文研究了带对角线的配置空间的等变向量丛，构造了诱导等变函子并证明了其与限制函子的伴随性，定义了Hadamard和Cauchy张量积并证明了它们形成对称2-幺半结构，最后展示了如何构造自由交换2-代数丛及Poisson括号。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究配置空间（包括对角线）在置换群作用下的等变向量丛结构，建立其严格的范畴论框架，为后续的2-代数与Poisson几何研究奠定基础。

Method: 利用等变向量丛的等价语言，构造诱导等变函子；定义Hadamard和Cauchy张量积并验证其对称2-幺半结构；构造张量和对称代数丛；通过斜对称丛映射诱导Poisson括号。

Result: 建立了诱导等变函子与限制函子的伴随关系；证明Hadamard和Cauchy张量积构成对称2-幺半结构；证明了S^⊠(S^⊗(V))是由V生成的自由交换2-代数丛；构造了相容的Poisson括号。

Conclusion: 该工作为配置空间上的等变向量丛提供了系统的2-代数框架，自由交换2-代数丛和Poisson结构的构造具有理论意义，可推广到更一般的对称张量范畴中。

Abstract: We study equivariant vector bundles over configuration spaces with diagonals included, viewed as orbifold quotients $M^n/\mathfrak{S}_n$ by permutation groups. Working in the equivalent language of equivariant vector bundles, we construct an induced-equivariance functor and prove its adjunction with restriction. We then define Hadamard and Cauchy tensor products and show that they form a symmetric $2$-monoidal structure. We construct the corresponding tensor and symmetric algebra bundles and prove that, for a local vector bundle $V \rightarrow M$, the bundle $\mathbf{S}^{\boxtimes} \big( \mathbf{S}^{\otimes}(V) \big)$ is the free commutative $2$-algebra generated by $V$. Finally, we show that any skew-symmetric bundle map $k : V \boxtimes V \rightarrow \mathbf{I}_{\otimes}$ induces a compatible Poisson bracket on this $2$-algebra bundle.

### [2] [Fundamental fields in the deformed $W$-algebras](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.09471)
*Hicham Assakaf*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种新框架来重新定义Frenkel-Reshetikhin的变形W-代数，证明了一个受Frenkel-Mukhin算法启发的算法的良定义性，并用它构造了具体元素，验证了Frenkel与Reshetikhin在Bℓ、Cℓ等类型中的猜想。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Frenkel和Reshetikhin引入了变形W-代数W_{qt}(g)，但已有定义在不同语境下需要形式化重述；同时有一个受Frenkel-Mukhin算法启发的算法（arxiv:2103.15247, arxiv:2205.08312）虽被使用但其良定义性尚未在形式化框架中证明。此外，Frenkel和Reshetikhin在arXiv:q-alg/9708006中提出的猜想在Bℓ、Cℓ等类型中尚未被证明。

Method: 提出一个形式化重述框架来重新定义变形W-代数；在该框架下重新表述并证明一个受Frenkel-Mukhin算法启发的算法的良定义性（该算法从满足某些次数条件的 dominant monomial m 出发，产生变形W-代数的元素）；将该算法应用于具体构造W_{q,t}(g)中的特定元素。

Result: 成功证明了该算法的良定义性；利用算法构造了变形W-代数的具体元素；在Bℓ、Cℓ类型以及其他类型的部分节点上证明了Frenkel与Reshetikhin的猜想。

Conclusion: 该框架为显式研究变形W-代数W_{q,t}(g)中的场提供了新的可能性，并验证了相关猜想在多种Lie代数类型中的正确性。

Abstract: Let $\mathfrak{g}$ be a simple Lie algebra. Frenkel and Reshetikhin introduced the deformed $W$-algebra $\mathbf{W}_{qt}(\mathfrak{g})$. In this work, we propose a formal reformulation of this definition in a different context. In this framework, we reformulate and prove the well-definedness of an algorithm (arxiv:2103.15247, arxiv:2205.08312) inspired by the Frenkel-Mukhin algorithm (arXiv:math/9911112) which, starting from a given dominant monomial $m$ satisfying some degree conditions, produces elements of the deformed $W$-algebra. Then, we apply this algorithm to construct explicitly some specific elements of $\mathbf{W}_{q,t}(\mathfrak{g})$. In particular, we apply this to prove a conjecture of Frenkel and Reshetikhin in arXiv:q-alg/9708006 in types $B_\ell$, $C_\ell$, and for some nodes in other types. This framework opens up new possibilities for studying explicitly fields in the deformed $W$-algebra $\mathbf{W}_{q,t}(\mathfrak{g})$.

</details>


### [3] [Extensions of a commuting pair of quantum toroidal $\mathfrak{gl}_1$](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.21750)
*B. Feigin,M. Jimbo,E. Mukhin*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 本文引入一族代数 $\mathcal{A}_{M,N}$，作为一对交换的量子环面 $\mathfrak{gl}_1$ 子代数的推广，并探讨了其余积结构与模的构造。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 为了推广量子环面代数结构，特别是将一对交换的量子环面 $\mathfrak{gl}_1$ 子代数 $\mathcal{E}_1,\check{\mathcal{E}}_1$ 进行扩展，并建立与 shifted 量子环面 $\mathfrak{gl}_2$ 代数的联系。

Method: 通过引入族代数 $\mathcal{A}_{M,N}$，参数 $M,N$ 按特定方式调谐；猜想存在余积同态 $\mathcal{A}_{M,N_1+N_2}\to\mathcal{A}_{M,N_1}\hat\otimes\mathcal{A}_{M,N_2}$，限制到子代数上退化为标准 Drinfeld 余积；并在 Fock 模的张量积直和上构造了 $\mathcal{A}_{M,N}$ 模的例子。

Result: 证明了当 $M=\pm1$ 时，$\mathcal{A}_{\pm1,N}$ 即为 [FJM2] 中引入的 shifted 量子环面 $\mathfrak{gl}_2$ 代数；给出了余积同态的猜想；并构造了具体的模实例。

Conclusion: 该工作为量子环面代数族提供了统一框架，并为进一步研究其表示论与余积结构奠定了基础。

Abstract: We introduce a family of algebras $\mathcal{A}_{M,N}$, $M,N\in\mathbb{Z}$, as an extension of a pair of commuting quantum toroidal $\mathfrak{gl}_1$ subalgebras $\mathcal{E}_1,\check{\mathcal{E}}_1$, wherein the parameters are tuned in a specific way according to $M,N$. In the case $M=\pm 1$, algebra $\mathcal{A}_{\pm1,N}$ is a shifted quantum toroidal $\mathfrak{gl}_2$ algebra introduced in [FJM2]. Conjecturally there is a coproduct homomorphism $\mathcal{A}_{M,N_1+N_2}\to\mathcal{A}_{M,N_1}\hat\otimes\mathcal{A}_{M,N_2}$ to a completed tensor product, whose restriction to the subalgebras $\mathcal{E}_1,\check{\mathcal{E}}_1$ coincides with the standard Drinfeld coproduct. We give examples of $\mathcal{A}_{M,N}$ modules constructed on certain direct sums of tensor products of Fock modules of $\mathcal{E}_1\otimes\check{\mathcal{E}}_1$.

</details>

### [4] [Pro-Tensor Network](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.06661)
*Gen Yue,Ansi Bai,Linqian Wu,Tian Lan*

Main category: cond-mat.str-el

TL;DR: 本文提出了pro-tensor network（投射张量网络）框架，作为张量网络的一种范畴化推广，用于研究"多-多体理论"（many-many-body theory），并展示了其在Levin-Wen模型、粒子分类、对称性及拓扑全息等方面的应用，同时摆脱了半单性、有限性和刚性等假设限制。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有张量网络框架在处理多体理论时存在局限性，尤其受限于半单性、有限性和刚性等假设。作者希望建立一个更普遍、更严格的图形化框架，以统一和推广多种多体理论的研究。

Method: 通过范畴化（categorification）的方式引入pro-tensor network，提供了一套完整的图形化计算工具箱，并利用promonad上的模来刻画粒子，同时将string-net pro-tensor network解释为对称张量网络空间。

Result: 成功恢复了Levin-Wen模型作为"uniform" pro-tensor network的表述，推广了Kitaev和Kong关于粒子分类的结果，并将框架应用于广义对称性和拓扑全息的研究。

Conclusion: 该框架在保持严格性和图形透明性的同时，去除了传统张量网络理论中的半单性、有限性和刚性等约束，为探索超越这些限制的多体物理提供了新的可能性。

Abstract: We introduce the pro-tensor network, a categorification of the tensor network, as a fully rigorous yet graphically transparent framework for studying the collection of many many-body theories, which we dub many-many-body theory. We provide a comprehensive toolbox for the graphical calculations using pro-tensor networks. As applications, we recover the Levin-Wen model as a "uniform" pro-tensor network and generalize a result of Kitaev and Kong by characterizing particles as modules over promonads. One can also interpret the string-net pro-tensor network as the space of symmetric tensor networks, thus our framework also applies to the study of generalized symmetry and topological holography. Notably, our generalization dispenses with the assumptions of semisimplicity, finiteness, and rigidity, potentially facilitating the exploration of many-body physics beyond these constraints.

</details>

### [5] [Representation theory of mirabolic quantum $\mathfrak{sl}_n$](https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.07469)
*Pallav Goyal,Daniele Rosso*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 本文证明了Mirabolic量子群MU(n)是量子包络代数U_v(sl_n)上的余模代数，并利用此结构完整分类了MU(n)的有限维表示，证明了其表示范畴是半单的，关键步骤是构造和分析了Verma型泛表示。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 为了完整分类Mirabolic量子群MU(n)的有限维表示，并证明其表示范畴的半单性。

Method: 首先证明MU(n)是U_v(sl_n)上的余模代数，然后构造并分析Verma型泛表示，进而显式构造所有不可约有限维表示。

Result: 完整分类了MU(n)的所有不可约有限维表示，并证明有限维表示范畴是半单的。

Conclusion: 通过余模代数结构和Verma型表示，成功解决了Mirabolic量子群有限维表示的分类问题。

Abstract: We show that the mirabolic quantum group $MU(n)$ is a comodule algebra over the quantized enveloping algebra $U_v(\mathfrak{sl}_n)$, and use this structure to give a complete classification of its finite dimensional representations. In particular, we explicitly describe the construction of all irreducible finite dimensional representations of $MU(n)$ and show that the category of finite dimensional representations is semisimple. A crucial step involves constructing and analyzing Verma-type universal representations of $MU(n)$.

</details>
