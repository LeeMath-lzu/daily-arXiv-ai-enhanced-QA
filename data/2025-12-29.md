### [1] [Classifying fusion rules of anyons or SymTFTs: A general algebraic formula for domain wall problems and quantum phase transitions](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.21687)
*Yoshiki Fukusumi*

Main category: hep-th

TL;DR: 该论文提出了一个基于SymTFT的公式，用于描述任意子在拓扑有序相或TQFT中通过有隙或对称性保持畴壁的变换规律，并应用于CFT间的无质量RG流分类。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究任意子在拓扑有序相或TQFT中通过畴壁的变换规律，建立系统化的数学框架来分类与拓扑序、CFT RG流相关的物理现象。

Method: 基于SymTFT的C-线性交换融合环之间的环同态，利用Verlinde公式，结合轨道化、扩展和相似变换等操作。

Result: 提出了任意子变换的通用公式，能够分类与范畴公式兼容的任意子，描述CFT间的无质量RG流，以及测量诱导的量子相变。

Conclusion: 该公式为拓扑序、对称性富集拓扑序和量子临界性提供了统一的分类框架，连接了TQFT和CFT的对应关系。

Abstract: We propose a formula for the transformation law of anyons in topologically ordered phases or topological quantum field theories (TQFTs) through a gapped or symmetry-preserving domain wall. Our formalism is based on the ring homomorphism between the $\mathbb{C}$-linear commutative fusion rings, also known as symmetry topological field theories (SymTFTs). The fundamental assumption in our formalism is the validity of the Verlinde formula, applicable to commutative fusion rings. By combining it with more specific data of the settings, our formula provides classifications of anyons compatible with developing categorical formulations. It also provides the massless renormalization group (RG) flows between conformal field theories (CFTs), or a series of measurement-induced quantum phase transitions, in the language of SymTFT, through the established correspondence between CFTs and TQFTs. Moreover, by studying the correspondence between the ideal structure in the massless RG and the module in the related massive RG, one can make the Nambu-Goldstone-type arguments for generalized symmetry. By combining our formula with orbifolding, extension, and similarity transformation, one can get a series of classifications for the corresponding extended models, or symmetry-enriched topological orders and quantum criticalities.

</details>

### [2] [Solutions of 3D Reflection Equation from Quantum Cluster Algebra Associated with Symmetric Butterfly Quiver](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.22004)
*Rei Inoue,Atsuo Kuniba*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 本文构造了三维反射方程的新解(R,K)，其中R算子是已有结果，K算子涉及十个量子对数函数，基于C型布线图的对称蝴蝶箭图的量子丛代数方法。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 三维反射方程是四面体方程的边界类比，在数学物理中具有重要意义。本文旨在构造该方程的新解，特别是边界K算子，扩展相关理论框架。

Method: 基于C型布线图的对称蝴蝶箭图，利用量子丛代数理论构造解。R算子采用Sun等人2024年的结果（涉及四个量子对数函数），新构造的K算子涉及十个量子对数函数，参数在q-Weyl代数中。

Result: 成功构造了三维反射方程的新解(R,K)，其中K算子包含十个量子对数函数，这是该方程边界算子的新构造结果。

Conclusion: 通过量子丛代数方法，本文为三维反射方程提供了新的解构造，特别是边界K算子的构造，丰富了该方程的解空间和相关数学结构。

Abstract: We construct a new solution $(R,K)$ to the three-dimensional reflection equation, a boundary analogue of the tetrahedron equation. The $R$-operator is the one obtained by Sun, Terashima, Yagi, and the authors in 2024, involving four quantum dilogarithms with arguments in the $q$-Weyl algebra. The new $K$-operator similarly involves ten such quantum dilogarithms. Our approach is based on the quantum cluster algebra associated with the symmetric butterfly quiver on the wiring diagram of type C.

### [3] [Extensions of a commuting pair of quantum toroidal $\mathfrak{gl}_1$](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.21750)
*B. Feigin,M. Jimbo,E. Mukhin*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 该论文引入了一个代数族 $\mathcal{A}_{M,N}$，作为量子环面 $\mathfrak{gl}_1$ 子代数对的扩展，并在特定参数条件下与量子环面 $\mathfrak{gl}_2$ 代数相关联。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究量子代数结构的扩展，探索量子环面代数 $\mathfrak{gl}_1$ 子代数对的参数化扩展，并建立与量子环面 $\mathfrak{gl}_2$ 代数的联系。

Method: 引入代数族 $\mathcal{A}_{M,N}$，通过特定参数调整扩展量子环面 $\mathfrak{gl}_1$ 子代数对；研究其与量子环面 $\mathfrak{gl}_2$ 代数的关系；提出余积同态猜想并构造具体模示例。

Result: 当 $M=\pm 1$ 时，$\mathcal{A}_{\pm1,N}$ 对应于文献中的移位量子环面 $\mathfrak{gl}_2$ 代数；提出了余积同态猜想；构造了基于 $\mathcal{E}_1\otimes\check{\mathcal{E}}_1$ 的Fock模张量积直和的 $\mathcal{A}_{M,N}$ 模示例。

Conclusion: 成功定义了一个参数化的代数族 $\mathcal{A}_{M,N}$，建立了与已知量子代数的联系，提出了结构猜想，并展示了具体的模构造方法。

Abstract: We introduce a family of algebras $\mathcal{A}_{M,N}$, $M,N\in\mathbb{Z}$, as an extension of a pair of commuting quantum toroidal $\mathfrak{gl}_1$ subalgebras $\mathcal{E}_1,\check{\mathcal{E}}_1$, wherein the parameters are tuned in a specific way according to $M,N$. In the case $M=\pm 1$, algebra $\mathcal{A}_{\pm1,N}$ is a shifted quantum toroidal $\mathfrak{gl}_2$ algebra introduced in [FJM2]. Conjecturally there is a coproduct homomorphism $\mathcal{A}_{M,N_1+N_2}\to\mathcal{A}_{M,N_1}\hat\otimes\mathcal{A}_{M,N_2}$ to a completed tensor product, whose restriction to the subalgebras $\mathcal{E}_1,\check{\mathcal{E}}_1$ coincides with the standard coproduct of the latter. We give examples of $\mathcal{A}_{M,N}$ modules constructed on certain direct sums of tensor products of Fock modules of $\mathcal{E}_1\otimes\check{\mathcal{E}}_1$.

</details>


### [4] [Quantum $SL^+(N,\mathbb{R})$ as a locally compact quantum group](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.21579)
*K. De Commer,G. Schrader,A. Shapiro,C. Voigt*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 构建了首个高秩纯连续q-变形李型局部紧量子群，基于Drinfeld-Jimbo量子化，使用量子丛理论方法


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 构建高秩情况下纯连续的q-变形李型局部紧量子群，填补该领域的研究空白

Method: 基于Drinfeld-Jimbo量子化方法，在单模变形参数下，利用量子丛理论（特别是Fock和Goncharov的工作）处理A型高秩分裂实李群的全正部分

Result: 成功构造了首个高秩纯连续q-变形李型局部紧量子群的例子

Conclusion: 通过量子丛理论方法，成功实现了高秩情况下纯连续q-变形李型局部紧量子群的构造，为相关研究提供了新范例

Abstract: We construct the first examples of purely continuous, $q$-deformed Lie type locally compact quantum groups in higher rank. They arise from Drinfeld-Jimbo quantization, at unimodular deformation parameter, of the totally positive part of higher rank split real Lie groups in type $A$. Our techniques are based on quantum cluster theory, in particular as developed through the work of Fock and Goncharov.

</details>
