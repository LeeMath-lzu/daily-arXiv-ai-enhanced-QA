### [1] [Subsystem-Resolved Spectral Theory for Quantum Many-Body Hamiltonians](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.21929)
*MD Nahidul Hasan Sabit*

Main category: quant-ph

TL;DR: 本文提出一种基于子系统的量子多体哈密顿量谱分析方法，证明子系统谱具有局部逼近性和近似可加性，揭示谱结构直接反映相互作用几何。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有量子多体系统的谱分析方法通常整体性较强，难以直接从谱结构上理解相互作用几何的影响。本文旨在建立一套子系统层面的谱分析框架，将谱数据按相互作用结构组织起来，从而揭示谱与局域性之间的内在联系。

Method: 对形如 H = ∑ Φ(X) 的哈密顿量，为每个子系统 S 定义子系统哈密顿量 H_S 及其谱 S(S)。证明 H_S 可由有限邻域支撑的算子逼近，误差以指数衰减界 |S| e^{-μr}∥Φ∥_μ 控制；进而利用该逼近证明不相交子集 S₁, S₂ 的谱近似可加性，误差由距离 D 的指数衰减函数控制。

Result: (1) 子系统谱在截断下稳定：d_H(S(S), σ(H_{S,r})) ≤ |S| e^{-μr}∥Φ∥_μ；(2) 不相交子集的谱近似可加：d_H(S(S₁∪S₂), S(S₁)+S(S₂)) ≤ (|S₁|+|S₂|) e^{-μD}∥Φ∥_μ；(3) 有限程情形下该关系精确成立。

Conclusion: 谱性质不仅反映算符层面的局域性，也反映谱结构本身的局域性。该框架为研究相互作用几何直接塑造谱行为的多体系统提供了有效工具。

Abstract: We study spectral properties of quantum many-body Hamiltonians through a subsystem-based framework. Given a Hamiltonian of the form $H = \sum_{X \subseteq Λ} Φ(X)$ acting on a tensor product Hilbert space, we associate to each subset $S \subseteq Λ$ a subsystem Hamiltonian $H_S$ and its spectrum $\mathcal{S}(S) = σ(H_S)$. This produces a family of spectra indexed by subsystems, allowing spectral data to be organized according to interaction structure. We show that subsystem Hamiltonians admit local approximations: $H_S$ can be approximated by operators supported on finite neighborhoods with an error bounded by $\|H_S - H_{S,r}\| \le |S| e^{-μr} \|Φ\|_μ$. As a consequence, subsystem spectra are stable under truncation in the sense that $d_H(\mathcal{S}(S), σ(H_{S,r})) \le |S| e^{-μr} \|Φ\|_μ.$ We then prove that for disjoint subsets $S_1, S_2 \subseteq Λ$, the subsystem spectrum is approximately additive: $d_H\big(\mathcal{S}(S_1 \cup S_2), \mathcal{S}(S_1) + \mathcal{S}(S_2)\big) \le (|S_1| + |S_2|) e^{-μD} \|Φ\|_μ,$ where $D = d(S_1, S_2)$. In the finite-range case, this relation becomes exact. The results show that spectral properties reflect the locality of interactions not only at the level of operators, but also at the level of spectra. The framework provides a way to study many-body systems in which interaction geometry directly shapes spectral behavior.

</details>

### [2] [On quantum $K$-groups of partial flag manifolds](https://arxiv.org/abs/1906.09343)
*Syu Kato*

Main category: math.AG

TL;DR: 本文证明部分旗流形的等变小量子K-群是全旗流形等变小量子K-群的商，且保持Schubert类结构，这是Peterson定理的抛物版本在K-理论中的类比，但与量子上同调情况不同。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 建立部分旗流形与全旗流形在量子K-理论中的代数结构关系，推广Peterson定理到K-理论框架。

Method: 构造商映射，将部分Novikov变量设为1，证明商结构保持Schubert类。

Result: 部分旗流形的等变小量子K-群是全旗流形的商，且该商映射保持Schubert类；部分Novikov变量被映射为1。

Conclusion: 该结果建立了量子K-理论中两类旗流形之间的代数关系，但其几何意义尚不明确，与量子上同调情形存在本质差异。

Abstract: We show that the equivariant small quantum $K$-group of a partial flag manifold is a quotient of that of the full flag manifold in a way that respects the Schubert classes. This is a $K$-theoretic analogue of the parabolic version of Peterson's theorem [Lam-Shimozono, Acta Math. {\bf 204} (2010)] that exhibits a different behavior from the case of quantum cohomology. Our quotient maps send some of the Novikov variables to $1$, and the geometric meaning of this specialization is unclear in quantum $K$-theory.

</details>

Our isomorphism is compatible with the internal structure of generalized doubles. We also relate different sign conventions through 2-cocycle twists. Furthermore, we establish a factorization of the reduced R-matrix within the RLL-realization.

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Result: 获得了任意奇偶序列下的三角正交辛R-矩阵显式公式及其有序分解，证明了仿射R-矩阵的 intertwining 性质，并复现了 Jimbo (1986) 与 Mehta et al. (2006) 的经典结果。

Conclusion: 该工作统一并推广了正交辛超代数框架下 R-矩阵的构造，为相关可积系统提供了更一般的数学基础。

Abstract: We present a formula for trigonometric orthosymplectic $R$-matrices associated with any parity sequence, and establish their factorization into the ordered product of $q$-exponents parametrized by positive roots in the corresponding reduced root systems. The latter is crucially based on the construction of orthogonal bases of the positive subalgebra through $q$-bracketings and combinatorics of dominant Lyndon words, as developed in [Clark, Hill, Wang, "Quantum shuffles and quantum supergroups of basic type", Quantum Topol. 7 (2016), no.3, 553-638]. ### [5] [Gauging the Categorical Connes' $\tildeχ(M)$](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.21833)
*Quan Chen*

Main category: math.OA

TL;DR: 论文研究了McDuff II₁因子上的外作用，建立群作用与范畴化Connes χ理论之间的联系，并首次构造出非模的辫子融合范畴作为χ̃的例子。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将Connes经典短正合列推广到范畴层面，理解群作用在算子代数范畴结构中的体现，并构造具有特定辫子融合范畴结构的II₁因子。

Method: 利用McDuff II₁因子的外作用，结合范畴化Connes χ̃理论(来自arXiv:2111.06378)，分析群的中心平凡部分K和近似内部分L，给出G/K-规范变换的显式公式。

Result: 证明了Rep(G/KL)是χ̃(M⋊G)的辫子幺半全子范畴；当L平凡时给出了G/K-规范变换公式；对任意有限群G构造了McDuff II₁因子M使得χ̃(M)辫子等价于Rep(G)。

Conclusion: 首次构造了非模的辫子融合范畴作为χ̃的例子，这是Connes理论在范畴层面的重要推广。

Abstract: We prove that if a finite group $G$ acts outerly on a McDuff $\rm II_1$ factor $M$, then $\mathsf{Rep}(G/KL)$ is a braided monoidal full subcategory of the categorical Connes' $\tildeχ(M\rtimes G)$ defined in arXiv:2111.06378, where $K$ and $L$ are the centrally trivial and approximately inner parts in $G$ respectively.
  When $L$ is trivial, we give an explicit formula for the $G/K$-gauging procedure on $\tildeχ(M\rtimes G)$. This is the categorical generalization of Connes' short exact sequence on $χ(M\rtimes G)$. Using this machinery, for any finite group $G$, we construct a McDuff $\rm II_1$ factor $M$, whose $\tildeχ(M)$ is braided equivalent to $\mathsf{Rep}(G)$. This is the first example of a braided fusion category which is not modular as $\tildeχ$.

### [7] [Reflections and Drinfeld twists for set-theoretic Yang-Baxter maps](https://arxiv.org/abs/2504.21678)
*Davide Ferri*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 本文研究了笛卡尔幺半范畴中反射方程与Yang-Baxter方程的关系，证明了反射方程的解可为YBE提供Drinfeld扭曲，并探讨了群反射与结构群之间的扩展条件。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Yang-Baxter方程和反射方程源自数学物理，可在任意幺半范畴中定义。理解两者之间的关系，特别是Drinfeld扭曲如何联系YBE的解，以及这些解如何诱导出等价的辫群表示，是本文的核心动机。

Method: 在笛卡尔幺半范畴中，证明RE的每个解可为YBE的解提供Drinfeld扭曲。利用De Commer的辫作用概念，在Lu-Yan-Zhu意义下的辫群上定义群反射，并证明其提供Ghobadi意义下的群Drinfeld扭曲。最后刻画何时集合上YBE的解的反射可扩展为其结构群上的群反射。

Result: 证明了RE解为YBE解提供Drinfeld扭曲；建立了群反射与群Drinfeld扭曲的对应关系；给出了反射从解扩展到结构群的特征刻画。

Conclusion: 本文建立了反射方程与Yang-Baxter方程在笛卡尔幺半范畴中的深层联系，并通过群反射的概念将之提升到结构群层面，为辫群表示等价性提供了新视角。

Abstract: The Yang-Baxter equation (YBE) and the reflection equation (RE) both come from mathematical physics, and they can be defined in any monoidal category.
  For cartesian monoidal categories, we prove that every solution to the RE provides a Drinfeld twist for a solution of the YBE. As we observe, Drinfeld twists of solutions are relevant for the following reason: two solutions to the YBE (in any strict monoidal category) are related by a Drinfeld twist, if and only if they induce equivalent representations of the braid group.
  In the category of sets, it is known that every solution is associated with a structure group, which is a braided group in the sense of Lu, Yan, and Zhu (2000). Using De Commer's notion of a braided action, we then define group reflections for a braided group. We prove that group reflections provide group Drinfeld twists in the sense of Ghobadi.
  Finally, we characterise when a reflection on a solution (X,r) can be extended to a group reflection on its structure group G(X,r).

</details>
