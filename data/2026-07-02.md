### [1] [Double Transposed Poisson Algebras](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.01066)
*Maxime Fairon,Nikita Safonkin*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 本文引入了双转置泊松代数，这是Bai等人提出的转置泊松代数的非交换版本，并与Kontsevich-Rosenberg原理兼容。作者首先研究了简化版本（id-适配双转置泊松代数），然后探索了一般定义。证明了每个幺结合代数上的这类结构由单个导子控制，并诱导出表示代数上的GL_N-等变转置泊松结构。还定义了H_0-转置泊松结构，并通过迹映射在GL_N-不变量环上获得转置泊松结构。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 为转置泊松代数建立非交换框架，使其与Kontsevich-Rosenberg原理相容，从而将泊松几何的概念推广到非交换代数表示论的语境中。

Method: 通过定义id-适配双转置泊松代数作为简化版本，再推广到一般定义；利用导子映射到A⊗S(A/[A,A])来描述结构；使用迹映射从表示代数到GL_N-不变量环传递泊松结构。

Result: 证明了每个幺结合代数上的双转置泊松结构由单个导子完全刻画；该结构诱导表示代数上的GL_N-等变幻方泊松结构；定义了H_0-转置泊松结构，并在不变量环上构造了转置泊松结构。

Conclusion: 双转置泊松代数提供了一个系统的非交换转置泊松理论框架，统一了表示论中代数与几何的交互，为后续研究非交换泊松几何奠定了基础。

Abstract: We introduce double transposed Poisson algebras, a noncommutative analogue of the transposed Poisson algebras of Bai, Bai, Guo and Wu that is compatible with the Kontsevich--Rosenberg principle. We first consider a simplified version which we call id-adapted double transposed Poisson algebras and then explore the general definition. We prove that every such structure on a unital associative algebra $\mathbb{A}$ is governed by a single derivation $\mathbb{A}\to\mathbb{A}\otimes\operatorname{S}(\mathbb{A}/[\mathbb{A},\mathbb{A}])$. Furthermore, this induces a $\operatorname{GL}_N$-equivariant transposed Poisson structure on each representation algebra $\mathbb{A}_N=\Bbbk[\operatorname{Rep}_N(\mathbb{A})]$. We also define $H_0$-transposed Poisson structures, the transposed counterpart of Crawley-Boevey's $H_0$-Poisson structures, and use the trace map to obtain a transposed Poisson structure on the ring of $\operatorname{GL}_N$-invariants $\mathbb{A}_N^{\operatorname{GL}_N}$.

</details>


### [2] [Knizhnik-Zamolodchikov equations in Deligne categories](https://arxiv.org/abs/2504.02266)
*Pavel Etingof,Ivan Motorin,Alexander Varchenko,Isaac Zhu*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 本文研究了Deligne范畴中的Knizhnik-Zamolodchikov方程，在$(\mathfrak{gl}_m,\mathfrak{gl}_{n})$和$(\mathfrak{so}_m,\mathfrak{so}_{2n})$对偶性背景下，推导了前者的积分公式解，并计算了两种情形下的单值性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 在Deligne范畴中研究KZ方程，并探讨其在两类李代数对偶性下的表现，旨在从范畴化角度理解经典对偶结构的解与单值性质。

Method: 在Deligne范畴框架下，利用$(\mathfrak{gl}_m,\mathfrak{gl}_{n})$和$(\mathfrak{so}_m,\mathfrak{so}_{2n})$的对偶结构分析KZ方程，推导积分公式并计算单值性。

Result: 在$(\mathfrak{gl}_m,\mathfrak{gl}_{n})$情形下得到了KZ方程的积分公式解，并在两种对偶情形下均完成了单值性的计算。

Conclusion: 将KZ方程的解与单值性分析从经典李代数推广到Deligne范畴，为范畴化对偶性提供了新的计算工具和理论支撑。

Abstract: We consider the Knizhnik-Zamolodchikov equations in Deligne Categories in the context of $(\mathfrak{gl}_m,\mathfrak{gl}_{n})$ and $(\mathfrak{so}_m,\mathfrak{so}_{2n})$ dualities. We derive integral formulas for the solutions in the first case and compute monodromy in both cases.

</details>

### [3] [Orthogonal Dualities of Dynamic Stochastic Higher Spin Vertex Models, using the Drinfeld Twister](https://arxiv.org/abs/2305.17602)
*Jeffrey Kuan,Zhengye Zhou*

Main category: math.PR

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种基于 $U_q(\mathfrak{sl}_2)$ 泛扭转子的新代数方法，用于构造动态可积模型的对偶函数，并将其应用于动态随机高阶自旋顶点模型，证明了对偶函数为 ${}_3 \varphi_2$ 函数，其退化形式给出对偶 $q$-Krawtchouk 多项式，与 Groenevelt--Wagenaar 的结果一致。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 为动态可积模型建立一种系统性的代数方法来构造对偶函数，以推广和统一已有的对偶性结果。

Method: 利用 $U_q(\mathfrak{sl}_2)$ 的泛扭转子，将其视为拟三角拟Hopf代数，通过代数构造得到动态随机高阶自旋顶点模型与静态模型之间的对偶函数。

Result: 构造出的对偶函数为 ${}_3 \varphi_2$ 超几何函数，其退化形式退化为对偶 $q$-Krawtchouk 多项式，与已知结果吻合。

Conclusion: 该代数方法具有一般性，有望推广到其他动态可积模型的对偶函数构造中。

Abstract: We introduce a new algebraic method to construct duality functions for integrable dynamic models. This method will be implemented on dynamic stochastic higher spin vertex models, where we prove that the resulting duality functions between the dynamic stochastic higher spin vertex models and non-dynamic stochastic higher spin vertex models are the ${}_3 \varphi_2$ functions. A degeneration of these duality functions is dual $q$-Krawtchouk polynomials, which agree with the orthogonal polynomial dualities of Groenevelt--Wagenaar arXiv:2306.12318 between dynamic ASEP and ASEP. The method relies on the universal twister of $U_q(\mathfrak{sl}_2)$, regarded as a quasi-triangular quasi-Hopf algebra. Since the algebraic construction is formulated in a general setting, it is expected to produce duality functions for many other dynamic integrable models as well.

</details>
