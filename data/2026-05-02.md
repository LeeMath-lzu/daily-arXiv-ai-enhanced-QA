### [1] [Resolving spurious topological entanglement entropy in stabilizer codes](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.27053)
*Peilun Han,Zijian Liang,Yifei Wang,Bowen Yang,Yingfei Gu,Yu-An Chen*

Main category: quant-ph

TL;DR: 本文识别了拓扑纠缠熵(TEE)虚假贡献的微观起源，引入了一种无虚假贡献的凹分割方法来计算Levin-Wen TEE，并通过双变量自行车码展示了纠缠熵对圆柱周长的敏感依赖性，揭示了任意子的拓扑阻挫。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 拓扑纠缠熵(TEE)是二维能隙相中长程纠缠的关键诊断工具，但会受到虚假贡献的影响，从而高估拓扑序的总量子维度。本文旨在识别虚假TEE的微观起源并提出无虚假贡献的修正方案。

Method: 1. 识别虚假TEE的微观起源；2. 引入一种凹分割(concave partition)方法来计算平移不变稳定子码的Levin-Wen TEE；3. 在双变量自行车码(bivariate bicycle codes)的双粒子圆柱几何上进行验证。

Result: 1. 严格证明凹分割方法无虚假贡献；2. 发现纠缠熵对圆柱周长有敏感依赖性，揭示了任意子的拓扑阻挫现象。

Conclusion: 提出的凹分割方法能有效消除虚假TEE贡献，为准确诊断拓扑序提供了可靠工具；同时，双变量自行车码中的拓扑阻挫现象进一步丰富了我们对纠缠熵与拓扑序关系的理解。

Abstract: Topological entanglement entropy (TEE) is a key diagnostic of long-range entanglement in two-dimensional gapped phases of matter, but it can suffer from spurious contributions that overestimate the total quantum dimension of the underlying topological order. In this work, we identify the microscopic origin of spurious TEE and introduce a concave partition for computing the Levin-Wen TEE of translation-invariant stabilizer codes of prime-dimensional qudits. We rigorously prove that this prescription is free of spurious contributions. As a complementary probe, we study bivariate bicycle codes on a bipartite cylinder and show that the entanglement entropy depends sensitively on the cylinder circumference, revealing topological frustration of the underlying anyons.

### [2] [Holomorphic Jet Modules and Holomorphic Connections for Noncommutative Complex Curves](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.27481)
*Indranil Biswas,Satyajit Guin,Pradip Kumar*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 本文将Atiyah的全纯jet丛形式推广到非交换代数上的全纯向量丛，在非交换复曲线背景下构造了jet模上的全纯结构，证明了全纯连接的存在性等价于Atiyah类消失，并推广到量子射影直线$\mathbb{CP}_q^1$。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将Atiyah关于Riemann曲面上全纯向量丛的经典jet丛理论推广到非交换几何框架下，建立非交换复曲线上的全纯连接与Atiyah类之间的对应关系。

Method: 在带有限制于双度(1,1)的大域微分计算的非交换代数上，定义全纯向量丛；构造第一jet模$J_E^1$及其全纯结构$\overline{\nabla}_J$；证明jet序列在全纯范畴中正合；利用Atiyah类刻画全纯连接的存在性；最后特例化到量子射影直线$\mathbb{CP}_q^1$。

Result: 成功构造了非交换复曲线上的jet模全纯结构；证明了全纯向量丛存在全纯连接当且仅当jet序列在全纯范畴中分裂，等价于Atiyah类为零；在量子射影直线上给出了bimodule连接的条件。

Conclusion: 该工作建立了非交换复曲线上Atiyah类与全纯连接之间的等价关系，是Atiyah经典理论在非交换几何中的自然类比，并具体应用于量子射影直线。

Abstract: We extend Atiyah's holomorphic jet bundle formalism to holomorphic vector bundles over noncommutative algebras endowed with a bigraded differential calculus truncated at bidegree $(1,1)$; we refer to such structures as noncommutative complex curves. For a holomorphic vector bundle $(E,\overline{\nabla}_E)$ over such an algebra $\mathcal{A}$, we construct a canonical holomorphic structure $\overline{\nabla}_J$ on the first jet module $J_E^1\,$, making the jet sequence \[ 0\longrightarrow Ω^{1,0}(\mathcal{A})\otimes_{\mathcal A}E\longrightarrow J_E^1\longrightarrow E\longrightarrow 0 \] exact in the holomorphic category. The association $(E,\overline\nabla_E)\rightsquigarrow(J_E^1\,,\overline\nabla_J)$ defines an endofunctor on the category of holomorphic vector bundles over $\mathcal{A}$. We define the notion of holomorphic connection in this setting and prove that a holomorphic vector bundle admits a holomorphic connection if and only if the jet sequence splits in the holomorphic category, or equivalently, if and only if its Atiyah class vanishes. This yields a noncommutative analogue of Atiyah's classical correspondence for Riemann surfaces. Finally, we specialize to the quantum projective line $\mathbb{CP}_q^1\,$ and determine when $\overline{\nabla}_J$ defines a bimodule connection, assuming that $\overline{\nabla}_E$ does.

</details>


### [3] [Frobenius monoidal functors induced by Frobenius extensions of Hopf algebras](https://arxiv.org/abs/2412.15056)
*Johannes Flake,Robert Laugwitz,Sebastian Posur*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 本文证明了沿Frobenius Hopf代数扩张的诱导函子是Frobenius幺半函子，适用于有限维和尖点Hopf代数，并进一步研究了其在幺模Hopf子代数到小量子群中的应用，以及向辫化Frobenius幺半函子的推广条件。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究沿Frobenius Hopf代数扩张的诱导函子是否具有Frobenius幺半结构，并探索其在量子群和Yetter-Drinfeld模范畴中的应用。

Method: 通过理论分析，证明了沿Frobenius扩张的诱导函子是Frobenius幺半函子的一般性结论，并进一步给出了诱导函子扩展到辫化Frobenius幺半函子的更强条件。

Result: 证明了该结论对所有有限维和尖点Hopf代数成立；分类了到小量子群的幺模Hopf子代数；给出了诱导函子成为辫化Frobenius幺半函子的充分条件，并验证了该条件对有限维半单余半单（或幺模对偶幺模）Hopf代数扩张成立。

Conclusion: 沿Frobenius Hopf代数扩张的诱导函子在广泛条件下具有Frobenius幺半结构，并可在更强条件下推广到辫化情形，具有重要的代数结构理论和量子群应用价值。

Abstract: We show that induction along a Frobenius extension of Hopf algebras is a Frobenius monoidal functor in great generality, in particular, for all finite-dimensional and all pointed Hopf algebras. As an application, we show that induction functors from unimodular Hopf subalgebras to small quantum groups at roots of unity are Frobenius monoidal functors and classify such unimodular Hopf subalgebras. Moreover, we present stronger conditions on Frobenius extensions under which the induction functor extends to a braided Frobenius monoidal functor on categories of Yetter--Drinfeld modules. We show that these stronger conditions hold for any extension of finite-dimensional semisimple and co-semisimple (or, more generally, unimodular and dual unimodular) Hopf algebras.

</details>
