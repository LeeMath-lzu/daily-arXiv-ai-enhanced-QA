### [1] [Weyl Mutations in Quiver Yangians](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.11736)
*Dmitry Galakhov,Alexei Gavshin,Alexei Morozov*

Main category: hep-th

TL;DR: 该论文研究了与D-膜在Calabi-Yau紧化上有效动力学相关的箭图规范理论的模空间和对偶性，重点关注覆盖简单李代数Dynkin图的箭图子族，其中BPS代数预期为Yangian代数Y(g)。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究非线性方程求解问题，探索将已知解转化为新解的系统方法，特别是在描述箭图簇的ADHM型方程背景下。通过箭图规范理论的模空间和对偶性研究，为Yangian代数的表示理论提供物理实现。

Method: 专注于箭图子族Q_g（覆盖简单李代数g的Dynkin图），研究g=sl_{n+1}情况。将Weyl群W_g视为箭图规范理论上的电磁Seiberg型对偶群（称为Weyl突变），并将其提升到与矩形Young图相关的高维表示情况。

Result: 建立了箭图与Dynkin图的对应关系，Weyl群作用可解释为箭图规范理论的电磁对偶变换。研究了Weyl突变对ADHM解的作用，并讨论了其对BPS Yangian代数的作用。

Conclusion: 通过箭图规范理论框架，为Yangian代数的表示理论提供了系统方法，将Weyl群作用解释为物理对偶变换，为非线性方程求解和代数结构研究提供了新视角。

Abstract: The problem of solving non-linear equations would be considerably simplified by a possibility to convert known solutions into the new ones. This could seem an element of art, but in the context of ADHM-like equations describing quiver varieties there is a systematic approach. In this note we study moduli spaces and dualities of quiver gauge theories associated to effective dynamics of D-branes compactified on Calabi-Yau resolutions. We concentrate on a subfamily of quivers $\mathfrak{Q}_{\mathfrak{g}}$ covering Dynkin diagrams for simple Lie algebras $\mathfrak{g}$, where the respective BPS algebra is expected to be the Yangian algebra $Y(\mathfrak{g})$. For Yangians labeled by quivers their representations are described by solutions of ADHM-like equations. As quivers substitute Dynkin diagrams a generalization of the Weyl group $\mathcal{W}_{\mathfrak{g}}$ acts on the ADHM solutions. Here we work with the case $\mathfrak{g}=\mathfrak{sl}_{n+1}$ and treat this group as a group of electro-magnetic Seiberg-like dualities (we call them Weyl mutations) on the respective quiver gauge theories. We lift it to the case of higher representations associated to rectangular Young diagrams. An action of Weyl mutations on the BPS Yangian algebra is also discussed.

### [2] [Quantum Harish-Chandra isomorphism for the double affine Hecke algebra of $GL_n$](https://arxiv.org/abs/2309.00823)
*Joshua Jeishing Wen*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 该论文证明了在一般参数条件下，Varagnolo和Vasserot的量子径向部分映射给出了GL_n的球面双仿射Hecke代数与Jordan定义的量子化乘法箭图簇之间的同构。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究球面双仿射Hecke代数与量子化乘法箭图簇之间的关系，建立这两个重要数学结构之间的精确对应，深化对表示论和几何表示论的理解。

Method: 使用Varagnolo和Vasserot提出的量子径向部分映射，在一般参数条件下证明该映射构成同构。方法涉及表示论、代数几何和量子化技术。

Result: 证明了对于一般参数，量子径向部分映射确实给出了GL_n的球面双仿射Hecke代数与量子化乘法箭图簇之间的同构。

Conclusion: 该结果建立了球面双仿射Hecke代数与量子化乘法箭图簇之间的深刻联系，为相关领域的进一步研究提供了重要工具和理论基础。

Abstract: We prove that for generic parameters, the quantum radial parts map of Varagnolo and Vasserot gives an isomorphism between the spherical double affine Hecke algebra of $GL_n$ and a quantized multiplicative quiver variety, as defined by Jordan.

</details>

### [3] [Noncommutative Minkowski integral inequality and a unitary categorification criterion for fusion rings](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.13490)
*Junhwi Lim*

Main category: math.OA

TL;DR: 该论文证明了迹von Neumann代数交换平方的非交换Minkowski积分不等式，并推导出四重图作为多矩阵代数交换平方包含图的可实现性必要条件，以及基于环（特别是融合环）的酉范畴化准则。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究非交换Minkowski积分不等式在迹von Neumann代数交换平方中的推广，探索多矩阵代数交换平方的包含图结构，为基于环（特别是融合环）的酉范畴化提供理论工具。

Method: 证明迹von Neumann代数交换平方的非交换Minkowski积分不等式，利用该不等式分析四重图作为多矩阵代数交换平方包含图的可实现性条件。

Result: 成功证明了非交换Minkowski积分不等式，得到了四重图作为多矩阵代数交换平方包含图的可实现性必要条件，并推导出基于环（特别是融合环）的酉范畴化准则。

Conclusion: 该工作建立了非交换Minkowski积分不等式与交换平方结构之间的联系，为基于环的酉范畴化提供了新的判别准则，在算子代数和范畴理论中具有重要应用价值。

Abstract: We prove a noncommutative analogue of Minkowski's integral inequality for commuting squares of tracial von Neumann algebras. The inequality implies a necessary condition for a quadruple of graphs to be realized as inclusion graphs of a commuting square of multi-matrix algebras. As a corollary, we obtain a unitary categorification criterion for based rings, in particular, fusion rings.

</details>

### [4] [From affine algebraic racks to Leibniz algebras and Yang-Baxter operators](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.15909)
*Luc Ta*

Main category: math.AG

TL;DR: 该论文引入了代数架的概念，作为代数群的类似物，并构建了从仿射代数架到左右Leibniz代数的函子，恢复了线性代数群的李代数结构，并利用架方案构造了Yang-Baxter算子。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 解决Loday提出的问题，构建代数群在架理论中的类似物，将代数群理论扩展到更一般的代数结构，并建立与Leibniz代数、Yang-Baxter方程的联系。

Method: 引入代数架作为方案范畴中的点架对象，构造从仿射代数架到左右Leibniz代数的函子，研究坐标代数和Leibniz代数的性质，利用架方案函子化构造Yang-Baxter算子。

Result: 成功构建了兼容闭子架和理想的函子，恢复了线性代数群的李代数结构，建立了代数架与Leibniz代数、代数Lie架的联系，并在各种范畴中构造了(共)非退化Yang-Baxter算子。

Conclusion: 代数架为代数群提供了自然的类似物，建立的函子连接了代数架理论与Leibniz代数理论，架方案为构造Yang-Baxter算子提供了系统方法，扩展了代数结构的研究框架。

Abstract: We introduce analogues of algebraic groups called algebraic racks, which are pointed rack objects in the category of schemes over a ground field. Addressing a problem of Loday, we construct functors assigning left and right Leibniz algebras to affine algebraic racks. These functors are compatible with closed subracks and ideals, and they recover the Lie algebras of linear algebraic groups (via conjugation quandles) and the Leibniz algebras of algebraic Lie racks. We also study properties of coordinate algebras and Leibniz algebras of affine algebraic racks. Finally, we use rack schemes to functorially construct (co-)nondegenerate Yang-Baxter operators in various categories.

</details>

### [5] [A lifting theorem for Grothendieck-Verdier categories](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.14812)
*Max Demirdilek*

Main category: math.CT

TL;DR: 该论文研究了如何将Grothendieck-Verdier结构从目标范畴提升到源范畴，使得保守的lax幺半函子成为Frobenius线性分配函子，并应用于Hopf幺半子、Hopf代数胚等结构的模范畴。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究保守的lax幺半函子从闭幺半范畴到Grothendieck-Verdier范畴时，如何通过附加结构使得目标范畴的Grothendieck-Verdier结构能够提升到源范畴，从而扩展这类结构在各种代数结构中的应用。

Method: 在保守的lax幺半函子上识别额外的结构条件，使得Grothendieck-Verdier结构能够提升；构造严格的2-等价关系，将Grothendieck-Verdier范畴的2-范畴与带否定的线性分配范畴的2-范畴联系起来，并扩展到辫子设置。

Result: 建立了提升定理，恢复了Hopf幺半子和Hopf代数胚模范畴继承Grothendieck-Verdier结构的条件；刻画了Grothendieck-Verdier范畴内(交换)代数的双模、模和局部模范畴何时具有此类结构；结果适用于量子代数、smash积代数、斜群代数和Lie-Rinehart代数的包络代数。

Conclusion: 通过识别保守lax幺半函子的附加结构，成功实现了Grothendieck-Verdier结构的提升，建立了范畴间的严格2-等价关系，为多种代数结构的模范畴提供了统一的Grothendieck-Verdier结构理论框架。

Abstract: We identify additional structure on a conservative lax monoidal functor from a closed monoidal category $\mathcal{C}$ to a Grothendieck-Verdier category $\mathcal{D}$, such that the Grothendieck-Verdier structure of $\mathcal{D}$ lifts to $\mathcal{C}$ and the functor becomes Frobenius linearly distributive. As an application, we recover and extend conditions under which modules over Hopf monads and Hopf algebroids inherit Grothendieck-Verdier structures. We also characterize when categories of bimodules, modules, and local modules over (commutative) algebras internal to a Grothendieck-Verdier category admit such structures. Our results apply to quantales, smash product algebras, skew group algebras, and enveloping algebras of Lie-Rinehart algebras.
  For applications of the lifting theorem, we construct a strict $2$-equivalence between a $2$-category of Grothendieck-Verdier categories and one of linearly distributive categories with negation, and extend this $2$-equivalence to the braided setting.

</details>
