### [1] [A new new coproduct on quantum loop algebras](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.01130)
*Andrei Neguţ*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 本文在一般量子环形代数上定义了余乘法结构，推广了$U_q(\widehat{\mathfrak{g}})$的Drinfeld-Jimbo余乘法，并利用该构造证明了$R$-矩阵的有理性、$q$-特征标的可乘性以及theta级数的多项式性等基本表示性质。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 量子环形代数推广了量子仿射李代数$U_q(\widehat{\mathfrak{g}})$，包含量子Kac-Moody李代数的仿射化、箭图的K-理论Hall代数以及环三维Calabi-Yau流形的BPS代数等重要实例，但一般量子环形代数上缺乏统一的余乘法结构，限制了其表示理论的发展。

Method: 在一般量子环形代数上定义了余乘法（coproduct），该余乘法在$U_q(\widehat{\mathfrak{g}})$的特殊情形下退化回Drinfeld-Jimbo余乘法。

Result: 证明了该余乘法结构下，量子环形代数的$R$-矩阵具有有理性、$q$-特征标满足可乘性、theta级数满足多项式性。

Conclusion: 本文建立的余乘法为一般量子环形代数的表示理论提供了统一框架，并验证了若干核心性质在该框架下仍然成立。

Abstract: Quantum loop algebras generalize $U_q(\widehat{\mathfrak{g}})$ for simple Lie algebras $\mathfrak{g}$, and they include examples such as quantum affinizations of Kac-Moody Lie algebras, K-theoretic Hall algebras of quivers, and BPS algebras for toric Calabi-Yau threefolds. In the present paper, we define a coproduct on general quantum loop algebras, which coincides with the Drinfeld-Jimbo coproduct in the particular case of $U_q(\widehat{\mathfrak{g}})$. We use our construction to prove fundamental facts about representations of quantum loop algebras, such as the rationality of $R$-matrices, multiplicativity of $q$-characters, and polynomiality of theta series.

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Method: 首先引入并研究带左右余除法的非余结合双幺半群，建立其与左右Hopf余拟群的联系；然后定义非余结合双幺半群的扭曲并证明其可诱导Hopf余拟群结构的形变；最后将理论应用于S⁷球面相关的Hopf余拟群构造新例子。

Conclusion: 成功将Drinfeld扭曲理论推广至Hopf余拟群，给出了系统的形变框架，并通过S⁷球面实例展示了理论的有效性。

Abstract: In this paper, we develop a general theory of twist deformations for Hopf coquasigroups in a symmetric monoidal category. To this end, we first introduce and study non-coassociative bimonoids endowed with left and right codivisions, and establish their connection with left and right Hopf coquasigroups. Next, motivated by the classical theory of Drinfeld twists for Hopf algebras, we define twists for non-coassociative bimonoids and prove that they induce deformations of Hopf coquasigroup structures through suitable modifications of the coproduct. In particular, we obtain explicit deformation procedures for right and left Hopf coquasigroups and analyze the corresponding antipodes. Finally, we apply the general theory to construct nontrivial examples arising from Hopf coquasigroups associated with the sphere ${\sf S}^7$, obtaining new examples of twisted Hopf coquasigroups that are neither commutative nor cocommutative.

TL;DR: 本文研究了量子场论中单尺度费曼积分的解析积分方法，构建了迭代积分和嵌套求和的μ-扩展，并分析了其函数空间和代数结构。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 在量子场论的微扰计算中，单尺度费曼积分的解析积分需要在特殊函数类中进行，这些函数是多对数函数的连续推广。为了更系统地处理这些积分并扩展其适用范围，需要引入μ-扩展的概念。

Method: 构造了迭代积分（包括多对数、Nielsen积分、循环字母、二次型字母和平方根字母）及其关联嵌套求和的μ-扩展；提供封闭解或算法；研究了μ-扩展函数空间的代数结构。

Result: 除平方根字母和含中心二项式系数的求和情况外，μ-扩展映射到同一函数空间的多项式形式，并保留了Hopf代数结构（(拟)洗牌积）。平方根字母情况下则导致更高阶的超越函数。

These integrals are solutions of first-order factorizing differential equations. They are related to specific nested sums via the Mellin transform and their expansions around $x=0$. We construct the $μ$-extensions of these iterated integrals and the associated nested sums. In all other cases the $μ$-extension preserves the Hopf algebra structure implied by the (quasi)shuffle product, by supplementing $μ$ to the ground field.

### [5] [Representation Theory of Solitons](https://arxiv.org/abs/2408.11045)
*Clay Cordova,Nicholas Holfester,Kantaro Ohmori*

Main category: hep-th

TL;DR: 本文为二维量子场论中的孤子发展了一种基于“条带代数”的表示理论，该代数由非可逆对称性（融合范畴）及其在边界条件上的作用（模范畴）定义，通过三维Drinfeld中心TQFT量子化得到。研究表明条带代数是C*-弱Hopf代数，其表示范畴为酉融合范畴，可用于分析孤子散射中的简并性和选择定则，并适用于粒子物理、共形场论及凝聚态系统中的边界问题。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Method: 基于非可逆对称性（融合范畴C）及其对边界条件的模作用（模范畴M），定义“条带代数”Str_C(M)。通过量化三维Drinfeld中心TQFT在带角空间流形上的结构，证明条带代数是C*-弱Hopf代数。利用箭图（quiver）表示方法，将真空视为节点、孤子视为箭头，系统分析表示范畴。

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
