### [1] [PBW bases and centralisers for the $q$-Onsager algebra](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.10097)
*Haoran Zhu*

Main category: math-ph

TL;DR: 本文证明在任意域且q非单位根时，Baseilhac-Kolb根向量构成q-Onsager代数的PBW基，无需超越假设，并建立了十二个PBW基，解决了Terwilliger的四个猜想及Baseilhac-Belliard的一个猜想。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 移除此前对PBW基存在的超越性假设，解决关于q-Onsager代数结构的若干公开猜想。

Method: 通过代数方法，在任意域上系统构造并验证Baseilhac-Kolb根向量的PBW基性质，并研究交替中心扩张的标量中心特化下的持久性，以及负交替子代数的中心化子结构。

Result: 1) 去除了超越假设，证明所有正根序下根向量构成PBW基；2) 建立了十二个交替生成元下的PBW基；3) 证明了这些基在标量中心特化下保持；4) 确定了负交替子代数和第一个虚交替生成元的中心化子；5) 证明了四个单族交替多项式子代数是极大交换的。

Conclusion: 解决了Terwilliger的四个猜想和Baseilhac-Belliard的一个猜想（特征≠2时），完善了q-Onsager代数的PBW基理论。

Abstract: We prove that, over an arbitrary field and whenever $q$ is not a root of unity, the Baseilhac--Kolb root vectors form a PBW basis of the $q$-Onsager algebra for every total order on the positive roots of $\widehat{\mathfrak{sl}}_2$. This removes the previous transcendence hypothesis. We establish twelve PBW bases in the alternating generators and show that they persist under arbitrary scalar central specialisation of the alternating central extension. We determine the centraliser of the negative alternating subalgebra and that of the first imaginary alternating generator, and deduce that the four single-family alternating polynomial subalgebras are maximal commutative. Together, these results settle four conjectures of Terwilliger and, in characteristic different from $2$, a conjecture of Baseilhac and Belliard.

### [2] [Bockstein braiding statistics](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.02280)
*Po-Shen Hsin,Yu-An Chen*

Main category: quant-ph

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种名为"Bockstein辫子统计"的通用构造，用于描述p维和q维激发在d=p+q+1空间维度中的相互统计，通过4N步微观幺正过程测量贝里相位，适用于任意N的Z_N融合和所有激发维度，并证明该统计与Bockstein同态相关，可用于诊断混合反常。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 辫子现象是量子物理中拓扑的重要体现，但现有的相互辫子统计仅适用于d=p+q+2维空间。作者希望在相邻维度d=p+q+1中建立一种通用的相互统计构造，以统一描述各种维度激发之间的统计行为。

Method: 提出一种基于4N步微观幺正过程的通用构造，利用晶格上的局域激发算子构建，测量一个激发与另一个激发的N重融合结的链接所积累的贝里相位。该过程适用于任意N的Z_N融合和任意维度p,q的激发。

Result: 建立了该不变量的量子化和双线性性质，证明了其场论响应由Bockstein同态控制。在(1+1)D自旋链中展示了非平凡Bockstein辫子相位证实了自旋翻转对称性与最近邻CZ对称性之间的混合反常。构建了(2+1)D和(3+1)D晶格模拟，发现了新的反常对称对。

Conclusion: 非平凡Bockstein辫子统计排除了完全对称有能隙相的存���，阻碍了两种激发的同时凝聚，并意味着高形式对称性的分数量子化，为研究拓扑激发统计和对称性反常提供了统一框架。

Abstract: Braiding phenomena, from the charge-flux Aharonov-Bohm effect to anyonic statistics in fractional quantum Hall systems, are paradigmatic manifestations of topology in quantum physics. Ordinary mutual braiding between $p$- and $q$-dimensional excitations occurs in $d=p+q+2$ spatial dimensions. In this work, we introduce a universal construction of mutual statistics in the adjacent dimension $d=p+q+1$, applicable to excitations obeying $\mathbb Z_N$ fusion for arbitrary $N$ and all excitation dimensions $p$ and $q$. The corresponding invariant is the Berry phase accumulated in a simple $4N$-step microscopic unitary process built from local excitation operators on lattices. This process measures the linking of one excitation with the $N$-fold fusion junction of the other, encompassing particle-particle statistics in one dimension, particle-loop statistics in two dimensions, and loop-loop or particle-membrane statistics in three dimensions. We establish the quantization and bilinearity of the invariant and show that its field-theory response is governed by the Bockstein homomorphism, motivating the name Bockstein braiding statistics. Interpreting the excitation operators as open symmetry operators turns the same invariant into a direct microscopic diagnostic of mixed anomalies between symmetries. We demonstrate this diagnostic in a (1+1)D spin chain, where the nontrivial Bockstein braiding phase proves the mixed anomaly between the spin-flip symmetry $\prod X$ and the nearest-neighbor controlled-$Z$ symmetry $\prod CZ$. We construct explicit (2+1)D and (3+1)D lattice analogs, yielding new anomalous symmetry pairs, and apply the framework to strongly coupled (3+1)D continuum gauge theories. Nontrivial Bockstein braiding rules out a fully symmetric gapped phase, obstructs simultaneous condensation of the two excitations, and implies fractionalization of higher-form symmetries.

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 受群论中"群的自同构群平凡当且仅当阶为1或2"这一基本事实启发，研究者希望在更广泛的代数结构——斜纽带中探讨类似结论是否成立。

Result: 某些族类的斜纽带满足"平凡自同构群当且仅当阶为1或2"；且对任意奇素数p，存在阶为2p³的斜纽带具有平凡自同构群。

Conclusion: 斜纽带结构与群结构在平凡自同构群的性质上既有相似性（某些族类中一致），也有差异性（存在更大阶数的平凡自同构群斜纽带）。

Abstract: It is a simple fact that a group has a trivial automorphism group if and only if it is of order $1$ or $2$. We prove that the same holds for certain families of skew braces, and given any odd prime $p$, we construct a skew brace of order $2p^3$ that has a trivial automorphism group.

Method: 引入转置表示（transposed presentation），利用高斯分解与扭曲反射方程的交互作用，在R矩阵框架内推导Drinfeld型电流表示。

Abstract: We study twisted Yangians associated with the split symmetric pairs of types BI, CI and DI. As a consequence, we establish the isomorphism between the $R$-matrix and Drinfeld presentations of these twisted Yangians conjectured by Lu, Wang and Zhang. As a byproduct, we obtain a tensor product decomposition of $X^{\mathrm{tw}}(\mathfrak{g}_N)$ into the twisted Yangian in the Drinfeld presentation and a polynomial ring in countably many central variables. ### [7] [Quotients of strongly graded quasi-Hopf algebras](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.14381)

Main category: math.RA

TL;DR: 本文对强G-分次拟Hopf代数的拟Hopf理想进行了完全分类，建立了其与正规子群N及G/N-不变理想K之间的双射对应，并推广到具有拟Hopf收缩或交叉积提升的特殊情形，应用于无限分次、有限中性分量及非平凡重结合子的共中心阿贝尔扩张。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 拟Hopf代数是Hopf代数的重要推广，其理想结构对于理解量子群和共中心扩张等代数结构至关重要。本文旨在建立强G-分次拟Hopf代数中拟Hopf理想的完全分类，填补该领域理论空白。

Method: 通过建立拟Hopf理想I与正规子群N及G/N-不变拟Hopf理想K之间的双射对应，其中K是H_N中的理想且其像在分次映射下包含k[N]的增广理想。进一步针对具有拟Hopf收缩或交叉积提升的特殊情形进行精细化分类。

We further improve this correspondence for particular classes of quasi-Hopf ideals, such as those that have an associated quasi-Hopf retraction, or those whose lifted quotient is a crossed product.
