  <summary>Details</summary>

Method: 通过对一类满足特定条件（Coulomb支存在源/汇室，且BPS箭图中度数大于2的节点全为源或全为汇）的4d N=2超共形场论，提出Kontsevich-Soibelman算子的细化版本。

Result: 该细化算子的迹与Macdonald指标相关；对任意单李代数g的(A1,g) Argyres-Douglas理论，给出了Macdonald指标的闭式猜想表达式。

Conclusion: 本文为建立BPS结构与超共形指标之间的深层联系提供了新途径，并给出了具体理论的指标闭式表达。

We present strong evidence that the trace of this refined operator is related to the Macdonald index of the theory. In particular, we conjecture closed form expressions for the Macdonald indices of the $(A_1,\mathfrak{g})$ Argyres-Douglas theories for any simply-laced Lie algebra $\mathfrak{g}$.

### [2] [Bridging 4D QFTs and 2D VOAs via 3D high-temperature EFTs](https://arxiv.org/abs/2409.18130)
*Arash Arabi Ardehali,Mykola Dedushenko,Dongmin Gang,Mikhail Litvinov*

Main category: hep-th

TL;DR: 本文通过Di Pietro-Komargodski型超对称有效场论技术，研究了(A₁,A₂ₙ) Argyres-Douglas理论在r-twisted圆约化下的高温极限（特别是高叶极限），发现第二叶的结果是Gang-Kim-Stubbs族的3d 𝒩=2超对称增强的rank-0理论，其边界支持Virasoro最小模型VOA M(2,2n+3)，拓扑扭变后得到由M(2,2n+3)模张量范畴控制的非幺正TQFTs；其他叶的高温极限给出其幺正或非幺正的Galois共轭，从而为4d 𝒩=2 SCFT与2d VOA之间的对应关系提供了通过插值3d EFT的四超荷视角。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 超共形指标的高温极限（尤其是在高叶上）通常能捕捉理论的普适信息。对于具有分数r-荷的4d 𝒩=2超共形场论，存在一种特殊的高叶高温极限，可以捕捉来自r-twisted圆约化的三维拓扑量子场论（TQFT）的数据，这些TQFT与4d SCFT的VOA紧密相关。本文旨在系统研究这类高温极限。

Method: 应用Di Pietro-Komargodski型超对称有效场论技术，对(A₁,A₂ₙ) Argyres-Douglas理论进行r-twisted圆约化，利用其具有显式𝒩=1超对称的Maruyoshi-Song拉格朗日量，分析不同叶上的高温极限行为。

Result: 第二叶结果对应Gang-Kim-Stubbs族的3d 𝒩=2超对称增强rank-0理论（带单极超势），边界支持Virasoro最小模型VOA M(2,2n+3)；拓扑扭变后得到由M(2,2n+3) MTC控制的非幺正TQFTs。其他叶的高温极限给出其幺正或非幺正的Galois共轭。

Conclusion: 该工作开辟了通过插值3d EFT来理解4d 𝒩=2 SCFT与2d VOA之间对应关系的四超荷新视角。同时，提供了从4d超对称增强到3d超对称增强的系统方法，以及用3d QFT处理与4d 𝒩=2 SCFT相关的各种MTC的Galois轨道的工具。

Abstract: The high-temperature limit of the superconformal index, especially on higher sheets, often captures useful universal information about a theory. In 4d $\mathcal{N}=2$ superconformal field theories with fractional r-charges, there exists a special notion of high-temperature limit on higher sheets that captures data of three-dimensional topological quantum field theories arising from r-twisted circle reduction. These TQFTs are closely tied with the VOA of the 4d SCFT. We study such high-temperature limits. More specifically, we apply Di~Pietro-Komargodski type supersymmetric effective field theory techniques to r-twisted circle reductions of $(A_1,A_{2n})$ Argyres-Douglas theories, leveraging their Maruyoshi-Song Lagrangian with manifest $\mathcal{N}=1$ supersymmetry. The result on the second sheet is the Gang-Kim-Stubbs family of 3d $\mathcal{N}=2$ SUSY enhancing rank-$0$ theories with monopole superpotentials, whose boundary supports the Virasoro minimal model VOAs $M(2,2n+3)$. Upon topological twist, they give non-unitary TQFTs controlled by the $M(2,2n+3)$ modular tensor category (MTC). The high-temperature limit on other sheets yields their unitary or non-unitary Galois conjugates. This opens up the prospect of a broader four-supercharge perspective on the celebrated correspondence between 4d $\mathcal{N}=2$ SCFTs and 2d VOAs via interpolating 3d EFTs. Several byproducts follow, including a systematic approach to 3d SUSY enhancement from 4d SUSY enhancement, and a 3d QFT handle on Galois orbits of various MTCs associated with 4d $\mathcal{N}=2$ SCFTs.

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

We show that $\mathfrak{dmr}_0$ with the infinitesimal hexagon equation $[x,\varphi(-x,-y,x)]+[y,\varphi(-x-y,y)]=0$ injects to the symmetric Kashiwara-Vergne Lie algebra $\mathfrak{krv}^{\mathrm{sym}}_2$. Abstract: We show that the wheel classes in the Kontsevich graph complex $GC_d$ admit representatives supported on graphs with only $3$- and $4$-valent vertices. This verifies that Merkulov's low-valence conjecture holds for the wheel classes.

### [6] [Topological Field Theories and the Algebraic Structures of the Two-Sphere](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20846)
*Chris Li*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 本文研究了三维有向 bordism 范畴中 S² 的两种表示，定义了 P-幺半群和 L-幺半群，它们都是交换 Frobenius 幺半群，并携带由闭可约素三维流形标记的额外结构，两者等价并满足“legs relations”，在代数限制下这些素结构简化为素单位乘法。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 为了编码三维有向 bordism 范畴中 S² 的代数结构，并研究通过拓扑场论后产生的代数结构。

Method: 在三维有向 bordism 范畴中给出 S² 的两种表示，定义 P-幺半群和 L-幺半群，通过 legs relations 刻画素结构的约束，并引入 ∞-operad 概念。

Result: P-幺半群和 L-幺半群等价，legs relations 对素自同态施加强约束，使其退化为素单位乘法，并提出了包含 ∞-little 3-cube operad 作为子 operad 的 ∞-operad。

Conclusion: 素结构在代数限制下变得极其简单，为三维 TFT 的分类提供了新的代数框架。

Abstract: We give two presentations for bordisms of $S^2$ in the 3-dimensional oriented bordism category $\operatorname{Cob}(3) $, encoding the algebraic structures on $S^2$. After passing through topological field theories, we define two kinds of monoids which we call P-monoids and L-monoids. In addition to both being commutative Frobenius monoids, P-monoids are equipped with a class of endomorphisms while L-monoids are equipped with a class of unit morphisms, all of which are labelled by closed oriented irreducible prime 3-manifolds. They turn out to be equivalent. The new prime structures satisfy some countable relations with the commutative Frobenius structure, the most notable of which we call "legs relations." We then restrict to the setting of algebras and show that the legs relations place strong constraints on the new prime endomorphisms which forces them to act by multiplications by prime units, rendering the additional prime structures remarkably simple. We also propose an $\infty$-operad which encodes these prime structures and contains the $\infty$-little 3-cube operad as a sub-operad.% We briefly discuss the relations between P/L-algebras and J-algebras which classify 3-dimensional TFTs.

</details>
