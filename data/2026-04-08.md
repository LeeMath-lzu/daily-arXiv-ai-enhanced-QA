### [1] [Algebraic approach to quantum gravity IV: applications](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.06118)
*Shahn Majid*

Main category: gr-qc

TL;DR: 本文介绍了量子时空和量子黎曼几何在理论物理中的应用，包括量子引力单格点模型中时空曲率涨落真空能的计算、量子时空推导Kaluza-Klein假设、晶格变分法中的精确守恒Noether荷，以及经典和量子测地线的新理论。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 为理论物理学提供一个相对自包含的量子时空和量子黎曼几何应用介绍，展示这些数学工具如何解决量子引力、广义相对论和量子力学中的基本问题。

Method: 采用量子时空和量子黎曼几何的数学框架，结合单格点量子引力模型、晶格变分法、量子测地线理论等方法，研究时空的量子性质。

Result: 成功计算了量子引力单格点模型中时空曲率涨落的真空能，从量子时空推导出Kaluza-Klein假设，获得了晶格变分法中的精确守恒Noether荷，发展了适用于广义相对论中黑洞情况的一般协变量子力学理论，并在4点星形上发现了欧几里得量子引力的相变。

Conclusion: 量子时空和量子黎曼几何为理论物理提供了强大的数学工具，能够统一处理量子引力、广义相对论和量子力学中的基本问题，未来有望解决更多开放性问题。

Abstract: We provide a relatively self-contained introduction to the application of quantum spacetime and quantum Riemannian geometry to theoretical physics. Recent successes include calculation of the vacuum energy of spacetime curvature fluctuations in a single-plaquette model of quantum gravity, derivation of the Kaluza-Klein ansatz as a consequence of quantum spacetime, exactly conserved Noether charges from variational calculus on a lattice, and a new theory of classical and quantum geodesics. The latter leads to a theory of generally covariant quantum mechanics applicable in General Relativity with intriguing first results for the case of a black-hole. We discuss several open problems past and present, and how they might be addressed going forward. New results include a phase transition for Euclidean quantum gravity on a 4-pointed star.

</details>

### [2] [A multigraph approach to confusability in quantum channels](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.06072)
*Sk Asfaq Hossain,Angshuman Bhattacharya*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 该论文提出了一种新的量子信道混淆性表示方法——量子混淆性多重图，将输出信息纳入图形结构中，并建立了量子多重图理论。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统量子信道混淆性图仅使用单边表示，无法充分捕捉输出信息。为了更全面地描述量子信道的混淆特性，需要开发一种能够整合输出信息的图形表示方法。

Method: 引入量子混淆性多重图概念，从Weaver的量子关系角度出发，建立量子多重图理论，并探索其量子图论性质。

Result: 通过计算混淆性多重图中两个顶点之间的边数，可以恢复传统的单边混淆性图。建立了量子多重图的理论框架，并提供了量子混淆性多重图的充要条件。

Conclusion: 量子混淆性多重图为量子信道混淆性提供了更丰富的图形表示，建立了量子多重图的理论基础，并给出了量子混淆性多重图的完整特征化条件。

Abstract: We introduce a new approach to confusability in a quantum channel, namely quantum confusability multigraph, which incorporates the output information into the graphical structure. By``counting" the edges between two vertices of this confusability multigraph, one recovers the traditional confusability ``single-edged" graph of the channel. With this physical motivation, we therefore develop a theory of quantum multigraphs from Weaver's quantum relations point of view and explore its quantum graph theoretic properties. Finally, we provide a necessary and sufficient condition characterizing those quantum multigraphs that arise as quantum confusability multigraphs.

</details>


### [3] [A geometrical description of untwisted 3d Dijkgraaf-Witten TQFT with defects](https://arxiv.org/abs/2410.18049)
*João Faria Martins,Catherine Meusburger*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 该论文提出了3D无扭曲Dijkgraaf-Witten理论的简单几何构造，包含所有余维度的缺陷，作为从缺陷配边范畴到有限维复向量空间范畴的对称幺半函子。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 为3D Dijkgraaf-Witten理论提供一种简单、几何且显式的构造方法，包含所有余维度的缺陷，避免使用三角剖分上的状态求和或高阶范畴的图式计算。

Method: 使用同伦论方法构造对称幺半函子：将缺陷曲面映射到规范群胚的表示，将缺陷配边映射到群胚的纤维化跨度和边界表示之间的交织算子。基于基本群胚和丛等几何量构建。

Result: 成功构造了3D无扭曲Dijkgraaf-Witten理论的显式缺陷TQFT，能够计算具体例子，并展示了该理论的2D部分如何简单描述Kitaev量子双模型中所有余维度的缺陷。

Conclusion: 该工作提供了一种几何、显式且无需复杂计算的3D缺陷TQFT构造方法，为理解拓扑量子场论中的缺陷结构提供了新视角，并建立了与Kitaev量子双模型的联系。

Abstract: We give a simple, geometric and explicit construction of 3d untwisted Dijkgraaf-Witten theory with defects of all codimensions. It is given as a symmetric monoidal functor from a defect cobordism category into the category of finite-dimensional complex vector spaces. The objects of this category are oriented stratified surfaces and its morphisms are equivalence classes of stratified cobordisms, both labelled with higher categorical data. This TQFT is constructed in terms of geometric quantities such as fundamental groupoids and bundles and requires neither state sums on triangulations nor diagrammatic calculi for higher categories. It is obtained from a functor that assigns to each defect surface a representation of a gauge groupoid and to each defect cobordism a fibrant span of groupoids and an intertwiner between the groupoid representations at its boundary. It is constructed by homotopy theoretic methods and allows for an explicit computation of examples. In particular, we show how the 2d part of this defect TQFT gives a simple description of defects of all codimensions in Kitaev's quantum double model.

</details>

### [4] [Root lattices and invariant series for plumbed 3-manifolds](https://arxiv.org/abs/2405.14972)
*Allison H. Moore,Nicola Tarasca*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 该论文研究了与根格相关的3维流形不变量形式级数，扩展了BPS q-级数，证明了其唯一性并展示了在特定流形上的显式计算。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究管状3维流形与根格相关的不变量形式级数，扩展和精化现有的BPS q-级数理论，探索其在数学物理中的深层结构。

Method: 通过研究形式级数的不变性，证明其在适当意义下的唯一性，并展示其可分解为相关级数的平均值，这些级数在管状树的五种Neumann变换下保持不变量。

Result: 证明了BPS q-级数的唯一性，展示了其在Brieskorn球面和非Seifert流形上的显式计算，为相关数学结构提供了具体实例。

Conclusion: 该研究成功扩展了管状3维流形的不变量理论，建立了与根格相关的形式级数框架，为后续的数学物理研究提供了新的工具和视角。

Abstract: We study formal series which are invariants of plumbed 3-manifolds twisted by root lattices. These series extend the BPS $q$-series $\widehat{Z}(q)$ recently defined in Gukov-Pei-Putrov-Vafa, Gukov-Manolescu, Park, and further refined in Ri. We show that the series $\widehat{Z}(q)$ is unique in an appropriate sense and decomposes as the average of related series which are themselves invariant under the five Neumann moves amongst plumbing trees. Explicit computations are presented in the case of Brieskorn spheres and a non-Seifert manifold.

</details>

### [5] [Clones of pigmented words and realizations of special classes of monoids](https://arxiv.org/abs/2305.18115)
*Samuele Giraudo*

Main category: math.CO

TL;DR: 本文介绍了一种从幺半群范畴到克隆范畴的函子构造，通过考虑这些结构的商，构建了一个涉及组合对象的完整克隆层次结构，实现了幺半群的特殊类别的克隆表示。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 克隆作为操作代数的特化，能够描述允许变量重复的代数方程，是研究各种代数结构的强大工具。本文旨在通过函子构造建立幺半群与克隆之间的联系，从而为特定类型的幺半群提供克隆表示。

Method: 引入从幺半群范畴到克隆范畴的函子构造，获得的克隆涉及正整数上的单词，其中字母伴随幺半群元素。通过考虑这些结构的商，构建了一个涉及组合对象家族的完整克隆层次结构。

Result: 构建了一个完整的克隆层次结构，实现了已知和新颖的特殊幺半群类的克隆表示，包括左正则带、有界半格和正则带幺半群等代数结构。

Conclusion: 该函子构造提供了一种系统的方法，将幺半群转化为克隆结构，并通过商构造揭示了幺半群与组合对象之间的深刻联系，为研究代数结构的克隆表示开辟了新途径。

Abstract: Clones are specializations of operads forming powerful instruments to describe varieties of algebras wherein repeating variables are allowed in their equations. They allow us in this way to realize and study a large range of algebraic structures. A functorial construction from the category of monoids to the category of clones is introduced. The obtained clones involve words on positive integers where letters are accompanied by elements of a monoid. By considering quotients of these structures, we construct a complete hierarchy of clones involving some families of combinatorial objects. This provides clone realizations of some known and some new special classes of monoids as among others the variety of left-regular bands, bounded semilattices, and regular band monoids.

</details>

### [6] [Tensor-Hochschild complex](https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.14545)
*Slava Pimenov,Angel Toledo*

Main category: math.AG

TL;DR: 本文为单oidal dg-范畴构造了一个控制单oidal结构和底层dg-范畴变形的复形，在特殊情况下分别约化为Davydov-Yetter复形、计算operadic E2-上同调的复形和Gerstenhaber-Schack复形。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究单oidal dg-范畴的变形理论，为不同数学结构（如半单范畴、交换代数模范畴、结合双代数表示范畴）的变形问题提供统一框架。

Method: 构造一个复形来控制单oidal dg-范畴的变形，包括单oidal结构和底层dg-范畴本身的变形。通过在不同特殊情况下分析该复形，展示其与已知变形复形的关系。

Result: 1. 在半单范畴情况下，该复形约化为Davydov-Yetter复形；2. 在交换代数A的模范畴情况下，得到计算operadic E2-上同调的复形；3. 在结合双代数表示范畴情况下，恢复Gerstenhaber-Schack复形，并可推广到拟双代数。

Conclusion: 所构造的复形为单oidal dg-范畴的变形提供了统一理论框架，在不同特殊情况下与已知重要变形复形一致，展示了该构造的广泛适用性和理论价值。

Abstract: Let $(\mathcal{C}, \otimes)$ be a monoidal dg-category. We construct a complex controlling the deformation of the monoidal structure on $\mathcal{C}$ together with the deformation of the underlying dg-category itself. We show that in the case of a semisimple category $\mathcal{C}$ it reduces to the Davydov-Yetter complex. Furthermore, we study this complex in several special cases, in particular, in the case of the category of $A$-modules over a commutative algebra $A$ we obtain a complex computing operadic $E_2$-cohomology of $A$. And in the case of the category of representations of an associative bialgebra we recover the Gerstenhaber-Schack complex. In the latter case our construction can be considered as a generalization of the Gerstenhaber-Schack complex to quasi-bialgebras.

</details>
