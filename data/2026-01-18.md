### [1] [Twisted Cherednik spectrum as a $q,t$-deformation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.10500)
*A. Mironov,A. Morozov,A. Popolitov*

Main category: hep-th

TL;DR: 论文研究了扭曲Cherednik算子的共同本征函数，通过q→1极限分析，发现多项式本征函数形成简单集合，源于非零度的对称基态及其激发态，由弱组合枚举。这一模式在q≠1时作为变形继承，整个问题类似于典型的NP问题。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究扭曲Cherednik算子的本征函数结构，探索其在q→1极限下的简化形式，以及如何将这种简化模式推广到一般q值情况，揭示这类算子本征问题的数学结构特征。

Method: 首先在q→1极限下分析扭曲Cherednik算子的共同本征函数，研究多项式本征函数的集合结构。然后考虑q≠1时的变形情况，将极限下的模式继承到完整谱结构中。

Result: 发现多项式本征函数形成简单集合：源于非零度的对称基态，激发态由非对称多项式描述，用弱组合枚举。这一结构在q≠1时作为变形被继承，整个问题具有NP问题的特征：方程难解但解易验证。

Conclusion: 扭曲Cherednik算子的本征函数问题具有系统性的结构模式，从q→1极限的简化情况可以推广到一般变形情况，揭示了这类算子本征问题的数学本质和计算复杂性特征。

Abstract: The common eigenfunctions of the twisted Cherednik operators can be first analyzed in the limit of $q\longrightarrow 1$. Then, the polynomial eigenfunctions form a simple set originating from the symmetric ground state of non-vanishing degree and excitations over it, described by non-symmetric polynomials of higher degrees and enumerated by weak compositions. This pattern is inherited by the full spectrum at $q\neq 1$, which can be considered as a deformation. The whole story looks like a typical NP problem: the Cherednik equations are difficult to solve, but easy to check the solution once it is somehow found.

### [2] [Rational RG flow, extension, and Witt class](https://arxiv.org/abs/2412.08935)
*Ken Kikuchi*

Main category: hep-th

TL;DR: 该论文研究重整化群流中预模融合范畴的演化，发现隐藏的扩展顶点算子代数给出Witt等价类的唯一代表，并通过物理和数学验证支持这一猜想。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究重整化群流如何影响对称性结构，特别是预模融合范畴在流到有理共形场论时的演化，以及相关的数学结构变化。

Method: 通过澄清重整化群畴壁/界面或边界条件的数学（特别是范畴论）意义，分析隐藏的扩展顶点算子代数，建立Witt等价类代表。

Result: 发现隐藏扩展顶点算子代数给出唯一的完全各向异性代表，支持了数学猜想，并通过非/幺正最小模型、Wess-Zumino-Witten模型等具体例子验证。

Conclusion: 该框架建立了半整数条件，固定了红外共形维数模1/2，并导致双辫关系，成功解决了从E型最小模型到M(4,3)的流。

Abstract: Consider a renormalization group flow preserving a pre-modular fusion category $\mathcal S_1$. If it flows to a rational conformal field theory, the surviving symmetry $\mathcal S_1$ flows to a pre-modular fusion category $\mathcal S_2$ with monoidal functor $F:\mathcal S_1\to\mathcal S_2$. By clarifying mathematical (especially category theoretical) meaning of renormalization group domain wall/interface or boundary condition, we find the hidden extended vertex operator (super)algebra gives a unique (up to braided equivalence) completely $(\mathcal S_1\boxtimes\mathcal S_2)'$-anisotropic representative of the Witt equivalence class $[\mathcal S_1\boxtimes\mathcal S_2]$. The mathematical conjecture is supported physically, and passes various tests in concrete examples including non/unitary minimal models, and Wess-Zumino-Witten models. In particular, the conjecture holds beyond diagonal cosets.
  The picture also establishes the conjectured half-integer condition, which fixes infrared conformal dimensions mod $\frac12$. It further leads to the double braiding relation, namely braiding structures jump at conformal fixed points. As an application, we solve the flow from the $E$-type minimal model $(A_{10},E_6)\to M(4,3)$.

### [3] [Vertex operator algebra bundles on modular curves and their associated modular forms](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.10686)
*Daniel Barake,Owen Chuchman,Cameron Franc,Geoffrey Mason,Brett Nasserden*

Main category: math.NT

TL;DR: 该论文研究了椭圆模曲线上与顶点算子代数相关的向量丛，建立了V值拟模形式理论，并证明了其Hecke特征系统与标量值拟模形式相同。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究顶点算子代数在椭圆模曲线上的向量丛表示，旨在为研究顶点算子代数的特征函数提供理论基础，扩展经典拟模形式理论到V值情形。

Method: 通过顶点算子代数的截面模构造V值拟模形式空间Q(V)，赋予其双重拟顶点算子代数结构，引入提升算子和下降算子Λ，其中Λ的核对应V值模形式M(V)，建立M(V)与M⊗V的显式同构。

Result: 成功构建了V值拟模形式理论框架，证明了M(V)≅M⊗V的显式同构，并利用顶点算子的坐标不变性建立了M(V)的自然Hecke理论，发现其特征系统与标量值拟模形式的特征系统相同。

Conclusion: 该工作将经典拟模形式理论扩展到顶点算子代数背景，建立了完整的V值拟模形式理论，为研究顶点算子代数的特征函数提供了新的数学工具和理论基础。

Abstract: This paper describes the vector bundle on the elliptic modular curve that is associated to a vertex operator algebra $V$ (VOA) or more generally a quasi-vertex operator algebra (QVOA), with a view towards future applications aimed at studying the characters of VOAs. We explain how the modes of sections of $V$ give rise naturally to $V$-valued quasi-modular forms. The space $Q(V)$ of $V$-valued quasi-modular forms is endowed with the structure of a doubled QVOA, and in particular the algebra $Q$ of quasi-modular forms is itself a doubled QVOA. $Q(V)$ also admits a natural derivative operator arising from the connection on the bundle defined by $V$ and the modular derivative, which we call the raising operator. We introduce an associated lowering operator $Λ$ on $Q(V)$ having the property that the $V$-valued modular forms $M(V)\subseteq Q(V)$ are the kernel of $Λ$. This extends the classical theory of scalar-valued quasi-modular forms. We exhibit an explicit isomorphism of $M(V)$ with $M \otimes V$. Finally, the coordinate invariance of vertex operators implies that $M(V)$ has a natural Hecke theory, and we use this isomorphism to fully describe the Hecke eigensystems: they are the same as the systems of eigenvalues that arise from scalar-valued quasi-modular forms.

</details>

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究Borcherds代数的构造，特别是与Monster有限单群相关的Monstrous李代数$\mathfrak m_g$，探索这些代数在弦理论中的应用，并建立相应的李群理论。

Method: 通过综述性方法，总结Borcherds代数的构造过程，分析Carnahan构建的Monstrous李代数$\mathfrak m_g$，讨论其在异质弦理论紧致化模型中的出现，并概述将李群类比与这些李代数相关联的最新研究成果。

Result: 提供了Borcherds代数构造的全面概述，阐明了Monstrous李代数$\mathfrak m_g$的结构特性，展示了这些代数在弦理论物理模型中的具体应用，并总结了将李群理论扩展到这些特殊李代数的研究进展。

Conclusion: 该综述系统总结了Borcherds代数和Monstrous李代数的研究现状，指出了这些代数在弦理论中的重要性，同时提出了该领域尚未解决的一些开放性问题，为进一步研究提供了方向。

Abstract: We give an overview of the construction of Borcherds algebras, particularly the Monstrous Lie algebras $\mathfrak m_g$ constructed by Carnahan, where $g$ is an element of the Monster finite simple group. When $g$ is the identity element, $\mathfrak m_g$ is the Monster Lie algebra of Borcherds. We discuss the appearance of the $\mathfrak m_g$ in compactified models of the Heterotic String. We also summarize recent work on associating Lie group analogs to the Lie algebras $\mathfrak m_g$. We include a discussion of some open problems.
