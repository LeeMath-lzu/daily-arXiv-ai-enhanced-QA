### [1] [State integrals for the quantized $\operatorname{SL}_2(\mathbb{C})$ Chern-Simons invariant](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.05136)
*Calvin McPhail-Snyder*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 该论文展示了如何将作者之前定义的扭结不变量Z_N^ψ表达为在扭结补空间双曲结构参数空间上的围道积分和，这是研究量子不变量渐近行为的重要步骤。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 先前的工作定义了扭结不变量Z_N^ψ，它可以解释为Kasahev不变量的几何扭曲或SL(C)陈-西蒙斯不变量量子化。为了研究体积猜想预测的量子不变量渐近行为，需要建立该不变量的积分表示形式。

Method: 通过将离散状态和（涉及量子双对数）重新表达为在扭结补空间双曲结构参数空间上的围道积分和。具体来说，将Z_N^ψ表示为参数空间上多个围道积分的求和。

Result: 成功将Z_N^ψ表达为在扭结补空间双曲结构参数空间上的围道积分和。这种积分表示是确定量子不变量渐近行为的重要进展，为研究体积猜想提供了新工具。

Conclusion: 该论文建立了扭结不变量Z_N^ψ的积分表示，这是研究体积猜想和量子不变量渐近行为的关键步骤。虽然仍存在建立Z_N^ψ指数增长的障碍，但积分表示为进一步分析提供了重要基础。

Abstract: Previous work of the author and N. Reshetikhin defines an invariant $\operatorname{Z}_{N}^ψ(K, ρ, μ)$ of a knot $K$, a representation $ρ: π_{1}(S^{3} \setminus K) \to \operatorname{SL}_2(\mathbb{C})$, and a logarithm $μ$ of a meridian eigenvalue of $ρ$. It can be interpreted as a geometric twist of the Kasahev invariant or as a quantization of the $\operatorname{SL}(\mathbb{C})$ Chern-Simons invariant and is defined using a discrete state-sum involving quantum dilogarithms. In this paper we show how to express $\operatorname{Z}_{N}^ψ(K, ρ, μ)$ as a sum over contour integrals in a space parametrizing hyperbolic structures on the knot complement. Such integral presentations are an important step in determining the asymptotics of quantum invariants as predicted by the Volume Conjecture. We discuss this perspective and the remaining obstacles to establishing exponential growth of $\operatorname{Z}_{N}^ψ$.

### [2] [A supergroup series for knot complements](https://arxiv.org/abs/2508.10279)
*John Chae*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文引入了一个三变量级数不变量F_K(y,z,q)，用于与李超代数sl(2|1)相关的管道结补空间，是Ferrari-Putrov的sl(2|1)-级数不变量和Gukov-Manolescu二变量级数不变量的推广。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将李超代数sl(2|1)的理论应用于结理论，扩展现有的级数不变量框架，探索超代数结构带来的新特征，并为非半单Spin^c装饰TQFT提供证据。

Method: 引入三变量级数不变量F_K(y,z,q)，推导连接该不变量与闭3-流形不变量Ẑ(q)的手术公式，为特定无限族环面结找到合适的展开室并计算具体例子。

Result: 建立了F_K(y,z,q)与Ẑ(q)之间的手术关系，计算了特定环面结族的显式例子，发现超代数版本的不变量及其结果与GM级数相比具有独特特征，为非半单Spin^c装饰TQFT提供了证据。

Conclusion: sl(2|1)李超代数的三变量级数不变量成功扩展了现有理论框架，展示了超代数结构带来的新数学特征，为结理论和3-流形拓扑提供了新的工具和视角。

Abstract: We introduce a three variable series invariant $F_K (y,z,q)$ for plumbed knot complements associated with a Lie superalgebra $sl(2|1)$. The invariant is a generalization of the $sl(2|1)$-series invariant $\hat{Z}(q)$ for closed 3-manifolds introduced by Ferrari and Putrov and an extension of the two variable series invariant defined by Gukov and Manolescu (GM) to the Lie superalgebra. We derive a surgery formula relating $F_K (y,z,q)$ to $\hat{Z}(q)$ invariant. We find appropriate expansion chambers for certain infinite families of torus knots and compute explicit examples. Furthermore, we provide evidence for a non semisimple $Spin^c$ decorated TQFT from the three variable series. We observe that the super $F_K (y,z,q)$ itself and its results exhibit distinctive features compared to the GM series.

</details>
