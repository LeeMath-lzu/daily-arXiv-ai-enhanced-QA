### [1] [Towards a classification of graded unitary ${\mathcal W}_3$ algebras](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.15944)
*Christopher Beem,Harshal Kulkarni*

Main category: hep-th

TL;DR: 本文研究四维幺正性对通过SCFT/VOA对应从四维理论产生的W3顶点代数的约束，发现除了(3,q+4)极小模型的中心荷值外，其他所有值都与四维幺正性不兼容。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究四维幺正性（通过分级幺正性形式化）对可能从四维理论通过SCFT/VOA对应产生的W3顶点代数的约束条件，理解哪些代数与四维幺正性兼容。

Method: 假设R-滤过是关于顶点代数通常强生成元的基于权重的滤过，分析四维幺正性对W3顶点代数的约束，并与Drinfel'd-Sokolov约简和边界容许的sl3仿射流代数建立联系。

Result: 证明除了(3,q+4)极小模型的中心荷值外，所有其他中心荷值都与四维幺正性不兼容。这些代数恰好可以通过对边界容许的sl3仿射流代数进行主Drinfel'd-Sokolov约简得到，并与(A2,Aq) Argyres-Douglas理论相关联。

Conclusion: 四维幺正性严格限制了可能出现的W3顶点代数，只有那些与(3,q+4)极小模型相关的代数（对应(A2,Aq) Argyres-Douglas理论）满足幺正性条件，这为SCFT/VOA对应提供了重要的约束。

Abstract: We study constraints imposed by four-dimensional unitarity (formalised as graded unitarity in recent work by the first author) on possible ${\mathcal W}_3$ vertex algebras arising from four-dimensions via the SCFT/VOA correspondence. Under the assumption that the $\mathfrak{R}$-filtration is a weight-based filtration with respect to the usual strong generators of the vertex algebra, we demonstrate that all values of the central charge other than those of the $(3,q+4)$ minimal models are incompatible with four-dimensional unitarity. These algebras are precisely the ones that are realised by performing principal Drinfel'd--Sokolov reduction to boundary-admissible $\mathfrak{sl}_3$ affine current algebras; those affine algebras were singled out by a similar graded unitarity analysis in \cite{ArabiArdehali:2025fad}. Furthermore, these particular vertex algebras are known to be associated with the $(A_2,A_q)$ Argyres--Douglas theories.

### [2] [Drinfeld associators and Kashiwara-Vergne associators in higher genera](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.00473)
*Toyo Taniguchi*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 本文从Gonzalez-Drinfeld结合子直接构造了亏格g的Kashiwara-Vergne结合子，证明了特定框架选择下两种结合子的等价关系


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究亏格g的Kashiwara-Vergne结合子的构造问题，该结合子与Goldman-Turaev李双代数在带框架定向曲面上的形式化问题相关

Method: 基于Massuyeau在亏格0的工作，从Gonzalez-Drinfeld结合子直接构造亏格g的Kashiwara-Vergne结合子，框架由Gonzalez-Drinfeld结合子的选择决定

Result: 成功构造了亏格g的Kashiwara-Vergne结合子，在亏格1情况下证明了只有特定框架能够通过该构造实现

Conclusion: 建立了Gonzalez-Drinfeld结合子与Kashiwara-Vergne结合子之间的直接构造关系，揭示了框架选择在构造中的限制条件

Abstract: For $g\geq 0$, a genus $g$ Kashiwara-Vergne associator, introduced by Alekseev-Kawazumi-Kuno-Naef as a solution to the generalised KV equations in relation to the formality problem of the Goldman-Turaev Lie bialgebra on an oriented surface with a framing, is directly constructed from a genus $g$ analogue of a Drinfeld associator formulated by Gonzalez, which we call a Gonzalez-Drinfeld associator. The proof is based on Massuyeau's work in genus $0$. The framing is determined from the choice of a Gonzalez-Drinfeld associator, and in the case of genus $1$, we show that only particular framings are realised by our construction.

### [3] [Chevalley property and discriminant ideals of Cayley-Hamilton Hopf Algebras](https://arxiv.org/abs/2506.21879)
*Yimin Huang,Zhongkai Mi,Tiancheng Qi,Quanshui Wu*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 该论文研究了具有大中心Hopf子代数的仿射Hopf代数的Cayley-Hamilton结构，证明了其纤维代数模范畴的不可分解性，并给出了判别式理想零点的描述。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究具有大中心Hopf子代数的仿射Hopf代数的结构性质，特别是其Cayley-Hamilton Hopf代数结构，以及相关判别式理想的几何性质，推广Mi-Wu-Yakimov关于最低判别式理想的结果。

Method: 通过代数几何和表示论方法，利用仿射Hopf代数的中心子代数结构，研究Cayley-Hamilton Hopf代数的纤维代数模范畴，分析判别式理想的零点集与Chevalley性质的关系。

Result: 证明了具有大中心Hopf子代数的仿射Hopf代数可赋予Cayley-Hamilton结构；其纤维代数的有限维模范畴是恒等纤维代数模张量范畴的不可分解精确模范畴；给出了判别式理想零点的几何描述，并证明了最低判别式理想的水平与Frobenius-Perron维度的关系。

Conclusion: 该研究建立了具有大中心子代数的仿射Hopf代数的Cayley-Hamilton结构与判别式理想几何性质之间的联系，推广了相关结果，为理解这类代数的表示论和几何结构提供了新的视角。

Abstract: For any affine Hopf algebra $H$ which admits a large central Hopf subalgebra, $H$ can be endowed with a Cayley-Hamilton Hopf algebra structure in the sense of De Concini-Procesi-Reshetikhin-Rosso. The category of finite-dimensional modules over any fiber algebra of $H$ is proved to be an indecomposable exact module category over the tensor category of finite-dimensional modules over the identity fiber algebra $H/\mathfrak{m}_{\overline{\varepsilon}}H$ of $H$. For any affine Cayley-Hamilton Hopf algebra $(H,C,\text{tr})$ such that $H/\mathfrak{m}_{\overline{\varepsilon}}H$ has the Chevalley property, it is proved that if the zero locus of a discriminant ideal of $(H,C,\text{tr})$ is non-empty then it contains the orbit of the identity element of the affine algebraic group $\text{maxSpec}C$ under the left (or right) winding automorphism group action. Its proof relies on the fact that $H/\mathfrak{m}_{\overline{\varepsilon}}H$ has the Chevalley property if and only if the $\overline{\varepsilon}$-Chevalley locus of $(H,C)$ coincides with $\text{maxSpec}C$.
  Then, we provide a description of the zero locus of the lowest discriminant ideal of $(H,C,\text{tr})$. It is proved that the lowest discriminant ideal of $(H,C,\text{tr})$ is of level $\text{FPdim}(\text{Gr}(H/\mathfrak{m}_{\overline{\varepsilon}}H))+1$, where $\text{Gr}(H/\mathfrak{m}_{\overline{\varepsilon}}H)$ is the Grothendieck ring of the finite-dimensional Hopf algebra $H/\mathfrak{m}_{\overline{\varepsilon}}H$ and $\text{FPdim}(\text{Gr}(H/\mathfrak{m}_{\overline{\varepsilon}}H))$ is the Frobenius-Perron dimension of $\text{Gr}(H/\mathfrak{m}_{\overline{\varepsilon}}H)$. Some recent results of Mi-Wu-Yakimov about lowest discriminant ideals are generalized. We also prove that all the discriminant ideals are trivial if $H$ has the Chevalley property.

</details>


### [4] [2-categorical affine symmetries of quantum enveloping algebras](https://arxiv.org/abs/2502.08039)
*Sam Qunell*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 该论文在A_n型中构造了仿射量子包络代数正部分在其有限维对应物上的2-表示，扩展了右乘2-表示，并与量子群的评估态射相关。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究仿射量子包络代数正部分在其有限维对应物上的2-表示，探索量子群表示理论中的新结构，并与已知的评估态射建立联系。

Method: 在A_n型中构造具体的2-表示，将其推广到其他简单类型（在D_4和C_2型中验证1-表示存在），并证明A_n型中特定商表示与预基本表示同构。

Result: 成功构造了A_n型中的2-表示，证明了D_4和C_2型中相应1-表示的存在性，建立了A_n型中商表示与预基本表示的同构关系，并由此给出了预基本表示特征公式的新证明。

Conclusion: 该工作为仿射量子包络代数提供了新的2-表示结构，建立了与评估态射的联系，并期望这一构造在所有简单类型中都存在，为量子群表示理论提供了新的视角和工具。

Abstract: We produce 2-representations of the positive part of affine quantum enveloping algebras on their finite-dimensional counterparts in type $A_n$. These 2-representations naturally extend the right-multiplication 2-representation of $U_q^+(\mathfrak{sl}_{n+1})$ on itself and are closely related to evaluation morphisms of quantum groups. We expect that our 2-representation exists in all simple types and show that the corresponding 1-representation exists in types $D_4$ and $C_2$. We also show that a certain quotient of our 1-representation in type $A_n$ is isomorphic to a prefundamental representation. We use this to provide a new proof of the prefundamental representation character formulas in these cases.

</details>
