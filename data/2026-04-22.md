### [1] [Graded Unitarity in the SCFT/VOA Correspondence](https://arxiv.org/abs/2507.23781)
*Arash Arabi Ardehali,Christopher Beem,Madalena Lemos,Leonardo Rastelli*

Main category: hep-th

TL;DR: 该论文研究了从四维N=2超共形场论中产生的顶点代数的结构特性，特别是当母理论是幺正时，对应的顶点代数不是传统意义上的幺正。作者提出了"分级幺正性"的新概念，并开始对分级幺正的Virasoro和仿射Kac-Moody顶点代数进行分类。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 从四维N=2超共形场论中产生的顶点代数具有特殊的结构特性。当母SCFT是幺正时，对应的顶点代数不是传统意义上的幺正，这需要一个新的概念来捕捉四维幺正性在顶点代数中的表现。

Method: 作者提出了"分级幺正性"的新概念来捕捉四维幺正性的后果。然后对分级幺正的Virasoro和仿射Kac-Moody顶点代数进行分类研究，在关于𝔯-滤过的自然假设下进行分析。

Result: 在自然假设下，只有(2,p)中心电荷的Virasoro VOA以及𝔰𝔩₂和𝔰𝔩₃的边界容许水平可能与分级幺正性兼容。这些情况恰好是已知从四维理论中出现的顶点代数。

Conclusion: 分级幺正性成功地捕捉了四维幺正性在顶点代数中的表现。分类结果表明，只有特定的Virasoro和仿射Kac-Moody顶点代数与分级幺正性兼容，这些恰好对应已知从四维理论中出现的代数，验证了该概念的合理性。

Abstract: Vertex algebras that arise from four-dimensional, $\mathcal{N}=2$ superconformal field theories inherit a collection of novel structural properties from their four-dimensional ancestors. Crucially, when the parent SCFT is unitary, the corresponding vertex algebra is not unitary in the conventional sense. In this paper, we motivate and define a generalized notion of unitarity for vertex algebras that we call \emph{graded unitarity}, and which captures the consequences of four-dimensional unitarity under this correspondence. We also take the first steps towards a classification program for graded-unitary vertex algebras whose underlying vertex algebras are Virasoro or affine Kac--Moody vertex algebras. Remarkably, under certain natural assumptions about the $\mathfrak{R}$-filtration for these vertex algebras, we show that only the $(2,p)$ central charges for Virasoro VOAs and boundary admissible levels for $\mathfrak{sl}_2$ and $\mathfrak{sl}_3$ Kac--Moody vertex algebras can possibly be compatible with graded unitarity. These are precisely the cases of these vertex algebras that are known to arise from four dimensions.

### [2] [Canonical differential calculi via functorial geometrization](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.20742)
*Keegan J. Flood,Gabriele Lobbia,Giacomo Tendas*

Main category: math.CT

TL;DR: 该论文建立了一个框架，将微分计算从代数推广到任意幺半加性范畴中的幺半群，统一处理了光滑环、交换代数和结合代数的微分理论。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究动机是建立一个统一的微分计算理论框架，能够同时推广光滑环上的de Rham复形、交换代数上的Kähler微分和结合代数上的通用微分计算，从而为各种数学结构提供自然的微分几何类比。

Method: 方法是通过忠实同构纤维化将范畴$\mathcal{E}$映射到幺半加性范畴$\mathcal{V}$中的幺半群范畴，然后建立从$\mathcal{E}$到$\mathcal{V}$中一阶微分计算范畴的规范函子，并推广一阶微分计算到其最大延拓的过程。

Result: 主要结果是证明了在特定条件下，范畴$\mathcal{E}$可以自然地映射到微分计算范畴，从而获得de Rham理论的函子化版本，以及光滑映射和微分同胚的类比概念。

Conclusion: 结论是该理论为非交换几何中的微分计算形式体系提供了从结合代数到幺半加性范畴中幺半群的扩展，建立了统一的微分几何框架，并揭示了不同微分理论之间的比较映射关系。

Abstract: Given a category $\mathcal{E}$, we establish sufficient conditions on a faithful isofibration $\mathcal{E}\rightarrow\operatorname{Mon}(\mathcal{V})$ valued in the category of monoids internal to a monoidal additive category $\mathcal{V}$ such that $\mathcal{E}$ admits a canonical functor to the category of first order differential calculi in $\mathcal{V}$. Generalizing the procedure of extending a first order differential calculus to its maximal prolongation to this setting, we obtain a canonical functor from $\mathcal{E}$ to the category of differential calculi in $\mathcal{V}$. This yields a simultaneous generalization of the de Rham complex on $C^{\infty}$-rings, the Kähler differentials on commutative algebras, and the universal differential calculus on associative algebras. As a consequence, such categories $\mathcal{E}$ admit natural analogues of the notions of smooth map and diffeomorphism, as well as a functorial de Rham theory. Moreover, whenever two such faithful isofibrations to $\operatorname{Mon}(\mathcal{V})$ factor suitably, their corresponding de Rham functors are related via a comparison map. Developing this theory requires first extending the noncommutative geometry formalism of differential calculi from associative algebras to the setting of monoids internal to monoidal additive categories.

</details>

### [3] [Some results on the higher-rank graphs associated to crystals of semisimple Lie algebras](https://arxiv.org/abs/2504.00684)
*Marco Matassa*

Main category: math.CO

TL;DR: 该论文继续研究由作者和R. Yuncken引入的与有限维复半单李代数相关的高秩图，其构造依赖于Kashiwara的晶体理论。首先证明了对应Weyl群的Bruhat图（弱和强）可以嵌入到高秩图中作为着色图。然后针对A型李代数，将高秩图构造的某些方面与组合学中的著名概念（特别是Lascoux和Schützenberger的key）联系起来。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 继续研究基于Kashiwara晶体理论构建的与有限维复半单李代数相关的高秩图，探索这些图与Weyl群Bruhat图之间的关系，并建立与组合学中已有概念的连接。

Method: 1. 使用Kashiwara的晶体理论构造高秩图；2. 证明Weyl群的弱和强Bruhat图可以嵌入到这些高秩图中作为着色图；3. 针对A型李代数，将高秩图的构造与组合学概念（特别是Lascoux和Schützenberger的key）建立联系。

Result: 1. 成功证明了对应Weyl群的弱和强Bruhat图可以嵌入到高秩图中作为着色图；2. 对于A型李代数，建立了高秩图构造与组合学中Lascoux和Schützenberger的key概念之间的具体联系。

Conclusion: 该研究深化了对基于晶体理论的高秩图的理解，建立了这些图与Weyl群Bruhat图之间的嵌入关系，并为A型情况提供了与组合学中已有重要概念的连接，为进一步研究李代数表示论与组合学之间的交叉提供了新的视角。

Abstract: In this paper we continue the study of the higher-rank graphs associated to finite-dimensional complex semisimple Lie algebras, introduced by the author and R. Yuncken, whose construction relies on Kashiwara's theory of crystals. First we prove that the Bruhat graphs of the corresponding Weyl groups, both weak and strong, can be embedded into the higher-rank graphs as colored graphs. Next, specializing to Lie algebras of type $A$, we connect some aspects of the construction of the higher-rank graphs with some well-known notions in combinatorics, most notably the keys of Lascoux and Schützenberger.

### [4] [On the classification of finite GK-dimensional pre-Nichols algebras and quasi-quantum groups](https://arxiv.org/abs/2504.12206)
*Yuping Yang*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 该论文证明了在扭曲Yetter-Drinfeld范畴中，非对角对象的预Nichols代数具有无限Gelfand-Kirillov维数，从而完全刻画了该范畴中有限GK维数的Nichols代数。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究扭曲Yetter-Drinfeld范畴中Nichols代数的Gelfand-Kirillov维数特性，旨在建立有限GK维数Nichols代数的完整分类理论。

Method: 通过证明非对角对象的预Nichols代数具有无限GK维数，结合对角类型Nichols代数的有限根系统条件，建立分类判据。

Result: 完全刻画了有限GK维数Nichols代数的充要条件：必须是对角类型且具有有限根系统（算术根系统）。

Conclusion: 该结果通过bosonization方法，进一步分类了有限GK维数的余基分级点状余拟Hopf代数，为相关代数结构的研究提供了理论基础。

Abstract: We prove that every pre-Nichols algebra of a nondiagonal object in the twisted Yetter-Drinfeld category ${_{\k G}^{\k G} {\mathcal{YD}^Φ}}$ has infinite Gelfand-Kirillov dimension, where $G$ is a finite abelian group and $Φ$ is a $3$-cocycle on $G$. This leads to a complete characterization of finite GK-dimensional Nichols algebras in this category. Specifically, for any finite-dimensional $V\in {_{\k G}^{\k G} {\mathcal{YD}^Φ}}$, we show that the Nichols algebra $B(V)$ has finite Gelfand-Kirillov dimension if and only if it is of diagonal type and its associated root system is finite, that is, an arithmetic root system. Via bosonization, this result yields a classification of finite GK-dimensional coradically graded pointed coquasi-Hopf algebras over finite abelian groups that are generated by group-like and skew-primitive elements.

</details>

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

### [6] [Verma Bases and Kashiwara-Nakashima Tableaux of $\mathfrak{sp}_4$](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.19490)
*Bintao Cao,Ye Huang*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 该论文建立了sp4李代数的有限维不可约表示L(λ)的Verma基向量与形状为λ的Kashiwara-Nakashima表之间的一一对应关系，并直接证明了Verma向量系的线性无关性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究sp4李代数的表示理论，特别是建立Verma基与Kashiwara-Nakashima表之间的明确对应关系，这对于理解该李代数的表示结构具有重要意义。

Method: 构造了sp4李代数的有限维不可约表示L(λ)的Verma基向量与形状为λ的Kashiwara-Nakashima表之间的一一对应，并直接证明了Verma向量系的线性无关性。
