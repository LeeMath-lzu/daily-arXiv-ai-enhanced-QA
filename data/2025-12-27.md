<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Method: 基于Positselski的反导出范畴建立同调框架，定义相应的Hochschild复形来控制变形，并证明形式性定理。

Result: 证明了Fréchet代数、de Rham代数和Dolbeault代数的形式性定理；对于复流形情况，Hochschild上同调等价于Kontsevich的扩展变形复形、光滑射影簇的导出范畴Hochschild上同调，以及Gualtieri的广义复流形变形复形；计算了多种矩阵分解范畴的连续Hochschild上同调。

Conclusion: 建立了局部凸(弯曲)dg-代数变形理论的完整同调框架，将几何代数对象的变形理论与已知的变形复形联系起来，为相关几何结构的研究提供了新工具。

Abstract: We define the appropriate homological setting to study deformation theory of complete locally convex (curved) dg-algebras based on Positselski's contraderived categories. We define the corresponding Hochschild complex controlling deformations and prove formality theorems for the Fréchet algebras of smooth functions on a manifold, the de Rham algebra and for the Dolbeault algebra of a complex manifold. In the latter case, the Hochschild cohomology is equivalent to Kontsevich's extended deformation complex, the Hochschild cohomology of the derived category in case $X$ is a smooth projective variety and to Gualtieri's deformation complex of $X$ viewed as generalized complex manifold. We also compute the continuous Hochschild cohomology for various categories of matrix factorisations.

### [3] [Freidel-Maillet type equations on fused K-matrices over the positive part of $U_q(\widehat{\mathfrak{sl}}_2)$](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.00819)
*Chenwei Ruan*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 该论文研究了量子包络代数U_q^+的反射方程表示，通过融合技术构造任意维度的K-矩阵，并证明它们满足Freidel-Maillet型方程。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究量子包络代数U_q^+的反射方程表示，特别是K-矩阵的构造和性质，为相关代数结构提供新的理解和工具。

Method: 1. 利用Baseilhac 2021的反射方程表示；2. 通过Rosso 1995的q-洗牌代数嵌入；3. 使用Terwilliger 2019的PBW基；4. 采用Ruan 2025的统一方法处理PBW基；5. 应用自然融合技术构造任意维度的K-矩阵。

Result: 成功构造了任意有意义的维度的融合K-矩阵的闭形式表达式，并证明了任意一对这样的融合K-矩阵都满足Freidel-Maillet型方程。

Conclusion: 该研究为量子包络代数的反射方程表示提供了系统的K-矩阵构造方法，建立了不同PBW基的统一处理框架，并验证了融合K-矩阵的代数性质。

Abstract: The positive part $U_q^+$ of the quantized enveloping algebra $U_q(\widehat{\mathfrak{sl}}_2)$ has a reflection equation presentation of Freidel-Maillet type, due to Baseilhac 2021. This presentation involves a K-matrix of dimension $2 \times 2$. Under an embedding of $U_q^+$ into a $q$-shuffle algebra due to Rosso 1995, this K-matrix can be written in closed form using a PBW basis for $U_q^+$ due to Terwilliger 2019. This PBW basis, together with two PBW bases due to Damiani 1993 and Beck 1994, can be obtain from a uniform approach by Ruan 2025. Following a natural fusion technique, we will construct fused K-matrices of arbitary meaningful dimension in closed form using the uniform approach. We will also show that any pair of these fused K-matrices satisfy Freidel-Maillet type equations.

### [4] [Canonical differential calculi via functorial geometrization](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.20742)
*Keegan J. Flood,Gabriele Lobbia,Giacomo Tendas*

Main category: math.CT

TL;DR: 该论文提出了一种将一阶微分计算理论从结合代数推广到幺半加法范畴内幺半群的方法，建立了从范畴E到微分计算范畴的规范函子，统一了光滑环上的de Rham复形、交换代数的Kähler微分和结合代数的通用微分计算。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 动机在于统一不同数学背景下的微分计算理论，包括光滑环上的de Rham复形、交换代数的Kähler微分和结合代数的通用微分计算。通过建立更一般的理论框架，为这些看似不同的构造提供统一的处理方式。

Method: 方法是通过建立从范畴E到幺半加法范畴V内幺半群范畴Mon(V)的忠实同构纤维化的充分条件，使得E可以规范地映射到V中的一阶微分计算范畴。然后将一阶微分计算扩展到其最大延拓的过程推广到这个设置中，从而获得从E到V中微分计算范畴的规范函子。

Result: 结果表明，满足条件的范畴E自然地获得了光滑映射和微分同胚的类似概念，以及函子化的de Rham理论。当两个忠实同构纤维化适当地分解时，它们对应的de Rham函子通过比较映射相关联。

Conclusion: 结论是成功地将非交换几何中的微分计算形式体系从结合代数推广到幺半加法范畴内的幺半群，为不同数学背景下的微分计算理论提供了统一的框架，并建立了相应的de Rham理论和比较映射。

Abstract: Given a category $\mathcal{E}$, we establish sufficient conditions on a faithful isofibration $\mathcal{E}\rightarrow\operatorname{Mon}(\mathcal{V})$ valued in the category of monoids internal to a monoidal additive category $\mathcal{V}$ such that $\mathcal{E}$ admits a canonical functor to the category of first order differential calculi in $\mathcal{V}$. Generalizing the procedure of extending a first order differential calculus to its maximal prolongation to this setting, we obtain a canonical functor from $\mathcal{E}$ to the category of differential calculi in $\mathcal{V}$. This yields a simultaneous generalization of the de Rham complex on $C^{\infty}$-rings, the Kähler differentials on commutative algebras, and the universal differential calculus on associative algebras. As a consequence, such categories $\mathcal{E}$ admit natural analogues of the notions of smooth map and diffeomorphism, as well as a functorial de Rham theory. Moreover, whenever two such faithful isofibrations to $\operatorname{Mon}(\mathcal{V})$ factor suitably, their corresponding de Rham functors are related via a comparison map. Developing this theory requires first extending the noncommutative geometry formalism of differential calculi from associative algebras to the setting of monoids internal to monoidal additive categories.

</details>

### [5] [Shifted twisted Yangians and affine Grassmannian islices](https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.10652)
*Kang Lu,Weiqiang Wang,Alex Weekes*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 该论文研究了与ADE型拟分裂Satake图相关的移位iYangians代数，证明其量化了来自ADE型仿射Grassmannian的对合不动点轨迹，并提出了从分裂和非分裂Satake框架双箭图产生正交辛（及混合）Coulomb分支的框架。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 在前篇论文中引入了与ADE型拟分裂Satake图相关的移位iYangians代数并构造了其表示。本文旨在证明这些代数量化了来自仿射Grassmannian的对合不动点轨迹，并建立与正交辛Coulomb分支的联系。

Method: 通过识别AI型中的islices与BCD型合适的幂零Slodowy切片，基于Lusztig和Mirković-Vybornov在A型的工作。提出从分裂和非分裂Satake框架双箭图产生正交辛（及混合）Coulomb分支的理论框架。

Result: 证明了移位iYangians代数量化了来自ADE型仿射Grassmannian的对合不动点轨迹，为截断移位iYangians代数量化仿射Grassmannian islices的期望提供了有力证据。建立了AI型islices与BCD型幂零Slodowy切片的对应关系。

Conclusion: 该研究建立了移位iYangians代数与仿射Grassmannian几何结构之间的量化关系，提出了连接正交辛Coulomb分支与islices及iYangians代数的理论框架，为相关几何表示论研究提供了新方向。

Abstract: In a prequel we introduced the shifted iYangians ${}^\imath Y_μ$ associated to quasi-split Satake diagrams of type ADE and even spherical coweights $μ$, and constructed the iGKLO representations of ${}^\imath Y_μ$, which factor through truncated shifted iYangians ${}^\imath Y_μ^λ$. In this paper, we show that ${}^\imath Y_μ$ quantizes the involutive fixed point locus ${}^\imath W_μ$ arising from affine Grassmannians of type ADE, and supply strong evidence toward the expectation that ${}^\imath Y_μ^λ$ quantizes a top-dimensional component of the affine Grassmannian islice ${}^\imath\overline{W}_μ^λ$. We identify the islices ${}^\imath\overline{W}_μ^λ$ in type AI with suitable nilpotent Slodowy slices of type BCD, building on the work of Lusztig and Mirković-Vybornov in type A. We propose a framework for producing ortho-symplectic (and hybrid) Coulomb branches from split (and nonsplit) Satake framed double quivers, which are conjectured to relate closely to the islices ${}^\imath\overline{W}_μ^λ$ and the algebras ${}^\imath Y_μ^λ$.
