### [6] [The R-matrix of the affine Yangian](https://arxiv.org/abs/2309.02377)
*Andrea Appel,Sachin Gautam,Curtis Wendlandt*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 该论文证明了仿射李代数g的Yangian Y_hg表示范畴O中任意两个表示存在两个亚纯R矩阵，它们通过酉约束相关，具有R(s)=R^+(s)R^0(s)R^-(s)的乘积形式，其中R^0(s)是亚纯阿贝尔R矩阵，R^-(s)是有理扭曲。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究仿射李代数Yangian表示范畴中的R矩阵构造问题，解决任意两个表示之间的R矩阵存在性和具体构造方法。

Method: 1. 引入一个不规则、阿贝尔、加法差分方程，其差分算子由g的q-Cartan矩阵给出，正则化后得到R^0(s)作为两个规范基本解的指数；2. 提出仿射Cartan子代数在Y_hg上的高阶伴随作用，产生线性方程组，从中恢复R^-(s)作为唯一解。

Result: 证明了两个亚纯R矩阵的存在性，它们通过酉约束相关，且在任何两个最高权表示的张量积上给出相同的有理R矩阵。

Conclusion: 成功构造了仿射李代数Yangian表示范畴中的R矩阵，揭示了其与q-Cartan矩阵和新型高阶伴随作用的深刻联系，为量子可积系统的研究提供了新工具。

Abstract: Let g be an affine Lie algebra with associated Yangian Y_hg. We prove the existence of two meromorphic R-matrices associated to any pair of representations of Y_hg in the category O. They are related by a unitary constraint and constructed as products of the form R(s)=R^+(s)R^0(s)R^-(s), where R^+(s) = R^-_{21}(-s)^{-1}. The factor R^0(s) is a meromorphic, abelian R-matrix, and R^-(s) is a rational twist. Our proof relies on two novel ingredients. The first is an irregular, abelian, additive difference equation whose difference operator is given in terms of the q-Cartan matrix of g. The regularization of this difference equation gives rise to R^0(s) as the exponentials of the two canonical fundamental solutions. The second key ingredient is a higher order analogue of the adjoint action of the affine Cartan subalgebra of g on Y_hg. This action has no classical counterpart, and produces a system of linear equations from which R^-(s) is recovered as the unique solution. Moreover, we show that both operators give rise to the same rational R-matrix on the tensor product of any two highest-weight representations.

</details>
