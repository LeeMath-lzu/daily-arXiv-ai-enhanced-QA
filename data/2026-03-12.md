### [1] [Asymmetric noncommutative torus has vanishing Einstein tensor](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.15539)
*Deeponjit Bose,Andrzej Sitarz*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 在非交换环面上计算了一个非平凡谱三元组的谱度量、挠率和爱因斯坦张量，结果显示挠率为零且爱因斯坦张量恒为零


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究非交换几何中的谱三元组，特别是非交换环面上的谱度量、挠率和爱因斯坦张量，探索非交换几何与广义相对论的对应关系

Method: 使用部分共形重标（如[1]中引入）的Dirac算子，显式计算非交换环面上非平凡谱三元组的谱度量、挠率和爱因斯坦张量

Result: 计算结果显示该谱三元组具有零挠率，且爱因斯坦张量恒为零

Conclusion: 在非交换环面上构造的谱三元组具有零挠率和零爱因斯坦张量，这为理解非交换几何中的引力理论提供了重要实例

Abstract: We explicitly compute the spectral metric, torsion and Einstein tensors for a nontrivial spectral triple on a noncommutative torus, with the Dirac operator related to the fully equivariant Dirac by a partial conformal rescaling (as introduced in [1]). The results show that the spectral triple has vanishing torsion and the Einstein tensor also identically vanishes.

### [2] [Murnaghan-Nakayama rule for the cyclotomic Hecke algebra and applications](https://arxiv.org/abs/2504.18825)
*Naihuan Jing,Ning Liu*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 本文为分圆Hecke代数建立了Murnaghan-Nakayama规则，提供了计算其不可约特征标的组合方法，并推导了多种应用公式。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 为分圆Hecke代数提供直接计算不可约特征标的组合方法，统一并推广之前已知的A型和B型Iwahori-Hecke代数以及复反射群的特征标公式。

Method: 基于Macdonald多项式的多参数Murnaghan-Nakayama规则，在Shoji标准元上建立分圆Hecke代数的Murnaghan-Nakayama规则；同时利用Schur函数的顶点算子实现，推导出上多分划的迭代公式作为对偶规则。

Result: 获得了分圆Hecke代数的完整不可约特征标表，推导了Regev型和Lübeck-Prasad-Adin-Roichman型公式，引入了多重双迹概念并给出了组合公式，特别得到了复反射群W_{m,n}的第二正交关系。

Conclusion: 本文建立的分圆Hecke代数Murnaghan-Nakayama规则提供了计算不可约特征标的有效组合框架，统一了多种特殊情况，并提供了SageMath实现用于实际计算。

Abstract: We establish a Murnaghan--Nakayama rule for the irreducible characters of the cyclotomic Hecke algebra $\mathscr H_{m,n}(q,u)$ on Shoji's standard elements. Combined with Shoji's determinacy result, our formula provides a direct combinatorial route to the full irreducible character table of $\mathscr H_{m,n}(q,u)$. Our construction is based on our recent multi-parameter Murnaghan--Nakayama rule for Macdonald polynomials and specializes uniformly to several previously known formulas, including those for the complex reflection group of type $G(m,1,n)$ and the Iwahori--Hecke algebras of types $A$ and $B$. In a dual framework, using the vertex operator realization of Schur functions, we also derive a complementary iterative formula for irreducible characters on upper multipartitions, which may be viewed as a dual Murnaghan--Nakayama rule.
  As applications, we obtain a Regev-type formula and a Lübeck--Prasad--Adin--Roichman-type formula for cyclotomic Hecke algebras, extending the corresponding formulas for the Iwahori--Hecke algebra of type $A$ and the complex reflection group, respectively. We further introduce the notion of multiple bitrace for cyclotomic Hecke algebras and give a general combinatorial formula for the multiple bitrace. As a specialization, this yields the second orthogonality relation for irreducible characters of the complex reflection group $W_{m,n}$. For practical computation, we also include in an appendix a SageMath implementation of our Murnaghan--Nakayama rule, which computes individual character values and the full character table.

### [3] [Quantized flag manifolds and non-restricted modules over quantum groups at roots of unity](https://arxiv.org/abs/2109.03319)
*Toshiyuki Tanisaki*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 证明了Lusztig猜想的多重性公式在非限制模上的应用，针对De Concini-Kac型量子包络代数在ℓ次单位根处的情况


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Lusztig提出了关于量子群表示理论中多重性公式的猜想，该猜想在表示论中具有重要意义。本文旨在证明这一猜想在特定条件下的成立性

Method: 使用代数表示论方法，针对De Concini-Kac型量子包络代数在ℓ次单位根处的情况，其中ℓ是满足特定条件的奇素数幂，证明Lusztig猜想的多重性公式

Result: 成功证明了Lusztig猜想的多重性公式在非限制模上的成立，为量子群表示理论提供了重要的理论支持

Conclusion: 该研究验证了Lusztig猜想在特定条件下的正确性，深化了对量子群表示理论中多重性公式的理解，为相关领域的研究提供了理论基础

Abstract: We give a proof of Lusztig's conjectural multiplicity formula for non-restricted modules over the De Concini-Kac type quantized enveloping algebra at the $\ell$-th root of unity, where $\ell$ is an odd prime power satisfying certain reasonable conditions.

</details>
