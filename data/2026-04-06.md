### [1] [Invariants and representations of the $Γ$-graded general linear Lie $ω$-algebras](https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.21795)
*R. B. Zhang*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 该论文系统发展了由交换因子ω的Abel群Γ分级的广义李代数理论，重点研究了Γ-分级的广义线性李ω-代数𝔤𝔩(V(Γ, ω))的表示论和不变理论。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 当前对具有交换因子ω的Abel群Γ分级的广义李代数应用有浓厚兴趣，需要系统发展这类代数结构的理论，特别是在表示论和不变理论方面。

Method: 建立了对称(Γ, ω)-代数上的广义Howe对偶性，推导了不变理论的第一和第二基本定理，以及广义Schur-Weyl对偶性。构造了Hopf (Γ, ω)-代数，并模仿经典Borel-Weil定理实现了简单张量模及其对偶模。

Result: 分类了两种"紧致"∗-结构下的酉化𝔤𝔩(V(Γ, ω))-模，证明V(Γ, ω)的张量幂及其对偶分别在两种紧致∗-结构下是酉化的。特别分析了Γ=ℤ^{dim V(Γ, ω)}且ω依赖于复参数q≠0的情况，发现𝔤𝔩(V(Γ, ω))与量子一般线性(超)群有共同特征，但在q是单位根时表现更好。

Conclusion: 该研究系统建立了Γ-分级广义李代数的理论框架，发展了其表示论和不变理论，建立了重要的对偶性关系，并构造了相应的Hopf代数结构，为这类代数结构的应用提供了坚实的理论基础。

Abstract: There is considerable current interest in applications of generalised Lie algebras graded by an abelian group $Γ$ with a commutative factor $ω$. This calls for a systematic development of the theory of such algebraic structures. We treat the representation theory and invariant theory of the $Γ$-graded general linear Lie $ω$-algebra $\mathfrak{gl}(V(Γ, ω))$, where $V(Γ, ω)$ is any finite dimensional $Γ$-graded vector space. Generalised Howe dualities over symmetric $(Γ, ω)$-algebras are established, from which we derive the first and second fundamental theorems of invariant theory, and a generalised Schur-Weyl duality. The unitarisable $\mathfrak{gl}(V(Γ, ω))$-modules for two ``compact'' $\ast$-structures are classified, and it is shown that the tensor powers of $V(Γ, ω)$ and their duals are unitarisable for the two compact $\ast$-structures respectively. A Hopf $(Γ, ω)$-algebra is constructed, which gives rise to a group functor corresponding to the general linear group in the $Γ$-graded setting. Using this Hopf $(Γ, ω)$-algebra, we realise simple tensor modules and their dual modules by mimicking the classic Borel-Weil theorem. We also analyse in some detail the case with $Γ={\mathbb Z}^{\dim{V(Γ, ω)}}$ and $ω$ depending on a complex parameter $q\ne 0$, where $\mathfrak{gl}(V(Γ, ω))$ shares common features with the quantum general linear (super)group, but is better behaved especially when $q$ is a root of unity.

### [2] [Classification of Extended Abelian Chern-Simons Theories](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02929)
*Daniel Galviz*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 该论文证明了扩展阿贝尔Chern-Simons理论可以通过有限二次模分类，这些理论对应于(2+1)维拓扑量子场论、阿贝尔Reshetikhin-Turaev TQFT和点化模张量范畴。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究扩展阿贝尔Chern-Simons理论的分类问题，建立这些理论与有限代数结构之间的对应关系，从而为拓扑量子场论提供系统的分类框架。

Method: 使用偶数积分非退化格点(Λ,K)及其判别二次模(G_K,q_K)作为分类工具。证明每个扩展阿贝尔Chern-Simons理论都可由其对应的有限二次模唯一确定（在对称幺半自然同构意义下），且每个有限二次模都能通过某个偶数积分非退化格点实现。

Result: 建立了有限二次模与扩展阿贝尔Chern-Simons理论之间的一一对应关系，证明了有限二次模可以分类扩展阿贝尔Chern-Simons理论、点化阿贝尔Reshetikhin-Turaev TQFT以及点化模张量范畴。

Conclusion: 有限二次模为扩展阿贝尔Chern-Simons理论提供了完整的分类方案，这一结果统一了拓扑量子场论、模张量范畴和格点理论中的相关结构，为这些领域的进一步研究提供了理论基础。

Abstract: We classify extended Abelian Chern-Simons theories with gauge group $U(1)^n$ as extended $(2+1)$-dimensional topological quantum field theories. For an even integral nondegenerate lattice $(Λ,K)$, let $(G_K,q_K)$ denote its discriminant quadratic module. We prove that the associated theory is determined, up to symmetric monoidal natural isomorphism, by this finite quadratic module, and that every finite quadratic module is realized as the discriminant quadratic module of an even integral nondegenerate lattice. It follows that finite quadratic modules classify extended Abelian Chern-Simons theories, pointed Abelian Reshetikhin-Turaev TQFTs, and pointed modular tensor categories.

</details>

### [4] [Deformations of mixed associators in module categories](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.00837)
*Matthieu Faitg,Azat M. Gainutdinov,Christoph Schweigert,Jan-Ole Willprecht*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 该论文建立了控制模范畴混合结合子形变的复形，证明了它与Davydov-Yetter复形的同构关系，并给出了有限情况下的同调维数公式和刚性结果。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究模范畴混合结合子的形变理论，建立相应的上同调理论，并将其与已知的Davydov-Yetter上同调和Drinfeld中心理论联系起来。

Method: 构造了控制混合结合子形变的复形C^•_mix(M)，证明其与表示函子ρ的Davydov-Yetter复形同构，利用先前DY上同调结果建立与Drinfeld中心相对Ext群的联系。

Result: 证明了有限情况下H^•_mix(M)同构于相对Ext群，给出了维数公式，证明了H^{>0}_mix(C)=0，建立了Ocneanu刚性的推广版本，并在Sweedler Hopf代数上构造了新的连续非精确模范畴族。

Conclusion: 成功建立了模范畴混合结合子形变的上同调理论，将其与Drinfeld中心理论联系起来，推广了刚性定理，并为非精确模范畴提供了新的构造方法。

Abstract: We set up a cochain complex $C^\bullet_{\mathrm{mix}}(\mathcal{M})$ whose cohomology controls deformations of the mixed associator of a module category $\mathcal{M}$ over a $\Bbbk$-linear monoidal category $\mathcal{C}$. We show that $C^\bullet_{\mathrm{mix}}(\mathcal{M})$ is isomorphic to the Davydov-Yetter (DY) complex of the representation functor $ρ: \mathcal{C} \to \mathrm{End}(\mathcal{M})$. Using our previous results on DY cohomology (arXiv:2411.19111), we prove that if $\mathcal{C}$ and $\mathcal{M}$ are finite then the cohomology $H^\bullet_{\mathrm{mix}}(\mathcal{M})$ is isomorphic to the relative Ext groups $\mathrm{Ext}^\bullet_{\mathcal{Z}(\mathcal{C}),\mathcal{C}}(\boldsymbol{1},\mathcal{A}_{\mathcal{M}})$ for the usual adjunction between the Drinfeld center $\mathcal{Z}(\mathcal{C})$ and $\mathcal{C}$, where $\mathcal{A}_{\mathcal{M}}$ is the so-called adjoint algebra of $\mathcal{M}$. This allows us to give a dimension formula for $H^n_{\mathrm{mix}}(\mathcal{M})$ in terms of certain Hom spaces in $\mathcal{Z}(\mathcal{C})$, and also to prove that $H^{>0}_{\mathrm{mix}}(\mathcal{C}) = 0$. We also show that the algebra $\mathcal{A}_{\mathcal{M}}$ is the ``full center'' of an algebra in $\mathcal{C}$ realizing $\mathcal{M}$. We furthermore establish a generalized version of Ocneanu rigidity for monoidal functors with coefficients, and provide its application to general (non-exact and non-finite) $\mathcal{C}$-module categories over a fusion category $\mathcal{C}$ such that $\dim(\mathcal{C}) \neq 0$. We spell out these results for module categories defined by finite-dimensional comodule algebras over finite-dimensional Hopf algebras. Examples based on comodule algebras over Sweedler's Hopf algebra are worked out in detail and yield new continuous families of inequivalent non-exact module categories.

</details>

Abstract: We compute explicit homological invariants of a trimmed graded double Ore extension of the quantum plane.
