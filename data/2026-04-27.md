### [1] [Affine Supertrusses and Superbraces](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.22381)
*Andrew James Bruce*

Main category: math-ph

TL;DR: 本文定义了仿射超truss概念，将其与超brace和Yang-Baxter方程联系起来，推广到超数学领域。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Brzeziński的truss是一种"环状"代数结构，其加法由阿贝尔堆操作替代，乘法满足三元积的自然分配律。本文旨在定义truss的Z₂-梯度超版本。

Method: 受代数超群理论启发，将仿射超truss定义为从单位结合超交换超代数范畴到truss范畴的可表示函子。表示超代数配备了"cotruss"结构——一个全新的概念。

Result: 证明从仿射超truss可以构造仿射超brace，从而将Rump的brace推广到超数学。提出了集合论Yang-Baxter方程到仿射超概形设定的推广。

Conclusion: 成功建立了truss的超版本理论，并将其与超brace和Yang-Baxter方程联系起来，为超数学中的相关研究提供了新工具。

Abstract: Brzeziński's trusses are ``ring-like'' algebraic structures in which the addition is replaced with an abelian heap operation and the binary product satisfies a natural distributivity rule of the ternary product. The question of how to define ($\mathbb{Z}_2$-graded) super-versions of trusses is addressed in this note. Taking our cue from the theory of algebraic supergroups, we define an affine supertruss as a representable functor from the category of unital associative supercommutative superalgebras to the category of trusses. The representing superalgebras are equipped with a `cotruss' structure--a new concept in itself. We show that from an affine supertruss one can construct an affine superbrace, and so generalise Rump's braces to supermathematics. As an application of these constructions, we propose a generalisation of the set-theoretic Yang--Baxter equation to the setting of affine superschemes.

</details>


### [2] [Tetrahedral $L$-operators, tensor Schur polynomials and $q$-deformed loop elementary symmetric functions](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.22141)
*Shinsuke Iwao,Kohei Motegi,Ryo Ohkawa*

Main category: math-ph

TL;DR: 本文研究了基于四面体L-算子的三维配分函数，在q=0和通用q两种情形下分别得到若干结果，包括张量Schur多项式表达、shuffle公式推导、恒等式统一以及q-变形基本对称函数等。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 对Bazhanov-Sergeev和Kuniba-Maruyama-Okado提出的四面体L-算子所构建的三维配分函数进行深入研究，探索其在统计物理和代数组合中的意义。

Method: 通过分析q=0情形下的Zamolodchikov-Faddeev代数，引入可用张量Schur多项式表达的配分函数类；利用除以差算子构造Laurent多项式族；对通用q情形研究若干类配分函数并确定其明确形式。

Result: 推导了Schur多项式的shuffle公式（几何上对应Joźefiak-Pragacz-Lascoux推出公式）；统一了Gustafson-Milne和Fehér-Némethi-Rimányi恒等式；给出了多物种TASEP稳态的应用；在通用q情形得到了q-变形基本对称函数（包括q-变形环绕基本对称函数的推广）。

Conclusion: 三维配分函数与Schur多项式、对称函数及可积系统之间具有深刻联系，为相关领域提供了统一视角和新工具。

Abstract: We study three-dimensional partition functions constructed from the tetrahedral $L$-operator introduced and studied by Bazhanov-Sergeev and Kuniba-Maruyama-Okado. First, we explore the $q=0$ case, extending the authors' previous results and giving applications by a further analysis on the Zamolodchikov-Faddeev algebra. We introduce a class of partition functions which can be expressed as the tensor Schur polynomials, a class of products of Schur polynomials. As an application, we derive the shuffle formula for the Schur polynomials which is geometrically the pushforward formula by Joźefiak-Pragacz-Lascoux. We also give a derivation and a unification of the Gustafson-Milne and Feh{é}r--N{é}methi--Rim{á}nyi identities, and introduce a family of Laurent polynomials using divided difference operators which imitates the Schubert polynomials from the perspective of our study. We also present an application to the steady state of the multispecies totally asymmetric simple exclusion process. Second, we investigate several classes of partition functions for the generic $q$ case, and determine the explicit forms as deformations of the elementary symmetric functions. One of them can be regarded as (an extension of) a $q$-deformed loop elementary symmetric functions.

</details>

### [3] [Natural transformations between braiding functors in the Fukaya category](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.10462)
*Yujin Tong*

Main category: math.SG

TL;DR: 本文研究了$\mathfrak{sl}_2$ quiver规范理论Coulomb分支$\mathcal{M}(\bullet,1)$上Fukaya范畴的辫子函子之间的$A_\infty$-自然变换空间。计算了所有上同调不同的自然变换$\mathrm{Nat}(\mathrm{id}, \mathrm{id})$和$\mathrm{Nat}(\mathrm{id}, β_i^-)$，并通过Chouhy-Solotar约化系统得到对角双模的投射分解来计算Hochschild上同调，从而对所有自然变换进行了分类。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 理解辫子函子编码的更高$A_\infty$-数据，为Fukaya范畴上辫子协边作用的范畴化表述奠定基础。

Method: 利用与KLRW范畴嵌入兼容的图式框架；使用Chouhy-Solotar约化系统构造对角双模的显式投射分解来计算Hochschild上同调。

Result: 计算出了$\mathrm{Nat}(\mathrm{id}, \mathrm{id})$和$\mathrm{Nat}(\mathrm{id}, β_i^-)$的所有上同调不同的$A_\infty$-自然变换；得到了Fukaya范畴的Hochschild上同调。

Conclusion: 确定了辫子函子及其自然变换中编码的更高$A_\infty$-数据，为Fukaya范畴上辫子协边作用的范畴化表述迈出了第一步。

Abstract: We study the space of $A_\infty$-natural transformations between braiding functors acting on the Fukaya category associated to the Coulomb branch $\mathcal{M}(\bullet,1)$ of the $\mathfrak{sl}_2$ quiver gauge theory. We compute all cohomologically distinct $A_\infty$-natural transformations $\mathrm{Nat}(\mathrm{id}, \mathrm{id})$ and $\mathrm{Nat}(\mathrm{id}, β_i^-)$, where $β_i^-$ denotes the negative braiding functor. Our computation is carried out in a diagrammatic framework compatible with the established embedding of the KLRW category into this Fukaya category. We then compute the Hochschild cohomology of the Fukaya category using an explicit projective resolution of the diagonal bimodule obtained via the Chouhy-Solotar reduction system, and use this to classify all cohomologically distinct natural transformations. These results determine the higher $A_\infty$-data encoded in the braiding functors and their natural transformations, and provide the first step toward a categorical formulation of braid cobordism actions on Fukaya categories.

</details>

### [4] [Boundary $q$-characters of evaluation modules for split quantum affine symmetric pairs](https://arxiv.org/abs/2504.14042)
*Jian-Rong Li,Tomasz Przezdziecki*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 本文研究了仿射型 $\mathsf{AI}$ 的量子对称配对余理想子代数的评价模，通过 Gelfand-Tsetlin 基计算了生成元的作用，确定了 Lu-Wang 表示中一大类交换子代数的谱，得到了边界 $q$-特征显式公式，并用半标准杨表进行了组合解释。结果揭示边界 $q$-特征既有与普通 $q$-特征相似的最高权性质，也展现出额外对称性等新现象，首次给出了非由普通 $q$-特征限制得到的边界 $q$-特征例子。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究量子仿射对称对偶的边界 $q$-特征，探索其与普通 $q$-特征的关系及新结构。

Method: 利用 Gelfand-Tsetlin 基计算 Lu-Wang Drinfeld 型表示中生成元的作用，确定交换子代数的谱，推导边界 $q$-特征的显式公式，并用半标准杨表进行组合解释。

Result: 得到了边界 $q$-特征的显式公式，证明了其具有最高权性质，同时展现出额外对称性等新现象，并首次构造了非由普通 $q$-特征限制得到的边界 $q$-特征例子。

Conclusion: 边界 $q$-特征既有与普通 $q$-特征相似的性质（如最高权性质），又包含全新的结构（如额外对称性），揭示了量子仿射对称对偶中更为丰富的代数结构。

Abstract: We study evaluation modules for quantum symmetric pair coideal subalgebras of affine type $\mathsf{AI}$. By computing the action of the generators in Lu and Wang's Drinfeld-type presentation on Gelfand-Tsetlin bases, we determine the spectrum of a large commutative subalgebra arising from the Lu-Wang presentation. This leads to an explicit formula for boundary analogues of $q$-characters in the setting of quantum affine symmetric pairs. We interpret this formula combinatorially in terms of semistandard Young tableaux. Our results imply that boundary $q$-characters share familiar features with ordinary $q$-characters - such as a version of the highest weight property - yet they also display new phenomena, including an extra symmetry. In particular, we provide the first examples of boundary $q$-characters for quantum affine symmetric pairs that do not arise from restriction of ordinary $q$-characters, thereby revealing genuinely new structures in this new setting.

</details>
