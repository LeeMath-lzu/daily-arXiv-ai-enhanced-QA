### [1] [Quantized Geodesic Lengths for Teichmüller Spaces: Algebraic Aspects](https://arxiv.org/abs/2405.14727)
*Hyun Kyu Kim*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文基于Chekhov-Fock量子Teichmüller理论，通过量子迹映射构造量子单闭曲线迹算子，验证Teschner使用的特殊递推关系，并证明不相交环路的量子迹算子满足强交换性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Verlinde在1980年代提出用量子Teichmüller空间构造Liouville共形场论的共形块空间，这导致了Fock、Goncharov和Shen提出的模函子猜想。本文旨在基于Chekhov-Fock理论直接解决这一猜想。

Method: 使用Bonahon和Wong在2010年代发展的量子迹映射，构造简单环路量子迹算子，研究其代数结构，最终构建量子测地线长度算子。

Result: 证明了Teschner使用的特殊递推关系对量子迹算子成立，并且不相交环路的量子迹算子在强意义下交换。

Conclusion: 基于Chekhov-Fock量子Teichmüller理论的方法为直接解决模函子猜想提供了基础，量子迹算子的代数性质为构造量子测地线长度算子铺平了道路。

Abstract: In 1980's H. Verlinde suggested to construct and use a quantization of Teichmüller spaces to construct spaces of conformal blocks for the Liouville conformal field theory. This suggestion led to a mathematical formulation by Fock in 1990's and later by Fock, Goncharov and Shen, called the modular functor conjecture, based on the Chekhov-Fock quantum Teichmüller theory. In 2000's, Teschner combined the Chekhov-Fock version and the Kashaev version of quantum Teichmüller theory to construct a solution to a modified form of the conjecture. We embark on a direct approach to the conjecture based on the Chekhov-Fock(-Goncharov) theory. We construct quantized trace-of-monodromy along simple loops via Bonahon and Wong's quantum trace maps developed in 2010's, and investigate algebraic structures of them, which will eventually lead to construction and properties of quantized geodesic length operators. We show that a special recursion relation used by Teschner is satisfied by the quantized trace-of-monodromy, and that the quantized trace-of-monodromy for disjoint loops commute in a certain strong sense.

### [2] [The R-matrix of the affine Yangian](https://arxiv.org/abs/2309.02377)
*Andrea Appel,Sachin Gautam,Curtis Wendlandt*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 该论文证明了与仿射李代数相关的Yangian表示范畴中两种亚纯R-矩阵的存在性，通过q-Cartan矩阵的非常规差分方程和仿射Cartan子代数的非经典高阶作用构造


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究仿射李代数相关Yangian表示范畴中的R-矩阵结构，建立两种亚纯R-矩阵的存在性及其相互关系，探索非经典的高阶作用机制

Method: 1. 构造R(s)=R^+(s)R^0(s)R^-(s)形式的乘积分解；2. 利用q-Cartan矩阵构建非常规的亚伯尔加法差分方程，正则化得到R^0(s)；3. 引入仿射Cartan子代数的高阶伴随作用（无经典对应），从线性方程组中恢复有理扭曲R^-(s)

Result: 证明了两种亚纯R-矩阵的存在性，它们通过酉约束相关，且在任何两个最高权表示的张量积上给出相同的有理R-矩阵

Conclusion: 成功建立了仿射李代数Yangian表示范畴中R-矩阵的系统构造方法，揭示了q-Cartan矩阵的非常规差分方程和仿射Cartan子代数高阶作用的关键作用

Abstract: Let g be an affine Lie algebra with associated Yangian Y_hg. We prove the existence of two meromorphic R-matrices associated to any pair of representations of Y_hg in the category O. They are related by a unitary constraint and constructed as products of the form R(s)=R^+(s)R^0(s)R^-(s), where R^+(s) = R^-_{21}(-s)^{-1}. The factor R^0(s) is a meromorphic, abelian R-matrix, and R^-(s) is a rational twist. Our proof relies on two novel ingredients. The first is an irregular, abelian, additive difference equation whose difference operator is given in terms of the q-Cartan matrix of g. The regularization of this difference equation gives rise to R^0(s) as the exponentials of the two canonical fundamental solutions. The second key ingredient is a higher order analogue of the adjoint action of the affine Cartan subalgebra of g on Y_hg. This action has no classical counterpart, and produces a system of linear equations from which R^-(s) is recovered as the unique solution. Moreover, we show that both operators give rise to the same rational R-matrix on the tensor product of any two highest-weight representations.

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Abstract: In this paper, we study the $n$-point function of $t$-core partitions. As a consequence, we provide a closed formula for the $n$-point function of $t$-core partitions in terms of theta functions, and prove that the corresponding correlation functions are quasimodular forms.
