### [1] [Quantum Invariants of Ribbon Surfaces in $4$-Dimensional $2$-Handlebodies](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.15395)
*Anna Beliakova,Marco De Renzi,Quentin Faes*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 该论文利用幺模带状范畴构造了4维2-柄体上带状曲面的量子不变量，推广了多个已知不变量，不要求半单性，基于Kirby图的标记范畴推广了Reshetikhin-Turaev函子。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究4维2-柄体中带状曲面的量子不变量，统一并推广Bobtcheva-Messia、Broda-Petit、Gainutdinov-Geer-Patureau-Runkel、Lee-Yetter等人的工作，构建不依赖于半单性假设的更一般理论框架。

Method: 使用幺模带状范畴，将Reshetikhin-Turaev函子推广到标记Kirby图范畴，通过Hennings和Bobtcheva-Messia引入的方程、修正迹、以及满足Hughes-Kim-Miller图式计算条件的Frobenius代数模等多种构造方法。

Result: 成功构造了4维2-柄体中带状曲面在1-同痕下的量子不变量，同时恢复了多个已知不变量，并得到了4维2-柄体边界中框架链环在2-形变下的不变量。

Conclusion: 该方法具有高度灵活性，不要求半单性假设，为4维拓扑中的量子不变量理论提供了统一框架，能够通过多种代数结构实现不变量构造。

Abstract: We use unimodular ribbon categories to construct quantum invariants of ribbon surfaces in $4$-dimensional $2$-handlebodies up to $1$-isotopy. In the process, we recover invariants due to Bobtcheva-Messia, Broda-Petit, Gainutdinov-Geer-Patureau-Runkel (in collaboration with the second author), and Lee-Yetter. Our approach does not assume semisimplicity, and is based on a generalization of the Reshetikhin-Turaev functor to the category of labeled Kirby graphs which also yields invariants of framed links in the boundary of $4$-dimensional $2$-handlebodies up to $2$-deformations. The setup is very flexible, and allows for several different constructions, using central elements satisfying equations introduced by Hennings and Bobtcheva-Messia, modified traces, and modules over Frobenius algebras satisfying conditions dictated by the diagrammatic calculus for embedded surfaces developed by Hughes, Kim, and Miller.

### [2] [Algebraic Presentation of $4$-Dimensional $2$-Handlebodies and $3$-Dimensional Cobordisms](https://arxiv.org/abs/2312.15986)
*Anna Beliakova,Ivelina Bobtcheva,Marco De Renzi,Riccardo Piergallini*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文提供了Bobtcheva和Piergallini结果的直接证明，该结果给出了两个范畴的有限代数表示：3维流形范畴3Cob和4维2-手柄体范畴4HB。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究动机是为Bobtcheva和Piergallini关于3维和4维流形范畴的代数表示结果提供新的直接证明，并建立与Habiro猜想的一致性。

Method: 方法是通过显式构造函子Φ: 4Alg → 4HB的逆映射，其中4Alg是由Bobtcheva-Piergallini Hopf代数生成的自由幺半范畴。

Result: 成功构造了函子Φ的逆映射，从而得到了4HB范畴的代数表示，并推导出3Cob范畴的代数表示，证明了该表示与Habiro猜想等价。

Conclusion: 结论是给出了Bobtcheva-Piergallini结果的直接证明，建立了3Cob和4HB范畴的有限代数表示，并验证了Habiro猜想的正确性。

Abstract: In this paper, we give a new direct proof of a result by Bobtcheva and Piergallini that provides finite algebraic presentations of two categories, denoted $3\mathrm{Cob}$ and $4\mathrm{HB}$, whose morphisms are manifolds of dimension $3$ and $4$, respectively. More precisely, $3\mathrm{Cob}$ is the category of connected oriented $3$-dimensional cobordisms between connected surfaces with connected boundary, while $4\mathrm{HB}$ is the category of connected oriented $4$-dimensional $2$-handlebodies up to $2$-deformations. For this purpose, we explicitly construct the inverse of the functor $Φ: 4\mathrm{Alg} \to 4\mathrm{HB}$, where $4\mathrm{Alg}$ denotes the free monoidal category generated by a Bobtcheva--Piergallini Hopf algebra. As an application, we deduce an algebraic presentation of $3\mathrm{Cob}$ and show that it is equivalent to the one conjectured by Habiro.

*Ming Lu,Zhuoyi Zhao*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 该论文构建了量子对称对中余理想子代数的Hall代数框架，并证明了嵌入映射和余积保持积分形式


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 量子对称对由量子群及其余理想子代数组成，已有研究分别给出了量子群和余理想子代数的Hall代数构造。本文旨在为余理想子代数结构及其量子对称对关系建立统一的Hall代数框架。

Method: 构建量子对称对中余理想子代数结构的Hall代数框架，研究嵌入映射和余积在积分形式上的性质。

Result: 证明了自然嵌入映射和余积保持量子群及其余理想子代数的积分形式，这些积分形式用于构造对偶典范基。

Conclusion: 成功建立了量子对称对中余理想子代数结构的Hall代数框架，为相关代数结构的研究提供了新的工具和方法。

Abstract: A quantum symmetric pair consists of a quantum group $\widetilde{\mathbf{U}}$ and its coideal subalgebra $\widetilde{\mathbf{U}}^\imath$. The Hall algebra constructions of $\widetilde{\mathbf{U}}$ and $\widetilde{\mathbf{U}}^\imath$ are given by Bridgeland and Lu--Wang, respectively. In this paper, we construct a Hall algebra framework for the coideal subalgebra structure of $\widetilde{\mathbf{U}}^\imath$ in $\widetilde{\mathbf{U}}$, and for the quantum symmetric pair $(\widetilde{\mathbf{U}},\widetilde{\mathbf{U}}^\imath)$. As an application, we prove that the natural embedding $\imath:\widetilde{\mathbf{U}}^\imath\to \widetilde{\mathbf{U}}$, and the coproduct $Δ:\widetilde{\mathbf{U}}^\imath\to \widetilde{\mathbf{U}}^\imath\otimes \widetilde{\mathbf{U}}$ preserve the integral forms of $\widetilde{\mathbf{U}}^\imath$ and $\widetilde{\mathbf{U}}$, which are used to construct the dual canonical bases.

### [5] [On the colored Links--Gould polynomial](https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.10911)

TL;DR: 本文给出了用$4n$维不可约表示着色的Links-Gould多项式的电缆公式，并将其与$n=2$时的$V_n$多项式等同。利用电缆公式获得了亏格界限和与$n$无关的Alexander多项式特化，验证了先前关于$V_2$多项式的猜想，并将$n=1$的结果推广到$n=2$。

Method: 1. 推导了用$4n$维不可约表示着色的Links-Gould多项式的电缆公式；2. 将$n=2$时的Links-Gould多项式与$V_2$多项式等同；3. 利用电缆公式推导亏格界限和Alexander多项式特化；4. 结合arXiv:2409.03557的工作验证亏格界限的尖锐性。

### [6] [Algebraic structures of Vassiliev invariants for knot families](https://arxiv.org/abs/2508.02385)
*E. Lanina,A. Sleptsov*

Main category: hep-th

TL;DR: 该论文研究了Vassiliev结不变量在3维Chern-Simons理论中的代数关系，探讨了k参数结族中Vassiliev不变量的多项式行为和代数独立性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究Vassiliev结不变量在3维Chern-Simons理论中的代数关系，特别是探讨在k参数结族中这些不变量的多项式行为和代数独立性，以理解结不变量的代数结构。

Method: 通过分析k参数结族中的Vassiliev不变量，研究其多项式行为。对于单参数情况，提出有限生成猜想；对于多参数情况，构造具有无限生成元的例子。研究代数独立Vassiliev不变量的数量限制。

Result: 发现对于k参数结族，代数独立的Vassiliev不变量数量≤k。在所有例子中，代数独立不变量的数量恰好为k。对于某些k参数结族，k个Vassiliev不变量构成完全结不变量。

Conclusion: Vassiliev不变量在结族中表现出丰富的代数结构。单参数情况下可能有限生成，多参数情况下可能无限生成。代数独立不变量的数量受参数数量限制，但存在是否存在少于k个代数独立不变量的开放问题。

Abstract: We explore algebraic relations on Vassiliev knot invariants related with correlators in the 3-dimensional Chern--Simons theory. Vassiliev invariants form infinite-dimensional algebra. We focus on $k$-parametric knot families with Vassiliev invariants being polynomials in family parameters. We conjecture that such 1-parametric algebra of Vassiliev invariants is always finitely generated, while in the case of more parameters, we provide example of the knot family with infinite number of generators. Inside a knot family, there appear extra algebraic relations on Vassiliev invariants. We show that there are $\leq k$ algebraically independent Vassiliev invariants for $k$-parametric knot family. However, in all our examples, the number of algebraically independent Vassiliev invariants is exactly $k$, and it is open question if there exists a $k$-parametric knot family with a fewer number of algebraically independent Vassiliev invariants. We also demonstrate that a complete knot invariant of some $k$-parametric knot families consists of $k$ Vassiliev invariants.

</details>
