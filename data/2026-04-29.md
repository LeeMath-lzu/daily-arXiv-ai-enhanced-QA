### [1] [Representations of Super Yangians with Gelfand-Tsetlin bases](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.25462)
*Vyacheslav Futorny,Zheng Li,Jian Zhang*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 本文研究了超Yangian $\Ymn$到通用包络代数$\U(\gl_{m|n})$的评估同态，通过协变张量模构造了$\Ymn$的有限维单模，并给出了这些模为驯服的充要条件，将Nazarov和Tarasov关于$\Y(\mathfrak{gl}_m)$的工作推广到了超情况。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将Nazarov和Tarasov关于$\Y(\mathfrak{gl}_m)$的驯服模分类工作推广到超李代数$\gl_{m|n}$对应的超Yangian情形。

Method: 利用评估同态将协变张量模视为$\Ymn$模，研究最高权向量张量积生成的子模的单商，并给出驯服性的充要条件。

Result: 给出了所构造的单模为驯服的充要条件，当$n=0$时恢复了$\Y(\mathfrak{gl}_m)$的所有有限维单模。

Conclusion: 成功将驯服模的分类从一般线性李代数推广到了超李代数情形。

Abstract: The evaluation homomorphisms from the super Yangian $\Ymn$ to the universal enveloping algebra $\U(\gl_{m|n})$ allows one to regard the covariant tensor module of $\gl_{m|n}$ as $\Ymn$ modules. We study simple quotients of the submodules generated by a tensor product of highest weight vectors inside the tensor products of covariant evaluation modules. In the case $n=0$, this recover all finite-dimensional simple modules of $\Y(\mathfrak{gl}_m)$. We give a necessary and sufficient condition for such modules to be tame, which generalizes the earlier work of Nazarov and Tarasov for $\Y(\mathfrak{gl}_m)$ to the super case.

### [2] [A Gauge-Invariant Bundle Isomorphism Between Complex Velocity Fields and Symmetric Logarithmic Derivatives](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.12187)
*Jorge Meza-Domínguez*

Main category: quant-ph

TL;DR: 本文建立了复速度场与量子估计理论中SLD算子之间的严格丛同构，证明了该构造与背景高斯测度无关，并推导出Fisher度量的简洁形式，预言了时空非收缩环上可观测的拓扑相位。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 旨在建立随机引力涨落下物质动力学平均场的复速度场与量子估计理论中对称对数导数算子之间的严格数学联系，从而统一经典随机性与量子信息几何。

Method: 通过构造从拉回丛的规范等价类到SLD算子的丛同构$\widetilde{\mathcal{T}}$，在无限维Fréchet流形上利用固定高斯测度定义Hilbert空间，并证明同构与Fisher度量的测度无关性。

Result: 证明了$\widetilde{\mathcal{T}}$与量子Fisher度量均不依赖于背景高斯测度$\nu_0$的选择；得到Fisher度量的Madelung-Bohm速度形式；发现由$\eta_\mu$定义的平坦$U(1)$联络在非收缩时空环路上产生量子化的拓扑相位。

Conclusion: 该同构为经典随机涨落与量子信息几何提供了统一框架，并通过预言可观测的拓扑相位（如原子干涉实验），为检验引力诱导的量子效应提供了新途径。

Abstract: We establish a rigorous bundle isomorphism between the complex velocity field $η_μ = π_μ - i u_μ$, obtained by averaging matter dynamics over stochastic gravitational fluctuations, and the symmetric logarithmic derivative (SLD) operator $L_μ$ of quantum estimation theory. The isomorphism $\widetilde{\mathcal{T}}: Γ(E/{\sim}) \to Γ(\mathcal{L})$ maps gauge-equivalence classes of sections of the pullback bundle $E = π_2^*(T^*M)$ over $\mathcal{C} \times M$ to SLD operators on the Hilbert space $\mathcal{H}_0 = L^2(\mathcal{C}, ν_0)$, where $\mathcal{C}$ is the infinite-dimensional Fréchet manifold of matter fields and $ν_0$ is a fixed Gaussian measure. We prove that $\widetilde{\mathcal{T}}$ and the associated quantum Fisher metric are independent of the choice of $ν_0$, rendering the construction intrinsic to the physical probability density. The Fisher metric acquires a simple form in terms of the Madelung--Bohm velocities: $g_{μν}^{\mathrm{FS}} = \frac{4m^2}{\hbar^2} \bigl[\operatorname{Cov}(π_μ,π_ν) + \operatorname{Cov}(u_μ,u_ν)\bigr]_{\mathcal{P}}$. As a consequence, the flat $U(1)$ connection defined by $η_μ$ yields a quantized holonomy for non-contractible spacetime loops, predicting topological phases that may be observable in atom interferometry.

### [3] [Derivations on the triplet $W$-algebras with $\mathfrak{sl}_2$-symmetry](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.25729)
*Hiromu Nakano*

Main category: math-ph

TL;DR: 本文通过精化Frobenius同态，构造了三重W-代数$\mathcal{W}_{p_+,p_-}$上的导子，并证明Adamović-Milas导子性质可推广到这些导子上，进而揭示了$\mathfrak{sl}_2$对称性，并将方法推广到三重W-超代数$\mathcal{SW}(m)$，确定了其全自同构群为$PSL_2(\mathbb{C})\times \mathbb{Z}_2$。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有关于三重W-代数导子的研究主要局限于$\mathcal{W}_{2,p}$情形，缺乏对一般$\mathcal{W}_{p_+,p_-}$的导子构造及其性质分析，同时三重W-超代数的自同构群尚未完全确定。

Method: 精化Tsuchiya-Wood的Frobenius同态来构造一般情形的导子；通过推广Adamović-Milas的导子性质来验证构造的有效性；将构造方法推广到三重W-超代数$\mathcal{SW}(m)$并计算其全自同构群。

Result: 成功构造了$\mathcal{W}_{p_+,p_-}$上的导子，验证了Adamović-Milas性质的推广，揭示了$\mathfrak{sl}_2$对称性；证明了$\mathcal{SW}(m)$的全自同构群为$PSL_2(\mathbb{C})\times \mathbb{Z}_2$。

Conclusion: 本文给出了三重W-代数及超代数上导子的统一构造，完整确定了$\mathcal{SW}(m)$的自同构群。

Abstract: We construct derivations on the triplet $W$-algebras $\mathcal{W}_{p_+,p_-}$ by refining the Frobenius homomorphisms of Tsuchiya-Wood and show that the property of the Adamović-Milas derivation for $\mathcal{W}_{2,p}$ extends to our derivations. As an application, we show that the $\mathfrak{sl}_2$-symmetry of $\mathcal{W}_{p_+,p_-}$ arises naturally from our construction. We further show that our method applies to the triplet $W$-superalgebra $\mathcal{SW}(m)$ and that the full automorphism group ${\rm Aut}(\mathcal{SW}(m))$ is $PSL_2(\mathbb{C})\times \mathbb{Z}_2$.

### [4] [Prefactorization algebras of superselection sectors](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.24865)
*Marco Benini,Victor Carmona,Alexander Schenkel*

Main category: math-ph

TL;DR: 本文从预因子化代数的新视角重新审视了代数量子场论中的超选择扇区理论。在Haag对偶性和局部忠实真空表示的标准假设下，证明了每个定义在满足一定几何假设的时空区域过滤正交范畴上的AQFT，都有一个相关联的局部常值C*-范畴预因子化代数。对于闵可夫斯基时空中(n≥2)维的双锥区域，该方法通过Dunn-Lurie可加性，将Haag对偶性的E1-幺半结构与洛伦兹几何的E_{n-1}-幺半结构结合，为超选择扇区的En-幺半结构提供了概念性解释。还提供了在离散群G下等变情形的推广。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 用现代预因子化代数语言重新解释和统一代数量子场论中的超选择扇区理论，为超选择扇区已知的En-幺半结构提供更深刻的概念性理解。

Method: 基于预因子化代数框架，在Haag对偶性和局部忠实真空表示的假设下，对过滤正交范畴上的AQFT构造局部常值C*-范畴预因子化代数，并利用Dunn-Lurie可加性定理分析其幺半结构。

Result: 证明了每个满足条件的AQFT都有相关联的局部常值C*-范畴预因子化代数，揭示了超选择扇区的En-幺半结构是Haag对偶性的E1-结构与洛伦兹几何的E_{n-1}-结构的结合，并推广到了离散群等变情形。

Conclusion: 该工作为AQFT中的超选择扇区提供了预因子化代数的现代视角，统一了代数方法和几何方法，并为未来研究提供了新工具。

Abstract: This paper revisits the theory of superselection sectors in algebraic quantum field theory from the modern perspective of prefactorization algebras. Under the standard assumptions of Haag duality and a locally faithful vacuum representation, it is shown that every AQFT defined over a filtered orthogonal category of spacetime regions, satisfying some mild additional geometric hypotheses, has an associated locally constant $C^\ast$-categorical prefactorization algebra of superselection sectors over the same orthogonal category. In the case of double cones in the $(n\geq 2)$-dimensional Minkowski spacetime, our approach provides a conceptual explanation for the well-known $\mathbb{E}_n$-monoidal structure on the $C^\ast$-category of superselection sectors as the combination, through Dunn-Lurie additivity $\mathbb{E}_n\simeq \mathbb{E}_1\otimes \mathbb{E}_{n-1}$, of the familiar $\mathbb{E}_1$-monoidal structure from Haag duality and an $\mathbb{E}_{n-1}$-monoidal structure from Lorentzian geometry. A refinement of our results to equivariant contexts under a discrete group $G$ is also provided.

</details>
