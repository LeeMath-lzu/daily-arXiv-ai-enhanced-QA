### [1] [On amplified graph C*-algebras as cores of Cuntz-Krieger algebras](https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.16430)
*Francesco D'Andrea,Sophie Emma Zegers*

Main category: math.OA

TL;DR: 本文从有限有向无环图R构造出两个图E_R和F_R，并证明图C*-代数C^*(F_R)同构于C^*(E_R)的AF核，应用于量子旗流形和量子泪滴等例子，并详细讨论了量子Grassmannian Gr_q(2,4)的CW结构。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 通过图C*-代数构造，研究量子旗流形和量子Grassmannian等非交换几何对象的C*-代数结构及其AF核表示。

Method: 从有限有向无环图R出发，构造加环图E_R和无限箭图F_R，证明C^*(F_R)与C^*(E_R)的AF核同构，并应用于具体量子空间。

Result: 建立了图C*-代数与AF核之间的同构关系，并成功应用于量子旗流形、量子泪滴以及量子Grassmannian Gr_q(2,4)的CW结构分析。

Conclusion: 该构造为理解量子齐性空间的C*-代数结构提供了新视角，AF核描述有助于研究其拓扑性质。

Abstract: Given a finite directed acyclic graph $R$, we construct from it two graphs $E_R$ and $F_R$, one by adding a loop at every vertex of $R$ and one by replacing every arrow of $R$ by countably infinitely many arrows. We show that the graph C*-algebra $C^*(F_R)$ is isomorphic to the AF core of $C^*(E_R)$. Examples include C*-algebras of a quantum flag manifolds and quantum teardrops. We discuss in detail the quantum Grassmannian $Gr_q(2,4)$ and use our description as AF core to study its CW-structure.

</details>

### [2] [Time-slicing quantum spacetimes](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.00520)
*Shahn Majid*

Main category: gr-qc

TL;DR: 本文提出了量子时空中的 Levi-Civita 联络构造方法，将空间切片替换为量子黎曼几何，并针对模糊球面等空间代数给出了完整解，特别在空间度量随时间旋转时得到简洁形式，同时构建了离散圆环上的 Z_n-FLRW 模型。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 在弯曲时空量子场论中，时空的类空切片（ADM分解）至关重要。作者希望将这些经典空间切片替换为量子黎曼几何，从而构建出时空量子 Levi-Civita 联络，为量子引力中的时空几何提供更一般的框架。

Method: 采用ADM形式下的量子时空构造方法，将每个空间切片视为量子黎曼几何，然后求解相应的量子 Levi-Civita 联络。针对模糊球面等空间代数进行了完全求解，并推导了空间度量按一阶ODE演化的条件。

Result: 给出了一般构造的完整解；发现当空间度量按一阶ODE演化时结果尤为简洁；对于模糊球面，空间度量需按移位向量值随时间旋转；同时构建了Z_n-FLRW模型等旋转不变时空的离散版本。

Conclusion: 该工作原则上为大多数（伪）黎曼流形提供了模糊版本，并在离散圆环上构造了新的Z_n-FLRW宇宙学模型，扩展了量子时空几何的应用范围。

Abstract: For quantum field theory on curved spacetimes, a critical role is played by their foliation into spacelike time-slices at each value $t$ of a coordinate time, with corresponding metric in ADM form. We provide a general construction for the spacetime quantum Levi-Civita connection when each spatial slice is replaced by a quantum Riemannian geometry. This is then fully solved for a class of spatial algebras including fuzzy spheres and for any time-dependent spatial quantum metric, shift 1-form and lapse function. The result takes a particularly simple form if the spatial metric evolves in time according to a first order ODE which, in the case of a fuzzy sphere, requires the spatial metric to rotate in time according to the value at each $t$ of the shift vector. As an application, our results provide in principle fuzzy versions of most (pseudo)-Riemannian manifolds. We also fully solve the case of rotationally invariant spacetimes with angular directions replaced by a discrete circle, including a new $\Bbb Z_n$-FLRW model.

</details>

### [3] [Signs, growth and admissibility of quasi-characters and the holomorphic modular bootstrap for RCFT](https://arxiv.org/abs/2507.07170)
*Arpit Das,Sunil Mukhi*

Main category: hep-th

TL;DR: 本文通过Frobenius递归关系研究模线性微分方程，证明了准特征系数在n~c/12区域具有稳定的交替符号模式，为任意Wronskian指数的有理共形场论配分函数分类提供了实用方法。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 有理共形场论的配分函数由全纯特征构成，其分类可通过全纯模自举实现。准特征系数的符号和增长对于构建具有非负整数系数的允许特征至关重要，而Cardy渐近在n~c/12区域失效，需要新的分析方法。

Method: 利用模线性微分方程的Frobenius递归关系，估计准特征系数在n~c/12区域的增长行为，并严格证明其具有稳定的交替符号模式。

Result: 成功估计了准特征系数在Cardy渐近不可达区域的增长，并证明了其符号在特定阶数稳定为交替模式。

Conclusion: 该方法为在任意Wronskian指数下获得候选RCFT配分函数提供了实用途径。

Abstract: Rational conformal field theories in 2d have partition functions built from holomorphic characters, whose classification can be addressed via the holomorphic modular bootstrap. This is facilitated by a special basis of ``quasi-characters'' that has been completely classified for rank-2. Suitably combining these to form admissible characters with non-negative integral coefficients $a_n$ depends crucially on the signs and growth of the quasi-character coefficients. We use Frobenius recursion relations for Modular Linear Differential Equations to estimate the growth with $c$ of these coefficients in the region $n\sim\frac{c}{12}$ that is inaccessible to Cardy asymptotics, and to prove rigorously that they have alternating signs that stabilise to a fixed sign at this order. This provides a practical path to obtain candidate RCFT partition functions at arbitrary Wronskian index.

</details>

### [4] [Report on $AS$-Gorenstein Hopf algebras](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.00774)
*Ken A. Brown*

Main category: math.RA

TL;DR: 这篇综述论文讨论了诺特Hopf代数是否总是具有有限内射维数及相关良好同调性质的问题，总结了30年来的研究进展、肯定性结论、开放问题，并提供了详细定义、参考文献和部分证明概要。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 探索诺特Hopf代数是否总是具有有限内射维数这一长期未决问题，以及与之相关的良好同调性质。

Method: 采用综述方法，系统梳理已有研究成果，详细讨论特定类Hopf代数的肯定性结论，描述其推论，并列出开放问题。

Result: 对于特定类别的Hopf代数，已获得肯定性答案并描述了其同调性质推论；但原始问题本身经过30年仍未完全解决。

Conclusion: 诺特Hopf代数的有限内射维数问题至今仍是开放问题，尽管在特定类别上取得了进展，但整体上仍需进一步研究。

Abstract: This is a review of progress on the question whether noetherian Hopf algebras always have finite injective dimension and related good homological properties. As well as discussing in detail the main results giving positive answers for particular classes of Hopf algebras, some consequences of such positive answers are also described. Full definitions and references are included, also sketches of some proofs. A considerable number of open questions are listed, additional to the original question, which itself remains open after 30 years.

</details>
