### [1] [The quantum group structure of long-range integrable deformations](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.27189)
*Koen Schouten,Marius de Leeuw*

Main category: math-ph

TL;DR: 本文从量子群理论角度描述了Yang-Baxter可积自旋链的长程形变，证明这些形变可通过twist量子群的代数结构实现，并揭示了Drinfeld结合子与长程相互作用项之间的联系。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 尽管长程形变在电荷层面已被充分理解，但其背后的量子群结构尚未被认识。本文旨在填补这一理论空白，提供量子群理论框架下的描述。

Method: 通过twist量子群的代数结构来构造长程形变，引入代数荷密度（algebraic charge densities）概念，分析Drinfeld结合子与非结合代数结构，并验证RLL关系和Yang-Baxter方程至一阶精度。

Result: 证明了长程形变可通过量子群twist实现；发现了非平凡Drinfeld结合子编码长程相互作用信息；构建了形变后的Lax算子和R-矩阵的显式表达式；证明了形变量子群中存在大尺度扰动结合子结构，保证了可积性。

Conclusion: 量子群理论为可积自旋链的长程形变提供了统一的代数框架，twist机制和Drinfeld结合子在其中起核心作用。

Abstract: Quantum integrable spin chains are known to possess a large family of long-range deformations generated by the local, boost and bilocal operators. Although these deformations are well-understood on the level of the pairwise commuting charges, the underlying quantum group structures had not yet been recognised. In this paper, we provide a quantum group-theoretical description for the family of long-range deformations of arbitrary homogeneous Yang-Baxter integrable spin chains up to first order in the deformation parameter. In particular, we show that the deformations are obtained via a twist of the algebraic structure of the underlying quantum group. This twisting results in a generally non-associative algebra that has a non-trivial Drinfeld associator. The Drinfeld associator is then shown to encode the information about the long-range interaction terms for the integrable spin chain. Moreover, the deformed quantum group is shown to contain a large perturbatively associative substructure, thus ensuring the perturbative integrability of the long-range model. The deformed quantum group provides explicit expressions for the Lax operators and R-matrices of the long-range deformed models, which manifestly satisfy the RLL relation and the Yang-Baxter equation up to first order in the deformation parameter. In order to derive the results, we introduce algebra elements that we call the algebraic charge densities. As a side result, we provide a conjecture for the explicit expressions of the undeformed charge densities in terms of these algebra elements.

### [2] [Projective and anomalous representations of categories and their linearizations](https://arxiv.org/abs/2506.01521)
*Domenico Fiorenza,Chetan Vuppulury*

Main category: math.CT

TL;DR: 本文研究了范畴的射影表示与异常表示之间的关系，通过将异常J与范畴的扩张C^J及其子范畴C^J_ST相关联，建立了异常表示与线性表示之间的等价关系，推广了群的射影表示与中心扩张的经典理论。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将物理学中用于线性化异常函子场论的技术推广到范畴论层面，建立射影表示与异常表示之间的系统化联系。

Method: 对任意异常J: C→2Vect，构造范畴的扩张C^J及其子范畴C^J_ST，证明异常表示等价于Vect线性函子，进而等价于"J作为标量作用"的线性表示。

Result: (i) 带有异常J的C的异常表示等价于Vect线性函子E: C^J→Vect；(ii) 这些进一步等价于C^J_ST的线性表示，其中J作为标量作用。

Conclusion: 该构造是群G的射影表示（给定2-上循环α）与中心扩张G^α的线性表示之间经典关系的多对象推广。

Abstract: We invesigate the relation between projective and anomalous representations of categories, and show how to any anomaly $J\colon \mathcal{C}\to 2\mathrm{Vect}$ one can associate an extension $\mathcal{C}^J$ of $\mathcal{C}$ and a subcategory $\mathcal{C}^J_{\mathrm{ST}}$ of $\mathcal{C}^J$ with the property that: (i) anomalous representations of $\mathcal{C}$ with anomaly $J$ are equivalent to $\mathrm{Vect}$-linear functors $E\colon \mathcal{C}^J\to \mathrm{Vect}$, and (ii) these are in turn equivalent to linear representations of $\mathcal{C}^J_{\mathrm{ST}}$ where "$J$ acts as scalars". This construction, inspired by and generalizing the technique used to linearize anomalous functorial field theories in the physics literature, can be seen as a multi-object version of the classical relation between projective representations of a group $G$, with given $2$-cocycle $α$, and linear representations of the central extension $G^α$ of $G$ associated with $α$.

### [3] [Defects in skein theory and TQFT](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.07432)
*Patrick Kinnear,Ingo Runkel*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种新的缺陷编织模（defect skein module），推广了仅允许边界点缺陷的经典情形，并证明了在半单数据标记下，该模与Carqueville-Runkel-Schaumann构造的缺陷版本Reshetikhin-Turaev TQFT的边界态空间同构。该构造通过全局化模范畴及其函子的图形演算自然实现，并将缺陷TQFT的缺陷数据类型推广到非半单情形。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有的编织模理论主要处理3维流形边界上仅有点缺陷的情况，而实际物理和数学问题中常出现线缺陷（如弦、结等）。本文旨在建立一种能同时处理边界上线缺陷和点缺陷的编织模理论，并将缺陷TQFT从半单情形推广到更一般的非半单数据。

Method: 通过将模范畴（module categories）及其函子的图形演算进行全局化（globalizing），定义含线缺陷和点缺陷的3维流形的编织模。关键步骤是构建缺陷编织模与缺陷TQFT态空间之间的同构，并突破半单限制。

Result: 1. 成功定义了含线缺陷和点缺陷的3维流形的编织模。2. 证明了半单标记下该模与Carqueville-Runkel-Schaumann缺陷TQFT的态空间同构。3. 将缺陷数据类型推广到非半单情形，拓展了缺陷TQFT的适用范围。

Conclusion: 本文建立的缺陷编织模理论统一并推广了现有的编织模与缺陷TQFT框架，为研究含缺陷的3维拓扑量子场论提供了更灵活的工具，尤其为非半单模范畴在拓扑学中的应用开辟了道路。

Abstract: Given a 3-manifold $M$ with a network of line and point defects in its boundary, we define the skein module of this configuration, generalizing the well-studied case of 3-manifolds which only admit point defects in the boundary. We prove that when all defects are labelled by semisimple data, our skein module is isomorphic to the state space of $\partial M$ in the defect version of the Reshetikhin-Turaev TQFT constructed by Carqueville-Runkel-Schaumann. Our defect skein modules follow naturally by globalizing the graphical calculus of module categories and functors thereof, and generalize the possible defect data considered in the defect TQFT beyond the semisimple case.

</details>
