### [1] [Quantum Invariants and Fiberedness](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.23700)
*Paul Orland,Toby Saunders-A'Court,Lara San Martín Suárez,Josef Svoboda*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 该论文研究了Gukov-Manolescu提出的纽结双变量级数F_K的拓扑意义，证明了其首项系数是单项式，并建立了与Hopf不变量的关系，提出了F_K的斜率猜想。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 探索纽结双变量级数F_K的拓扑意义，该级数由Gukov-Manolescu提出并由Park定义，旨在理解其在纽结理论中的几何和代数含义。

Method: 1. 证明F_K的首项系数是单项式，并用Hopf不变量表示其指数；2. 对所有齐次辫子纽结和12交叉以下的纤维化纽结进行计算验证；3. 研究非纤维化强拟正纽结中F_K与着色Jones函数稳定性级数的关系；4. 探索F_K与纽结Floer同调之间的相似性；5. 提出F_K的斜率猜想。

Result: 1. 证明了F_K的首项系数是单项式，其指数可用Hopf不变量表示；2. 推导出用着色Jones多项式表示Hopf不变量的显式公式；3. 建立了非纤维化强拟正纽结中F_K与着色Jones函数稳定性级数的关系；4. 发现了F_K与纽结Floer同调之间的相似性；5. 提出了将F_K与纽结边界斜率相关联的斜率猜想。

Conclusion: 该研究揭示了纽结双变量级数F_K的重要拓扑性质，建立了其与Hopf不变量、着色Jones多项式和纽结Floer同调的联系，并提出了新的斜率猜想，为进一步研究纽结不变量之间的关系提供了新视角。

Abstract: We explore the topological significance of the knot two-variable series $F_K$, proposed by Gukov--Manolescu and defined by Park for a class of `nice' knots. We show that the leading coefficient of $F_K$ is a monomial and express its exponent in terms of the Hopf invariant for all homogeneous braid knots and fibered knots up to 12 crossings. As an application, we deduce an explicit formula for the Hopf invariant in terms of colored Jones polynomials.
  For non-fibered strongly quasipositive knots, we study a relation between $F_K$ and the stability series of the colored Jones function, and explore similarities between $F_K$ and knot Floer homology. Finally, we propose a slope conjecture for $F_K$, relating it to the boundary slopes of the knot.

### [2] [Panhandle polynomials of torus links and geometric applications](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.22967)

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文利用量子群表示分解和Rosso-Jones公式，揭示了反向平行环面纽结和链环的HOMFLY-PT多项式具有特殊的"平底锅"形状，并建立了与几何不变量之间的联系。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究环面纽结和链环的HOMFLY-PT多项式的特殊结构，并将其几何性质与代数不变量联系起来，扩展Etnyre-Honda关于弧指数和最大Thurston-Bennequin不变量的结果。

Method: 使用量子群U_q(sl_N)基本表示的张量分解和Rosso-Jones公式，分析反向平行环面纽结和链环的HOMFLY-PT多项式结构，引入ℓ-不变量进行推广。

Result: 发现了HOMFLY-PT多项式具有"平底锅"形状的特殊结构，将Etnyre-Honda结果推广到链环，建立了与辫指数、最小弦Bennequin曲面、Bennequin锐度问题、拟正性和强拟正性等价性的联系。

Conclusion: 反向平行环面纽结和链环的HOMFLY-PT多项式具有独特的"平底锅"结构，这一代数性质与多种几何不变量密切相关，为理解环面链环的Thurston-Bennequin不变量分类提供了新视角。

Abstract: We use a decomposition of the tensor of the fundamental representation of the quantum group $U_q(\mathfrak{sl}_N)$ and the Rosso-Jones formula to establish a peculiar ``panhandle'' shape of the HOMFLY-PT polynomial of the reverse parallel of torus knots and links. Due to their panhandle-like intrinsic properties, the HOMFLY-PT polynomial is referred to as a ``panhandle polynomial''. With the help of the $\ell$-invariant, this extends to links the Etnyre-Honda result about the arc index and maximal Thurston-Bennequin invariant of torus knots. It has further geometric consequences, related to the braid index, the existence of minimal string Bennequin surfaces for banded and Whitehead doubled links, the Bennequin sharpness problem, and the equivalence of their quasipositivity and strong quasipositivity. We extend these properties to torus links, which relate to the classification of their component-wise Thurston-Bennequin invariants. Finally, we discuss the definition of the $\ell$-invariant for general links.
