*Shintarou Yanagida*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 本文构建了超对称顶点代数，通过超对称增强的Costello-Gwilliam因子化代数在超黎曼曲面上的实现，证明了超对称版本的提取定理，并将结果应用于全纯西格玛模型，识别出与手性德·拉姆复形对应的超对称顶点代数。

  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将超对称顶点代数与因子化代数框架联系起来，建立超对称版本的提取定理，从而为全纯西格玛模型等物理理论提供严格的数学基础。

Method: 引入定义在超对称圆盘上的超对称因子化代数，结合自然对称条件，证明超对称版本的Costello-Gwilliam提取定理；通过BV形式化分析全纯西格玛模型，分别处理线性靶空间与一般复靶空间的情况。

Result: 对于线性靶空间，获得了自由$bc$-$\\beta\\gamma$系统并恢复了其超对称顶点代数结构；对于一般复靶空间，将所得到的超对称顶点代数识别为手性德·拉姆复形；进一步证明Ricci-flat Kähler和hyperkähler靶空间分别给出$N=2$和$N=4$超对称增强。

Conclusion: 成功将超对称顶点代数纳入因子化代数框架，为理解全纯西格玛模型的超对称结构提供了严格途径，并建立了与已知超对称代数增强的联系。

Abstract: We construct $N=1$ supersymmetric (SUSY) vertex algebras from supersymmetric enhancements of Costello--Gwilliam factorization algebras on super Riemann surfaces. Introducing SUSY factorization algebras defined on embedded SUSY disks together with natural symmetry conditions, we prove a SUSY analogue of the Costello--Gwilliam extraction theorem. As an application, we study the holomorphic sigma model in the BV formalism. For a linear target, we obtain the free $bc$-$βγ$ system and recover its structure as a SUSY vertex algebra. For general complex targets, we describe the descent of the theory under coordinate changes and identify the resulting SUSY vertex algebra with the chiral de Rham complex. We further show that Ricci-flat Kähler and hyperkähler targets give rise to $N=2$ and $N=4$ supersymmetric enhancements introduced by Ben-Zvi--Heluani--Szczesny.

### [2] [Equivariant Quantum Cohomology of Grassmannians via the Clifford algebra](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.06352)
*Christian Korff,Mikhail Vasilev*

Main category: math.CO

TL;DR: 本文构建了格拉斯曼流形的等变量子Satake映射，将其环面等变量子上同调与射影空间的量子上同调联系起来，进而利用Clifford代数结构导出新的递归关系，为计算等变Gromov-Witten不变量提供了基于Wick定理的新方法，并证明了Graham正性等组合结果。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 建立格拉斯曼流形与射影空间之间等变量子上同调的显式联系，并利用Clifford代数结构简化等变Gromov-Witten不变量的计算，同时为组合Schubert演算提供新工具。

Method: 构造显式的等变量子Satake映射；利用外代数与Clifford代数的典范同构；通过push-pull几何映射和shuffle乘积两种方式描述作用；应用Wick定理推导递归关系。

Result: 导出了等变Gromov-Witten不变量新的递归关系；提供了等变量子Pieri规则的Graham正性的组合证明；将部分结果推广至量子三重Schubert演算。

Conclusion: 等变量子Satake映射为格拉斯曼流形的等变量子上同调研究提供了统一框架，Clifford代数方法有效简化了计算，并推动了Schubert演算的组合理解。

Abstract: We construct an explicit equivariant quantum Satake map for Grassmannians, which enables us to express their torus-equivariant quantum cohomology in terms of that of projective space. We then consider the exterior algebra of the latter, which admits a canonical identification with a Clifford algebra. We describe the resulting action in several complementary ways: first, from a geometric perspective via push-pull maps, and second, in terms of the shuffle product, which also arises in the simplest cohomological Hall algebra associated with the $A_1$-quiver. Exploiting the Clifford algebra structure, we derive new recurrence relations among equivariant Gromov-Witten invariants, yielding a new method for their computation in terms of Wick's Theorem. As an application, we provide combinatorial proofs of Graham positivity for both equivariant quantum Pieri rules, and in one case extend these results to quantum triple Schubert calculus.

### [3] [Hypergroup Symmetry in Relative Quantum Field Theories and Chiral Algebras](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.05279)
*Terry Gannon,Brandon C. Rayhaun*

Main category: hep-th

TL;DR: 本文提出了相对QFT的框架，研究二维时空中的非可逆对称性，揭示了对称性与边界条件、手征代数的深刻联系，并通过多种例子验证了理论结果。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 扩展绝对QFT中关于非可逆对称性的已知结果到相对理论，揭示相对设定中对称性的新特征与结构。

Method: 利用拓扑QFT边界理论框架，引入超群（hypergroups）和圆顶代数（dome algebras）等数学工具，建立手征代数对称性与共形嵌入之间的一一对应。

Result: 实现了左右手征代数对称性的"粘合"，建立了2D CFT边界条件与手征代数对称性的精确对应，证明了对角有理CFT中拓扑线缺陷可传递作用在边界条件上，且恒等Cardy态的g-函数最小。

Conclusion: 提供了统一的理论框架，并通过具体例子（如Haagerup融合范畴与SU(3)_1⊗(E_6)_1手征代数的关系）展示了理论的预测能力。

Abstract: A QFT is said to be relative if it lives at the boundary of a topological QFT in one higher dimension. We develop a general framework for working with noninvertible symmetries of relative theories in two spacetime dimensions, extending several well-known results for absolute QFTs. We emphasize various new features which arise in the relative setting, including the role of topological surfaces of the bulk, and the appearance of hypergroups and certain generalizations of tube algebras known as dome algebras. Our formalism is particularly well-suited for studying rational chiral algebras, where it predicts that finite symmetries are in explicit one-to-one correspondence with conformal embeddings of finite index.
  We describe several implications of our framework for absolute theories. First, we explain how to "glue" together symmetries of the left- and right-moving chiral algebras of a 2D CFT to produce topological line defects of the full theory. Second, we derive a precise correspondence between boundary conditions of a 2D CFT and symmetries of its chiral algebra. This correspondence has several structural corollaries: in diagonal rational CFTs, we demonstrate that the topological line defects of the theory act transitively on its boundary conditions, and further that the identity Cardy state has the smallest $g$-function amongst all boundary conditions, including those which only preserve Virasoro symmetry.
  We conclude by illustrating our results in a variety of examples. For instance, we show that, if there exists a rational chiral algebra with central charge $c=8$ whose modular tensor category is the Drinfeld center of the Haagerup fusion category, then it must arise as the fixed points of a rank-2 hypergroup acting on the $SU(3)_1\otimes (E_{6})_1$ chiral algebra.

</details>
