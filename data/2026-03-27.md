### [1] [Uniformity and isotypic smallness for quantum-group representations](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.24855)
*Alexandru Chirvasitu*

Main category: math.OA

TL;DR: 该论文研究了紧量子群在Banach空间上表示的范数连续性，证明了当且仅当表示具有有限谱时，紧群的表示是范数连续的。对于量子群，范数连续性可以类比地表述为表示所附映射的有界弱*-范数连续性。虽然均匀性/等型有限性等价性在一般情况下不再成立，但对于共可容或具有维数有界不可约表示的紧量子群，这种等价性仍然成立。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究紧量子群在Banach空间上表示的范数连续性条件，将经典紧群的有限谱与范数连续性等价关系推广到量子群情形，探索量子群特有的机制来恢复这种等价性。

Method: 将经典紧群表示的范数连续性条件（有限谱）类比到量子群情形，通过定义表示所附映射的有界弱*-范数连续性来刻画量子群表示的范数连续性。分析紧量子群在共可容或具有维数有界不可约表示条件下的特殊性质。

Result: 证明了对于紧量子群，均匀性/等型有限性等价性在一般情况下不再成立，但对于共可容或具有维数有界不可约表示的紧量子群，这种等价性仍然成立。这提供了两种独立的量子特定机制来恢复经典等价关系。

Conclusion: 该研究成功地将经典紧群表示的范数连续性与有限谱的等价关系推广到量子群情形，发现了量子群特有的两种机制（共可容性和维数有界不可约表示）可以恢复这种等价性，深化了对量子群表示理论的理解。

Abstract: Compact-group representations on Banach spaces are known to be norm-continuous precisely when they have finite spectra. For a quantum group with continuous-function algebra $\mathcal{C}(\mathbb{G})$ norm continuity can be cast analogously as the bounded weak$^*$-norm continuity of the representation's attached map $\mathcal{C}(\mathbb{G})^*\to \mathrm{End}(E)$. While the uniformity/isotypic finiteness equivalence no longer holds generally, it does for compact quantum groups either coamenable or having dimension-bounded irreducible representations. This generalizes the aforementioned classical variant, providing two independent quantum-specific mechanisms of recovering it.

### [2] [On the quantum $\mathfrak{sl}_3$ invariant of positive links](https://arxiv.org/abs/2508.15153)
*Matthew Harper,Efstratia Kalfagianni*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 该论文使用𝔰𝔩₃-网的绞线理论研究正链环的量子𝔰𝔩₃-链环多项式性质，给出了多项式前三项与正图图解量的显式公式，建立了正链环纤维化与多项式第二系数的等价关系，并证明第三系数为正辫子表示的障碍条件。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究正链环的量子𝔰𝔩₃-链环多项式性质，探索多项式系数与链环拓扑性质（如纤维化）以及表示形式（如正辫子表示）之间的深层联系。

Method: 采用𝔰𝔩₃-网的绞线理论框架，通过分析正链环的正图图解量，推导量子𝔰𝔩₃-链环多项式的前三项显式表达式，并建立系数与拓扑性质的对应关系。

Result: 1. 给出了正链环量子𝔰𝔩₃-链环多项式前三项的显式公式；2. 证明了正链环是纤维化的当且仅当多项式第二系数等于1；3. 发现第三系数为正辫子表示的障碍条件。

Conclusion: 量子𝔰𝔩₃-链环多项式的前三项系数编码了正链环的重要拓扑信息：第二系数完全刻画纤维化性质，第三系数则提供了链环能否用正辫子表示的判别标准，揭示了多项式系数与链环拓扑结构之间的深刻联系。

Abstract: We use the skein theory of $\mathfrak{sl}_3$-webs to study the properties of the quantum $\mathfrak{sl}_3$-link polynomial of positive links. We give explicit formulae for the three leading terms of the polynomial on positive links in terms of diagrammatic quantities of their positive diagrams. We show that a positive link is fibered if and only the second coefficient of the polynomial is equal to one. We also show that the third coefficient provides obstructions to representing links by positive braids.

### [3] [Gauging Non-Invertible Symmetries: Topological Interfaces and Generalized Orbifold Groupoid in 2d QFT](https://arxiv.org/abs/2311.17044)
*Oleksandr Diatlyk,Conghuan Luo,Yifan Wang,Quinten Weller*

TL;DR: 该论文系统研究了二维量子场论中离散广义对称性的规范理论，将传统规范理论推广到非可逆对称性，通过拓扑界面方法建立了广义规范化的物理框架。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统规范理论主要针对可逆对称性，但量子场论中存在大量非可逆的广义对称性（由拓扑缺陷线描述）。需要建立系统框架来研究这类广义对称性的规范化操作，以连接不同理论并揭示隐藏结构。

Method: 采用拓扑界面方法，将广义规范化表述为量子场论之间的拓扑界面。利用融合范畴的代数对象和模范畴数学概念，通过自举分析分类拓扑界面，并在具体共形场论中验证。

Result: 成功将传统规范理论的所有性质推广到非可逆对称性情况。建立了广义规范化的系统框架，分类了可能的拓扑界面，发现了新的自对偶性，并识别了描述拓扑界面融合的广义轨道群群胚结构。

Abstract: Gauging is a powerful operation on symmetries in quantum field theory (QFT), as it connects distinct theories and also reveals hidden structures in a given theory. We initiate a systematic investigation of gauging discrete generalized symmetries in two-dimensional QFT. Such symmetries are described by topological defect lines (TDLs) which obey fusion rules that are non-invertible in general. Despite this seemingly exotic feature, all well-known properties in gauging invertible symmetries carry over to this general setting, which greatly enhances both the scope and the power of gauging. This is established by formulating generalized gauging in terms of topological interfaces between QFTs, which explains the physical picture for the mathematical concept of algebra objects and associated module categories over fusion categories that encapsulate the algebraic properties of generalized symmetries and their gaugings. This perspective also provides simple physical derivations of well-known mathematical theorems in category theory from basic axiomatic properties of QFT in the presence of such interfaces. We discuss a bootstrap-type analysis to classify such topological interfaces and thus the possible generalized gaugings and demonstrate the procedure in concrete examples of fusion categories. Moreover we present a number of examples to illustrate generalized gauging and its properties in concrete conformal field theories (CFTs). In particular, we identify the generalized orbifold groupoid that captures the structure of fusion between topological interfaces (equivalently sequential gaugings) as well as a plethora of new self-dualities in CFTs under generalized gaugings.

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
