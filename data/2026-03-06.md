  <summary>Details</summary>

*Bowen Li,Gongxiang Liu*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 该论文研究了任意余拟Hopf代数上Nichols代数的反射理论，推广了先前局限于点余半单情形的结果。通过建立有理Yetter-Drinfeld模范畴间的辫子幺半等价性，证明了有限维不可约Yetter-Drinfeld模的反射性质可导出半Cartan图。并以秩三Nichols代数为应用实例，证明其产生标准Cartan图，且为仿射Nichols代数。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Method: 通过建立有理Yetter-Drinfeld模范畴间的辫子幺半等价性（利用对偶对），证明有限维不可约Yetter-Drinfeld模的反射性质可导出半Cartan图。并以文献[41]中的秩三Nichols代数为具体实例进行验证。

Abstract: We investigate the reflection theory of Nichols algebras over arbitrary coquasi-Hopf algebras with bijective antipode, generalizing previous results restricted to the pointed cosemisimple setting [47]. By establishing a braided monoidal equivalence between categories of rational Yetter-Drinfeld modules via a dual pair, we demonstrate that a tuple of finite-dimensional irreducible Yetter-Drinfeld modules admitting all reflections gives rise to a semi-Cartan graph. <details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
