### [1] [Zhu algebras of superconformal vertex algebras](https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.13124)
*Ryo Sato,Shintarou Yanagida*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 本文展示了黄永忠提出的任意顶点代数朱代数定义的优势，通过两个目标：(1)确定了N=1,2,3,4和大N=4超共形顶点代数的朱代数；(2)引入了N_K=N超对称顶点代数的朱代数。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 展示黄永忠提出的任意顶点代数（不一定配备哈密顿算子或Virasoro元素）的朱代数定义的优势。

Method: 使用黄永忠提出的任意顶点代数的朱代数定义，应用于超共形顶点代数和超对称顶点代数。

Result: 成功确定了N=1,2,3,4和大N=4超共形顶点代数的朱代数结构，并引入了N_K=N超对称顶点代数的朱代数。

Conclusion: 黄永忠的朱代数定义在处理任意顶点代数时具有显著优势，能够成功应用于超共形和超对称顶点代数的研究。

Abstract: The purpose of this note is to demonstrate the advantages of Y.-Z.~Huang's definition of the Zhu algebra
  (Comm.\ Contemp.\ Math., 7 (2005), no.~5, 649--706) for an arbitrary vertex algebra, not necessarily equipped with a Hamiltonian operator or a Virasoro element, by achieving the following two goals: (1) determining the Zhu algebras of $N=1, 2, 3, 4$ and big $N=4$ superconformal vertex algebras, and (2) introducing the Zhu algebras of $N_K=N$ supersymmetric vertex algebras.

### [2] [Poisson Vertex Algebra of Seiberg-Witten Theory](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.03500)
*Ahsan Z. Khan*

Main category: hep-th

TL;DR: 该论文研究了四维N=2纯SU(2)规范理论中，与全纯-拓扑超荷Q上同调相关的泊松顶点代数结构，提出了一个明确的泊松顶点代数A，并探讨了其与微扰理论、Schur指数以及非微扰修正的关系。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究四维N=2规范理论中全纯-拓扑可观测量的代数结构，特别是纯SU(2)规范理论中与Q上同调相关的泊松顶点代数，旨在理解微扰和非微扰效应对这些代数结构的影响。

Method: 提出一个明确的泊松顶点代数A，声称它与所有阶微扰理论中的全纯-拓扑可观测代数同构。计算A的Hilbert-Poincaré级数，并引入一个额外的微分Q_inst来捕捉非微扰修正，计算该微分的上同调。

Result: A的Hilbert-Poincaré级数细化了纯SU(2)理论的Schur指数。通过引入Q_inst微分并计算其上同调，得到了一个明确的候选空间，用于描述Seiberg-Witten理论中的非微扰全纯-拓扑可观测量。

Conclusion: 该研究为四维N=2纯SU(2)规范理论中的全纯-拓扑可观测量的代数结构提供了明确的描述，将微扰理论结果与Schur指数联系起来，并通过引入额外的微分提出了处理非微扰修正的方法，为理解Seiberg-Witten理论中的非微扰效应提供了具体候选。

Abstract: The space of local operators in the $Q$-cohomology of the holomorphic-topological supercharge in a four-dimensional $\mathcal{N}=2$ theory carries the structure of a Poisson vertex algebra. This note studies the Poisson vertex algebra associated to the pure $\mathcal{N}=2$ gauge theory with gauge group $SU(2)$. We propose an explicit Poisson vertex algebra $A$, claimed to be isomorphic to the algebra of holomorphic-topological observables to all orders in perturbation theory. We compute the Hilbert-Poincaré series of $A$ and show that it refines the Schur index of the pure $SU(2)$ theory. We show that $A$ admits a further differential $Q_{\text{inst}}$ which we hypothesize captures non-perturbative corrections, and compute the cohomology of this differential. We thus present an explicit candidate for the space of non-perturbative holomorphic-topological observables of Seiberg-Witten theory.

### [3] [Genus Zero Kashiwara-Vergne Solutions from Braids](https://arxiv.org/abs/2507.16243)
*Zsuzsanna Dancso,Iva Halacheva,Guillaume Laplante-Anfossi,Marcy Robertson,Chandan Singh*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 该论文使用moperads语言重新解释Alekseev-Enriquez-Torossian的Kashiwara-Vergne解构造，建立了从辫子moperad到弦图moperad的等价与KV解之间的联系。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 重新解释Alekseev-Enriquez-Torossian关于从结合子构造Kashiwara-Vergne解的方法，使用moperads语言提供更清晰的理论框架。

Method: 采用moperads（operad上右模范畴中的幺半群）的语言，研究带冻结弦的括号化辫子moperad与弦图moperad之间的等价关系，分析这些等价如何产生KV解。

Result: 证明：1）两个moperad之间的任何等价都会产生由单个经典KV解操作生成的零亏格KV解族；2）Grothendieck-Teichmüller模群作用于后者，与KV对称群作用交织；3）任何对称KV解都会产生从带冻结弦的括号化辫子到自由李代数切向自同构的模映射。

Conclusion: 该映射通过弦图moperad分解当且仅当相关的KV结合子是Drinfeld结合子，建立了KV解理论与Drinfeld结合子理论之间的深刻联系。

Abstract: Using the language of moperads -- monoids in the category of right modules over an operad -- we reinterpret the Alekseev--Enriquez--Torossian construction of Kashiwara--Vergne (KV) solutions from associators. We show that any equivalence between the moperad of parenthesized braids with a frozen strand and the moperad of chord diagrams gives rise to a family of genus zero KV solutions operadically generated by a single classical KV solution. We show that the Grothendieck--Teichmüller module groups act on the latter, intertwining the actions of the KV symmetry groups. In the other direction, we show that any symmetric KV solution gives rise to a module map from parenthesized braids with a frozen strand to tangential automorphisms of free Lie algebras. This map factors through the moperad of chord diagrams if and only if the associated KV associator is a Drinfeld associator.

### [4] [Monoidal Jantzen filtrations](https://arxiv.org/abs/2402.13544)
*Ryo Fujita,David Hernandez*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 该论文在具有一般辫子的幺正阿贝尔范畴框架下引入了Jantzen滤过的幺正类似物，导致Grothendieck环乘法的变形，并证明在许多重要情况下这种变形是结合的，从而实现了Grothendieck环的量子化。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 为Grothendieck环提供统一的表示论解释，建立代数几何构造与表示论构造之间的联系，并研究表示的同调结构。

Method: 在具有一般辫子的幺正阿贝尔范畴中引入Jantzen滤过的幺正类似物，通过这种滤过构造Grothendieck环乘法的变形，证明这种变形在多种重要情况下的结合性。

Result: 1. 对于单连通量子环代数有限维表示，证明了结合性，且得到的量子化与Nakajima和Varagnolo-Vasserot几何构造的量子Grothendieck环一致；2. 对于对称箭图Hecke代数有限维模的幺正范畴，建立了类似结果；3. 获得了关于表示同调结构的各种应用。

Conclusion: 该工作为量子Grothendieck环提供了统一的表示论解释，建立了代数几何构造与表示论构造之间的对应关系，并发展了研究表示同调结构的新方法。

Abstract: We introduce a monoidal analogue of Jantzen filtrations in the framework of monoidal abelian categories with generic braidings. It leads to a deformation of the multiplication of the Grothendieck ring. We conjecture, and we prove in many remarkable situations, that this deformation is associative so that our construction yields a quantization of the Grothendieck ring as well as analogs of Kazhdan-Lusztig polynomials. As a first main example, for finite-dimensional representations of simply-laced quantum loop algebras, we prove the associativity and we establish that the resulting quantization coincides with the quantum Grothendieck ring constructed by Nakajima and Varagnolo-Vasserot in a geometric manner. Hence, it yields a unified representation-theoretic interpretation of the quantum Grothendieck ring. As a second main example, we establish an analogous result for a monoidal category of finite-dimensional modules over symmetric quiver Hecke algebras categorifying the coordinate ring of a unipotent group associated with a Weyl group element. We obtain various applications, in particular on the homological structure of representations.

### [5] [From BV-BFV Quantization to Reshetikhin-Turaev Invariants](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.04556)
*Nima Moshayedi*

Main category: math-ph

TL;DR: 该论文提出一个连接Chern-Simons理论的微扰BV-BFV量子化与非微扰Reshetikhin-Turaev不变量之间的桥梁程序，通过E_n-代数的分解同调与特征栈的导出代数几何实现。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 弥合Chern-Simons理论的微扰BV-BFV量子化与非微扰Reshetikhin-Turaev不变量之间的理论鸿沟，建立两者之间的数学联系，为拓扑量子场论提供统一框架。

Method: 通过E_n-代数的分解同调和特征栈的导出代数几何作为桥梁，提出七个猜想，包括主要猜想断言BV-BFV与RT构造作为(3-2-1)-扩展拓扑量子场论的自然等价性，开发通过移位辛栈的形变量子化的证明策略，并阐明E_n-Koszul对偶在翻译微扰与非微扰数据中的作用。

Result: 在阿贝尔情形、低亏格情形和Seifert纤维化情形中检验了支持证据，讨论了与复现理论、范畴化和几何Langlands纲领的联系，但承认仍有重要的技术缺口需要填补。

Conclusion: 提出了一个连接微扰与非微扰量子场论的理论框架，为理解Chern-Simons理论的量子化提供了新的视角，但该方案仍处于猜想阶段，需要进一步的数学发展来完善证明。

Abstract: We propose a program for bridging the gap between the perturbative BV-BFV quantization of Chern-Simons theory and the non-perturbative Reshetikhin-Turaev (RT) invariants of 3-manifolds, passing through factorization homology of $\mathbb{E}_n$-algebras and the derived algebraic geometry of character stacks. We conjecture that the modular tensor category underlying the RT construction arises as the $\mathbb{E}_2$-category from BV-BFV quantization of Chern-Simons theory on the disk, with the derived character stack $\mathrm{Loc}_G(Σ)$ and its shifted symplectic structure mediating the proposed identification. We formulate seven conjectures, including a main conjecture asserting natural equivalence of the BV-BFV and RT constructions as (3-2-1)-extended topological quantum field theories, develop a proof strategy via deformation quantization of shifted symplectic stacks, and clarify the role of $\mathbb{E}_n$-Koszul duality in translating between perturbative and non-perturbative data. Supporting evidence is examined in the abelian, low-genus, and Seifert fibered cases. Connections to resurgence, categorification, and the geometric Langlands program are discussed as further motivation, though significant technical gaps remain open.

</details>
