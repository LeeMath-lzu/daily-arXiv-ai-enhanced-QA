<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Method: 采用系列研究中建立的微分光滑性分析方法，针对Bell和Smith描述的3维斜多项式环族进行具体研究

Result: 论文得出了这类3维斜多项式环族的微分光滑性特征，为理解非交换代数族的几何性质提供了新的结果

Conclusion: 该研究扩展了对非交换代数族微分光滑性的理解，特别是对Bell和Smith提出的3维斜多项式环族，为相关领域的进一步研究奠定了基础

Abstract: This paper is part of a series of papers in which we have investigated the differential smoothness of families of noncommutative algebras. Here, we consider this topic for the family 3-dimensional skew polynomial rings characterized by Bell and Smith \cite{BellSmith1990}.

### [2] [The Einstein condition for quantum irreducible flag manifolds](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.11786)
*Marco Matassa*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 量子不可约旗流形在量子化参数经典值附近的小开区间内满足爱因斯坦条件的类似物


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究量子群和量子几何中的爱因斯坦条件，探索量子流形上的曲率性质

Method: 利用量子代数相关的规范构造，如微分计算和双模连接，这些由不同作者先前引入

Result: 证明任何量子不可约旗流形在量子化参数经典值附近的小开区间内满足爱因斯坦条件的类似物

Conclusion: 量子不可约旗流形在量子化参数经典值附近具有与经典爱因斯坦条件类似的性质

Abstract: We show that any quantum irreducible flag manifold satisfies an analogue of the Einstein condition, expressing proportionality between the Ricci tensor and the metric, at least in a small open interval around the classical value of the quantization parameter. This makes use of various canonical constructions associated to these algebras, such as differential calculi and bimodule connections, which were previously introduced by various authors.

</details>


### [3] [A construction of vertex algebra bundles on logarithmic smooth curves](https://arxiv.org/abs/2502.03175)
*Xi-Chuan Tan*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 该论文构建了对数光滑曲线族上的顶点代数丛和共形块空间，推广了Frenkel和Ben-Zvi在复光滑代数曲线上的结果


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 推广Frenkel和Ben-Zvi关于复光滑代数曲线上顶点代数丛的结果到更一般的对数光滑曲线族，处理更复杂的几何结构

Method: 构建对数光滑曲线族上的顶点代数丛和共形块空间，建立真空传播的较弱版本，并在典型节点曲线上计算共形块空间

Result: 成功构建了对数光滑曲线族上的顶点代数丛和共形块空间，建立了真空传播的较弱版本，并计算了节点曲线上共形块空间的具体例子

Conclusion: 该工作将顶点代数丛和共形块理论推广到更一般的对数光滑曲线族，为处理具有奇点的曲线提供了理论框架

Abstract: We present a construction of vertex algebra bundles and spaces of conformal blocks over families of logarithmic smooth curves. This work generalizes some earlier results by Frenkel and Ben-Zvi on vertex algebra bundles over complex smooth algebraic curves. We establish a weaker version of the propagation of vacua, and compute the space of conformal blocks over a typical example of a nodal curve.

</details>

### [4] [Spectral finiteness, quantum norm continuity and classical points](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.12090)
*Alexandru Chirvasitu*

Main category: math.FA

TL;DR: 该论文证明了紧量子群在希尔伯特或巴拿赫空间上表示的各种一致连续性概念等价于具有有限谱（即有限多个等型分量），推广了紧群在巴拿赫空间上表示的经典类似结果。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究紧量子群表示的一致连续性性质，建立与有限谱条件的等价关系，推广经典紧群表示理论到量子群框架。

Method: 利用黎曼-勒贝格型衰减性质，分析紧量子群函数代数与最小张量积中元素的傅里叶系数，证明各种一致连续性概念与有限谱条件的等价性。

Result: 证明了紧量子群在希尔伯特或巴拿赫空间上表示的各种一致连续性概念等价于具有有限谱，即表示只有有限多个等型分量。

Conclusion: 该研究成功将经典紧群表示理论中的一致连续性与有限谱等价关系推广到紧量子群框架，为量子群表示理论提供了新的分析工具和结果。

Abstract: We prove various notions of uniform continuity for compact-quantum-group representations on Hilbert or Banach spaces equivalent to having finite spectrum, i.e. finitely many isotypic components. This generalizes the classical analogue for compact-group representations on Banach spaces, and relies in part on Riemann-Lebesgue-type decay properties for Fourier coefficients of elements in minimal tensor products with compact-quantum-group function algebras.

</details>
