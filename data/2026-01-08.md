Main category: hep-th

TL;DR: 该论文研究多体系统哈密顿量的本征函数，这些哈密顿量源于DIM代数的交换子代数，本征函数可由非对称Macdonald多项式构建，为可积系统理论提供了新维度。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究广义(a-twisted) Cherednik算子构造的多体系统哈密顿量的本征函数，探索非对称多项式在可积系统理论中的作用，扩展传统对称多项式理论。

Method: 基于DIM代数交换子代数构造哈密顿量，利用扭曲Baker-Akhiezer函数作为基态本征函数，通过具有通用系数的展开式构建一般本征函数，从非对称Macdonald多项式推导构造公式。

Result: 建立了非对称Macdonald多项式与广义Cherednik算子本征函数之间的联系，提出了从非对称Macdonald多项式构造这些本征函数的通用公式，揭示了扭曲参数a仅隐藏在基态本征函数中的特性。

Conclusion: 该研究为可积系统理论引入了非对称多项式的新视角，扩展了由对称Macdonald多项式、非扭曲Baker-Akhiezer函数和Noumi-Shiraishi级数组成的三元体系，提供了理论新维度。

Abstract: We consider eigenfunctions of many-body system Hamiltonians associated with generalized (a-twisted) Cherednik operators used in construction of other Hamiltonians: those arising from commutative subalgebras of the Ding-Iohara-Miki (DIM) algebra. The simplest example of these eigenfunctions is provided by non-symmetric Macdonald polynomials, while generally they are constructed basing on the ground state eigenfunction coinciding with the twisted Baker-Akhiezer function being a peculiar (symmetric) eigenfunction of the DIM Hamiltonians. Moreover, the eigenfunctions admit an expansion with universal coefficients so that the dependence on the twist $a$ is hidden only in these ground state eigenfunctions, and we suggest a general formula that allows one to construct these eigenfunctions from non-symmetric Macdonald polynomials. This gives a new twist in theory of integrable systems, which usually puts an accent on symmetric polynomials, and provides a new dimension to the {\it triad} made from the symmetric Macdonald polynomials, untwisted Baker-Akhiezer functions and Noumi-Shiraishi series.

### [2] [On the Strong Unital Property for the Affine VOAs](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.04187)
*Angela Cai*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 该论文研究了顶点算子代数（VOA）表示中模式转移代数的强单位性，特别针对仿射sl2 VOA。证明了在非临界水平下，通用仿射VOA的模式转移代数不具有强单位性，但为特定水平1的简单VOA构造了显式强单位。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 顶点算子代数表示在共形块层构造中有重要应用。当模式转移代数具有强单位性时，相关的共形块层是向量丛而非仅仅是凝聚层。然而，强单位性在实际中难以验证，且具有其他重要含义。

Method: 针对仿射sl2 VOA，作者构造了L_{\widehat{\mathfrak{sl}_2}}(1,0)（水平1的简单仿射VOA）的显式强单位。同时证明了对于通用仿射VOA，在任意非临界水平k ≠ -2下，模式转移代数都不具有强单位性。

Result: 成功为水平1的简单仿射sl2 VOA构造了显式强单位。证明了通用仿射sl2 VOA在非临界水平下（k ≠ -2）的模式转移代数永远不具有强单位性。

Conclusion: 强单位性是顶点算子代数表示理论中的重要性质，影响共形块层的结构。对于仿射sl2 VOA，强单位性只在特定情况下存在：通用VOA在非临界水平下不具有强单位性，但特定简单VOA（如水平1）可以构造显式强单位。

Abstract: Representations of vertex operator algebras $V$ (VOAs) have numerous applications, including the construction of sheaves of conformal blocks on moduli spaces of curves. For a $V$-module $W = \oplus W_d$, a sequence of associative algebras $\mathfrak{A}_d$ acts on each graded component $W_d$. When these $d$th-mode transition algebras $\mathfrak{A}_d$ are strongly unital - meaning they are unital with units acting as the identity on $W_d$ - the associated sheaves of conformal blocks are vector bundles rather than merely coherent sheaves. This strong unital property, while difficult to verify in practice, has other important implications as well. Here we construct explicit strong units for $L_{\widehat{\mathfrak{sl}_2}}(1,0)$, the simple affine VOA for $\mathfrak{sl}_2$ at level $1$, and establish that mode transition algebras for universal affine VOAs for $\mathfrak{sl}_2$ are never strongly unital at any level $k$ not equal to the critical level $-2$.

### [4] [Generalized digroups, di-skew braces, and solutions of the set-theoretic Yang-Baxter equation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.15387)

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 该论文引入了一种称为di-skew brace的新代数结构，用于系统地从广义双群构造出集合论Yang-Baxter方程的双射非退化解，并研究了这些解的结构特性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究集合论Yang-Baxter方程的解是数学物理中的重要课题，特别是寻找系统构造双射非退化解的方法。论文旨在通过新的代数结构来统一和扩展现有的解构造方法。

Method: 引入di-skew brace代数结构，通过广义双群系统构造Yang-Baxter方程的解。研究这些解的左派生架结构，证明它们属于共轭架类。将解分解为具有特定兼容作用的斜括号解的半半直积。

Abstract: We introduce a novel algebraic structure called di-skew brace by which we show that generalized digroups systematically yield bijective, non-degenerate solutions to the set-theoretic Yang-Baxter equation. Consistently, we show that these solutions belong to a broader class that includes skew brace solutions. ### [6] [Weight filtration of Hurwitz spaces and quantum shuffle algebras](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.03871)
*Zhao Yu Ma*

Main category: math.NT

TL;DR: 该论文建立了原始双代数滤过与可分解反常层滤过之间的等价关系，并将量子洗牌代数的字长滤过对应到反常层的余维数滤过，同时发现反常层的几何权滤过对应到量子洗牌代数的代数权滤过。通过比较定理应用于Hurwitz空间，利用代数权滤过显式计算了上同调权。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 扩展Kapranov-Schechtman的结果，建立原始双代数与可分解反常层之间的滤过等价关系，并应用于Hurwitz空间的上同调权计算。

Method: 1. 证明原始双代数滤过与可分解反常层滤过的等价性；2. 将量子洗牌代数的字长滤过对应到反常层的余维数滤过；3. 定义量子洗牌代数的代数权滤过对应反常层的几何权滤过；4. 证明Hurwitz空间在有限域和复数域上的权滤过比较定理；5. 利用代数权滤过显式计算Hurwitz空间的上同调权。

Abstract: We prove an equivalence between filtrations of primitive bialgebras and filtrations of factorizable perverse sheaves, generalizing the results obtained by Kapranov-Schechtman. Furthermore, we find that the geometric weight filtration of factorizable perverse sheaves corresponds to a filtration on quantum shuffle algebras which has not been previously defined in the literature, and we call this the algebraic weight filtration. This allows us to determine the cohomological weights for Hurwitz spaces explicitly using the algebraic weight filtration of the corresponding quantum shuffle algebra. As a consequence, we find that most weights of Hurwitz spaces are smaller than expected from cohomological degree, and we prove explicit nontrivial upper bounds for weights in some cases, such as when $G=S_3$ and $c$ is the conjugacy class of transpositions.
