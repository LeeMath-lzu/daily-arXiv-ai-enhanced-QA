### [1] [Intersecting Surface Operators in 6d Holomorphic Field Theories](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.26080)
*Meer Ashwinkumar*

Main category: hep-th

TL;DR: 本文研究了六维全纯场论中曲面算子的相交结构，发现其关联函数在交点处会产生类似有理R-矩阵的局域算子，并证明了该算子满足Yang-Baxter型关系，揭示了与量子可积结构的深刻联系。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 旨在通过研究六维全纯场论中曲面算子的相交行为，揭示其背后隐藏的量子可积结构，特别是探索Costello预言的R-矩阵与弦理论中可积性的联系。

Method: 1. 在六维全纯Chern-Simons理论中计算曲面算子相交关联函数，导出R-矩阵形式的局域算子；2. 验证该算子满足Yang-Baxter型关系；3. 通过算子乘积展开(OPE)推导手征代数的余积结构；4. 将方法推广到六维全纯BF理论，分析其R-矩阵型算子。

Result: 1. 在六维全纯Chern-Simons理论中，曲面算子相交点处出现与有理R-矩阵准经典展开主导项形式一致的局域算子；2. 该算子满足Yang-Baxter型关系；3. 导出了曲面算子支撑的手征代数的余积结构；4. 在六维全纯BF理论中同样得到了R-矩阵型局域算子的主导形式。

Conclusion: 六维全纯场论中的曲面算子相交结构天然地编码了量子可积系统的核心代数结构，特别是R-矩阵和Yang-Baxter关系。将BF理论置于扭量空间时，其自对偶Yang-Mills描述中的相应算子有望成为量子可积结构的局域构建基元。

Abstract: We study intersecting surface operators in 6d holomorphic field theories with the aim of unraveling associated quantum integrable structures. We first study the intersections of surface operators in 6d holomorphic Chern-Simons theory on $\mathbb{C}^3$. Computing their correlation function, we find a local operator at the intersection of the surface operators with a form reminiscent of the leading nontrivial term in the quasi-classical expansion of a rational $R$-matrix, as predicted by Costello. We provide evidence that this $R$-matrix-like operator satisfies a Yang-Baxter-type relation. We then derive the associated coproduct of the chiral algebra supported by surface operators from their OPE. We also study intersecting surface operators in 6d holomorphic BF theory and derive the local leading form of the corresponding $R$-matrix-like operator. When this theory is placed on twistor space, where it describes the self-dual sector of 4d Yang-Mills theory, this operator is expected to provide a local building block for quantum integrable structures anticipated in that setting.

### [3] [Difference $2$-algebras and difference $A_\infty$-algebras](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.25587)
*Apurba Das*

Main category: math.RA

TL;DR: 本文引入了结合2-代数上的差分算子，并证明了差分结合2-代数范畴与2-项差分A∞-代数范畴等价，同时用第三上循环和差分代数的交叉模分别刻画了骨架和严格的2-项差分A∞-代数，最后定义了差分代数上的2-项同伦双模并构造了2-项差分A∞-代数。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将差分算子从结合代数推广到高维代数结构（结合2-代数和A∞-代数），建立不同范畴间的等价关系，并利用上同调和交叉模工具刻画差分结构。

Method: 通过Maurer-Cartan特征引入A∞-代数上的差分算子；构建范畴等价；利用第三上循环和交叉模进行分类刻画；通过2-项同伦双模构造差分A∞-代数。

Result: 证明了差分结合2-代数范畴与2-项差分A∞-代数范畴等价；给出了骨架2-项差分A∞-代数与第三上循环、严格2-项差分A∞-代数与交叉模的对应关系；从2-项同伦双模出发构造了2-项差分A∞-代数。

Conclusion: 本文为差分代数的高维推广提供了系统的理论框架，建立了不同代数结构之间的联系，丰富了差分算子的代数理论。

Abstract: A difference operator on an associative algebra is an algebraic abstraction of the forward and backward difference operators. In this paper, we first introduce difference operators on associative $2$-algebras and consider the category of difference associative $2$-algebras. Subsequently, we also introduce difference operators on a given $A_\infty$-algebra in terms of their Maurer-Cartan characterization. We prove that the category of difference associative $2$-algebras and the category of $2$-term difference $A_\infty$-algebras are equivalent. We characterize skeletal and strict $2$-term difference $A_\infty$-algebras by respectively third cocycles and crossed modules of difference algebras. Finally, we define the notion of a $2$-term bimodule up to homotopy over a difference algebra, which in turn yields a construction of a $2$-term difference $A_\infty$-algebra.

</details>

### [4] [Irremediably singular quantum branched covers](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.24007)
*Alexandru Chirvasitu*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 本文证明了有理参数下的量子球面在特定条件下可对偶化为分支覆盖，并揭示了其经典性与C*丛局部平凡性之间的刚性现象。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究非交换球面在有理形变参数下的对偶化性质，探索其是否能作为量子分支覆盖嵌入到局部平凡的C*丛中。

Method: 利用量子3-球面的Heegaard分解（两个实心环的推出）作为证明刚性的核心工具。

Result: 只有当球面是经典时，这些C*丛才是局部平凡的；而所有情况下这些丛在维度分层上都是有限类型的。

Conclusion: Natsume-Olsen非交换球面在有理参数下可对偶化为量子分支覆盖，但嵌入到局部平凡C*丛的条件强制球面为经典情形，体现了刚性。

Abstract: We prove that the Natsume-Olsen non-commutative spheres $\mathbb{S}^{2n-1}_θ$ dualize for rational deformation parameters to provide examples of quantum branched covers over their respective centers' maximal spectra, embeddable into locally trivial $C^*$ bundles precisely when said spheres are classical; this, despite the bundles in question being of finite type over dimensional strata. The proof of the noted rigidity phenomenon relies on the quantum analogue of the 3-sphere's Heegaard splitting as a pushout of two solid tori.

</details>


### [5] [An abelian formula for the quantum Weyl group action of the coroot lattice](https://arxiv.org/abs/2501.02365)
*S. Gautam,V. Toledano-Laredo*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 本文给出了量子环代数Uq(Lg)上余根格Q的量子Weyl群作用的显式公式，该公式用交换生成元表示，并通过Chari-Pressley级数表达，同时证明了该级数的强理性，并将其应用于Nakajima箭图簇的等变K理论中。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 为量子环代数Uq(Lg)的余根格Q的量子Weyl群作用提供显式公式，并建立其与Nakajima箭图簇等变K理论中行列式线丛作用的联系。

Method: 利用Chari-Pressley级数及其强理性结果，结合量子环代数的交换生成元，推导出量子Weyl群作用的显式表达式。

Result: 得到了量子Weyl群作用的显式公式，并证明了Chari-Pressley级数的强理性性质。

Conclusion: 该公式将量子Weyl群作用与Nakajima箭图簇等变K理论中的行列式线丛作用等同起来。

Abstract: Let g be a complex simple Lie algebra and Uq(Lg) its quantum loop algebra, where q is not a root of unity. We give an explicit formula for the quantum Weyl group action of the coroot lattice Q of g on finite-dimensional representations of Uq(Lg) in terms of its commuting generators. The answer is expressed in terms of the Chari-Pressley series, whose evaluation on highest weight vectors gives rise to Drinfeld polynomials. It hinges on a strong rationality result for that series, which is derived in the present paper. As an application, we identify the action of Q on the equivariant K-theory of Nakajima quiver varieties with that of explicitly given determinant line bundles.

</details>
