### [1] [Quantum group origins of edge states in double-scaled SYK](https://arxiv.org/abs/2503.20691)
*Andreas Belaey,Thomas G. Mertens,Thomas Tappeiner*

Main category: hep-th

TL;DR: 该论文改进了DSSYK中的量子群结构描述，并应用于体引力理论，解释了体离散化、推导了振幅，并实现了体希尔伯特空间的因子化。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: DSSYK已知具有量子群理论基础，但需要更精确地定位其量子群结构，以便更好地应用于体引力理论。

Method: 利用量子群理论框架，通过表示不可约性解释体离散化，使用量子群特征插入推导振幅，并通过体纠缠面上的边缘自由度实现希尔伯特空间因子化。

Result: 改进了DSSYK的量子群结构描述，简化了振幅计算，实现了体希尔伯特空间的因子化，并将方法扩展到$\mathcal{N}=1$ DSSYK。

Conclusion: 量子群理论为DSSYK提供了强大的数学框架，能够统一处理体引力应用，包括离散化、振幅计算和希尔伯特空间因子化。

Abstract: Double-scaled SYK (DSSYK) is known to have an underlying quantum group theoretical description. We precisely pinpoint the quantum group structure, improving upon earlier work in the literature. This allows us to utilize this framework for bulk gravitational applications. We explain bulk discretization in DSSYK from the underlying irreducibility of the representations. We derive trumpet and brane amplitudes using character insertions of the quantum group, simplifying earlier calculations. Most importantly, we factorize the bulk Hilbert space dual to DSSYK in the quantum group description using a complete set of edge degrees of freedom living at a bulk entangling surface. An analogous treatment for $\mathcal{N}=1$ DSSYK is provided in the same quantum group theoretical framework.

### [2] [Mirabolic Hecke algebras, Schur-Weyl duality and Frobenius character formulas](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.00603)
*Jinkui Wan*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 本文为mirabolic Hecke代数引入了新的表示和基，构造了其cocenter的基以定义特征表，建立了与量子群的Schur-Weyl对偶，并推导了特征公式和递归计算规则。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究mirabolic Hecke代数的表示理论，特别是其特征表的构造和计算问题，建立与量子群的联系，为特征计算提供有效方法。

Method: 首先为mirabolic Hecke代数引入新的表示和基；在复数域上构造cocenter的基以定义特征表；建立与量子群U_q(gl_r)的Schur-Weyl对偶；应用对偶得到对称函数环中的Frobenius特征公式；推导递归的Murnaghan-Nakayama规则。

Result: 成功构造了mirabolic Hecke代数的新表示和基；定义了特征表；建立了与量子群的Schur-Weyl对偶；得到了不可约特征的Frobenius公式；推导了特征表的递归计算规则。

Conclusion: 本文为mirabolic Hecke代数提供了完整的表示理论框架，包括特征表的定义、计算方法和与量子群的联系，为相关代数结构的研究提供了有力工具。

Abstract: We first introduce a new presentation for the mirabolic Hecke algebra $\mathscr{H}_{n,R}(q)$ over an arbitrary commutative ring $R$ and derive a new basis. Based on this presentation, specializing to the case of $\mathscr{H}_n(q)$ over the field $\mathbb{C}(q)$, we construct a basis for the cocenter of $\mathscr{H}_n(q)$, which facilitates the definition of its character table. We further establish a Schur--Weyl duality between $\mathscr{H}_n(q)$ and the quantum group $U_q(\mathfrak{gl}_r)$. As an application, we obtain Frobenius character formulas for the irreducible characters of $\mathscr{H}_n(q)$ within the ring of symmetric functions. Finally, we derive a recursive Murnaghan--Nakayama rule for the computation of the character table.

</details>
