### [1] [Minimal Factorization of Chern-Simons Theory -- Gravitational Anyonic Edge Modes](https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.00501)
*Thomas G. Mertens,Qi-Feng Wu*

Main category: hep-th

TL;DR: 该论文提出了一种用于Chern-Simons理论的最小化边缘模式构造方法，特别应用于三维引力理论，通过量子群边缘模式分解体态空间，与Bekenstein-Hawking熵和拓扑纠缠熵的关系一致。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究规范理论中纠缠分析的方法，特别是通过将拓扑量子场论嵌入到具有边缘模式的因子化理论中。对于Chern-Simons理论，需要找到与拓扑不变性兼容的最小边缘模式集合。

Method: 构造了与Chern-Simons理论拓扑不变性兼容的最小边缘模式集合，提出了最小因子化映射。这些边缘模式可以解释为量子群上粒子的自由度。特别关注三维引力作为SL(2,R)×SL(2,R)规范群的Chern-Simons理论。

Result: 最小因子化方案唯一地产生了量子群边缘模式，分解了三维引力的体态空间。这与早期将三维引力中的Bekenstein-Hawking熵与拓扑纠缠熵相关联的提议一致。

Conclusion: 通过构造最小边缘模式，为Chern-Simons理论提供了新的因子化方法，特别在三维引力理论中验证了量子群边缘模式的有效性，支持了Bekenstein-Hawking熵与拓扑纠缠熵之间的关系。

Abstract: One approach to analyzing entanglement in a gauge theory is embedding it into a factorized theory with edge modes on the entangling boundary. For topological quantum field theories (TQFT), this naturally leads to factorizing a TQFT by adding local edge modes associated with the corresponding CFT. In this work, we instead construct a minimal set of edge modes compatible with the topological invariance of Chern-Simons theory. This leads us to propose a minimal factorization map. These minimal edge modes can be interpreted as the degrees of freedom of a particle on a quantum group. Of particular interest is three-dimensional gravity as a Chern-Simons theory with gauge group SL$(2,\mathbb{R}) \times$ SL$(2,\mathbb{R})$. Our minimal factorization proposal uniquely gives rise to quantum group edge modes factorizing the bulk state space of 3d gravity. This agrees with earlier proposals that relate the Bekenstein-Hawking entropy in 3d gravity to topological entanglement entropy.

### [2] [Kohn--Nirenberg quantization of the affine group and related examples](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.08274)
*Pierre Bieliavsky,Victor Gayral,Sergey Neshveyev,Lars Tuset*

Main category: math.OA

TL;DR: 该论文展示了如何为一类与仿射群相似的半直积构造酉对偶2-上循环，主要应用于李代数为Frobenius海藻的李群，基于表示理论和Kohn-Nirenberg型量化方法。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究一类与仿射群结构相似的半直积群的酉对偶2-上循环构造问题，这类群在数学物理中具有重要应用价值，特别是来自Frobenius海藻李代数的李群。

Method: 将半直积G=H⋉V表示为双交叉积G=P⋈N，利用表示理论分析其唯一平方可积不可约表示的结构，并构建与标量傅里叶变换相关的Kohn-Nirenberg型量化方法。

Result: 成功构造了这类半直积群的酉对偶2-上循环，证明了其唯一平方可积不可约表示具有特别简洁的形式，并建立了与dressing变换相关的傅里叶变换联系。

Conclusion: 该研究为Frobenius海藻李代数相关的李群提供了系统的酉对偶2-上循环构造方法，深化了对这类群表示理论和量化结构的理解。

Abstract: We show how to construct unitary dual $2$-cocycles for a class of semidirect products that exhibit many similarities with the affine group ${\rm Aff}(V)=\GL(V)\ltimes V$ of a finite dimensional vector space over a local skew field. The primary source of examples comes from Lie groups whose Lie algebras are Frobenius seaweeds. The construction builds on our earlier results and relies heavily on representation theory and an associated quantization procedure of Kohn--Nirenberg type.
  On the technical side, the key point is the observation that any semidirect product $G=H\ltimes V$ in our class can be presented as a double crossed product $G=P\bowtie N$ with respect to which the unique square-integrable irreducible representation of $G$ takes a particularly nice form. The Kohn--Nirenberg quantization that we construct is intimately related to a scalar Fourier transform $\CF\colon L^2(N)\to L^2(P)$ intertwining the left regular representations of $P$ and $N$ with representations defined by the dressing transformations.

### [3] [Classification of prime modules of quantum affine algebras corresponding to 2-column tableaux](https://arxiv.org/abs/2406.16879)
*Nick Early,Jian-Rong Li*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 该论文研究了量子仿射代数A型有限维单模与矩形形状半标准杨表的对应关系，重点分类了对应2列半标准杨表的所有素模，并对多列情况给出了素模的充分条件猜想。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 量子仿射代数的有限维单模与半标准杨表之间存在对应关系，特别是矩形形状的杨表。然而，对于这些模的素性（primeness）分类尚不完全清楚。本文旨在解决对应2列半标准杨表的素模分类问题，并为多列情况提供理论框架。

Method: 作者基于量子仿射代数A型有限维单模与矩形半标准杨表的对应理论，首先分类了所有对应2列半标准杨表的素模（基于一个猜想性质）。然后通过理论分析，提出了一个猜想性的充分条件，用于判断对应多于两列的杨表的模是否为素模。

Result: 1. 在假设某个猜想性质成立的前提下，完全分类了所有对应2列半标准杨表的素模；2. 提出了一个猜想性的充分条件，用于判断对应多于两列杨表的模是否为素模。

Conclusion: 该研究推进了量子仿射代数A型有限维单模素性的分类理论，为2列情况提供了完整分类（依赖于一个猜想），并为更一般的多列情况建立了理论框架，为进一步研究这类模的素性性质奠定了基础。

Abstract: Finite dimensional simple modules of quantum affine algebras of type A correspond to semistandard Young tableaux of rectangular shapes. In this paper, we classify all prime modules corresponding to 2-column semistandard Young tableaux, up to a conjectural property. Moreover, we give a conjectural sufficient condition for a module corresponding to a tableau with more than two columns to be prime.

### [4] [On Dehornoy's representation for the Yang-Baxter equation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2409.10648)
*Carsten Dietzel,Edouard Feingesicht,Silvia Properzi*

Main category: math.GR

TL;DR: 本文研究了与Yang-Baxter方程集合论解相关的结构群和类Coxeter群的Dehornoy单项式表示，证明了这些表示不可约性与底层解不可分解性的等价关系（除Dehornoy类为2的情况外）。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究Yang-Baxter方程集合论解相关的结构群和类Coxeter群的表示理论，特别是Dehornoy单项式表示的性质，探索这些表示与底层解的代数结构之间的关系。

Method: 利用这些群的brace结构和循环集语言，分析Dehornoy单项式表示。通过代数方法证明表示不可约性与解不可分解性的等价关系，并对不可分解解的情况构造一维表示。

Result: 证明了关联单项式表示的不可约性等价于底层解的不可分解性（Dehornoy类为2的情况除外）。对于不可分解解，这些表示可以从某些显式构造的一维表示诱导得到。

Conclusion: Dehornoy单项式表示的性质与Yang-Baxter方程集合论解的代数结构密切相关，为理解这些群表示与底层解之间的关系提供了理论框架。

Abstract: This article investigates Dehornoy's monomial representations for structure groups and Coxeter-like groups associated with a set-theoretic solution to the Yang--Baxter equation. Using the brace structure of these groups and the language of cycle sets, we prove that the irreducibility of the associated monomial representations is equivalent to the indecomposability of the underlying solutions, except when the Dehornoy class is two. For indecomposable solutions, we show that these representations are induced from certain explicitly constructed one-dimensional representations.

</details>
