### [1] [Finiteness of specializations of the $q$-deformed modular group at roots of unity](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.08439)
*Takuma Byakuno,Xin Ren,Kohji Yanagawa*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 该论文研究了q-变形模群的有限性条件，证明了当且仅当q取特定单位根时，相关群结构是有限的，并给出了与二元多面体群的同构关系。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究Morier-Genoud和Ovsienko引入的q-变形模群的有限性条件，探索该群在特殊参数值下的代数结构特性。

Method: 通过分析群G_q在复数参数ζ下的具体化G_q(ζ)，研究其有限性条件，建立与单位根ζ_n的对应关系，并证明与二元多面体群的同构。

Result: 证明了PSL_q(2,Z)在q=ζ时有限当且仅当G_q(ζ)有限，当且仅当ζ是n=2,3,4,5次单位根。当n=3,4时与二元四面体群同构，n=5时与二元二十面体群同构，n=6时群无限但"温和"。

Conclusion: q-变形模群仅在特定单位根参数下呈现有限结构，这些有限群与经典二元多面体群有深刻联系，为有理链环的Jones多项式特殊值等应用提供了理论基础。

Abstract: Recently, Morier-Genoud and Ovsienko introduced the $q$-deformed modular group.
  For construction, they first gave a group $G_q \subset \operatorname{GL}(2, {\mathbb Z}[q^{\pm}])$ and then set $\operatorname{PSL}_q(2,{\mathbb Z}):=G_q/Z(G_q)$. We show that for $ζ\in {\mathbb C}^*$, $\operatorname{PSL}_q(2,{\mathbb Z})|_{q=ζ}$ is finite, if and only if so is $G_q(ζ):=G_q|_{q=ζ} \subset \operatorname{GL}(2,{\mathbb C})$, if and only if $ζ=ζ_n$ for $n=2,3,4,5$, where $ζ_n$ is a primitive $n$-th root of unity. Moreover, $G_q(ζ_n) \cap \operatorname{SL}(2,\mathbb{C})$ is isomorphic to the binary tetrahedral group (resp. the binary icosahedral group), if $n=3,4$ (resp. $n=5$). When $n=6$, the groups are infinite, but still "mild". We also give several applications (e.g., the special values of the normalized Jones polynomials of rational links).

</details>

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 该论文将fc-多重范畴（虚拟双重范畴）应用于辛几何，在拉格朗日子流形的伪全纯多边形模空间上建立了fc-多重范畴结构，并发展了微分分级版本，为A∞型结构提供了统一的operadic表述。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Method: 1. 在辛流形中拉格朗日子流形序列上的伪全纯多边形模空间集合上建立自然的fc-多重范畴结构；2. 发展微分分级版本的fc-多重范畴理论；3. 证明各种A∞型结构（如A∞代数、A∞（双）模、A∞范畴等）都可以统一表述为微分分级fc-多重范畴上的代数。

Result: 成功在伪全纯多边形模空间上建立了fc-多重范畴结构，该结构是Fukaya范畴构造的基础。发展了微分分级fc-多重范畴理论，并证明A∞代数、A∞（双）模、A∞范畴（可能弯曲）等各种A∞型结构都可以统一表示为微分分级fc-多重范畴上的代数。

Conclusion: 该研究将fc-多重范畴应用于辛几何，为Fukaya范畴的构造提供了operadic框架，并通过发展微分分级fc-多重范畴理论，为各种A∞型结构提供了统一的operadic表述，连接了几何构造与代数结构。

Abstract: Operads often arise from geometry. The standard $A_\infty$ operad can be derived from the cellular chains on the Stasheff associahedra, and an $A_\infty$ algebra is an algebra over this operad. The notion of an $\mathbf{fc}$-multicategory, also called a virtual double category, is a two-dimensional generalization of operads and multicategories. Here $\mathbf{fc}$ stands for the free category monad.

These moduli spaces are known to underlie the construction of Fukaya categories. Based on this, we develop the theory of differential graded (dg) variants of $\mathbf{fc}$-multicategories and show that a broad range of $A_\infty$-type structures, such as $A_\infty$ algebras, $A_\infty$ (bi)modules, and $A_\infty$ categories (possibly curved), admit a uniform operadic formulation as algebras over dg $\mathbf{fc}$-multicategories.

### [7] [Skein theory for the Links-Gould polynomial](https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.19251)

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 该论文基于Marin和Wagner的工作，为Links-Gould多项式不变量建立了三次辫型skein理论，证明可用于计算任意有向链环，并发现该skein理论与V1多项式共享，从而证明两个链环多项式的等价性。
