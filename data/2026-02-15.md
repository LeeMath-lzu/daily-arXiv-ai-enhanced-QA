*Yingjin Bi*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 该论文为简单、单连通、单根的代数群G构造了扭曲旗簇乘积坐标环的幺半范畴化，并定义了玻色扩张代数的子代数作为其量子化。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究扭曲旗簇乘积的坐标环的范畴化和量子化，这类簇包括辫子簇和限制双Bruhat胞腔等重要几何对象。

Method: 为简单、单连通、单根的代数群G构造了扭曲旗簇乘积坐标环的幺半范畴化，并定义了玻色扩张代数$\widehat{\mathcal{A}}$的自然子代数。

Result: 成功实现了扭曲旗簇乘积坐标环的幺半范畴化，并证明所定义的子代数提供了该坐标环的量子化。

Abstract: For a simple, simply-connected, simply-laced algebraic group $G$, we construct a monoidal categorification of the coordinate ring of twisted products of flag varieties. This class of varieties includes, in particular, braid varieties and restricted double Bruhat cells. In addition, we define a natural subalgebra of the bosonic extension algebra $\widehat{\mathcal{A}}$ and show that this subalgebra provides a quantization of the coordinate ring of twisted products of flag varieties.

### [3] [Refined half-integer condition on RG flows](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.12085)
*Ken Kikuchi*

Main category: hep-th

TL;DR: 该论文研究重整化群流中对称性范畴的额外结构（如辫子结构）如何提供比传统't Hooft反常更强的约束条件，特别是半整数条件及其细化。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统上重整化群流主要受对称性的't Hooft反常约束，但对称性范畴可能具有额外结构（如辫子结构），这些结构可能提供更强的约束条件，需要系统研究这些额外结构对重整化群流的影响。

Method: 利用对称性范畴的辫子结构，分析重整化群缺陷存在时紫外和红外共形维度的关系，推导半整数条件的必要条件和细化版本。

Result: 发现辫子结构表征的共形维度在重整化群流中满足半整数条件：h_c^UV + h_c^IR ∈ ½ℤ，并找到了该和为半整数的必要条件，应用细化条件解决了一些具体的重整化群流。

Conclusion: 对称性范畴的辫子结构为重整化群流提供了比传统反常更强的约束条件，半整数条件及其细化版本是研究重整化群流的重要工具，有助于理解量子场论中对称性结构的深层含义。

Abstract: Renormalization group flows are constrained by symmetries. Traditionally, we have made the most of 't Hooft anomalies associated to the symmetries. The anomaly is mathematically part of the data for the monoidal structure on symmetry categories. The symmetry categories sometimes admit additional structures such as braiding. It was found that the additional structures give further constraints on renormalization group flows. One of these constraints is the half-integer condition. The condition claims the following. Braidings are characterized by conformal dimensions. A symmetry object $c$ in a braided symmetry category surviving all along the flow thus has two conformal dimensions, one in ultraviolet $h_c^\text{UV}$ and the other in infrared $h_c^\text{IR}$. In a renormalization group flow with a renormalization group defect, they add up to a half-integer $h_c^\text{UV}+h_c^\text{IR}\in\frac12\mathbb Z$. We find a necessary condition for the sum to be half-integer. We solve some flows with the refined half-integer condition.

</details>

### [7] [The Dehn Twist Action for Quantum Representations of Mapping Class Groups](https://arxiv.org/abs/2311.16020)
*Lukas Müller,Lukas Woike*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 该论文计算了非半单模范畴的共形块空间上的Dehn扭转作用，给出了闭曲面映射类群表示下Dehn扭转的阶，并应用于Johnson核和Torelli群的可见性分析。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究非半单模范畴中Dehn扭转在共形块空间上的作用，推广半单情况下的结果，建立与ribbon扭转阶的关系，并应用于映射类群的表示理论。

Method: 计算Dehn扭转在共形块空间上的作用，分析非分离和分离简单闭曲线的情况，建立Dehn扭转阶与ribbon扭转阶的关系，使用正则自同态的双辫子结构。

Result: 对于非分离曲线，Dehn扭转的阶等于ribbon扭转的阶；对于分离曲线，阶由正则自同态的张量幂的ribbon扭转阶表示；Johnson核平凡作用的条件是正则自同态的ribbon扭转和与自身的双辫子都平凡。

Conclusion: 该工作将De Renzi等人的小量子群结果推广到一般非半单模范畴，建立了Dehn扭转阶与ribbon扭转阶的精确关系，为映射类群在共形块上的表示提供了新的结构理解。

Abstract: We calculate the Dehn twist action on the spaces of conformal blocks of a not necessarily semisimple modular category. In particular, we give the order of the Dehn twists under the mapping class group representations of closed surfaces. In the separating case, we express the order using the order of the ribbon twist on monoidal powers of the canonical end. As an application, we prove that the Johnson kernels of the mapping class groups act trivially if and only if for the canonical end the ribbon twist and double braiding with itself are trivial. We give a similar result for the visibility of the Torelli groups.
