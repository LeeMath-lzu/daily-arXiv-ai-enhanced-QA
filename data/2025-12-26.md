<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Method: 1. 证明移位iYangians代数量化了来自ADE型仿射Grassmannian的固定点轨迹；2. 在AI型中识别islices与BCD型幂零Slodowy切片的关系；3. 提出从分裂/非分裂Satake框架双箭图产生正交-辛（混合）Coulomb分支的框架。

In this paper, we show that ${}^\imath Y_μ$ quantizes the involutive fixed point locus ${}^\imath W_μ$ arising from affine Grassmannians of type ADE, and supply strong evidence toward the expectation that ${}^\imath Y_μ^λ$ quantizes a top-dimensional component of the affine Grassmannian islice ${}^\imath\overline{W}_μ^λ$. We propose a framework for producing ortho-symplectic (and hybrid) Coulomb branches from split (and nonsplit) Satake framed double quivers, which are conjectured to relate closely to the islices ${}^\imath\overline{W}_μ^λ$ and the algebras ${}^\imath Y_μ^λ$.

### [2] [Canonical differential calculi via functorial geometrization](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.20742)
*Keegan J. Flood,Gabriele Lobbia,Giacomo Tendas*

Main category: math.CT

TL;DR: 该论文建立了一个框架，将微分计算从代数推广到单子范畴中的幺半群，统一了光滑环、交换代数和结合代数的微分理论。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 论文旨在统一不同数学背景下的微分计算理论，包括光滑环上的de Rham复形、交换代数的Kähler微分和结合代数的通用微分计算，为这些理论提供一个共同的范畴论框架。

Method: 通过建立忠实同构纤维化到幺半加性范畴中幺半群范畴的条件，构造从范畴E到一阶微分计算范畴的典范函子，并推广"最大延拓"过程得到微分计算范畴的函子。

Result: 获得了范畴E到微分计算范畴的典范函子，统一了多种微分理论，并自然地导出了光滑映射、微分同胚等概念以及函子性de Rham理论。

Conclusion: 该理论为非交换几何中的微分计算提供了从结合代数到幺半加性范畴中幺半群的推广框架，建立了不同微分理论之间的比较映射关系。

Abstract: Given a category $\mathcal{E}$, we establish sufficient conditions on a faithful isofibration $\mathcal{E}\rightarrow\operatorname{Mon}(\mathcal{V})$ valued in the category of monoids internal to a monoidal additive category $\mathcal{V}$ such that $\mathcal{E}$ admits a canonical functor to the category of first order differential calculi in $\mathcal{V}$. Generalizing the procedure of extending a first order differential calculus to its maximal prolongation to this setting, we obtain a canonical functor from $\mathcal{E}$ to the category of differential calculi in $\mathcal{V}$. This yields a simultaneous generalization of the de Rham complex on $C^{\infty}$-rings, the Kähler differentials on commutative algebras, and the universal differential calculus on associative algebras. As a consequence, such categories $\mathcal{E}$ admit natural analogues of the notions of smooth map and diffeomorphism, as well as a functorial de Rham theory. Moreover, whenever two such faithful isofibrations to $\operatorname{Mon}(\mathcal{V})$ factor suitably, their corresponding de Rham functors are related via a comparison map. Developing this theory requires first extending the noncommutative geometry formalism of differential calculi from associative algebras to the setting of monoids internal to monoidal additive categories.

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Method: 基于Positselski的反导出范畴理论，定义了适当的同调框架，构造了控制形变的Hochschild复形，并证明了多个重要代数类别的形式性定理。

Result: 证明了Fréchet代数、de Rham代数和Dolbeault代数的形式性定理；对于复流形情况，Hochschild上同调等价于Kontsevich扩展形变复形、光滑射影簇导出范畴的Hochschild上同调以及Gualtieri广义复流形形变复形；计算了多种矩阵分解范畴的连续Hochschild上同调。

Conclusion: 成功建立了完备局部凸（弯曲）微分分次代数形变理论的同调框架，为几何代数的形变研究提供了统一的理论工具，并将多个重要几何对象的形变理论联系起来。

Abstract: We define the appropriate homological setting to study deformation theory of complete locally convex (curved) dg-algebras based on Positselski's contraderived categories. We define the corresponding Hochschild complex controlling deformations and prove formality theorems for the Fréchet algebras of smooth functions on a manifold, the de Rham algebra and for the Dolbeault algebra of a complex manifold. In the latter case, the Hochschild cohomology is equivalent to Kontsevich's extended deformation complex, the Hochschild cohomology of the derived category in case $X$ is a smooth projective variety and to Gualtieri's deformation complex of $X$ viewed as generalized complex manifold. We also compute the continuous Hochschild cohomology for various categories of matrix factorisations.

### [5] [Freidel-Maillet type equations on fused K-matrices over the positive part of $U_q(\widehat{\mathfrak{sl}}_2)$](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.00819)
*Chenwei Ruan*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 该论文研究了量子包络代数U_q^+的反射方程表示，通过融合技术构造任意维度的K-矩阵，并证明它们满足Freidel-Maillet型方程。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究量子包络代数U_q^+的反射方程表示，特别是K-矩阵的构造和性质，为相关代数结构提供新的理解和工具。

Method: 采用Freidel-Maillet型反射方程表示，利用q-洗牌代数嵌入，结合Terwilliger、Damiani和Beck的PBW基，通过Ruan的统一方法，使用自然融合技术构造任意维度的融合K-矩阵。

Result: 成功构造了任意有意义的维度的融合K-矩阵的闭式表达式，并证明了任意一对这样的融合K-矩阵都满足Freidel-Maillet型方程。

Conclusion: 该研究为量子包络代数的反射方程表示提供了系统化的构造方法，建立了融合K-矩阵的理论框架，扩展了相关代数结构的研究工具。

Abstract: The positive part $U_q^+$ of the quantized enveloping algebra $U_q(\widehat{\mathfrak{sl}}_2)$ has a reflection equation presentation of Freidel-Maillet type, due to Baseilhac 2021. This presentation involves a K-matrix of dimension $2 \times 2$. Under an embedding of $U_q^+$ into a $q$-shuffle algebra due to Rosso 1995, this K-matrix can be written in closed form using a PBW basis for $U_q^+$ due to Terwilliger 2019. This PBW basis, together with two PBW bases due to Damiani 1993 and Beck 1994, can be obtain from a uniform approach by Ruan 2025. Following a natural fusion technique, we will construct fused K-matrices of arbitary meaningful dimension in closed form using the uniform approach. We will also show that any pair of these fused K-matrices satisfy Freidel-Maillet type equations.

</details>
