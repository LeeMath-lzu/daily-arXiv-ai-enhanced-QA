### [1] [Bicovariant Codifferential Calculi](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.12493)
*Andrzej Borowiec,Patryk Mieszkalski*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 该论文研究一阶余微分计算（FOCCs）的分类问题，特别关注双协变余微分计算在Hopf代数上的分类，并将其与量子李代数和量子向量场联系起来。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究Doi和Quillen在循环上同调背景下提出的一阶余微分计算（FOCCs）的分类问题。特别关注双协变余微分计算在Hopf代数上的分类，并探索其与Woronowicz构造的量子切空间的关系。

Method: 通过将余代数上的FOCCs分类问题简化为通用双余模中子双余模的分类问题。利用一维生成空间（单例）来完成任务。对于双协变余微分计算，将其分类问题进一步简化为Yetter-Drinfeld子模的分类问题。论文识别了Hopf代数上两种相互对偶的Y-D结构。

Result: 证明了双协变余微分计算更适合Drinfeld-Jimbo型量子包络代数，因为它们与矩阵量子群上的Woronowicz双协变计算是对偶的。展示了与量子李代数和量子向量场的关系，并在多个例子中呈现了分类结果。

Conclusion: 该研究为FOCCs提供了系统的分类框架，特别强调了双协变余微分计算在量子群理论中的重要性。通过建立与Woronowicz构造的联系，表明这类余微分计算更适合量子包络代数，为量子几何结构的研究提供了新视角。

Abstract: We develop a technique for studying first-order codifferential calculi (FOCCs) initiated by Doi and Quillen in the context of cyclic cohomology. Their classification, for a given coalgebra, reduces to the classification of subbicomodules in the universal bicomodule. For completing this task, the role of one-dimensional generating spaces (a.k.a. singletons) is found to be useful. We are particularly interested in classifying bicovariant codifferential calculi, which we define over Hopf algebras. This, in turn, can be reduced to classifying Yetter-Drinfeld (Y-D) submodules. In fact, there are two, mutually dual, Y-D structures on arbitrary Hopf algebra: one used by Woronowicz for constructing bicovariant differential calculi, and the another used here for FOCCs and shown to be related with Woronowicz construction of quantum tangent space. This argues that such codifferential calculi are better suited to Drinfeld-Jimbo type quantized enveloping algebras, as they are dual to Woronowicz' bicovariant calculi over matrix quantum groups. Relations with quantum Lie algebras and quantum vector fields are also shown. Some classification results are presented in numerous examples.

### [2] [Classification of prime modules of quantum affine algebras corresponding to 2-column tableaux](https://arxiv.org/abs/2406.16879)
*Nick Early,Jian-Rong Li*

Main category: math.QA

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 量子仿射代数的有限维表示理论中，简单模与半标准杨表之间存在对应关系。对于矩形形状的杨表，特别是2列杨表对应的模的素性分类问题尚未完全解决，需要进一步研究其结构性质。

Method: 作者基于量子仿射代数表示理论，针对对应于2列半标准杨表的模，在假设某个猜想性质成立的前提下，对这些模进行了完整的素性分类。同时，对于超过2列的杨表对应的模，提出了一个判定其素性的充分条件猜想。

Result: 1. 在假设某个猜想性质成立的前提下，完全分类了所有对应于2列半标准杨表的素模；2. 提出了一个关于多列（超过2列）杨表对应模素性的充分条件猜想。

Conclusion: 该工作推进了量子仿射代数A型表示理论中模的素性研究，为2列杨表对应的模提供了完整的分类结果（依赖于一个猜想），并为更一般情况下的素性判定提供了理论框架和猜想。

Abstract: Finite dimensional simple modules of quantum affine algebras of type A correspond to semistandard Young tableaux of rectangular shapes. In this paper, we classify all prime modules corresponding to 2-column semistandard Young tableaux, up to a conjectural property. Moreover, we give a conjectural sufficient condition for a module corresponding to a tableau with more than two columns to be prime.

### [3] [Minimal Factorization of Chern-Simons Theory -- Gravitational Anyonic Edge Modes](https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.00501)
*Thomas G. Mertens,Qi-Feng Wu*

Main category: hep-th

TL;DR: 该论文提出了一种用于Chern-Simons理论的最小化边缘模构造方法，通过最小因子化映射实现拓扑不变性，并将三维引力作为SL(2,R)×SL(2,R)的Chern-Simons理论进行应用。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究规范理论中纠缠分析的方法，特别是在拓扑量子场论中，传统方法通过添加与CFT相关的局部边缘模来实现因子化，但作者希望构造与Chern-Simons理论拓扑不变性兼容的最小边缘模集合。

Method: 提出最小因子化映射方法，构造与Chern-Simons理论拓扑不变性兼容的最小边缘模集合。这些最小边缘模可以解释为量子群上粒子的自由度。特别关注三维引力作为SL(2,R)×SL(2,R)的Chern-Simons理论。

Result: 最小因子化方案唯一地产生了量子群边缘模，这些边缘模因子化了三维引力的体态空间。这与早期将3d引力中的Bekenstein-Hawking熵与拓扑纠缠熵联系起来的研究提案一致。

Conclusion: 通过构造与Chern-Simons理论拓扑不变性兼容的最小边缘模，实现了规范理论的因子化分析，为三维引力中的纠缠熵研究提供了新的理论框架，并验证了Bekenstein-Hawking熵与拓扑纠缠熵之间的关系。

Abstract: One approach to analyzing entanglement in a gauge theory is embedding it into a factorized theory with edge modes on the entangling boundary. For topological quantum field theories (TQFT), this naturally leads to factorizing a TQFT by adding local edge modes associated with the corresponding CFT. In this work, we instead construct a minimal set of edge modes compatible with the topological invariance of Chern-Simons theory. This leads us to propose a minimal factorization map. These minimal edge modes can be interpreted as the degrees of freedom of a particle on a quantum group. Of particular interest is three-dimensional gravity as a Chern-Simons theory with gauge group SL$(2,\mathbb{R}) \times$ SL$(2,\mathbb{R})$. Our minimal factorization proposal uniquely gives rise to quantum group edge modes factorizing the bulk state space of 3d gravity. This agrees with earlier proposals that relate the Bekenstein-Hawking entropy in 3d gravity to topological entanglement entropy.

### [4] [On Dehornoy's representation for the Yang-Baxter equation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2409.10648)
*Carsten Dietzel,Edouard Feingesicht,Silvia Properzi*

Main category: math.GR

TL;DR: 该研究探讨了与Yang-Baxter方程集合论解相关的结构群和类Coxeter群的Dehornoy单项式表示，证明了表示不可约性与解不可分解性的等价关系（除Dehornoy类为2的情况外），并展示了不可分解解对应的表示可由一维表示诱导得到。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究Dehornoy单项式表示的性质，特别是其不可约性与Yang-Baxter方程集合论解的不可分解性之间的关系，旨在深入理解结构群和类Coxeter群的表示理论。

Method: 利用这些群的brace结构，通过cycle set语言，分析Dehornoy单项式表示的性质，证明不可约性与不可分解性的等价关系，并构造一维表示来诱导这些表示。

Result: 证明了关联的单项式表示的不可约性等价于底层解的不可分解性（除Dehornoy类为2的情况外），对于不可分解解，这些表示可由明确构造的一维表示诱导得到。

Conclusion: Dehornoy单项式表示的性质与Yang-Baxter方程集合论解的结构密切相关，不可约表示对应不可分解解，且可通过一维表示诱导构造，为理解这些群的表示理论提供了重要见解。

Abstract: This article investigates Dehornoy's monomial representations for structure groups and Coxeter-like groups associated with a set-theoretic solution to the Yang--Baxter equation. Using the brace structure of these groups and the language of cycle sets, we prove that the irreducibility of the associated monomial representations is equivalent to the indecomposability of the underlying solutions, except when the Dehornoy class is two. For indecomposable solutions, we show that these representations are induced from certain explicitly constructed one-dimensional representations.

### [5] [Kohn--Nirenberg quantization of the affine group and related examples](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.08274)
*Pierre Bieliavsky,Victor Gayral,Sergey Neshveyev,Lars Tuset*

Main category: math.OA

TL;DR: 该论文展示了如何为一类半直积群构造酉对偶2-上循环，这些群与仿射群有相似性，主要例子来自李代数为Frobenius seaweed的李群。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究一类与仿射群相似的半直积群的酉对偶2-上循环构造，为李代数为Frobenius seaweed的李群提供具体例子。

Method: 利用表示理论和Kohn-Nirenberg型量化程序，将半直积群表示为双交叉积，通过标量傅里叶变换连接左正则表示和dressing变换表示。

Result: 成功构造了酉对偶2-上循环，并建立了Kohn-Nirenberg量化与标量傅里叶变换之间的紧密联系。

Conclusion: 该工作扩展了酉对偶2-上循环的构造方法，为相关数学物理问题提供了新的工具和视角。

Abstract: We show how to construct unitary dual $2$-cocycles for a class of semidirect products that exhibit many similarities with the affine group ${\rm Aff}(V)=\GL(V)\ltimes V$ of a finite dimensional vector space over a local skew field. The primary source of examples comes from Lie groups whose Lie algebras are Frobenius seaweeds. The construction builds on our earlier results and relies heavily on representation theory and an associated quantization procedure of Kohn--Nirenberg type.
  On the technical side, the key point is the observation that any semidirect product $G=H\ltimes V$ in our class can be presented as a double crossed product $G=P\bowtie N$ with respect to which the unique square-integrable irreducible representation of $G$ takes a particularly nice form. The Kohn--Nirenberg quantization that we construct is intimately related to a scalar Fourier transform $\CF\colon L^2(N)\to L^2(P)$ intertwining the left regular representations of $P$ and $N$ with representations defined by the dressing transformations.

</details>
