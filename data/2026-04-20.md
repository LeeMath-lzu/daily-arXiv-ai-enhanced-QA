### [1] [$D$-bialgebras, dendrification and embeddings into AWB of almost Poisson algebras](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.15346)
*Sami Mabrouk*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 该论文研究了代数与括号结构，引入了几乎泊松Drinfel'd双代数概念，建立了匹配对、Manin三元组与几乎泊松D-双代数的等价关系，并定义了新的几乎三叉泊松代数结构。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究带有括号的代数结构，作为几乎泊松代数的非交换推广，探索其与Drinfel'd双代数理论的联系，并建立相关的代数结构理论框架。

Method: 1. 引入几乎泊松Drinfel'd双代数概念；2. 建立匹配对、Manin三元组与几乎泊松D-双代数的等价关系；3. 定义几乎三叉泊松代数结构；4. 通过平均算子和相对平均算子将几乎泊松代数嵌入到AWB结构中。

Result: 1. 证明了匹配对、Manin三元组与几乎泊松D-双代数之间的等价性；2. 定义了新的几乎三叉泊松代数结构；3. 证明了每个几乎泊松代数可以通过平均算子嵌入到AWB中，更一般地可以通过相对平均算子实现嵌入。

Conclusion: 该工作系统地发展了几乎泊松代数的相关理论，建立了与Drinfel'd双代数、匹配对、Manin三元组等结构的联系，并提出了新的代数结构，为相关领域的研究提供了理论框架。

Abstract: An algebra with bracket ({\sf AWB} for short) is an associative algebra endowed with a bilinear bracket satisfying a Leibniz-type compatibility condition, as introduced in \cite{casas}. It can be viewed as a noncommutative generalization of an almost Poisson algebra; indeed, when the associative product is commutative and the bracket is skew-symmetric, one recovers the notion of an almost Poisson algebra. In this paper, we introduce the notion of {almost Poisson Drinfel'd bialgebras ($D$-bialgebras)} as an analogue of Poisson $D$-bialgebras, and we establish the equivalence between matched pairs, Manin triples, and almost Poisson $D$-bialgebras. Furthermore, we define a new algebraic structure, called {almost tridendriform Poisson algebras}, which can be regarded as the underlying algebraic structures associated with relative Rota-Baxter operators on almost Poisson algebras. Finally, we show that every almost Poisson algebra can be embedded into an algebra with bracket ({\sf AWB}) via averaging operators, and more generally via relative averaging operators associated to a given representation of the almost Poisson algebra.

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Method: 结合Batalin-Vilkovisky形式体系和调和分析，在λ-Minkowski空间上构建立方标量场理论。使用两种不同的L∞代数结构：辫子L∞代数和经典L∞代数，分别对应两种不同的量子场论。

### [5] [Chevalley property of module-finite Hopf algebras and discriminant ideals](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.15986)
*Yimin Huang,Tiancheng Qi,Quanshui Wu,Ruipeng Zhu*

Main category: math.RA

TL;DR: 该论文利用判别式理想研究Cayley-Hamilton Hopf代数的Chevalley性质，建立了不可约表示的张量非退化行为与判别式理想之间的联系，并解决了Huang-Mi-Qi-Wu提出的问题。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究Cayley-Hamilton Hopf代数的Chevalley性质，特别是通过判别式理想来刻画这一性质，解决Huang-Mi-Qi-Wu提出的问题，并为构造具有Chevalley性质和有限GK维数的Hopf代数提供方法。

Method: 使用判别式理想作为主要工具，分析Cayley-Hamilton Hopf代数的结构。通过研究最低判别式理想与不可约表示的张量非退化行为之间的关系，建立代数性质与表示论性质的联系。

Result: 1. 证明了不可约H-模V满足V⊗W完全可约当且仅当V被最低判别式理想零化；2. 解决了Huang-Mi-Qi-Wu的问题：证明了Cayley-Hamilton Hopf代数具有Chevalley性质当且仅当其单位纤维代数具有该性质且所有判别式理想平凡；3. 发现最低判别式子簇是闭子群，具有刚性性质；4. 为构造具有Chevalley性质和有限GK维数的Hopf代数提供了方法。

Conclusion: 判别式理想是研究Cayley-Hamilton Hopf代数Chevalley性质的有效工具，建立了代数结构与表示论性质之间的深刻联系。该方法不仅解决了理论问题，还为构造具有特定性质的Hopf代数和张量范畴提供了框架，特别适用于量子群和低GK维数的Artin-Schelter Gorenstein Hopf代数。

Abstract: In this paper, we study the Chevalley property of Cayley-Hamilton Hopf algebras in the sense of De Concini-Procesi-Reshetikhin-Rosso using discriminant ideals. For any affine Cayley-Hamilton Hopf algebra $(H,C,\text{tr})$ whose identity fiber algebra has the Chevalley property, we prove that an irreducible $H$-module $V$ has the property that $V\otimes W$ is a completely reducible $H$-module for every irreducible $H$-module $W$ if and only if $V$ is annihilated by the lowest discriminant ideal of $(H,C,\text{tr})$, which establishes a bridge between the tensor-nondegenerate behaviour of the irreducible representations of $H$ and the lowest discriminant ideal of $(H,C,\text{tr})$. Using discriminant ideals, we prove that an affine Cayley-Hamilton Hopf algebra $(H,C,\text{tr})$ has the Chevalley property if and only if its identity fiber algebra $H/\mathfrak{m}_{\overline{\varepsilon}}H$ has the Chevalley property and all the discriminant ideals of $(H,C,\text{tr})$ are trivial, thereby resolving a question posed by Huang-Mi-Qi-Wu. Moreover, it is shown that the lowest discriminant subvariety $\mathcal{V}_{\ell}$ of the algebraic group $\operatorname{maxSpec}C$ is a closed subgroup, which reflects the rigid nature of $\mathcal{V}_{\ell}$ and is effective in determining the lowest discriminant subvarieties in certain examples of low GK dimension. This rigidity property provides a method, via the lowest discriminant ideals, for constructing a large family of Hopf algebras with the Chevalley property and finite GK dimension. The results are illustrated through applications to the big quantized Borel subalgebras at roots of unity and to certain Artin-Schelter Gorenstein Hopf algebras of low GK dimension. In particular, the framework yields (non-finite) tensor categories with the Chevalley property arising from some big quantum groups at roots of unity.

</details>
