*Takara Kondo*

Main category: math.CO

TL;DR: 本文完全分类了由自正交码$C$通过Construction A和B构造的格$L_A(C)$和$L_B(C)$的同构类，证明了当且仅当码$C$和$D$同构时，对应的格才同构。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 源于对格顶点算子代数及其orbifolds的研究，旨在建立格与码之间同构分类的对应关系，类似于顶点算子代数分类的格类比。

Method: 推广了格的frame概念，定义了类似于Höhn研究的Kleinian码的模拟码结构，通过码的同构来刻画格的同构。

Result: 对于自正交码$C$和$D$，$L_X(C) \cong L_X(D)$当且仅当$C \cong D$，其中$X=A$或$B$。

Conclusion: 给出了由Construction A和B从自正交码构造的格的完全同构分类，建立了码与格之间的一一对应关系。

Abstract: In this paper, we completely classify the isomorphism classes of certain lattices $L_A(C)$ and $L_B(C)$ from a self-orthogonal code $C$ over the finite field $\mathbb{F}_p$, where $p$ is an odd prime. These lattices are obtained by \emph{Construction A} and \emph{B} for a code $C$ over $\mathbb{F}_p$ introduced by Lam and Shimakura, which arose from a study of orbifolds of lattice vertex operator algebras. For self-orthogonal codes $C$ and $D$ of the same length over $\mathbb{F}_p$, we show that $L_X(C) \cong L_X(C)$ as lattices if and only if $C \cong D$ as codes, where $X=A$ or $B$. This can be expected to be lattice analogues of classifications of the isomorphism classes of lattice vertex operator algebras and its orbifolds. To prove the result, we generalize the notion of a frame of a lattice and define some codes which are analogues of codes constructed from Kleinian codes studied by H{ö}hn.

### [2] [Monomial web basis for the SL(N) skein algebra of the twice punctured sphere](https://arxiv.org/abs/2407.04178)
*Tommaso Cremaschi,Daniel C. Douglas*

Main category: math.GT

TL;DR: 本文为带两个穿孔的球面构造了SL(n) skein代数的线性基，证明其是n-1个生成元的交换多项式代数，并探讨了与量子Trace映射、Fock-Goncharov对偶的关系。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 推广Queffelec--Rose的结果，构造双穿孔球面上SL(n) skein代数的显式线性基，并理解其代数结构。

Method: 利用SL(n)量子Trace映射（Bonahon-Wong对n=2的推广）证明基的生成和线性无关性；通过分裂映射将多项式代数嵌入到Fock-Goncharov量子Teichmüller空间和Lê-Sikora stated skein代数中。

Result: 对于任意非零复数q（排除有限个低阶单位根），双穿孔球面的SL(n) skein代数是n-1个生成元的交换多项式代数，生成元由无交叉的显式SL(n) web表示；q=1时与SL(n)特征簇的坐标环一致。

Conclusion: 量子Trace映射和分裂映射将多项式代数嵌入到量子Teichmüller空间和stated skein代数中，并与Fock-Goncharov对偶相联系。

Abstract: We give a new proof of a slightly modified version of a result of Queffelec--Rose, by constructing a linear basis for the $\mathrm{SL}(n)$ skein algebra of the twice punctured sphere for any non-zero complex number $q$, excluding finitely many roots of unity of small order. In particular, the skein algebra is a commutative polynomial algebra in $n-1$ generators, where each generator is represented by an explicit $\mathrm{SL}(n)$ web, without crossings, on the surface. This includes the case $q=1$, where the skein algebra is identified with the coordinate ring of the $\mathrm{SL}(n)$ character variety of the twice punctured sphere. The proof of both the spanning and linear independence properties of the basis depends on the so-called $\mathrm{SL}(n)$ quantum trace map, due originally to Bonahon--Wong in the case $n=2$. Two consequences of our method are that the quantum trace map and the so-called splitting map embed the polynomial algebra into the Fock--Goncharov quantum higher Teichmüller space and the Lê--Sikora stated skein algebra, respectively, of the annulus. We end by discussing the relationship with Fock--Goncharov duality.

### [3] [Subsystem-Resolved Spectral Theory for Quantum Many-Body Hamiltonians](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.21929)
*MD Nahidul Hasan Sabit*

Main category: quant-ph

TL;DR: 本文提出了一个基于子系统的框架来研究量子多体哈密顿量的谱性质，证明了子系统哈密顿量具有局部近似性，并且其谱在截断下是稳定的。进一步证明了不相交子系统的谱具有近似可加性，在有限程作用下该关系精确成立。该框架揭示了相互作用几何结构如何直接影响谱行为。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有研究多关注哈密顿量算符层面的局域性，但谱性质如何反映相互作用的局域结构尚不清晰。本文旨在建立一个将谱数据与相互作用几何结构直接关联的框架。

Method: 将哈密顿量H=∑Φ(X)分解到子系统上，定义每个子集S对应的子系统哈密顿量H_S及其谱S(S)=σ(H_S)。利用局域近似方法，证明H_S可以被有限邻域上的算子逼近，误差随距离指数衰减；进而推导谱的稳定性与近似可加性。

Result: (1) 子系统谱在截断下稳定：d_H(S(S), σ(H_{S,r})) ≤ |S| e^{-μr}‖Φ‖_μ；(2) 不相交子系统的谱近似可加：d_H(S(S₁∪S₂), S(S₁)+S(S₂)) ≤ (|S₁|+|S₂|) e^{-μD}‖Φ‖_μ，有限程时精确成立。

Conclusion: 谱性质不仅反映算符层面的局域性，也直接体现相互作用的几何结构。该框架为研究相互作用几何塑造谱行为的量子多体系统提供了有效途径。

Abstract: We study spectral properties of quantum many-body Hamiltonians through a subsystem-based framework. Given a Hamiltonian of the form $H = \sum_{X \subseteq Λ} Φ(X)$ acting on a tensor product Hilbert space, we associate to each subset $S \subseteq Λ$ a subsystem Hamiltonian $H_S$ and its spectrum $\mathcal{S}(S) = σ(H_S)$. This produces a family of spectra indexed by subsystems, allowing spectral data to be organized according to interaction structure. We show that subsystem Hamiltonians admit local approximations: $H_S$ can be approximated by operators supported on finite neighborhoods with an error bounded by $\|H_S - H_{S,r}\| \le |S| e^{-μr} \|Φ\|_μ$. As a consequence, subsystem spectra are stable under truncation in the sense that $d_H(\mathcal{S}(S), σ(H_{S,r})) \le |S| e^{-μr} \|Φ\|_μ.$ We then prove that for disjoint subsets $S_1, S_2 \subseteq Λ$, the subsystem spectrum is approximately additive: $d_H\big(\mathcal{S}(S_1 \cup S_2), \mathcal{S}(S_1) + \mathcal{S}(S_2)\big) \le (|S_1| + |S_2|) e^{-μD} \|Φ\|_μ,$ where $D = d(S_1, S_2)$. In the finite-range case, this relation becomes exact. The results show that spectral properties reflect the locality of interactions not only at the level of operators, but also at the level of spectra. The framework provides a way to study many-body systems in which interaction geometry directly shapes spectral behavior.

</details>

### [6] [Reductions in Khovanov-Rozansky operator formalism](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.01584)
*D. Galakhov,E. Lanina,A. Morozov*

Main category: hep-th

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种新的Khovansky-Rozansky（KhR）多项式构造方法，将原本复杂的矩阵因子化替换为基于局部算子和共轭的双复形框架，并展示了在特定情况下可简化为经典Khovanov复形。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统KhR描述中矩阵因子化方法缺乏直观物理意义，作者希望用更简单的局部算子和共轭操作重新构建KhR多项式，使构造过程更具物理解释性。

Method: 用局部算子D（在无外线时幂零）替代矩阵因子化，通过共轭χ^(±)关联不同MOY分辨，建立“垂直”上同调（对应超立方体顶点）和“水平”上同调（对应边上的态射），最终组合成Poincaré多项式。

Result: 该构造保持全局性（结果依赖整体链环图），但所有构建块（算子与态射）均为局部定义。发现局部化简规则，包括Reidemeister等价和反平行锁链结的简化，在N=2和任意N二部情况下可全局约化为Khovanov单复形。

Conclusion: 这种局部化框架不仅赋予了KhR多项式更清晰的物理意义，还揭示了其与经典Khovanov同调之间的深层联系，并为实际计算提供了简化途径。

Abstract: Sophisticated Khovanov-Rozansky (KhR) description of knot invariants in the fundamental representation can be reformulated in terms of bicomplex with a simple physical meaning. Namely, the counterintuitive matrix factorization is substituted by simple operators $D$, locally constructed for every MOY resolution of a link diagram, which becomes nilpotent when the diagram has no external lines. Operators for different resolutions are related by equally simple conjugations $χ^{(\pm)}$. The KhR procedure then splits in two steps - defining ``vertical'' cohomologies of $D$, which are associated with particular resolutions and will be put at vertices of the hypercube, and conjugations $χ^{(\pm)}$, that define morphisms along its edges. As usual, standard combinations of morphisms are nilpotent, and one can define ``horizontal'' cohomologies - which are then combined into Poincaré polynomial, called KhR polynomial in application to links. This construction remains global in the sense that resulting cohomologies depend on the entire link diagram, but all its building blocks, including the operators and morphisms are local in the sense that they are defined for its particular vertices. Sometimes, this allows simple local reductions, allowing to eliminate or change particular vertices or sets of those. Along with the obvious case of Reidemeister equivalencies this happens also for antiparallel-lock tangles, what is responsible for simplification of bipartite calculus. In the $N=2$ and arbitrary $N$ bipartite cases, one can also provide global reductions transferring the local construction of the KhR double-complex to the global construction of the Khovanov(-like) single-complex.

</details>
