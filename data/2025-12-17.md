### [1] [Power counting in the spectral action matrix model](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.14581)
*Eva-Maria Hekkelman,Teun D. H. van Nuland,Jesse Reimann*

Main category: math-ph

TL;DR: 本文推导了带状图振幅的幂计数公式，该公式最近在两个独立研究中被发现：作为Kontsevich模型的推广，以及作为谱作用矩阵模型方法的一部分。Feynman规则基于抽象Dirac算子特征值的差商函数。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究带状图振幅的幂计数行为，这些振幅出现在两个看似不同的数学物理背景中：一是Kontsevich模型的推广，二是谱作用的矩阵模型方法。需要理解这些振幅的发散阶数如何依赖于谱维度、测试函数衰减阶数和图的性质。

Method: 1. 基于差商函数的Feynman规则；2. 建立差商函数的两侧界；3. 将Hunter的正性定理推广到更广泛的函数类；4. 推导发散阶数的显式公式，依赖于谱维度d、测试函数f的衰减阶数以及图的拓扑性质。

Result: 获得了带状图振幅发散阶数的显式幂计数公式。发现所有具有最大发散阶数（在给定圈数和外顶点数下）的图都是平面图。建立了差商函数的新不等式，推广了Hunter的正性定理。

Conclusion: 本文成功推导了带状图振幅的幂计数公式，揭示了谱维度、测试函数衰减和图形性质之间的精确关系。结果统一了两个独立研究背景中的数学结构，并为相关模型的发散分析提供了系统工具。

Abstract: We derive power counting formulas for ribbon graph amplitudes that were recently independently discovered in two contexts, namely as a generalization of the Kontsevich model, and as corresponding to a matrix model approach to the spectral action. The Feynman rules are based on divided difference functions of eigenvalues of an abstract Dirac operator. We obtain formulas for the order of divergence, depending on the spectral dimension $d$, the order of decay of the test function $f$ of the spectral action, and the graph properties. Several consequences are discussed, such as the fact that all graphs with maximal order of divergence (at a given loop order and number of external vertices) are planar. To derive our main results we establish two-sided bounds for divided differences, and in particular generalize Hunter's positivity theorem to a larger class of functions.

### [2] [Line bundles on Contractions of $\overline{\rm{M}}_{0,n}$ via Coinvariant Divisors](https://arxiv.org/abs/2404.10860)
*Daebeom Choi*

Main category: math.AG

TL;DR: 该论文利用顶点算子代数的表示，描述了稳定n点有理曲线模空间收缩上的线丛，证明了这些态射的强化收缩定理，揭示了F-曲线类似K_X-负曲线的行为特征。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究稳定n点有理曲线模空间收缩上的线丛结构，特别是Kapranov、Keel和Knudsen等经典构造，理解这些收缩态射的几何性质。

Method: 使用顶点算子代数的表示理论，证明了一个强化版本的收缩定理，应用于广泛的模空间收缩情形，包括Kapranov、Keel和Knudsen的构造。

Result: 发现许多所谓的F-曲线虽然不满足K_X-负性，但表现出类似K_X-负曲线的行为；特别揭示了Knudsen构造的独特性质，能够分类其所有可能的因子分解。

Conclusion: 顶点算子代数表示为模空间收缩上的线丛描述提供了有力工具，揭示了F-曲线的特殊几何行为，并为Knudsen构造的因子分解分类及其他应用和推广奠定了基础。

Abstract: Using representations of vertex operator algebras, we describe the line bundles on a wide range of contractions of $\overline{\rm{M}}_{0,n}$, the moduli space of stable $n$-pointed rational curves, by proving a stronger version of the contraction theorem for these morphisms. These include the celebrated constructions of Kapranov, Keel, and Knudsen. Our main result suggests that while many so-called F-curves are not $K_X$-negative, they exhibit behavior similar to $K_X$-negative curves. This reveals for instance, a distinguished property of Knudsen's construction $f_{\text{Knu}}:\overline{\rm{M}}_{0,n}\to \overline{\rm{M}}_{0,n-1}\times_{\overline{\rm{M}}_{0,n-2}}\overline{\rm{M}}_{0,n-1}$, allowing for the classification of all possible factorizations of $f_{\text{Knu}}$, as well as further applications, and generalizations.

</details>
