### [2] [Clifford quantum cellular automata from topological quantum field theories and invertible subalgebras](https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.07099)
*Meng Sun,Bowen Yang,Zongyuan Wang,Nathanan Tantivasadakarn,Yu-An Chen*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 该论文提出了一个从拓扑量子场论和可逆子代数构建量子元胞自动机的通用框架，实现了所有Z2和Zp Clifford QCA的显式构造，并与代数L理论分类一致。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 建立量子元胞自动机与拓扑量子场论之间的系统联系，为Clifford QCA提供统一的维度周期性框架，并实现其在任意维度上的显式构造。

Method: 使用cup-product形式体系，从拓扑量子场论和可逆子代数构建量子元胞自动机，适用于任意维度，包括非立方晶格和任意胞腔剖分。

Result: 成功构造了所有Z2和Zp Clifford QCA，确定了它们的阶数，证明了(4l+1)维Z2 Clifford QCA可通过非Clifford有限深度量子电路解纠缠，并在高维构造了可逆子代数。

Conclusion: 该框架建立了Clifford QCA的统一维度周期性构造方法，连接了晶格实现与场理论，为量子元胞自动机研究提供了系统工具。

Abstract: We present a general framework for constructing quantum cellular automata (QCA) from topological quantum field theories (TQFT) and invertible subalgebras (ISA) using the cup-product formalism. This approach explicitly realizes all $\mathbb{Z}_2$ and $\mathbb{Z}_p$ Clifford QCAs (for prime $p$) in all admissible dimensions, in precise agreement with the classification predicted by algebraic $L$-theory. We determine the orders of these QCAs by explicitly showing that finite powers reduce to the identity up to finite-depth quantum circuits (FDQC) and lattice translations. In particular, we demonstrate that the $\mathbb{Z}_2$ Clifford QCAs in $(4l{+}1)$ spatial dimensions can be disentangled by non-Clifford FDQCs. Our construction applies beyond cubic lattices, allowing $\mathbb{Z}_2$ QCAs to be defined on arbitrary cellulations. Furthermore, we explicitly construct invertible subalgebras in higher dimensions, obtaining $\mathbb{Z}_2$ ISAs in $2l$ spatial dimensions and $\mathbb{Z}_p$ ISAs in $(4l{-}2)$ spatial dimensions. These ISAs give rise to $\mathbb{Z}_2$ QCAs in $(2l{+}1)$ dimensions and $\mathbb{Z}_p$ QCAs in $(4l{-}1)$ dimensions. We further prove that the QCAs in $3$ spatial dimensions constructed via TQFTs and ISAs are equivalent by identifying their boundary algebras, and show that this approach extends to higher dimensions. Together, these results establish a unified and dimension-periodic framework for Clifford QCAs, connecting their explicit lattice realizations to field theories.

### [3] [Deformation Quantization via Categorical Factorization Homology](https://arxiv.org/abs/2410.12516)
*Eilind Karlsson,Corina Keller,Lukas Müller,Ján Pulmann*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 该论文通过因子化同调发展了一种范畴形变量子化方法，建立了局部系数量子化与流形上一致量子化的等价性，并将该框架应用于特征栈得到与已有量子化理论的精确关系。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究范畴形变量子化的系统方法，通过因子化同调建立局部与整体量子化的一致性，为特征栈等具体数学结构的量子化提供统一框架。

Method: 引入移位几乎泊松范畴和BD范畴概念，计算取值于完备ℂ[[ħ]]模上带形范畴的因子化同调，定义富集辫子范畴以计算一般闭对称幺半范畴上的因子化同调。

Result: 建立了因子化同调局部系数量子化与流形上一致量子化的等价性；将框架应用于约化代数群G的平坦主丛特征栈，重现了Li-Bland和Ševera的形变；揭示了他们的量子化与Alekseev、Grosse、Schomerus量子化之间的精确关系。

Conclusion: 通过因子化同调为范畴形变量子化提供了系统框架，统一了不同数学物理背景下的量子化理论，建立了局部与整体量子化的深刻联系。

Abstract: This paper develops an approach to categorical deformation quantization via factorization homology. We show that a quantization of the local coefficients for factorization homology is equivalent to consistent quantizations of its value on manifolds. To formulate our results we introduce the concepts of shifted almost Poisson and BD categories. Our main example is the character stack of flat principal bundles for a reductive algebraic group $G$, where we show that applying the general framework to the Drinfeld category reproduces deformations previously introduced by Li-Bland and Ševera. As a direct consequence, we can conclude a precise relation between their quantization and those introduced by Alekseev, Grosse, and Schomerus. To arrive at our results we compute factorization homology with values in a ribbon category enriched over complete $\mathbb{C}[[\hbar]]$-modules. More generally, we define enriched skein categories which compute factorization homology for ribbon categories enriched over a general closed symmetric monoidal category $\mathcal{V}$.

### [4] [Batalin-Vilkovisky structures on moduli spaces of flat connections](https://arxiv.org/abs/2210.08944)
*Anton Alekseev,Florian Naef,Ján Pulmann,Pavol Ševera*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 该论文研究了曲面上的曲线代数结构与平坦联络模空间几何结构之间的对应关系，特别是Turaev余括号对应的几何结构，并针对超李群构造了BV结构和奇映射。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Goldman定理建立了曲线代数与平坦联络模空间函数代数之间的李代数同态，但Turaev余括号对应的几何结构尚不明确。本文旨在解决这个自然问题：Turaev余括号对应平坦联络模空间上的哪种几何结构？

Method: 对于具有奇数不变标量积的李超群，在边界非空的曲面上，通过显式组合Fock-Rosly公式构造平坦联络模空间的Batalin-Vilkovisky（BV）结构。特别针对queer超李群Q(N)，利用"奇迹"函数构造BV态射Φ^odd，该映射同时捕捉Goldman括号和Turaev余括号的信息。

Result: 证明了对于具有奇数不变标量积的李超群，平坦联络模空间M_Σ(G)具有自然的BV结构。对于G=Q(N)，构造了BV态射Φ^odd: ∧G_Σ → Fun(M_Σ(Q(N)))，该映射同时编码了Goldman括号和Turaev余括号的信息，从而回答了Turaev余括号对应的几何结构问题。

Conclusion: 本文成功建立了Turaev余括号与平坦联络模空间上BV结构之间的对应关系，通过构造奇映射Φ^odd统一了Goldman括号和Turaev余括号的几何实现，深化了对曲线代数结构与规范理论模空间几何之间关系的理解。

Abstract: Let $Σ$ be a compact oriented 2-manifold (possibly with boundary), and let $\mathcal G_Σ$ be the linear span of free homotopy classes of closed oriented curves on $Σ$ equipped with the Goldman Lie bracket $[\cdot, \cdot]_\mathrm{Goldman}$ defined in terms of intersections of curves. A theorem of Goldman gives rise to a Lie homomorphism $Φ^\mathrm{even}$ from $(\mathcal G_Σ, [\cdot, \cdot]_\text{Goldman})$ to functions on the moduli space of flat connections $\mathcal{M}_Σ(G)$ for $G=U(N), GL(N)$, equipped with the Atiyah-Bott Poisson bracket.
  The space $\mathcal{G}_Σ$ also carries the Turaev Lie cobracket $δ_\mathrm{Turaev}$ defined in terms of self-intersections of curves. In this paper, we address the following natural question: which geometric structure on moduli spaces of flat connections corresponds to the Turaev cobracket?
  We give a constructive answer to this question in the following context: for $G$ a Lie supergroup with an odd invariant scalar product on its Lie superalgebra, and for nonempty $\partialΣ$, we show that the moduli space of flat connections $\mathcal{M}_Σ(G)$ carries a natural Batalin-Vilkovisky (BV) structure, given by an explicit combinatorial Fock-Rosly formula. Furthermore, for the queer Lie supergroup $G=Q(N)$, we define a BV-morphism $Φ^\mathrm{odd}\colon \wedge \mathcal{G}_Σ \to \mathrm{Fun}(\mathcal{M}_Σ(Q(N)))$ which replaces the Goldman map, and which captures the information both on the Goldman bracket and on the Turaev cobracket. The map $Φ^\mathrm{odd}$ is constructed using the "odd trace" function on $Q(N)$.

</details>
